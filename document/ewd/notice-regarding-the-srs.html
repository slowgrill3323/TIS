NOTICE REGARDING THE SRS

Notice:


Follow the safety precautions and instructions shown below as well as the repair procedures provided by the repair manual that corresponds to the applicable model year when inspecting or repairing the SRS airbag system.
  • Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs) are the most important source for identifying the causes of malfunction as the problem symptoms of the SRS airbag are difficult to confirm. Thus, always check for DTCs before disconnecting the vehicle battery whenever diagnostic troubleshooting procedures are conducted for the SRS airbag system.

  • After checking for DTCs, turn the ignition or power switch to the LOCK or OFF position, disconnect the negative (-) battery terminal cable, and wait for at least 90 seconds before performing troubleshooting.

  • The clock and audio settings stored in the memory will be erased when the negative (-) terminal cable is disconnected from the battery. For that reason, make a record of the audio system settings before disconnecting the battery cable. Restore the audio system settings and the clock time after troubleshooting or repair. Some vehicles are capable of saving the system setting memory for the power steering, power telescopic steering, power seats, and power outer rear view mirrors. However, these system settings cannot be recorded. Before performing troubleshooting or repairs, it is necessary that customers are informed of it and agree that they need to restore and adjust the system settings by themselves after the vehicle is returned to them. In order to prevent the saved settings in each system memory from being lost, DO NOT use a backup power source that is supplied from outside the vehicle.

  • If it is anticipated that the airbag sensors may be subject to impacts during repair of the vehicle, disconnect the airbag sensors before performing repairs.

  • DO NOT directly expose the SRS airbag system components to heat or fire.

  • Examine the SRS airbag system components following any collision, even when the collision was minor and the airbags did not deploy.

  • DO NOT reuse any SRS airbags that were used on another vehicle. Always use new ones if the SRS airbags need to be replaced.

  • DO NOT disassemble or repair the SRS airbags and related components for reuse.
