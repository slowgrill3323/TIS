FUNCTION

(a) Front Seat Belt Warning

(1) The seat belt warning light is for both the driver seat and the passenger seat.

(2) The front seat belt warning function indicates the state of the front seat belts using the combination meter assembly.

(3) The combination meter assembly operates an indicator based on the front seat belt buckle switches, parking brake signal, shift position signal and vehicle speed signal. If a warning is necessary, the buzzer in the combination meter assembly will also sound.

(b) Front Seat Belt Warning (Driver Side)

(1) The flow of operations that results in a seat belt warning is as follows.

Warning Light

Condition Transition

Seat Belt Warning Light
*1: HEV models

*2: Gasoline models

*3: Models with automatic transmission

Ignition switch off.

Warning is not displayed.
  • Ignition switch ON.
  • A driver's seat belt is unfastened.
  • The seat belt warning light blinks for 6 seconds.
  • After 6 seconds, the seat belt warning light changes from blinking to turns off.
  • Ignition switch ON (READY).*1
  • Engine start.*2
  • Except shift lever is in P.*3
  • The parking brake is released.
  • A driver's seat belt is unfastened.

Blinking
  • A driver's seat belt is unfastened.
  • The vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or higher.
  • The seat belt warning light is blinking.
  • After 106 seconds, the seat belt warning light changes from blinking to on.

When the seat belt warning light is a state blinking to on, and the following conditions were concluded, the seat belt warning light is light ON.

  • The vehicle speed decreases to approximately 1.75 km/h (1 mph) or less and the shift position is changed to P.*3
  • The vehicle speed decreases to approximately 1.75 km/h (1 mph) or less and the parking brake is not released.

Light ON

Fasten the driver's seat belt while the seat belt warning light is light ON or blinking to on.

(c) Front Seat Belt Warning (Passenger Side)

(1) The front seat belt warning (passenger side) function indicates the state of the front seat belts using the combination meter assembly.

(2) The combination meter assembly operates an indicator based on the front seat belt buckle switches (front passenger side), occupant classification sensor (front side/rear side), parking brake signal, shift position signal and vehicle speed signal. If a warning is necessary, the buzzer in the combination meter assembly will also sound.

(d) Rear Seat Belt Warning (for Double Cab)

(1) The rear seat belt warning function indicates the state of the rear seat belts using the combination meter assembly.

(2) The combination meter assembly operates an indicator based on the rear seat belt buckle switches, courtesy switches for the rear doors, parking brake signal, shift position signal and vehicle speed signal. If a warning is necessary, the buzzer in the combination meter assembly will also sound.
