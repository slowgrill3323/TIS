GRILLE SHUTTER CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Item

Function

Swing Grille Actuator Assembly
  • Actuates the radiator shutter sub-assembly based on the signal from the air conditioning amplifier assembly and ECM.
  • Sends a diagnosis signal to the combination meter assembly when a malfunction is detected.

Radiator Shutter Sub-assembly

The fins of the radiator shutter sub-assembly are closed by the swing grille actuator assembly to block the airflow to the engine compartment.

ECM

Sends an engine coolant temperature signal to the swing grille actuator assembly via CAN communication.

Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly

Sends the A/C refrigerant pressure and outside temperature to the ECM via CAN communication.

OPERATION

(a) The swing grille actuator assembly performs cold condition control while the engine coolant temperature is low.

(b) The swing grille actuator assembly performs high speed traveling control.

Item

Description

Cold Condition Control
  • When the ignition switch is ON at the engine coolant temperature is below 40°C (104°F) , the grille shutter is closed until the engine coolant temperature reaches 80°C (176°F).
  • Using the air conditioner with low outside temperature, the grille shutter is opened when the pressure of the refrigerant becomes higher.

High Speed Traveling Control
  • When the vehicle traveling speed is between approximately 70 to 144 km/h (43 to 89 mph), the grille shutter is closed.
  • When the vehicle is traveling and engine coolant temperature is approximately 97°C (207°F) or more, the grille shutter is opened.
  • When the vehicle is traveling and outside temperature is approximately 35°C (95°F) or more, the grille shutter is opened.
  • When the vehicle is traveling and air conditioning refrigerant pressure is high, the grille shutter opens and closes in accordance with the vehicle speed and the outside air temperature, etc.

HINT:

Sometimes, the grille shutter operates after the ignition switch turned to ON or Off. This does not indicate a malfunction.

FUNCTION

(a) Warning Indication

(1) The combination meter assembly uses the multi-information display and buzzer to provide the driver with grille shutter system malfunction information.

DIAGNOSIS

(a) If the ECM detects a malfunction in the grille shutter system, Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs) are stored in the ECM.

(1) The DTCs can be read by connecting the Global TechStream (GTS) to the DLC3. For details, refer to the Repair Manual.
