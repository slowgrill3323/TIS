SYSTEM MAIN RELAYS (SMRs)

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The SMRs are relays that connect and disconnect the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) and power cable in accordance with the signal from the hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly. 3 relays are provided: SMRB for the battery positive (+) side, SMRG for the battery negative (-) side, and SMRP for pre-charging.
