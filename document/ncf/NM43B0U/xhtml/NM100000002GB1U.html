SEAT HEATER CONTROL

SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) Front Seat Heater System

(1) When the seat heater switch (air conditioning control assembly) is turned on, the air conditioner amplifier assembly detects the temperature set by the seat heater switch (air conditioning control assembly) together with the temperature detected by the seat heater sensor in the front seat cushion heater, and depending on the conditions, regulates the temperature based on the settings by energizing the front seat heater.

(2) If the ignition switch was turned off while the front seat heater was operating, the front seat heater resumes the operating conditions when the ignition switch is turned to ON.
