WIRELESS TRAILER CAMERA CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

(a) The trailer camera system consists of the following parts:

Component

Function

Wireless Trailer Camera

Sends a video signal of the area to the rear of trailer to the trailer camera ECU.

Antenna

Connected to wireless trailer camera using BLTE and "Wi-Fi".

Trailer Camera ECU

Receives a video from wireless trailer camera. Then, the video is sent to the radio and display receiver assembly via parking assist ECU.

Parking Assist ECU

Sends date from trailer camera ECU to the radio and display receiver assembly.

Inner Rear View Mirror Assembly

Receives visual signals from the wireless trailer camera, and displays them.

Central Gateway ECU (Network Gateway ECU)

Relays the signal between the CAN communication lines.

OPERATING CONDITION

(a) The trailer camera operates when all of the following conditions are met:

(1) System ON

  1. IG ON

(2) Wireless Trailer Camera Connected

  1. The digital rear-view mirror system is available.
  2. The wireless trailer camera is charged/powered and ON mode.
  3. Camera pairing to vehicle is completed.
  4. The wireless trailer camera is within range of antenna.

(3) Inner Rear View Mirror Assembly

  1. "Wireless Camera Mode" is selected from the radio and display receiver assembly HMI menu.

(4) Radio and Display Receiver Assembly

  1. "AUX" is selected in PVM menu.

FUNCTION

(a) The trailer camera system will allow user to view wireless trailer camera feed through inner rear view mirror assembly and radio and display receiver assembly. HMI menu allows access to all trailer camera functions, including pairing/removing/connecting wireless cameras, toggling between CHMSL camera and wireless trailer camera in the inner rear view mirror assembly, and diagnostic feedback of the wireless camera system.
