MIRROR HEATER CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

(a) The main components in the mirror heater control system have the following functions:

Component

Outline

Air Conditioning Control Assembly

Outputs rear window defogger and mirror heater switch on signals to the air conditioning amplifier assembly.

Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly

Inputs rear window defogger and mirror heater switch on signals and outputs mirror heater operation request signals to the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU).

Main Body ECU (Multiplex Network Body ECU)

Sends the mirror heater operation request signals from the air conditioning amplifier assembly to the power distribution box assembly.

Power Distribution Box Assembly

Inputs mirror heater operation request signals to the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) and supplies power to the mirror heater.

Outer Rear View Mirror Assembly LH/RH

Heats the mirror surface mirror heater.

SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) When the rear window defogger and mirror heater switch are turned on with the ignition switch on, rear window defogger and mirror heater switch on signals are output to the air conditioning amplifier assembly. The air conditioning amplifier assembly, which inputs these signals, outputs mirror heater operation request signals to the power distribution box assembly, and the power distribution box assembly heats the mirror heater in the outer rear view mirror assembly LH/RH.

(b) The air conditioning amplifier assembly counts the time of the timer, and after the mirror heater is continuously on for approximately 15 minutes, it stops outputting the signal.
