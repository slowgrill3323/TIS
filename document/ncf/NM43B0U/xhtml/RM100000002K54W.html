COOLING FAN CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function
*: 1Motor-HEV models

ECM

Sends control signals to the cooling fan ECU (fan with motor assembly).

Cooling Fan ECU (Fan with Motor Assembly)

Operates according to signals received from the ECM.

Engine Coolant Temperature Sensor

Detects the engine coolant temperature.

Crank Position Sensor

Detects the engine speed.

Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly

Transmits the operating state of the air conditioning system and refrigerant pressure to the ECM.

Hybrid Vehicle Control ECU*

Transmits the hybrid system coolant temperature and vehicle speed signal to the ECM.

FUNCTION

(a) The cooling fan control system achieves an optimal fan speed in accordance with the engine coolant temperature, vehicle speed, engine speed, and air conditioning operating conditions.

(b) Gasoline Models

The fan with motor assembly (built-in cooling fan ECU) controls the speed of the fan in accordance with the engine coolant temperature, vehicle speed, engine speed and air conditioning operating conditions.

(c) 1Motor-HEV Models

The fan with motor assembly (built-in cooling fan ECU) controls the speed of the fan in accordance with the engine coolant temperature, hybrid system coolant temperature, vehicle speed, engine speed and air conditioning operating conditions.
