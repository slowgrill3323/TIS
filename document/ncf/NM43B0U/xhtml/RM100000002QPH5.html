Title: ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: ADVANCED DRIVE ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2025 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The Toyota Safety Sense consists of the driving assist systems and contributes to a safe and comfortable driving experience:

  • PCS (Pre-Collision System)
  • Dynamic Radar Cruise Control (DRCC)
  • LTA (Lane Tracing Assist)
  • LDA (Lane Departure Alert)
  • EDSS (Emergency Driving Stop System)*1
  • RSA (Road Sign Assist)*2
  • AHB (Automatic High Beam System)
  • PDA (Proactive Driving Assist)*3
    • *1: Models with EDSS (Emergency Driving Stop System)
    • *2: Models with RSA (Road Sign Assist)
    • *3: Models with PDA (Proactive Driving Assist)

(b) When the vehicle height is increased by 4.1 inches or more:

NOTICE:

The value of the vehicle height increase in inches is rounded up.

HINT:

When the total height is increased 4.1 inches or more, advanced driver assistance system is not supported and must be disabled.
