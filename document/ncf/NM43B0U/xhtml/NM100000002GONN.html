PURGE VSV

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The system controls the canister purge amount according to signals (duty signals) from the ECM. The purge amount is determined by the signal on/off ratio (duty ratio).

(b) The system is lighter and more compact due to using a purge valve integrated with the check valve.
