POWER CABLE (FRAME WIRE)

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The power cables are high voltage/high current electrical wires. They are mainly used between the HV supply battery assembly, the hybrid motor control inverter assembly, hybrid module assembly and compressor with motor assembly.

(b) The power cable is made of shielded cables in order to reduce electromagnetic interference.

(c) For identification purposes, the high-voltage wiring harness and connectors are color-coded orange to distinguish them from those of the ordinary low-voltage wiring.
