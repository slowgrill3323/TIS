COMBINATION METER

OPERATION

(a) Indicator Display

(1) When the BSM function is off, the driving assist information indicator illuminates.

(2) When the RCTA function is off, the driving assist information indicator illuminates.*

  • *: Models with RCTA function

(3) When a malfunction is occurred, the driving assist information indicator illuminates, the meter buzzer sounds and warnings are displayed on the multi-information display in order to inform the driver of a system malfunction.

(b) Warning Display

(1) When a malfunction occurs in the blind spot monitor system or the system cannot be used, a warning is displayed on the multi-information display, each indicator illuminates and the meter buzzer sounds to inform the driver that the system is unavailable.
