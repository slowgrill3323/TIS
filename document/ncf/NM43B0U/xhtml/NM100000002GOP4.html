OIL COOLER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) An engine oil cooler (oil cooler assembly) made from aluminum has been adopted, providing reduced weight as well as highly efficient cooling performance.

(b) Engine oil that is cooled via the engine oil cooler (oil cooler assembly) is supplied to the main oil hole. With this, excessive engine oil temperature and engine oil deterioration are minimized.

(c) Also, to improve serviceability, a hood is installed to prevent oil from dripping.
