RUST-RESISTANT BODY

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Anti-chipping Application and Rust-resistant Performance

(1) Wax is applied to the floor panel and edge of the door lower portion, door hinge and the hemmed portions of the hood panel to improve rust-resistant performance.

(2) Soft anti-chipping primer and Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) anti-chipping coating are applied to various parts of the body to help prevent rust.
