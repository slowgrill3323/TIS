DIFFERENTIAL LOCK MECHANISM

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The electronic differential lock case sub-assembly consists of a differential case, differential side gear, differential lock spring, differential lock cam ring, differential lock position plate, differential lock plunger and differential lock coil.

(b) The differential side gear (dog clutch) and the differential lock cam ring have been provided with teeth to function as a dog clutch.

(c) The 4 wheel drive control ECU supplies current to the differential lock coil, which generates magnetic flux and operates the differential lock plunger to engage the dog clutch, locking the differential.
