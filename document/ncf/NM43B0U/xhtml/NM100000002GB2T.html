Title: RC60F (MANUAL TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): MANUAL TRANSMISSION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) An RC60F 6-speed manual transmission is used.

(b) An output reduction method is used to achieve a compact construction and weight reduction.

(c) The gear range has been expanded due to the gear layout of the 4th gear direct coupling and double overdrive (5th and 6th gears), achieving superior fuel economy and drivability.

(d) A multi-cone synchromesh mechanism has been provided for the low gears (1st and 2nd) to achieve a superior shift feeling.

(e) An intelligent Manual Transmission (iMT) system is used. This system adjusts the engine speed during a shift change to match the transmission speed, achieving a smooth shift change.
