FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function

Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor Assembly

Radiates millimeter waves and uses the reflected waves to detect objects in front of the vehicle as well as their direction and relative velocity.

Blind Spot Monitor Sensor(LH/RH)

Radiates radar waves and uses the reflected waves to detect vehicles behind the vehicle in adjacent lanes as well as the vehicle distance and relative velocity.

Forward Recognition Camera
  • Detects preceding vehicles, obstructions, driving lanes and speed limit signs.
  • Obtains necessary information for system control from each ECU and sensor, and sends control signals as necessary.

Windshield Glass

Camera Heater

Performs defogging and prevents the windshield glass in front of the forward recognition camera from fogging up.

Steering Sensor

Detects the angle and direction of steering.

Airbag ECU Assembly

Yawrate Sensor

Detects the yaw rate and lateral/longitudinal deceleration of the vehicle.

Speed Sensors

Detect the wheel speed of each of the 4 wheels.

Steering Pad Switch Assembly

Changes the display of the multi-information display in the combination meter assembly.

ECM
  • Sends signals such as a throttle valve opening angle signal.
  • Receives control request signals and limits engine output.
  • Sends shift state signals.*1

Transmission Control ECU*2

Sends shift state signals.

Power Steering ECU
  • Receives control request signals and provides steering assistance force.
  • Receives control request signals and vibrates the steering wheel.
  • Detects steering wheel operation by the driver and sends signals.

Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly

Sends the ambient temperature signal.

Main Body ECU (Multiplex Network Body ECU)
  • Outputs vehicle specification information signals.
  • Outputs signals such as a horn operation request signal.

Skid Control ECU*3
  • Receives brake operation request signals and operates the brake.
  • Operates the stop light in accordance with brake control.

No.2 Skid Control ECU*4
  • Receives brake operation request signals and operates the brake.
  • Operates the stop light in accordance with brake control.

DCM (Telematics Transceiver)

Connects to call center.

Combination Meter Assembly

Multi-information Display
  • Notifies the driver of system operation.
  • Notifies the driver of system malfunctions or temporary system suspensions.

PCS OFF Warning
  • Illuminates when the PCS is disabled.
  • Illuminates when the PCS is temporarily stopped or malfunctioning.

Cruise Control Indicator (Adaptive Cruise Mode )
  • Illuminates when adaptive cruise mode of the dynamic radar cruise control is set to ON.
  • Illuminates (amber) when the dynamic radar cruise control is malfunctioning.

Cruise Control Indicator (Cruise Control Mode)
  • Illuminates when cruise control mode of the dynamic radar cruise control is set to ON.
  • Illuminates (amber) when the dynamic radar cruise control is malfunctioning.

LDA Indicator

(White)*5

  • Illuminates when the LDA is on standby.

(Green)

  • Illuminates when LDA is operating.

(Yellow)

  • Flashes when a lane departure alert is operated by the LDA.
  • Illuminates when the LDA is temporarily unavailable.
  • Illuminates when the LDA is malfunctioning.

(Yellow+OFF Indicator)*6

  • Illuminates when LDA is OFF.

LTA Indicator

(White)

  • Illuminates when the LTA is ON.

(Green)

  • Illuminates when the steering assist control is operated by the LTA.

(Yellow)

  • Flashes when a lane departure alert is operated by the LTA.
  • Illuminates when the LTA is temporarily unavailable.
  • Illuminates when the LTA is malfunctioning.

Steering Icon

(Gray)

  • Illuminates when the LTA is ON.

(Green)

  • Illuminates when steering assist control is being performed.

Road Sign Icons*7

Illuminates an icon in accordance with the road sign when the RSA detects a road sign.

Headlight High Beam Indicator

Illuminates when the high beams are ON.

Automatic High Beam Indicator

Illuminates when the AHB is operating.

Proactive Driving Assist Indicator*8

(White)

  • Illuminates when proactive driving assist is on standby.

(Green)

  • Illuminates when the proactive driving assist is operating in response to a pedestrian/bicycle crossing the road or operating in response to a pedestrian/bicycle/parked vehicle on the side of the road.

(Yellow)

  • Illuminates when the proactive driving assist is malfunctioning.

Driving Assist Information Indicator

Illuminates when any of the following systems are in the off state.

  • ParKing Support Brake (PKSB)*9
  • Blind Spot Monitor (BSM)*10
  • Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA)*11
  • Rear Camera Detection (RCD)*12

Illuminates when any of the following systems are abnormal.

  • ParKing Support Brake (PKSB)*9
  • Blind Spot Monitor (BSM)*10
  • Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA)*11
  • Rear Camera Detection (RCD)*12
  • Pre-Collision System (PCS)
  • Lane Departure Alert (LDA)

Buzzer
  • Sounds a buzzer when the alarm is activated and warns the driver about the danger of collision and notifies of system operation.
  • Sounds a buzzer when the PCS is malfunctioning and warns the driver that the PCS is disabled.

Headup Display (Meter Mirror Sub-assembly)*13

Notifies the driver of system operation.

High Pitched Horn Assembly

Sounds based on the signal from the main body ECU.

Low Pitched Horn Assembly
