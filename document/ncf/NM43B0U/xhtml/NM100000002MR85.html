PARKING LOCK MECHANISM

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The parking lock mechanism consists of a parking lock pawl and a parking lock gear, parking lock rod sub-assembly, manual valve lever sub-assembly.

(b) When the shift lever is moved to P, the parking lock pawl engages with the parking lock gear connected to the output shaft so that the rotation of the output shaft is fixed.
