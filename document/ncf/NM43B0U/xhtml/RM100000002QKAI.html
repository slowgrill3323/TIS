GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Design Theme : BAD ASS ADVENTURE MACHINE

(1) BAD Ass Adventure Machine interior combines Active Tech, durable lightweight structure, and a serious, yet FUN and FUNCTIONAL image.

(b) Overall Theme

(1) Proportion and Construction

  • Robust core beam unifies the instrument panel with a strong horizontal structural axis. The low axis emphasis gives an active lift-up driving feel. (*A in illustration)
  • Glove box is interlocked into the core beam. On upper grades this part is a wrapped part bringing soft quality into a common touch area. (*B in illustration)
  • Sporty grab handles with soft grip surface and integrated storage are positioned at door and console. (*C in illustration)
  • Integrated utility hook feature for quick storage of small handbag or carry-out parcels. (*D in illustration)
