Title: T24A-FTS (LUBRICATION): LUBRICATION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The lubrication system is fully pressurized, has an electronically controlled variable volume oil pump (fully variable oil pump/internal gear type), and all oil passes through an oil filter.

(b) By controlling the fully variable oil pump based on the engine oil temperature, engine oil pressure, engine speed, etc., the oil pressure and oil volume are optimally controlled to reduce friction.

(c) A water-cooled engine oil cooler (oil cooler assembly) is used.
