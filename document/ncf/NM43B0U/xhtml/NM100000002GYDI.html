VACUUM PUMP

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A vacuum pump assembly is used as the intake system used to increase fuel economy and ensure early catalyst start times immediately after cold start does not ensure a sufficient vacuum. With this, the vacuum required to operate the vacuum type brake booster and wastegate valve is ensured.

(b) The vacuum pump assembly is installed on the rear end face of the engine and driven by the exhaust camshaft.
