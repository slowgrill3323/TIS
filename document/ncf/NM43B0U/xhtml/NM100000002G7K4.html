SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) Operating Condition

(1) The automatic light control system operates as follows:

Function

Outline

Headlights and Taillights Turn On

When all of the following conditions are met, the headlights, parking lights, front side marker lights, taillights, rear side marker lights, and license plate lights automatically turn on:

  • The ambient light is dark or momentarily bright.
  • The ignition switch is ON.
  • The light control switch is in the AUTO position.

Headlights and Taillights Turn Off

When the ambient light is bright, the exterior lights automatically turn off.
