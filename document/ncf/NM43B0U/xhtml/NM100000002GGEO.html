ROOF HOUSING

CONSTRUCTION

(a) When the rear shoe moves forward with the sliding roof fully closed, the guide pin of the rear shoe slides along the guide of the function bracket. The function bracket uses the shape of the guide, which slides the guide pin, to raise the sliding roof glass. At this time, the rain channel that is connected to the function bracket is simultaneously raised.
