Driver/Front Passenger Seat Belt Warning

Red
  • Illuminates when the ignition switch is ON while the driver seat belt or passenger seat belt is unfastened.
  • Blinks when any of the following conditions while the seat belt is unfastened.
    • The vehicle speed is 12 mph or more.
    • When the shift position is other than P and the vehicle is not parked (vehicle speed is 1 mph or more).
    • When the parking brake is not engaged and the vehicle is not parked (vehicle speed is 1 mph or more).
  • Illuminates after a certain period of blinking.
