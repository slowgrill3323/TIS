METER/GAUGE

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Tachometer

(1) Displays the engine speed based on the signal transmitted from ECM.

(b) Speedometer

(1) Displays the vehicle speed based on the signal transmitted from No. 2 skid control ECU*1 or skid control ECU*2.

HINT:

*1: HEV Models

*2: Gasoline Models

(c) Engine Coolant Temperature Gauge

(1) Displays the engine coolant temperature based on the signal transmitted from ECM.

(d) Fuel Gauge

(1) Normal Operation

  • Displays the remaining amount of fuel and the drivable distance based on the value calculated by the meter ECU receiving the signal from fuel sender gauge assembly and ECM.

Drivable Distance

Display Range

1 to 9999 km (miles)

Minimum Unit

1 km (mile)

Function
  • Performs learning*1 of fuel economy based on the vehicle speed signal and fuel consumption signal, etc., and calculates and displays the possible driving distance*2 using the current fuel level signal.
  • The displayed value is updated every second.
  • When the value cannot be calculated, the display changes to blank.

HINT:

*1: Learning of fuel economy is updated at each refueling judgment based on the previous fuel economy before refueling and average fuel economy after refueling.

*2: Calculated based on the value when a successful refueling judgment is made during refueling. (Refueling judgment amount: 10 liters (2.6 gallon)*1, 2 liters (0.5 gallon)*2 or more)

HINT:

*1: Gasoline Models

*2: HEV Models

(2) Fuel Level Warning

  • Illuminates the low fuel level warning when the fuel level is below the threshold.

(3) Fuel Refueling Process

  • Refueling judgement processing is performed if refueling of 10 liters (2.6 gallon)*1, 2 liters (0.5 gallon)*2 or more is detected while the vehicle is stopped and the power source mode is turned ON.

HINT:

*1: Gasoline Models

*2: HEV Models

(e) Shift Position Indicator

(1) Displays the current shift position based on the signal transmitted from transmission control ECU.

(f) ODO Meter/Oil Maintenance

(1) Displays the total mileage or until next engine oil change. Also displays the power source status when the power source mode is turned on ACC or ON (engine system*1 or hybrid system*2 not started).

HINT:

*1: HEV Models

*2: Gasoline Models

(2) Displays the odometer for a certain period of time after the power source is turned off.

ODO Meter

Display Range

0 to 999999 km (miles)

Function

Displays 999999 km (miles) when there is a display overflow.

(g) Outside Temperature Gauge

(1) Displays the outside temperature.

(2) When the outside temperature drops below 3 째C (37 째F), an ice warning is displayed to warn the driver to drive with caution due to possibly icy road conditions.

Outside Temperature Gauge

Display Range

-40 to 50 째C (-40 to 122 째F)

Function
  • A signal from the air conditioning amplifier assembly is processed and the outside air temperature is displayed.
  • The displayed value is updated approximately every second.

(h) Clock

(1) Displays the current time.

(2) Displays for a certain period of time after the power source is turned off.

(i) Driving Assist System Information

(1) Displays the operating status of the following systems when the driving support screen is not displayed on any of the multi-information displays:

  • Dynamic Radar Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Alert (LDA)
  • Lane Tracing Assist System (LTA)

(j) Road Sign Icons (Models with RSA)

(1) Illuminates an icon in accordance with the road sign when the Road Sign Assist (RSA) detects a road sign.

(k) Rheostat

(1) A rheostat function which can be used to control the brightness of the meter panel is installed.

(2) The system is equipped with a function that varies the brightness depending on day/night judgment.
