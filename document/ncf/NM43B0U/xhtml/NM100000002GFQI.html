SHIFT CONTROL

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A shift control mechanism with a push-pull type transmission cable assembly is used.

(b) A straight type shift lever, which is installed on the instrument panel, is provided. Additionally, a transmission control switch, which allows a desired shift range to be selected by moving the shift lever toward "+" (upshift) or "-" (downshift), is provided, enabling the driver to select shift ranges with a manual-like feel.

(c) A shift lock release button is provided inside the shift lever housing in consideration of emergency situations.
