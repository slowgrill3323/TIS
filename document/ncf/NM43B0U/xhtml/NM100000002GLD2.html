CLEARANCE WARNING BUZZER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Different buzzer sounding intervals of the meter buzzer (combination meter assembly) are used to notify the driver of the approximate distance from the vehicle to stationary objects or system malfunctioning.

(b) Sounds the meter buzzer according to control of the clearance warning ECU assembly. For details, see the meter section.

OPERATION

(a) Meter Buzzer Sounding

(1) If a static object is detected at longest range, long-range or medium-range, the buzzer sounding interval changes according to the distance from the object, and the clearance warning buzzer sounds.

(2) If a static object is detected at short-range or shortest range, the clearance warning buzzer sounds continuously.
