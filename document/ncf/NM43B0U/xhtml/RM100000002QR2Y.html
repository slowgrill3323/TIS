GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Depending on the specifications, an AC 120 V power outlet socket (400 W) is installed to the rear right of the deck area.*

HINT:

*: Gasoline models

(b) An AC 120 V power outlet socket (2400 W) is installed to the rear right of the deck area.*

HINT:

*: HEV models

(c) Depending on the specifications, an outlet No. 2 power socket with an DC 12 V power outlet socket and a dual USB-C power outlet socket is installed to the rear left of the deck area.

PRECAUTION

(a) To prevent damage to the power outlet:

(1) Do not insert foreign objects into the ports.

(2) Do not spill water or other liquids into the ports.

(3) When the power outlets are not in use, close the lids. If a foreign object or liquid enters a port may cause a short circuit.

(4) Do not apply excessive force to or impact the power outlets ports.

(b) To prevent depletion of the auxiliary battery:

(1) If a device with a power consumption of 100W or more is to be used for an extended period of time, turn off all electrical devices and accessories (E.g. headlights, air conditioner, etc.).

(c) To prevent depletion of the auxiliary battery:*

HINT:

*: Gasoline models

(1) If a device with a power consumption of 100W or more is to be used for an extended period of time, turn off all electrical devices and accessories (E.g. headlights, air conditioner, etc.).

(d) To prevent depletion of the hybrid battery:*

HINT:

*: HEV models

(1) If a device with a power consumption of 100W or more is to be used for an extended period of time, turn off all electrical devices and accessories (E.g. headlights, air conditioner, etc.).

(e) Accessory power outlet system (2400 W power outlet socket)

  • Make sure that all devices connected across all accessory power outlets (2400 W) have a maximum combined power consumption of 2400 W or less. If the power consumption of all devices connected across all accessory power outlets exceeds the rated capacity, the protection function will operate and the accessory power outlet system will not be able to be used.
  • Depending on the device to be used, the current flow may be high and the initial peak wattage may exceed 2400 W. In this case, the protection circuit may operate, and the power outlets may not be able to be used.
  • Depending on the connected device, it may cause interference with TV and radio broadcasts.
  • The voltage of the power outlets cannot be measured with a commercially available electrical tester.

    CAUTION:

    • Do not perform an inspection of the output voltage as the accessory power outlet system uses high voltage supplied by the hybrid battery.
  • When using the accessory power outlet system, the noise of a cooling fan may be heard from under the center console. This is not a malfunction.

(f) Devices that may not operate correctly (2400 W power outlet socket)

(1) The following 120 V devices may not operate properly even if their power consumption is less than 2400 W:

  • Devices with high initial peak wattage
  • Measuring devices that process precise data
  • Devices that require an extremely stable power supply
  • Devices that require a constant power supply from the power outlet, such as a device with a timer
