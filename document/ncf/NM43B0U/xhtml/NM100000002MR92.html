HV BATTERY

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A service plug grip is provided to shut off the high-voltage circuit, ensuring safety during maintenance work.

(b) The HV supply battery assembly is composed of the HV battery modules, battery voltage sensor, battery cooling blower assembly, No. 2 battery cooling blower assembly, No. 1 traction battery device box assembly (System Main Relays (SMRs), battery current sensor and precharge resistor), hybrid battery thermistor and service plug grip.

(c) The HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) consists of 40 separate HV battery modules. They are connected to each other in series through 2 bus bar modules.

(d) These HV battery modules are each made up of 6 cells. The HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) has a total of 240 cells (6 cells x 40 modules) and a nominal voltage of 288 V (1.2 V x 240 cells).
