(g) IsoDynamic Performance Seat (Models with 6-way Power + 2-way Manual Seat)

(1) The seatback of the driver seat and front passenger seats are designed to be movable within a certain range to reduce the burden on the occupants when driving off-road.

(2) The seatback can be moved in vertical, lateral, and rotational directions, and shock absorbers are set to act in vertical and lateral direction to reduce excess vibration.

(3) The shock absorbers, which suppress vertical and lateral movement, can be adjusted to the occupant preference by operating a pressure adjustment valve on the seatback.

(4) A pressure gauge is placed on the upper side of the pressure adjustment knob, which can be used as a guide when making adjustments.
