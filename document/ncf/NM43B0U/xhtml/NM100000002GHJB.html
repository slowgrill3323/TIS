POWER TAIL GATE UNIT

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The power tail gate unit assembly RH consists of a motor and a reduction gear, and opens and closes the tail gate by converting the rotational motion of the motor into linear motion through the screw mechanism.

(b) The power tail gate unit assembly RH is equipped with rubber corrugated boot and waterproof wire harness connectors to prevent ingress of wind and rain.
