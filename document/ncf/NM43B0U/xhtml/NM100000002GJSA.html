Title: MIRROR (INT): TRAILER CAMERA SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

GENERAL

(a) The trailer camera system enhances driver visibility within the digital rear-view mirror system as well as backing up a trailer. Driver challenges include, but are not limited to, merging into traffic, lane changes, reversing, parking and monitoring surroundings.

(b) The trailer camera system was developed to transmit video from a wireless camera to the inner rear view mirror assembly for a "far view mode" useful for lane changes and merging with a trailer.

(c) Additionally, the system is capable of providing video to the audio head unit to provide a "near view mode" that will support parking and reversing a trailer.
