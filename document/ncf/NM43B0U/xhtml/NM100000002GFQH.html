TRANSMISSION CONTROL SWITCH

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The transmission control switch is installed inside the transmission floor shift assembly to detect the S mode position and inform the ECM. The ECM indicates the shift position indicator S in the combination meter assembly via CAN.

(b) The transmission control switch detects whether the shift lever is in D or S, detects whether the shift lever is moved to "+" (upshift) or "-" (downshift) when the S mode is selected and sends signals to the ECM. At this time, the ECM indicates the selected gear position in the combination meter assembly via CAN.
