CRANKSHAFT AND BEARING

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The crankshaft design has been optimized to reduce weight.

(b) The crankshaft has 5 main bearing journals and 8 balance weights.

(c) A balance shaft drive gear is provided on the crankshaft.

(d) Resin coated aluminum crankshaft bearings are used for the crankshaft bearings.

(e) The oil groove on the crankshaft bearing is made eccentric to reduce the amount of oil leakage from the bearing. This enables the capacity of the oil pump to be reduced in order to reduce parasitic losses.

(f) By using a grooved crankshaft bearing (lower main bearing) for the No. 3 crankshaft bearing journals, cavitation is suppressed.
