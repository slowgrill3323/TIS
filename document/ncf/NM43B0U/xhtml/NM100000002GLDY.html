REAR TELEVISION CAMERA

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The rear television camera assembly is located in the tailgate.

(b) The rear No. 2 television camera assembly is located in the center stop light assembly.

(c) The rear television camera assembly and rear No. 2 television camera assembly consists of a screen input component with a wide-angle lens and a video signal output component with a small type color Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) camera.

(d) The video screen of the rear television camera assembly and rear No. 2 television camera assembly reverses the video output from the rear television camera assembly and rear No. 2 television camera assembly, to match the view as if looking to the rear in the inner rear view mirror assembly. Note that this means that the video is horizontally reversed from what would be actually seen to the rear (objects on the left of the vehicle are on the left side of the screen, while objects on the right side of the vehicle are on the right side of the screen).
