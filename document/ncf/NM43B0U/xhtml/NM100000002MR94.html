SERVICE PLUG

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The service plug grip is connected to the HV battery module circuit and is used to manually shut off the high-voltage circuit.

(b) An interlock switch is provided on the service plug grip. When the grip section is unlocked, the interlock switch is turned off and the hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly shuts off the SMRs. However, to ensure safety, make sure to turn the ignition switch off before removing the service plug grip.

NOTICE:

For further details on how to handle the service plug grip and for other safety cautions, refer to the repair manual.
