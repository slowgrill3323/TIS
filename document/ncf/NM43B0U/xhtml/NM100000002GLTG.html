CAMERA HEATER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A heater for the forward recognition camera is installed to the forward recognition cover installation area of the windshield.

OPERATION

(a) When all of the following conditions have been met, the camera heater operates.

  • IG ON
  • The outside air temperature is approximately 10°C (50°F) or less.

(b) The camera heater is energized by the forward recognition camera and generates heat to defog or prevent fogging of the windshield.

(c) After the camera heater operates, the operation will stop when a certain length of time has elapsed.

HINT:

Operating time will differ depending on vehicle conditions (vehicle speed, outside air temperature, etc.).
