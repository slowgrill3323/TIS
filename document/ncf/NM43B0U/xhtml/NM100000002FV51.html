GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Power running board system has been used to automatically deploy and stow the steps when the doors are opened or closed.

(b) System control is regulated by the side auto step controller ECU assembly.

(c) A jam protection function has been used to reverse the motor and stow (or deploy) the step when a foreign object is detected during deployment (or stowing).

(d) If the step is not deployed to the proper position for any reason, a message is displayed on the multi-information display in the combination meter assembly.
