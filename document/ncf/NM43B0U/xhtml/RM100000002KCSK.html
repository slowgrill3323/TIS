SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) The light auto turn-off system operates as follows:

Function

Operation Condition

Taillight Auto Turn-off

When all of the following conditions are met, the exterior lights automatically turn off:

  • The ignition switch is turned from ON to off.
  • The taillight are on (light control switch is in the TAIL or AUTO position).
  • Any door is closed and then opened.

Headlight Auto Turn-off (Delay)

When all of the following conditions are met, the exterior lights automatically turn off after 30 seconds:

  • The ignition switch is turned from ON to off.
  • The headlights are on (light control switch is in the HEAD or AUTO position).
  • All doors are closed after any of the doors are opened.

Key-linked

When the delay function is active and the following condition is met, the exterior lights turn off immediately:

  • All doors are locked.
