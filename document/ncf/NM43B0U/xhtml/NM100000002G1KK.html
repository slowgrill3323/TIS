OPERATION

(a) The tail gate can be opened and closed by pressing the elbow switch (luggage electrical key switch).

HINT:

The tail gate can be opened and closed, without affecting the locking/unlocking of the power tail gate lock or the settings of the vehicle alarm by holding the electrical key transmitter sub-assembly and operating the tail gate handle switch (tail gate opener switch assembly) or the elbow switch (luggage electrical key switch).
