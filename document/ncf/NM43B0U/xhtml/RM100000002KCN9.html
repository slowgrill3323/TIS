ENGINE MOUNTING INSULATOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Gasoline Models

  1. A 3 mount construction is used (front engine mounting insulator RH, front engine mounting insulator LH and rear engine mounting insulator).
  2. For the front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH, liquid-filled engine mounts are used, resulting in reduced vibration and noise.*

HINT:

*: Except models with Automatic Transmission and SR grade package

(b) 1Motor-HEV Models

  1. A 3 mount construction is used (front engine mounting insulator RH, front engine mounting insulator LH and rear engine mounting insulator).
  2. For the front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH, a vacuum switching type engine mount is used which has variable damping, resulting in reduced vibration when idling.
