WIPER BLADE

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Front Wiper Blade

(1) A flat type wiper blade is used for a sharp look with a sense of luxury, and for an improved external appearance.

(2) The wiper blade length is 600 mm ( 23.6in.) for the driver side and 500 mm (19.7 in.) for the front passenger side. Superior forward visibility is ensured by the increased sweep range.

(3) The blade rubber surface is coated with graphite to reduce the impulsive noise that occurs when the blades operate in reverse and increase the lifespan of the blade rubber.
