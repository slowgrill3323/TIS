GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A drive belt operated mechanical water pump is used.

(b) The cooling system is a pressurized, forced-circulation type.

(c) An anti-freezing mechanism is provided that feeds warm water from the cylinder head sub-assembly to the throttle body with motor assembly, stabilizing the operations during extremely cold temperatures.

(d) A water control valve is used to optimally control the flow rate of the coolant by switching the coolant path and adjusting the flow rate depending on the operating condition of the engine, achieving improved fuel economy and air conditioning performance.

(e) TOYOTA genuine Super Long Life Coolant (SLLC) is used.

(f) By utilizing a grill shutter system, the amount of airflow to the coolant system can be optimized. This ensures early warm up of the engine.

SPECIFICATION

(a) Engine Coolant

(1) TOYOTA genuine Super Long Life Coolant (SLLC) is used. The maintenance interval is as shown in the table below:

Coolant Type

TOYOTA Genuine SLLC or the Following

Maintenance Intervals

First Time

160000 km (100000 miles)

Subsequent

Every 80000 km (50000 miles)

Color

Pink

HINT:

Similar high quality ethylene glycol based non-silicate, non-amine, non-nitrite, and non-borate coolant with long-life hybrid organic acid technology. (Coolant with long-life hybrid organic acid technology consists of a combination of low phosphates and organic acids.)

(2) SLLC is pre-mixed (50% coolant and 50% deionized water). Therefore, no dilution is needed when SLLC in the vehicle is added or replaced.
