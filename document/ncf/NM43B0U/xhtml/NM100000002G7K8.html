COLLISION-LINKED CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function
*: Models with side turn signal light assembly

LED Driver Module RH (Headlight LED ECU RH)

Relays front turn signal light flashing/turning off signals.

LED Driver Module LH (Headlight LED ECU LH)

Combination Meter Assembly
  • Receives hazard blink request signals from the airbag ECU and blinks the front turn signal lights, side turn signal lights* and rear turn signal lights.
  • Receives signals from switches and ECUs and turns off the front turn signal lights, side turn signal lights* and rear turn signal lights.

Airbag ECU (Airbag ECU Assembly)

Sends hazard blink request signals to the combination meter assembly.

Air Conditioning Control Assembly

Hazard Warning Signal Switch

Sends the light switch position signal to the combination meter assembly.

Central Gateway ECU (Network Gateway ECU)

Relays the signals between the CAN communication lines.

SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) The collision-linked control automatically flashes the turn signal lights when the SRS airbags are deployed or a strong impact is received from the rear.

HINT:

The turn signal lights may not flash depending on the type of collision or as a result of a communication malfunction.

(b) After the turn signal lights flash, if approximately 20 minutes elapse or the hazard warning signal switch is operated twice, control stops and the turn signal lights turn off.

FUNCTION

(a) When a collision occurs where the SRS airbags are deployed, the turn signal lights are flashed to inform people near the vehicle of its location.
