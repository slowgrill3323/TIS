REMOTE SERVICE CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function

DCM (Telephone Transceiver)
  • Sends vehicle information signals and vehicle states signals to the data center.
  • Receives remote operation signals from the data center and sends them to each ECU.
  • Collects diagnosis information from each ECU installed to the vehicle when a warning display signal is received from the combination meter assembly.

Telephone Antenna (Roof Antenna Assembly)

Telephone Antenna (Main)

Transmits signals from the DCM and receives signals from the service center.

Telephone and GNSS Antenna (Navigation Antenna Assembly)

Telephone Antenna (Sub)

Receives signals from the data center. It receives signals from the call center. It also functions as an antenna for transmitting when there is a malfunction in the telematics antenna (main).

GNSS Antenna

Receives signals from GNSS satellites and transmits these signals to the DCM.

Certification ECU (Smart Key ECU Assembly)

Receives the remote operation signal from the user's smartphone via the DCM and initiates the vehicle start operation.

Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly
  • Sends ambient temperature signals to the DCM.
  • Operates the air conditioning system by remote operation, based on signals from the DCM.

Main Body ECU (Multiplex Network Body ECU)

Locks or unlocks the door when a remote door lock or unlock operation signal is received.

Combination Meter Assembly

Displays a warning when a malfunction occurs in the vehicle and sends warning display signals to the DCM and audio head unit.

Audio Head Unit (Radio and Display Receiver Assembly)

Displays warning information that is received from the combination meter assembly on the vehicle alert screen.

FUNCTION

(a) Remote Connect

  • The main services of Remote Connect are as follows:

    Category

    Remote Service

    Function

    Remote Information

    Vehicle Status Report

    Allows the vehicle status (doors open/closed, etc.) and vehicle information (distance traveled, etc.) to be checked from a remote location via a smartphone, after the ignition switch has been turned off. By updating the information, the latest vehicle status can be displayed.*1

    Remote Notification

    Guest Driver Monitor
    • Allows the user to set limits on the vehicle speed, traveling time, etc. when a guest driver (a family member, valet, etc.) is driving the vehicle, via a smartphone.
    • If the guest driver exceeds a user set limit, a notification will be sent to the user so the driving status can be monitored.*2*3

    Vehicle Status Alerts

    Sends a notification to the user if certain operations are not performed within a certain amount of time after the ignition switch is turned off, such as locking the doors, closing the windows, etc.*2*4

    Remote Operation

    Remote Engine Start and Stop
    • Allows the user to start the engine and keep it running for a certain amount of time from a remote location via a smartphone.
    • Performing the remote stop operation after the remote start operation has been performed changes the vehicle to the ignition switch off condition.*5
    • Starts the air conditioning system when the engine is remotely started, if the air conditioning system was on when the ignition switch was last turned off.
    • The air conditioning system may be operated by signals from the DCM depending on the ambient temperature, even if the air conditioning system had been off when the ignition switch was last turned off.*6
    • When the engine is remotely started, the rear defogger may be operated by signals from the DCM depending on the ambient temperature.*6

    Remote Door Lock and Unlock

    Allows the doors to be locked/unlocked from a remote location via a smartphone.*7
    • *1: If the latest vehicle information has not been sent from the vehicle due to the condition of the vehicle (such as if the DCM is outside of the cellular phone reception area), the most recently received information will be displayed.
    • *2: No notifications will be sent if the latest vehicle information has not been sent from the vehicle due to the condition of the vehicle (such as if the DCM is outside of the cellular phone reception area). Notifications may be delayed if the smartphone has poor reception.
    • *3: Only one notification per set condition will be sent during a single trip.
    • *4: If the smart access system with push-button has been canceled, vehicle status alert notifications will not be sent to the user. If the electrical key transmitter sub-assembly is inside the vehicle or detected as being "inside cabin", vehicle status alert notifications will not be sent.
    • *5: If the vehicle cannot receive the remote engine start and stop operation signal due to the condition of the vehicle (such as if the DCM is outside of the cellular phone reception area), the engine will not be started remotely. Additionally, the remote engine start and stop operation signal may not be received depending on vehicle conditions.
    • *6: The air conditioning system and rear defogger will automatically be operated according to the ambient temperature as shown in the following table:

      Ambient Temperature

      Air Conditioning System

      Rear Defogger
      ○: Applicable

      -: Not applicable

      30°C (86°F) or higher

      ○

      (Temperature setting: 25°C (77°F))

    • *7: If the vehicle cannot receive a door lock/unlock signal due to the condition of the vehicle (such as if the DCM is outside of the cellular phone reception area), the door lock or unlock operation will not be performed. After a door unlock operation is performed, if the doors are not opened within a certain amount of time, the doors will automatically be locked.

(2) Guest Driver Monitor

  • The user can set vehicle limits via a smartphone:

    Setting Item

    Function

    Profile Name

    Select a guest driver.

    Speed Limit

    Set a speed limit.

    Curfew Limit

    Set a curfew time.

    Total Mile Limit

    Set a daily or weekly travel distance limit.

    Area Limit

    Set a drivable area.

    Total Time Limit

    Set a daily or weekly travel time limit.

    Ignition ON

    Set to send a notification when the ignition switch is turned ON.
  • If the guest driver exceeds a limit set via the smartphone, a notification will be sent to the user according to the following table:

    Notification Item

    Notification Condition

    Speed Limit

    When the vehicle speed exceeds the set limit

    Curfew Limit

    When the ignition switch is turned on during the set curfew time

    Distance Limit

    When the daily or weekly travel distance exceeds the set limit

    Area Limit

    When the vehicle travels outside of the set drivable area

    Time Limit

    When the daily or weekly travel time exceeds the set limit

    Ignition ON

    When the ignition switch is turned ON

(3) Vehicle Status Alerts

  • A notification will be sent to the user if certain procedures are not performed within a certain amount of time after the ignition switch is turned off.

    HINT:

    If the user performs the door lock/unlock operation after a notification was received and other vehicle conditions have not changed since the first notification was sent, another notification will not be sent.

    Notification Item

    Notification Condition

    Door

    Any door is unlocked.

    Any door is open.

    Window

    Any window is open.

    Sliding roof

    The sliding roof is open.

    Hood

    Open

    Hazard warning light

    The hazard warning lights are on.

(b) Service Connect

  • Service Connect provides the following services:

    Service Item

    Description
    *: The history and details of warnings can be checked on the vehicle alert history screen displayed on the multi-display.

    Vehicle Health Report

    Distributes required maintenance information or the vehicle status as a vehicle health report to the user and dealer. Required maintenance is determined from vehicle information such as the driving distance that is sent from the vehicle to the data center.

    Vehicle and Maintenance Alerts

    Notifies the driver and dealer of vehicle and maintenance alerts when a warning is displayed on the combination meter assembly and vehicle alert information is sent to the data center.*

(1) Vehicle Health Report

Vehicle Health Report Image

No.

Process of Vehicle Health Report
*1: Toyota Owners mobile app

*2: Toyota.com (https://www.toyota.com/owners)

(1)

Sends vehicle maintenance information, such as engine oil maintenance required soon, to the data center when a specific amount of time has elapsed since the engine was started.

(2)

Determines required maintenance, etc. based on received vehicle information and sends a vehicle health report (required maintenance and vehicle condition status) to a smartphone or tablet with a dedicated application*1 or to a dedicated web site*2.

(3)

Determines required maintenance, etc. based on received vehicle information and sends a vehicle health report (required maintenance and vehicle condition status) to the preferred dealer.

(2) Vehicle and Maintenance Alerts

Vehicle and Maintenance Alerts Image

No.

Process of Vehicle and Maintenance Alerts
*: It is necessary to register dealer information on the Toyota Owners mobile app.

(1)
  • Sends vehicle alert information to the data center if a warning is displayed on the combination meter assembly when a certain amount of time has elapsed since the engine was started.
  • If vehicle alert have been sent to the data center, subsequent vehicle alerts are sent the next time the engine is started and a certain amount of time has elapsed.

(2)
  • The vehicle alert information can be checked via the vehicle alert history screen displayed on the multi-display.
  • Displays and reads out the vehicle alert history and details on the vehicle alert history screen. Makes a phone call to the dealer* if a cellular phone is connected via "Bluetooth".

(3)

Sends vehicle and maintenance alerts based on vehicle alert information sent to the data center to a smartphone or tablet with a dedicated application or to a dedicated web site.

(4)

Distributes vehicle and maintenance alerts based on vehicle alert information sent to the preferred dealer.
