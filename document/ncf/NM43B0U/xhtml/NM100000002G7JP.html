Title: SLIDING ROOF / CONVERTIBLE: SLIDING ROOF SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The sliding roof system uses a sliding roof drive gear sub-assembly to perform the tilt up-and-down and open-and-close operations.

(b) The sliding roof control ECU controls the sliding roof system. This ECU uses a Hall IC to detect the position of the sliding roof. The sliding roof control ECU and Hall IC are integrated into the sliding roof drive gear sub-assembly.

(c) Remote services have been adopted on this vehicle. (Models with Data Communication Module)

(1) For the components and functions described in this section, remote information and remote notification of remote services are available. For details, refer to TELEMATICS SYSTEM.
