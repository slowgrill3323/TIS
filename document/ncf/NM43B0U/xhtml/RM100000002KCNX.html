Title: POWER OUTLETS (INT): WIRELESS CHARGING SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A wireless charging system has been provided next to the shift lever.

(b) The wireless charging system allows Qi-compatible* devices, such as cellular telephones and smartphones, to be charged without the use of a charging cable by placing them on the charging area of the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly.

HINT:

*: The Qi standard is an international wireless charging standard set by the Wireless Power Consortium (WPC). This system supports Qi standard Ver. 1.2.4 and allows a power output within the range specified by Power Class 0.

"Qi" and the "Qi" logo are registered trademarks of the Wireless Power Consortium (WPC).

(c) Qi-compatible devices that do not fit on the charging area cannot be used. Also, depending on the Qi-compatible device, it may not operate normally. Check the operating instructions for the Qi-compatible device to be used.

PRECAUTION

(a) For safety, when driving the vehicle, the driver should not operate a portable device that is charging.

(b) Anyone using an implanted pacemaker, biventricular pacing pulse generator, cardiac defibrillator or other medical electrical device should consult with their doctor before using the wireless charging system. Operation of the wireless charging system may affect the medical electrical device.

(c) Failure to abide by the following conditions may result in burns due to the device malfunctioning, becoming damaged, generating heat or igniting.

  • Do not place a clip, coin or other metal object between the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly and the Qi-compatible device.
  • Do not attach an aluminum sticker or other metallic object to the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly.
  • Do not cover the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly with a cloth or other material which may become electrostatically charged.
  • Do not charge a Qi-incompatible portable device.
  • Do not disassemble, modify or remove the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly.
  • Do not subject the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly to an impact.

(d) Qi-compatible devices may not charge in the following conditions:

  • The device is fully or nearly fully charged.
  • There is a clip, coin or other metal object between the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly and the Qi-compatible device.
  • If a Qi-incompatible case or accessory is attached to the Qi-compatible device.
  • The temperature of the Qi-compatible device which is being charged rises to above the usable range.
  • The Qi-compatible device is placed on the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly with the charging area of the device facing up.
  • The charging area of the Qi-compatible device is not correctly aligned with the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly.
  • The vehicle is in a location where strong electrical waves and noise are generated.
  • The Qi-compatible device is contacting or covered by a metallic object.
  • A wireless remote control is used nearby.

(e) Bringing a magnetic card or magnetic storage medium close to the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly may clear any stored data. Also, do not bring a wristwatch or other precision instrument close to the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly since doing so may cause it to malfunction.

(f) Do not use the wireless charging system when the engine*1 or HEV system*2 is stopped, as doing so may cause the auxiliary battery to become depleted.
