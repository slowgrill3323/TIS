TORQUE CONVERTER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A hyper-elliptical torque converter with a multi-plate lock-up clutch is used to achieve a direct driving feel while helping improve fuel economy.

(b) A multi-plate lock-up clutch is used to expand the lock-up range. As a result, the transmission efficiency and response of the transmission is improved.

(c) A dynamic lock-up damper is used to make lock-up possible at low vehicle speeds.
