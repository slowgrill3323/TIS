Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM: PTC HEATER; 2024 - 2025 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 - 11/2025]

PTC HEATER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The PTC heater (quick heater assembly) is located above the heater radiator unit sub-assembly in the air conditioning unit.

(b) The PTC heater (quick heater assembly) consists of Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) elements and aluminum fins. When current is applied to the PTC heater (quick heater assembly), it generates heat to warm the air that passes through the unit.
