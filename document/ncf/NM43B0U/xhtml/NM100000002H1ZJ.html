SUSPENSION ARM

CONSTRUCTION

(a) High strength sheet steel is used for the control arm. High strength aluminum is also used for the inner collar of the bush. As a result, rigidity is ensured while reducing weight. Furthermore, the center of rotation of the lower arm is positioned upward, tilting the trajectory of the wheel center backward and reducing body lifting when braking. As a result, a stable braking posture is achieved.
