Title: WIPER / WASHER: WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Front Wiper and Washer System

(1) The wiper motor is directly mounted in the center of the wiper link, reducing wiper operating sounds and decreasing fluctuations in the wiping range.

(2) The adjustable interval function is controlled by the windshield wiper relay assembly, and it automatically changes the wiper operation interval time according to the vehicle speed and interval time adjuster.

(3) Diffuser type washer nozzle sub-assemblies are provided under the engine hood to ensure a good appearance.

(4) A graphite coating is applied to the surface of the wiper rubber, reducing inversion noise and wiper flutter while the wipers are operating.

(b) Washer Fluid Level Warning Function

(1) A warning message display function, which shows a warning message on the combination meter assembly when the washer fluid is low, is provided.
