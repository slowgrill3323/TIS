CYLINDER HEAD GASKET

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A triple-layer metal type cylinder head gasket is used.

(b) The surface of the cylinder head gasket is coated with fluorine rubber to ensure a high level of reliability.

(c) The flow of coolant from the cylinder block sub-assembly to the cylinder head sub-assembly is controlled by the hole shape of the cylinder head gasket, optimizing the balance of coolant flow. Also, due to the hole shape of the cylinder head gasket allowing tumble flow of the coolant in the cylinder head sub-assembly, combustion chamber cooling is increased, and high output is realized.
