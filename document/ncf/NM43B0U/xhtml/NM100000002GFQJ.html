Title: AL80E (AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

  • An AL80E 8-speed automatic transmission (direct shift-8AT) is used.
  • A lightweight, compact and highly efficient 8-speed gear train is used.
  • A wide range of gear ratios is used, dramatically improving start-off acceleration performance by using a low gear ratio for the 1st gear and fuel economy at high speeds by using a high gear ratio for the 8th gear. Also, a close gear ratio is used for the low gear positions to achieve rhythmical gear changes. Furthermore, the transmission constantly selects the optimal gear ratio to achieve improved fuel economy in situations such as city driving.
  • The gear train is composed of 2 planetary gears. Also, the gear train uses a 2-stage clutch mechanism and aluminum parts, achieving weight reduction and compactness. As a result, superior packaging is also ensured.
  • An oil level detection mechanism (overflow method) is used. For details of fluid filling and adjustment procedures, refer to the Repair Manual.
  • On models with stop and start system, due to the use of stop and start control, an electromagnetic oil pump (oil pump assembly with solenoid (EMOP)) is installed in the transmission valve body assembly. When the engine is restarted after an idling stop is performed by stop and start control, the oil pressure required for the clutch and brake engagement is supplied to make smooth starting off possible. For details, see the Idling Stop Control section.

MAIN FEATURES

(a) Aim of Development

  1. A direct driving feel
  2. Rhythmical shifting
  3. Superior fuel economy performance
  4. A top-class level of compactness
  5. Achieving powerful and linear launching performance

(b) Close Gear Ratios and Wide Gear Ratios

  • Close gear ratios are used for high gear to ensure driving force at low and mid speeds and rhythmical gear shifting at high speeds.
  • Wide gear ratios are used to achieve smooth and powerful start-off acceleration performance in low gears and generate more marginal driving force in high gears, achieving powerful and linear lunching performance.
