CLIMATE CONTROL

FUNCTION

(a) The climate control seat system is operated by pushing the climate control seat switch which provides on the air conditioning control assembly, and the ventilation volume can be adjusted to 3 levels.

(b) When the user operates the ventilation volume switch, the blower motors in the seat cushion climate control blower assembly and seatback climate control blower work to direct the air that is drawn in through the seat cover into the seatback and seat cushion pads.

(c) After the air is drawn into the seatback and seat cushion pads, it travels along the grooves of the seatback and seat cushion pads through the slab urethane layer, and is discharged from the seat by the blower motors.
