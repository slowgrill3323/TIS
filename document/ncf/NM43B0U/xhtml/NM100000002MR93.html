HV BATTERY TEMPERATURE SENSOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The HV battery thermistor includes the 6 HV battery temperature sensors and HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) intake air temperature sensor.

(b) The hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly optimally controls the cooling system so that the HV battery temperature can be within a specified range according to the temperature information that is received via the battery voltage sensor.

*1

HV Battery Temperature Sensor

*2

HV Battery (HV Supply Battery Assembly) Intake Air Temperature Sensor
