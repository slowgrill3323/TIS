Title: T24A-FTS (FUEL): FUEL SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The T24A-FTS engine uses a Direct injection 4-stroke gasoline engine Superior version with Turbo (D-4ST) system, which has both direct and port type fuel injection. Fuel sent from the fuel pump (for low pressure side) in the fuel tank assembly is delivered to the low-pressure and high-pressure fuel systems. The fuel delivered to the low-pressure fuel system is injected from the port fuel injector assembly to the intake port. The fuel delivered to the high-pressure fuel system is pressurized by the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly (for high pressure side) and injected from the direct fuel injector assembly to the combustion chamber.

(b) The vehicle is equipped with a 69 liters (72.9 US qts, 60.7 Imp. qts) capacity resin fuel tank assembly below the rear seat.

(c) By using a resin fuel tank assembly, weight reduction has been achieved.

(d) A quick connector is used at each connection of the fuel pipes in consideration of serviceability and reducing the number of parts.

(e) A center direct injection system with the direct fuel injector assemblies and fuel delivery pipe sub-assembly (for direct injection) located on top of the engine is used.

(f) A fuel returnless system is used for low-pressure fuel system control and is performed by the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly and fuel main valve assembly.

(g) High pressure fuel system control is performed by the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly (for high pressure side) and the spill control valve.
