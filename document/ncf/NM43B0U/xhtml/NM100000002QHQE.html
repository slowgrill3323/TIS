Title: T24A-FTS (BATTERY / CHARGING): CHARGING SYSTEM (for 1Motor-HEV Model with Auxiliary Switch System): GENERAL; 2025 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [12/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) With the aim of increasing the ability of the power source to supply the electrical equipment, an auxiliary switch system that uses the hybrid motor control inverter assembly (DC/DC converter) and generator assembly is used.

(b) It has been made possible for the auxiliary switch system to supply optimal power by starting the engine to generate power using the generator assembly when the power consumption has exceeded the output power of the hybrid motor control inverter assembly (DC/DC converter).

(c) A more optimized diode specification is used for the generator assembly.
