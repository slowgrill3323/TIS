GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A ventilated disc brake is used.

(b) An opposed 4-piston S13W type and S14W type front brake caliper is used.*1

(c) An opposed 4-piston S14W type front brake caliper is used.*2

HINT:

*1: Gasoline Models

*2: HEV Models

(1) A ventilated type slotted fin is used for the front disc, ensuring superior cooling performance.

(2) The front brake caliper uses a structure in which the cylinder is offset from the caliper center. Uneven pad wear is suppressed even with high brake load, ensuring a stable pad surface pressure. As a result, excellent vibration performance is ensured, achieving a stable brake pedal feel.
