Title: ACOUSTIC VEHICLE ALERTING SYSTEM: ACOUSTIC VEHICLE ALERTING SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) When the vehicle is being driven quietly using the motor only, the acoustic vehicle alerting system outputs a warning sound from the vehicle approaching speaker assembly to inform pedestrians, cyclists etc., of the approach of the vehicle.

(b) The warning sound is output by the vehicle approaching speaker assembly at a volume level that will not be perceived as noise. Also, the pitch of the sound changes in accordance with the vehicle speed so that the sound reflects vehicle deceleration and acceleration.

(c) Acoustic vehicle alerting system operation automatically stops with the shift state park (P), or when the engine starts, or when the vehicle speed is more than 35 km/h (22 mph).
