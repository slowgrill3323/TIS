BRAKE PEDAL LOAD SENSING SWITCH

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The depression pressure of the brake pedal is detected by the brake pedal load sensing switch installed to the brake pedal support assembly.

(b) The skid control ECU uses the brake pedal load sensing switch signal as the control information of the servo abnormal assist control.
