Title: SEAT: SEAT HEATER SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A seat heater is available for the driver seat and front passenger seat, depending on the models.

(b) By using the seat heater switch which provides on the air conditioning control assembly, temperature can be adjusted to 3 different levels (Hi/Mid/Lo) and the seat heater operation status can be confirmed with the indicator lights.

(c) The air conditioning amplifier assembly controls the seat heater.

(d) The seat heater system and climate control seat system cannot be operated at the same time.
