INTERRUPTION DISPLAY

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Warnings/Advices

(1) Warnings are displayed by interrupting the current screen with a screen indicating that a vehicle malfunction has occurred, in order to warn the driver of the malfunction.

(2) The warning is displayed on the multi-information display and the buzzer also sounds based on the type of warning, in order to notify the driver as quickly as possible.

(3) If there is only 1 warning, it will continue to be displayed.

(4) If multiple warnings are output at the same time, they will be displayed in order from highest priority level (initial display will last 6 seconds). After all warnings have been displayed once, they will be repeatedly displayed in order from highest priority level at an interval of approximately 2 seconds.
