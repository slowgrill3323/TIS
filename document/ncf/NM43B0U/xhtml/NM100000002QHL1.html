Title: POWER DISTRIBUTION: POWER INTEGRATION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A semiconductor power integration ECU that combines multiple relays and fuses is used, achieving compactness and quietness.

(b) A multiplex communication function (CXPI communication line) is installed to the semiconductor power integration ECU to achieve a reduction in relay operation signal lines. Also, the fuse condition (blown fuse or not) is now able to be confirmed using the GTS, achieving superior serviceability.

HINT:

See the MULTIPLEX COMMUNICATION SYSTEM [CXPI] section for the details of the CXPI communication line.

(c) A scene-based power supply system is used to control the supply of power to different systems according to the vehicle conditions.

(d) A power source management control is now supported, which assists in securing electric power such as when the battery capacity has decreased, according to the vehicle conditions when driving or stopped.
