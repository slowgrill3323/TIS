GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Front Drive Shaft (4WD Models)

(1) The double offset type Constant-Velocity Joint (CVJ)*1 or double roller tripod type CVJ*2 is used on the front differential side, and Rzeppa type CVJ*1 or Rzeppa Counter Track type*2 is used on the wheel side.

  • *1: Gasoline models with TRD package or HEV models
  • *2: Except gasoline models with TRD package or HEV models

*B
