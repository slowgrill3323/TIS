INTERCOOLER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A air-cooled type intercooler assembly is used to enhance cooling efficiency.

(b) The intercooler is installed behind the radiator grill.

(c) The No. 1 air hose sends hot supercharged air from the turbocharger to the intercooler, where air-to-air heat transfer takes place. After that, the No. 2 air hose sends the cooled air to the throttle body of the engine.

(d) This layout configuration optimizes mass while meeting cooling requirements and improving fuel economy and engine performance.
