Title: ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: PROACTIVE DRIVING ASSIST: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Proactive Driving Assist

(1) The proactive driving assist are available depending on the model.

(2) Proactive driving assist is a function that predicts risk in accordance with the driving conditions such as a pedestrian crossing the road, a pedestrian who may jump out, etc. It supports driving operation to reduce the risk of driving and to ensure the safety of the driver.

(3) It also supports deceleration operations when approaching a preceding vehicle or curve. By reducing frequent depressing operations, it gently supports the driver on general roads.

(4) For vehicles equipped with steering assist, the system anticipates and supports the operations of the driver by changing the reaction force of the steering wheel to reduce the driving load of the driver.
