SLIDING ROOF CONTROL

SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) The sliding roof system has the following functions:

Function

Outline
*: Customizable parameters can be configured through customize settings. For details, refer to the Repair Manual.

Manual Open-and-close

Opens and closes the sliding roof when the open switch or close switch is pressed for less than 0.3 seconds. The sliding roof stops as soon as the switch is released.

One-touch Auto Open
  • Opens the sliding roof up to the fully opened position when the open switch is pressed for 0.3 seconds or more.
  • To reduce wind noise, this function stops opening the roof at an appropriate stop position. By pressing the open switch once again, the sliding roof can be fully opened.

One-touch Auto Close

Fully closes the sliding roof when the close switch is pressed for 0.3 seconds or more.

Manual Tilt Up-and-down

Tilts the sliding roof up or down when the up switch or down switch is pressed for less than 0.3 seconds. The sliding roof stops as soon as the switch is released.

One-touch Auto Tilt Up-and-down

Fully tilts the sliding roof up or down when the up switch or down switch is pressed for 0.3 seconds or more.

Jam Protection

Automatically stops the sliding roof and opens it almost fully if a foreign object gets jammed in the sliding roof during a close or tilt down operation.

Key Off Operation

Makes it possible to operate the sliding roof for approximately 45 seconds after the ignition switch is turned from ON to off, if the driver or front passenger door is not opened.

Key-linked Operation*
  • When the driver door key cylinder is turned and held in the unlock position for 2.5 seconds or more, the sliding roof control ECU activates the sliding roof motor to open the sliding roof while the key cylinder is turned.
  • When the driver door key cylinder is turned and held in the lock position for 2.5 seconds or more, the sliding roof control ECU activates the sliding roof motor to close the sliding roof while the key cylinder is turned.
  • Similarly, turning and holding the driver door key cylinder in the lock position will cause the sliding roof to close.

Transmitter-linked Operation*

When the certification ECU (smart key ECU assembly) receives an unlock signal from the key continuously for 4 seconds or more via the electrical key and TPMS receiver assembly the sliding roof control ECU opens the sliding roof.

Sliding Roof Open Warning

When the ignition switch is turned from ON to off and the driver door is opened with the sliding roof open, a warning message appears on the multi-information display and the buzzer sounds.

(b) Jam Protection Function

(1) To control the jam protection function, the sliding roof ECU monitors the amount of movement and judges jamming of the sliding roof based on the pulse signal from the Hall IC and the movement direction of the sliding roof from the magnetic fields.
