SHIFT SOLENOID VALVE

CONSTRUCTION

  • Transmission 3-way solenoid valve SC3 is used to switch the No. 2 B2 apply control valve.
  • Transmission 3-way solenoid valve SL is used to switch the lock-up relay valve.

(a) Solenoid Valves SL1, SL2, SL3, SL4, SL5, SL6, SLU and SLT

(1) In order to provide a hydraulic pressure that is proportional to the current that flows to the solenoid coil, solenoid valves SL1, SL2, SL3, SL4, SL5, SL6, SLU and SLT linearly control the line pressure and clutch and brake engagement pressure based on the signals from the ECM.

(2) Shift solenoid valves SL1, SL2, SL3 SL4, SL5, and SL6 are high flow linear solenoid valves that can supply more pressure than conventional ones. These shift solenoid valves control engagement elements by directly regulating the line pressure without using a pressure regulation valve (control valve) or a pressure reduction valve (solenoid modulator valve). Thus, the number of valves and the length of the valve body fluid passage have been reduced, the shifting response has been increased and shift shock has been minimized.

(b) Transmission 3-way Solenoid Valve SL and SC3

(1) Transmission 3-way solenoid valve SL and SC3 is a 3-way solenoid valve.

(2) A filter is provided at the tip of the solenoid valve to further improve operational reliability.
