Title: T24A-FTS (ENGINE MECHANICAL): ENGINE UNIT: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A T24A-FTS (2.4L) engine is used to achieve a higher dimension of balance between fuel efficiency, high performance, clean exhaust gas performance and quietness.

(b) A Direct injection 4-stroke gasoline engine Superior version (D-4S) system (which uses both a direct cylinder injection method and port injection method) is used to perform efficient combustion. In addition, a turbocharger, Electronic Throttle Control System-intelligent (ETCS-i) and Dual Variable Valve Timing-intelligent (Dual VVT-i) system (a continuously variable valve timing mechanism for intake and exhaust) are used to achieve low fuel efficiency, low emissions, high performance and high output.

(c) The D-4ST (Direct injection 4-stroke gasoline engine Superior version with Turbo) which performs comprehensive control over D-4S system and forced induction control of the turbocharger is used. With this, an increase in fuel economy and low speed torque has been realized due to the strong tumble flow created by the optimized combustion chamber shape and piston top shape along with forced induction high-level pressure control.

(d) The shape of the high-efficient intake port, offset crankshaft and combustion chamber is optimized to improve combustion efficiency, and the valve mechanism system is improved to reduce friction loss in each part of the engine. As a result, a high level of engine fuel efficiency is achieved.
