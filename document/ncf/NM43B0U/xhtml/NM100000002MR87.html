TRANSMISSION REVOLUTION SENSOR

CONSTRUCTION

  • A transmission revolution sensor (NT) is used. The sensor detects the speed of the rotor on the front planetary gear assembly and outputs the speed to the transmission control ECU assembly.
  • A transmission revolution sensor (SP2) is used. The sensor detects the speed of the rotor on the output shaft assembly and outputs the speed to the transmission control ECU assembly.
  • A Hall type revolution sensor is used for each sensor. The Hall type revolution sensor is composed of a magnet and a Hall IC. The Hall IC converts magnetic flux density changes that occur when the rotor rotates into electrical signals and outputs the signals to the transmission control ECU assembly.
