Title: T24A-FTS (EMISSION CONTROL): BLOWBY GAS VENTILATION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) By using a PCV system which directly intakes blowby gas from the crankcase to the No. 1 ventilation case, ventilation efficiency has been enhanced and oil deterioration is reduced.

(b) By integrating the PCV valve into the cylinder head, the ventilation hose has been discontinued.
