LIGHT CONTROL

FUNCTION

(a) The illuminated entry system has the following functions:

Function

Outline

Lights that Operate

Actuation Area-linked
  • When the electrical key transmitter enters any actuation area around the doors, the lights that operate turn on.*1*2
  • When the electrical key transmitter leaves all of the actuation areas around the doors, the lights that operate turn off.
  • Dome Lights
  • Footwell Lights*3

Door Lock/Unlock-linked
  • When any door is unlocked, the lights that operate turn on.*1*2
  • When all doors are locked, the lights that operate turn off.
  • Dome Lights
  • Footwell Lights*3

Door Open/Close-linked
  • When any door is opened, the lights that operate turn on.
  • When all doors are closed, the lights that operate turn off.
  • Dome Lights
  • Footwell Lights*3
  • Courtesy Lights*4

Engine switch-linked
  • When the ignition switch is turned from ACC or ON to off, the lights that operate turn on.
  • When the ignition switch is turned from off to ACC or ON, the lights that operate turn off.
  • Dome Lights

Tail Lights-linked

When the tail lights turn on, the lights that operate turn on.
  • Shift Illumination Lights

Delay

When approximately 15 seconds elapse after any of the following conditions is met, the lights that operate turn off:

  • An actuation area-linked function is operated.
  • The door unlock-linked function is operated.
  • Any door is opened and then all doors are closed when the ignition switch is off.
  • The ignition switch is turned from ACC or ON to off when all the doors are closed.
  • Dome Lights
  • Footwell Lights*3

Battery Saving
  • When 20 minutes elapse after the ignition switch is turned from ACC or ON to off and the lights that operate remain on, the lights are turned off.
  • The battery saving function is canceled when any of the following operations is performed before the lights are turned off by the function. After the operation is performed, the lights remain on for another 20 minutes and then the lights are turned off.
    • The ignition switch is turned ACC or ON.
    • Any door is opened and closed.
    • Any door is unlocked.
    • The electrical key transmitter enters any exterior actuation area.
  • Dome Lights
  • Footwell Lights*3

Interior Light Auto Cut

When no changes occur in the condition of the lights switches on for 20 minutes, these lights turn off.
  • Dome Lights
  • Map Lights
  • Courtesy Lights
  • Vanity Lights*5

HINT:

*1: The light illuminates at a brightness 32.7% that of the standard illuminating condition.

*2: The Footwell Light will be at 1.6% when the shift lever in P position, and at 100% when the shift lever in P position (when illuminate).

*3: Models with ambient illumination light

*4: Only the lights on the same seat as the door that was opened illuminate.

*5: Models with vanity light assembly

(b) Collision-linked Interior Light Control

(1) Condition for turning on the interior lights

  • The airbag ECU detects an impact

(2) Conditions for turning off the interior lights

  • Approximately 20 minutes elapse after the lights are turned on
  • A dome light off operation is performed

HINT:

The interior lights may not turn on depending on the severity of the impact, the type of collision or due to communication malfunctions.

(c) Customization Function

(1) Settings of some functions can be changed as desired by the user. Refer to the Repair Manual for details.
