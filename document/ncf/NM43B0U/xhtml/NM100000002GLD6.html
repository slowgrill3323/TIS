Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: PARKING SUPPORT BRAKE SYSTEM: COMBINATION METER; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

COMBINATION METER

OPERATION

(a) Indicator Display

(1) When the parking support brake system is off, the driving assist information indicator illuminates.

(2) When a malfunction is occurred, the driving assist information indicator illuminates, the meter buzzer sounds and warnings are displayed on the multi-information display in order to inform the driver of a system malfunction.

(b) Parking Support Brake System Operation Display

(1) When the parking support brake system operation conditions are met, a warning is displayed on the multi-information display, the driving assist information indicator illuminates/turns off and the meter buzzer sounds to inform the driver. For details, see the Parking Support Brake Control section.

(c) Warning Display

(1) When a malfunction occurs in the parking support brake system or the parking support brake system is temporarily unavailable, a warning is displayed on the multi-information display, the driving assist information indicator illuminates and the meter buzzer sounds to inform the driver that the system is unavailable.

(d) Automatic Off Display of the Parking Support Brake System

(1) When a trailer is connected, driving assist information indicator illuminates and meter buzzer sounds to notify the driver that the parking support brake system is turned off.
