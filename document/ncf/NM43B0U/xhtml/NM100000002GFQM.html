Title: AL80E (AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): SHIFT LOCK SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The shift lock system prevents the shift lever from being moved to any position other than P, unless the ignition switch is ON and the brake pedal is depressed. This prevents the vehicle from starting off suddenly.

(b) The shift lock system is controlled by the shift lock control ECU and the system has a shift lock function.

(c) A shift lock release button, which manually overrides the shift lock mechanism, is used.
