ATF FILLING PROCEDURE

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A special ATF filling procedure is used in order to improve the accuracy of the ATF level when the transaxle is being repaired or replaced. As a result, the oil filler tube and oil level gauge used in the conventional automatic transmission have been eliminated, with no need to inspect the fluid level as a part of routine maintenance.

(b) This filling procedure uses a refill plug, overflow plug, ATF temperature sensor, and the D indicator. For details about the ATF filling procedure, refer to the Repair Manual.
