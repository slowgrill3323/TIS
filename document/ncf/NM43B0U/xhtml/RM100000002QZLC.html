V-RIBBED BELT

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Gasoline Models

  1. Accessory components are driven by a serpentine belt consisting of a single V-ribbed belt. It reduces the overall engine length, weight and the number of engine parts.
  2. A friction type V-ribbed belt tensioner assembly is used. By reducing the tension, friction is reduced resulting in improved fuel economy.
  3. The generator pulley with clutch has torsional spring for the rotational fluctuation reduction function. This helps reduce fuel consumption by enabling reduced V-ribbed belt tension.
  4. A waterproof belt tensioner is used to improve the protection of sliding parts.*

(b) 1Motor-HEV Models

  1. A serpentine belt drive that drives all auxiliary equipment (generator*1 and water pump) with one belt (water pump and crankshaft pulley V belt) has been adopted. With this, a reduction in overall engine length, weight, and number of components has been achieved.
  2. A friction type V-ribbed belt tensioner assembly is used. By reducing the tension, friction is reduced resulting in improved fuel economy.
  3. A waterproof belt tensioner is used to improve the protection of sliding parts.*1, *2

HINT:

*1: Models with auxiliary switch system

*2: Models with TRD Off-Road package option, TRD Pro package option, and TrailHunter grade package option
