SUSPENSION ARM

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A clamshell structure is used for the upper arm to reduce weight while ensuring rigidity.*

HINT:

*: Models without 2.5" TRD Lift Kit

(b) A forged aluminum upper arm is used to achieve weight reduction and high rigidity performance.*

HINT:

*: Models with 2.5" TRD Lift Kit

(c) A clamshell structure made from high strength sheet steel is used for the lower arm, and a high strength forged structure is used around the knuckle connection area, ensuring rigidity and reducing weight.

(1) Aluminum material is used for the bush inner collar to ensure rigidity while reducing weight.

(2) The stabilizer link connection area is placed close to the outside of the vehicle to ensure superior rolling rigidity.

(d) A forged aluminum steering knuckle is used to achieve weight reduction and high rigidity performance.
