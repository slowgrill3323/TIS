MULTI-DISPLAY

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The multi-display of the audio head unit uses an LCD (color LCD display) panel with capacitive touch panel. Furthermore, in accordance with the enlargement of the pane, by using HD*1 and Full-HD*2 resolutions, a high resolution multi-display is realized.

  • *2: Models with 14-inch display

(b) The outermost surface of the multi-display uses a glass that has been treated with an AR (Anti-Reflective) coating. Furthermore, by bonding the glass panel and LCD (color LCD display) panel with a special laminating technology (OCR material direct bonding technology), low reflection, high contrast, and an enhanced quality feel have been realized.

(c) By using LEDs for the backlighting of the LCD (color LCD display) panel, a multi-display with high brightness and rich color reproduction has been realized.

FUNCTION

(a) A Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display (TFT LCD) is used for the multi-display of the audio head unit. The multi-display shows the audio screen, Bluetooth hands-free screen, vehicle information screen, etc. and can be operated using touch operation, flick operation, pinch in/pinch out or drag operation.

(b) The audio source can be switched on the audio source selection screen. The arrangement of the audio sources displayed on the audio source screen can be changed according to the frequency of use.

(c) The user can check the current time, Bluetooth connection status, signal strength, remaining battery level of a cellular phone, etc. using the status bar display.

(d) Fuel Consumption Function

(1) A fuel consumption screen is provided in consideration of the environment.

(2) An area to display the status bar in which the clock, connection status of a Bluetooth device, etc. can be checked has been provided.

(3) The fuel consumption screen displays information or history of the fuel consumption per minute or per trip, drivable distance, etc.

HINT:

Information displayed on the screen may slightly differ from the actual.

(4) The maximum fuel consumption and average fuel consumption is displayed, and the average fuel consumption information since the last reset until now is displayed on a graph.

(5) If "Clear" is selected on the screen, the average fuel consumption information up to that time is reset, the average fuel consumption information up to that time is recorded, and a new recording is started. Furthermore, the average fuel consumption of up to 5 previous fuel consumption averages are displayed in a graph as history.

(6) The displayed maximum fuel consumption will be updated when the average fuel consumption exceeds the displayed maximum fuel consumption.
