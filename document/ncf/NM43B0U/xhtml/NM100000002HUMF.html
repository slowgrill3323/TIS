STABILIZER CONTROL ACTUATOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The front active stabilizer control actuator assembly consists of a stabilizer control motor, rotation angle sensor, limit switch, reduction mechanism and a switching mechanism.

(b) A compact, high-output, DC motor is used as the stabilizer control motor.

OPERATION

(a) Operation to Lock State

(1) When the stabilizer control motor is rotated by the signal from the stabilizer control ECU, the slide ring of the switching mechanism is rotated by the reduction mechanism. This causes the lock hook to fit into the groove of the lock bar, which is integrated with the stabilizer bar LH, to lock the front stabilizer bar.

(b) Operation to Free State

(1) When the stabilizer control motor is rotated by the signal from the stabilizer control ECU, the slide ring of the switching mechanism is rotated by the reduction mechanism. This causes the lock hook to disengage from the groove of the lock bar, which is integrated with the stabilizer bar LH, to free the front stabilizer bar.
