CYLINDER HEAD COVER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A resin cylinder head cover sub-assembly is used.

(b) By welding resin baffle plate and oil delivery pipe to the cylinder head cover sub-assembly, the number of components has been reduced.

(c) By using a constant clearance fastening construction for the outer flange of the cylinder head cover sub-assembly, sealing performance has been ensured.

(d) An oil delivery pipe is installed inside the cylinder head cover sub-assembly. This ensures lubrication to the sliding parts of the No. 1 valve rocker arm sub-assemblies, improving reliability.

(e) A labyrinth construction is used for the oil separator. By improving the oil mist collection rate, oil consumption and generation of deposits have been reduced.

(f) Acrylic rubber, which excels in heat resistance and reliability, is used for the cylinder head cover gasket.
