Title: T24A-FTS (INTAKE / EXHAUST): EXHAUST SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A stainless steel converter with catalyst assembly, front exhaust pipe assembly and tall exhaust pipe are used for weight reduction and improved rust resistance.

(b) By using two 3-way catalysts for the converter with catalyst assembly, the catalyst warm up time is decreased and emissions are reduced directly after engine start (cold engine).

(c) The wall thickness of the exhaust pipes and capacity of the sub muffler and main muffler have been optimized, resulting in reduced weight.

(d) A large capacity main muffler has been used, achieving both quietness and a reduction in exhaust pressure loss.

(e) The placement of the main muffler has been optimized, contributing to secure steering stability performance and ride comfort.

(f) 2 types of exhaust pipes, TRD Performance Exhaust or Trail Tail Pipe, are available according to the specifications.
