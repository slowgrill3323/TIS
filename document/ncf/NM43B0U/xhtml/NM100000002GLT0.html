Title: ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: LANE DEPARTURE ALERT SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The Lane Departure Alert (LDA) system warns the driver if the vehicle may deviate from the current lane or course*, and also can slightly operate the steering wheel to help avoid deviation from the lane or course*. Also, when the vehicle is swaying, a notification is displayed and the buzzer sounds to urge the driver to take a break.

  • *: Boundary between asphalt and grass/dirt, or constructions such as curbstone, guard rail, etc.
