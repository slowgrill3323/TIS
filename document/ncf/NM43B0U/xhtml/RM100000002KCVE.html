PCV VALVE

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The PCV valve closes in order to decrease the amount of gas passing through when the manifold vacuum pressure is high. The valve opens in order to increase the amount of blowby gas passed into the intake manifold when the manifold vacuum pressure is low.
