REAR DEFOGGER CONTROL

FUNCTION

(a) When the ignition switch is ON and the rear window defogger (and mirror heater switch) is pushed, this system is activated to keep the defogger heater on for approximately 15 minutes.

(b) When the battery voltage drops, the rear window defogger system may not operate in order to ensure that there is enough power for other systems.
