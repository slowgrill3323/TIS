FUNCTION

(a) Scene-based Power Supply System

(1) An IGP relay supplying power to the drive force system and an IGR relay supplying power to the other systems are provided, and the power supply to each system is controlled by operating the IGP relay and IGR relay according to the conditions.

(2) In the "Emergency stop while driving" condition, only the power supply to the drive force system is stopped, while the power continues to be supplied to the other systems until the vehicle is stopped.

NOTICE:

  • This does not guarantee the operation of each system until the vehicle is stopped.
  • Since a system may stop operation before the vehicle is stopped depending on the remaining power in the auxiliary battery or how the battery is used, do not operate the emergency stop except in an actual emergency.

(b) Power Source Management Control

  • A power source management control is divided into control while driving and control while the vehicle is stopped.

(1) Power Source Management while Driving

  • When any of the following conditions occurs while driving, the target electrical loads are stopped and assistance in securing electric power is provided until the retreat is made.
    • When the auxiliary battery power is insufficient due to the operation of a device with high power consumption, such as the EPS (EPS request).
    • When a malfunction occurs in the auxiliary battery charging system (low voltage request).
    • When an emergency stop is operated.

(2) Power Source Management while Vehicle Stopped

  • When any of the following conditions occurs while the vehicle is stopped, the power source turns off automatically to reduce auxiliary battery depletion.
    • When the power source position on (IG or ACC) continues for a certain period of time (approximately 20 minutes).
    • When the power source position on (IG or ACC) and auxiliary battery capacity below a certain value (approximately 30%) continues for a certain period of time (approximately 4 minutes).

      HINT:

      When turning the ignition switch to ON after the power source turned off due to low auxiliary battery capacity, the power source off operation due to low auxiliary battery capacity is not performed.

  • When low auxiliary battery capacity is detected, a message is displayed on the multi-information display of the combination meter assembly to urge the driver to turn the power source off.

FAIL-SAFE

(a) When a malfunction occurs in the battery state sensor assembly, the engine switch off request due to low auxiliary battery capacity is stopped to prevent the power source from turning off unnecessarily.
