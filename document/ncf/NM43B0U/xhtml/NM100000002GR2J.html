GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Combination Meter Assembly

(1) A full graphic meter that displays the entire surface with a 12.3-inch color LCD is provided.

(2) The meter ECU built-into the combination meter assembly inputs signals from each ECU and sensor and displays the current vehicle information.

(3) The combination meter assembly has a built-in buzzer that sounds at each warning.

(4) A multi-information display on which various kinds of information is displayed is integrated.

MAIN FEATURES

(a) Combination Meter Assembly

(1) Layout / Taste

  • The design of the full graphic meter can be selected from a total of 12 patterns with 3 layouts (0-dial, 1-dial, and 2-dial) and 4 tastes (casual/smart/tough/sporty) according to the driver's preferences.

(2) Information Placement

  • Regardless of the layout or taste selected, digital speedometer and shift position indicator are displayed at almost the same position in the center of the combination meter.
  • The multi-information display of the full graphic meter is composed of 3 parts (left/center/right), enabling various types of vehicle information to be displayed simultaneously.
  • Frequently used and important information such as driving support and map is displayed in multi-information display (center).
  • Information such as fuel consumption and multimedia is displayed in multi-information display (left and right).
  • The 3 multi-information displays on the left, right and center can be customized for 3 different combinations, and the combinations can be switched with a few operations.
  • An additional widget that displays fuel consumption and multimedia information in a small window can be displayed when the meter layout is selected as 0-dial or 1-dial.
  • The ring display can be switched from speedometer, tachometer for 1-dial layout and from tachometer for 2-dial layout according to the driver's preferences.

(3) Special Display

  • The map is displayed on the multi-information display (center), and the enlarged display can be switched by the steering pad switch once.
  • The switching production is performed on the full screen when switching the drive mode.
  • The background color changes according to the selected drive mode.

(4) Opening Production

  • The opening production is performed on a simple startup screen when the ignition switch is ON.

(5) Settings

  • Various settings can be switched on the setting screen in multi-information display (center).
