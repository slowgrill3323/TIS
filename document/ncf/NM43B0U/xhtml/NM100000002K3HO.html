Title: T24A-FTS (BATTERY / CHARGING): CHARGING SYSTEM (for 1Motor-HEV Model): GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A charging control which maintains the optimal charge capacity for the auxiliary battery to improve fuel economy has been adopted.

(b) This charging control restricts the output voltage of the hybrid motor control inverter assembly (DC/DC converter) in accordance with the auxiliary battery conditions. By restricting the output voltage of the hybrid motor control inverter assembly (DC/DC converter), power consumption has been reduced.
