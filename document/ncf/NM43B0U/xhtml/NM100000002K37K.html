BRAKE CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function
*: Models with DAC/CRAWL and MTS switch

Brake Booster with Master Cylinder Assembly

No. 1 Skid Control ECU

ECU Portion

Calculates the optimal brake force based on the brake pedal stroke and No. 2 skid control ECU commands and sends signals to the brake actuator.

Solenoid Relay

Supplies power to the solenoid valves.

Motor Relay

Supplies power to the pump motor.

Brake Actuator

Accumulator Pressure Sensor (Pacc)

Detects the accumulator brake fluid pressure and is used to control the pump motor.

Stroke Simulator Pressure Sensor (Prct)

Detects the brake fluid pressure in the stroke simulator which is generated by the depression of the brake pedal by the driver.

Servo Pressure Sensor (Psrv)

Detects the fluid pressure of the servo chamber.

Gap Hold Valve (SGH)
  • During normal control, transmits the brake fluid pressure generated by the depression of the brake pedal by the driver to the stroke simulator.
  • During a malfunction, isolates the brake fluid pressure of the gap hold chamber.

Stroke Simulator Cut Valve (SSA)

Allows the stroke simulator pressure to flow to the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly such as when the system is malfunctioning.

Linear Solenoid Valve (SLA, SLR)

Based on commands from the No. 1 skid control ECU, outputs regulated brake fluid pressure.

Stroke Simulator

Generates a natural brake pedal feel in response to depression of the brake pedal by the driver.

Relief Valve

Opens to prevent excess pressure created by the pump motor.

Regulator
  • Supplies pressurized brake fluid which is controlled by the linear solenoid valves to the servo chamber when the No. 1 skid control ECU is operating.
  • Supplies brake fluid pressure from the accumulator to the servo chamber when the No. 1 skid control ECU is not operating or during back-up when the No. 1 skid control ECU is malfunctioning.

Master Cylinder

Generates brake fluid pressure in accordance with the depression amount of the brake pedal.

Brake Master Cylinder Reservoir Assembly

Brake Fluid Level Warning Switch

Detects when the brake fluid level is low.

Brake Booster with Accumulator Pump Assembly

Pump Motor

Pumps the brake fluid from the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly to the accumulator.

Accumulator

Accumulates brake fluid supplied by the pump motor under pressure.

Brake Actuator Assembly

No. 2 Skid Control ECU

ECU Portion
  • Determines the condition of the vehicle based on signals from each sensor, and sends control signals to the No. 1 skid control ECU and brake actuator.
  • Performs regenerative cooperative braking by communicating with each ECU.

Solenoid Relay

Supplies power to the solenoid valves.

Motor Relay

Supplies power to the pump.

Brake Actuator

Pump

Pumps brake fluid from the master cylinder or brake fluid reservoir.

Linear Solenoid Valve (SM1, SM2)

Regulates brake fluid pressure supplied from the pump.

Pressure Holding Solenoid Valve, Pressure Reduction Solenoid Valve

Independently controls the brake fluid pressure of the 4 wheels when ABS, TRAC, VSC, etc. is operating.

Master Cylinder Pressure Sensor (Pmc)

Built into the brake actuator and detects the master cylinder pressure.

Wheel Cylinder Pressure Sensor (Pwc)

Detects the brake fluid pressure in the wheel cylinder.

Speed Sensor

Detect the wheel speed of each of the 4 wheels.

Airbag ECU Assembly

ECU Portion

Sends the collision detection signal to the No. 2 skid control ECU.

Yawrate Sensor

Detects the vehicle's yaw rate.

Deceleration Sensor

Detects the vehicle's longitudinal and lateral deceleration.

Steering Sensor

Detects the direction and angle of the steering wheel.

Stop Light Switch Assembly

Detects the brake pedal depressing signal.

VSC OFF Switch

Enables the driver to select "Normal Mode", "TRAC OFF Mode" or "VSC OFF Mode".

Integration Control & Panel Assembly

Brake Hold Switch

Switches the brake hold on and off.

DAC/CRAWL Switch*

Switches downhill assist control or CRAWL on and off.

Mode Select Switch
  • Switches the multi-terrain select mode.
  • Switches the target vehicle speed of downhill assist control or CRAWL.

MTS Switch*

Switches multi-terrain select on and off.

Brake Pedal Stroke Sensor Assembly

Directly detects the extent of the brake pedal stroke operated by the driver.

Front Door Courtesy Light Switch Assembly (for Driver Door)

Detects whether the door is open or closed and sends the information to the No. 1 skid control ECU.

Hybrid Vehicle Control ECU Assembly
  • Controls the brake force of regenerative braking during regenerative brake cooperative control according to communication with the No. 2 skid control ECU.
  • Controls the motive force to communication with the No.2 skid control ECU.
  • Sends information regarding the motor to the No. 2 skid control ECU during regenerative cooperative braking.
  • Sends the accelerator pedal position signal and brake request signal to the No.2 skid control ECU.

Transmission Control ECU Assembly

Sends the shift position signal to the No. 2 skid control ECU.

4 Wheel Drive Control ECU

Sends the differential locked condition signal to the No. 2 skid control ECU.

Steering ECU with Motor Assembly

Power Steering ECU

Controls the steering assist torque based on the signals received from the No. 2 skid control ECU.

Main Body ECU (Multiplex Network Body ECU)

Detects whether the door is open or closed and sends the information to the No. 2 skid control ECU.

Central Gateway ECU (Network Gateway ECU)

Relays and transmits each CAN communication data signal.

Combination Meter Assembly

ECU Portion

Sends the driver side seat belt fasten condition signal to the No. 2 skid control ECU.

Brake System Warning Light (Red Indicator)
  • Illuminates to warn the driver when a malfunction that may affect braking performance is detected in the brake system.
  • Illuminates to warn the driver when the No. 1 skid control ECU or the No. 2 skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the ABS system or EBD function (the ABS warning light illuminates simultaneously).
  • Illuminates to warn the driver when the brake fluid level is low.

Brake System Warning Light (Yellow Indicator)
  • Illuminates to inform the driver when a minor malfunction that will not interfere with the brake control system is detected.
  • Illuminates to inform the driver that the No. 2 skid control ECU detected a malfunction in the electric parking brake system.

ABS Warning Light

Illuminates to warn the driver when the No. 1 skid control ECU or the No. 2 skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the ABS system or brake assist function.

Slip Indicator Light
  • Blinks to inform the driver when the VSC, TRAC, trailer sway control, downhill assist control* or CRAWL* system is operating.
  • Blinks during the brake control operation of multi-terrain select.*
  • Illuminates to warn the driver when the No. 2 skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the VSC, TRAC, trailer sway control, hill-start assist control, downhill assist control* or CRAWL* system.

Brake Hold Standby Indicator

Illuminates to inform the driver that the brake hold standby condition has been established.

Brake Hold Operated Indicator
  • Illuminates to inform the driver that the brake hold function is operating.
  • Blinks to alert the driver when the No. 2 skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the brake hold function.

VSC OFF Indicator

Illuminates to inform the driver when VSC OFF mode is selected.

Multi-terrain Select Mode Indicator*

Illuminates when multi-terrain select is available for operation.

Crawl Control Indicator*
  • Illuminates when CRAWL is available for operation.
  • Blinks when CRAWL cannot operate temporarily.

Crawl Control Speed Level Indicator*

Displays the target speed of CRAWL.

Downhill Assist Control System Indicator*
  • Illuminates when downhill assist control is available for operation.
  • Blinks when downhill assist control cannot operate temporarily.

Multi-information Display
  • Displays the information message when TRAC OFF mode is selected.
  • Displays a warning message when a malfunction is detected in the brake control system.

Buzzer

Sounds together with a message display on the multi-information display during a system malfunction.

FAIL-SAFE

(a) If a malfunction is detected in the brake system, the fail-safe function operates. Depending on the type of malfunction, the fail-safe function informs the driver via warning indicators in the combination meter assembly and continues brake control via the use of non-malfunctioning parts. Also, when the system is not operating, the fluid pressure generated in the brake master cylinder by the depression of the brake pedal works as a back-up mechanism to ensure braking force.

DIAGNOSIS

(a) A diagnosis function is provided in consideration of serviceability in order to simplify the inspection of systems. For details of the diagnosis function, refer to Repair Manual.
