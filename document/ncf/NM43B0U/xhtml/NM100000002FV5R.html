CONSTRUCTION

(a) A heater water pump (heater accessory assembly) is used to circulate coolant for the heater core in the air conditioning system. This provides a stable heater performance even if the engine is stopped due to the stop and start system.

(b) A heater water pump with reduced water flow resistance is used.
