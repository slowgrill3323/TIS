Title: REAR SUSPENSION: REAR SUSPENSION SYSTEM (for Coil Spring Type): GENERAL; 2025 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A 4-link suspension with lateral rod (lateral control arm) is used for the rear suspension.

(b) Maximum suspension stroke is ensured to provide superior off-road driveability. Rolling rigidity and rear shock absorber placement are optimized, reducing rolling when cornering and contributing toward a feeling of stability during towing. Friction during the stroke is reduced by using a four-link suspension and reducing vehicle pitching by optimizing the wheel rate balance between the front wheels and rear wheels, achieving excellent ride comfort. As a result, superior operation stability and driving stability when towing are ensured while achieving luxurious ride comfort.

(c) A Bilstein monotube shock absorber with remote reservoir is used for TRD Off-Road package option to enhance riding comfort, controllability, and off-road drivability.

(d) A FOX internal bypass shock absorber with remote reservoir is used for TRD Pro package option to improve handling stability, riding comfort, and high-speed performance when driving in off-road conditions.

(e) An Old Man Emu monotube shock absorber with remote reservoir is used for TrailHunter grade package option to achieve both off-road driving performance and riding comfort.

(f) A 2.5" TRD Lift Kit is available, according to the specifications.

(g) A Bilstein monotube shock absorber with lift kit specific tuning is used for 2.5" TRD Lift Kit to achieve both driving stability and off-road driving performance.
