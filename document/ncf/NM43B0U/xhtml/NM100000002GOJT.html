Stop Light Switch Assembly

Detects the brake pedal load.

VSC OFF Switch

Enables the driver to select "Normal Mode", "AUTO LSD Mode"*1, "TRAC OFF Mode" or "VSC OFF Mode".

Brake Hold Switch

Switches the brake hold on and off.

Integration Control & Panel Assembly

DAC/CRAWL Switch*4

Switches downhill assist control or CRAWL on and off.

Mode Select Switch
  • Switches the multi-terrain select mode.
  • Switches the target vehicle speed of downhill assist control or CRAWL.

MTS Switch*4

Switches multi-terrain select on and off.

Brake Actuator Assembly

Solenoid Relay (Built into Skid Control ECU)

Supplies power to the solenoid valves.

Motor Relay (Built into Skid Control ECU)

Supplies power to the pump motor.

Solenoid Valves

Change the fluid path based on the signals from the skid control ECU during the operation of the brake control system functions in order to control the fluid pressure applied to the wheel cylinders.

Pump Motor

Drives the pumps inside the brake actuator.

Master Cylinder Pressure Sensor

Detects the master cylinder pressure.

Skid Control ECU

Judges the vehicle driving condition based on the signals from each ECU, sensor and switch, and sends the brake control signals to the brake actuator.

ECM
  • Controls the drive force when TRAC and VSC are operating according to communication with the skid control ECU.
  • Sends the accelerator pedal position signal, throttle position signal, engine speed signal and brake request signal to the skid control ECU.

Power Steering ECU with Motor Assembly

Power Steering ECU

Controls the steering assist torque based on the signals received from the skid control ECU.

Main Body ECU (Multiplex Network Body ECU)

Sends the driver side door opening or closing state signal and seat belt fasten condition signal to the skid control ECU.

Transmission Control ECU Assembly

Sends the shift position signal to the skid control ECU.

4 Wheel Drive Control ECU*5

Sends the transfer and differential condition signal to the skid control ECU.

Airbag ECU Assembly

ECU Portion

Sends the collision detection signal to the skid control ECU.

Yawrate Sensor

Detects the vehicle's yaw rate.

Deceleration Sensor

Detects the vehicle's longitudinal and lateral deceleration.

Central Gateway ECU (Network Gateway ECU)

Relays and transmits each CAN communication data signal.

Combination Meter Assembly

ECU Portion

Sends the driver side seat belt fasten condition signal to the skid control ECU.

Brake System Warning Light (Red Indicator)
  • Illuminates to alert the driver when the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the EBD.
  • Illuminates to inform the driver when the brake fluid is low.

Brake System Warning Light (Yellow Indicator)

Illuminates to alert the driver when the skid control ECU detected a malfunction in the electric parking brake system.

ABS Warning Light

Illuminates to alert the driver when the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the ABS.

Slip Indicator Light
  • Blinks to inform the driver when the VSC, TRAC, AUTO LSD*1 trailer sway control, downhill assist control*4 or CRAWL*4 system is operating.
  • Blinks during the brake control operation of multi-terrain select.*4
  • Illuminates to warn the driver when the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the VSC, TRAC, trailer sway control, hill-start assist control, downhill assist control*4 or CRAWL*4 system.

AUTO LSD indicator light*1

Illuminates to inform the driver when the AUTO LSD Mode is entered using the VSC OFF switch.

VSC OFF Indicator Light

Illuminates to inform the driver when VSC OFF mode is selected.

Brake Hold Standby Indicator Light

Illuminates to inform the driver that the brake hold standby condition has been established.

Brake Hold Operated Indicator Light
  • Illuminates to inform the driver that the brake hold is operating.
  • Blinks to alert the driver when the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the brake hold.

Downhill Assist Control Indicator*4
  • Illuminates when downhill assist control is available for operation.
  • Blinks when downhill assist control cannot operate temporarily.

Multi-terrain Select Mode Indicator*4

Illuminates when multi-terrain select is available for operation.

Crawl Control Indicator*4
  • Illuminates when CRAWL is available for operation.
  • Blinks when CRAWL cannot operate temporarily.

Crawl Control Speed Level Indicator*4

Displays the target speed of CRAWL.

Multi-information Display
  • Displays the warning message when the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the system.
  • Displays the information message when TRAC OFF mode is selected.

Buzzer

Sounds together with a message display on the multi-information display during a system malfunction.

FAIL-SAFE

(a) If a failure occurs in the skid control ECU, sensors or brake actuator assembly, the system continues performing brake control by excluding the failed area and using only the areas that are operating normally.

DIAGNOSIS

(a) If the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the brake control system, the warning lights or indicator light illuminate. At the same time, a Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) is stored in the memory of the skid control ECU.

(b) This system has a sensor signal check (test mode) function.

(c) For details of DTCs and check function, refer to the Repair Manual.
