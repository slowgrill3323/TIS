POWER RUNNING BOARD

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The automatic running board sub-assembly is composed of the side auto step motor assembly (the drive motor, reduction gear and hall sensor), cab front mounting bracket sub-assembly, cab rear mounting bracket sub-assembly and torsion bar bracket.

(b) When the drive motor in the side auto step motor assembly operates, the output shaft rotates through the reduction gear. The rotation of the output shaft is transmitted through the cab front mounting bracket sub-assembly link and torsion bar bracket to the cab rear mounting bracket sub-assembly link, causing the automatic running board sub-assembly to deploy or stow.
