Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Safety Connect is a subscription-based telematics service*1 that uses the Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) and cellular service (telephone function).

(b) Safety Connect is a service that offers emergency assistance in the event of vehicle theft or an accident that causes the airbag ECU to send an airbag deployment signal, or door lock release signal. In accordance with the situation, the call center quickly calls the police or an ambulance via a Public Safety Answering Point (PSAP) and contacts Roadside Assistance services.

(c) Safety Connect offers 4 types of services*2: Automatic Collision Notification (ACN) service, Emergency Assistance button (SOS) service, Stolen Vehicle Location service and Enhanced Roadside Assistance service.

(d) Safety Connect operates in 2 modes depending on the status of the subscription: shipping mode and active mode.

(e) Safety Connect provides a voice guidance function that outputs voice messages depending on the situation.

(f) Safety Connect consists primarily of the Data Communication Module (DCM), telephone antenna, GNSS antenna, manual (SOS) switch, telephone microphone assembly, and manual (SOS) switch indicator.

HINT:

*1: The areas serviced by Safety Connect are subject to limitations. For details on the coverage area, refer to the Navigation System Owners Manual.

*2: The Safety Connect service requires a Safety Connect subscription.
