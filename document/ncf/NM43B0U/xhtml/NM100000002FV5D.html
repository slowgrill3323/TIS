BUS CONNECTOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A bus connector (Air Conditioning Harness Assembly) is used in the wire harness connection, which connects the servo motor to the air conditioning amplifier assembly.

(b) The bus connector (Air Conditioning Harness Assembly) has a built-in driver IC with a position detection function that communicates with each servo motor connector and actuates the servo motor. This enables bus communication for the servo motor wire harness with a more lightweight construction and a reduced number of wires.
