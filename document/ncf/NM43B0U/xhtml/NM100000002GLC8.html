TOYOTA AUDIO MULTIMEDIA CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function
*1: Except models with 10 speakers

*2: Models with 10 speakers

Audio Head Unit (Radio and Display Receiver Assembly)
  • Receives the microphone voice signal.
  • Sends and receives content information from the application server through the DCM.
  • When utilizing audio content, outputs the sound from applications to the vehicle speakers.*1
  • When utilizing audio content, outputs the sound from applications to the stereo component amplifier assembly.*2
  • Performs route guidance and displays the information on the navigation screen based on data such as POI information or route information received from the call center or server.
  • Sends data relating to the destination set by the user to the server via the DCM, and receives the route based on the results.

DCM (Telephone Transceiver)
  • When utilizing audio content, outputs the sound from applications to the vehicle speakers.
  • Receives map data, route information, etc., from the server and sends it to the audio head unit.
  • Connects to the call center when the destination assist service is active.
  • Sends the call center advisor's voice to the vehicle speakers.
  • Sends microphone voice signals sent from the telephone microphone assembly to the audio head unit.

Telephone Antenna (Roof Antenna Assembly)

Telephone Antenna (Main)

Transmits signals from the DCM and receives signals from the application server.

Telephone and GNSS Antenna (Navigation Antenna Assembly)

Telephone Antenna (Sub)

Receives signals from the application server.

GNSS Antenna

Receives signals from GNSS satellites and transmits these signals to the DCM and audio head unit.

Telephone Microphone Assembly
  • Receives the user's voice through the microphone module.
  • Recognizes speech during voice recognition system operation and sends the audio signal to the DCM.

Steering Pad Switch Assembly

Transmits signals from its volume switch, voice switch, and various switches to the audio head unit.

Stereo Component Amplifier Assembly*2

Receives the audio signal from the audio head unit and outputs it as sound from the vehicle speakers.

Front No. 2 Speaker Assembly RH

Outputs the call center advisor's voice.

FUNCTION

(a) Destination Assist*1

(1) Destination Assist is an in-vehicle service that allows users to request directions to any location. Users are connected to an agent through an operation on the navigation display or a voice control*2 and may ask the agent send the location of any destination to the navigation system on the audio head unit.

(2) The vehicle device can store up to 2 POI at a time, which can be added to the device queue as the first or last entry or as a replacement for all entries.

HINT:

*1: Destination Assist can be used when there is Navigation Function service contract.

*2: It is necessary to enter a subscription service contract of Intelligent Assistant to call the agent using voice control.
