FUEL DELIVERY PIPE (FOR DIRECT INJECTION)

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The direct fuel injector assembly and fuel delivery pipe sub-assembly (for direct injection) are located on top of the engine.

(b) The fuel delivery pipe sub-assembly (for direct injection) made of forged iron and the fuel pressure sensor located in the center of the pipe are used.

(c) A nozzle holder clamp is provided for each area where a direct fuel injector assembly is installed. This clamp applies a constant spring force to the direct fuel injector assembly to prevent it from moving when the combustion pressure is applied to the injector while the engine is being started, during which the fuel pressure is low. As a result, the clamp increases the sealing performance of the injector, while reducing vibration and noise.

(d) An O-ring seal is used for the fastening of the fuel pressure sensor.

(e) Metal-to-metal surface seal connections are used for the joint areas of the high-pressure fuel pipes.
