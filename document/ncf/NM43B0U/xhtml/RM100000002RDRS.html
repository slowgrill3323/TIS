OPERATION

(a) The trailer hitch light illuminates when any of the following conditions is met.

(1) The shift position is changed to P or R when the deck light switch is "ON".*1

(2) The shift position is changed to N or R when the deck light switch is "ON".*2

(3) The shift position is changed to P or R and a door or the tail gate is opened when the deck light switch is "DOOR".*1

(4) The shift position is changed to N or R and a door or the tail gate is opened when the deck light switch is "DOOR".*2

(5) The shift position is in R.*3

(6) The trailer hitch light illuminates after the driver presses the trailer backup guide switch and confirms/selects the trailer in the message displayed on the multi-information display. (This is unrelated to the deck light switch position)*4

  • *1: Models with automatic transmission
  • *2: Models with manual transmission
  • *3: Models without panoramic view monitor system
  • *4: Models with trailer backup guide
