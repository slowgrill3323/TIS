Title: ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: PRE-COLLISION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The pre-collision system uses sensors to detect objects in the path of the vehicle. When the system determines that the possibility of a frontal collision with a detected object is high, a warning operates to urge the driver to take evasive action and the potential brake pressure is increased to help the driver avoid the collision. If the system determines that the possibility of a collision is extremely high, the brakes are automatically applied to help avoid the collision or help reduce the impact of the collision.

(b) The pre-collision system can be disabled/enabled and the warning timing can be changed as necessary.

PRECAUTION

(a) Pre-collision Brake

(1) When pre-collision brake control operates, a large amount of braking force will be generated.

(2) If the vehicle is stopped by pre-collision brake control, the driver should operate the brakes as necessary.

(3) Pre-collision brake control may not operate if certain operations are performed by the driver. If the accelerator pedal is being depressed strongly or the steering wheel is being turned, the system may determine that the driver is taking evasive action and the pre-collision brake control may not operate or operation may be canceled.

(4) If the brake pedal is being depressed, the system may determine that the driver is taking evasive action and possibly delay the operation timing of the pre-collision brake control.

(b) Low Speed Sudden Acceleration Suppression Function

(1) If the driver is operating the steering wheel, the system may determine that the driver is taking evasive action and low speed sudden acceleration suppression function may not operate.

(c) Emergency Steering Assist

(1) Emergency steering assist will be canceled when the system determines that lane departure prevention control has completed.

(2) Depending on operations performed by the driver, emergency steering assist may not operate or operation may be canceled.

  • If the accelerator pedal is depressed strongly, the steering wheel is turned heavily, the brake pedal is depressed, or the turn signal lever is operated, the system may determine that the driver is taking evasive action and emergency steering assist may not operate.
