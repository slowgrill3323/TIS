AIRBAG SENSOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Front Airbag Sensor

(1) The front airbag sensors LH and RH are electrical type deceleration sensor. As a result of the deceleration of the vehicle during a frontal collision, distortion is created in the internal element in the sensor and is converted into an electrical signal.

(b) Side Airbag Pressure Sensor

(1) The side airbag pressure sensors LH and RH consist of a pressure sensor, communication circuit, etc. Each sensor detects an impact to the respective side of the vehicle and sends a pressure value signal caused by deformation of the front door to the airbag ECU assembly.

(c) Floor Side Airbag Sensor

(1) A deceleration sensor is built into each floor side airbag sensor. As a result of the deceleration of the vehicle during a side collision, distortion is created in the internal element in the sensor and is converted into an electrical signal.

(d) No. 2 Side Airbag Sensor (Double Cab Models)

(1) A deceleration sensor is built into each No. 2 side airbag sensor. As a result of the deceleration of the vehicle during a side collision, distortion is created in the internal element in the sensor and is converted into an electrical signal.
