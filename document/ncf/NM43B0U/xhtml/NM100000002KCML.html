ENGINE MOUNTING CONTROL

FUNCTION

(a) By switching the vacuum switching valve on and off based on the engine speed signal sent by the ECM, the amount of vacuum between the vacuum pump assembly, front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH are controlled.

(b) When the vacuum switching valve is on, vacuum is not applied to the front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH and the diaphragm is closed. At this time, liquid dose not pass through the orifices, keeping the spring constant.

(c) When the vacuum switching valve is off, vacuum is applied to the front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH and the diaphragm is open. At this time, liquid passes through the orifices, reducing the spring constant.
