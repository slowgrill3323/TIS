BODY SHELL

CONSTRUCTION

(a) Body Shell

(1) The position of the body's structural parts is optimized, optimized connection methods are used and reinforcing materials are provided, creating a lightweight body structure with high rigidity and ensuring driving stability.

(b) Floor

(1) The front seat rail installation area is directly installed to the body instead of passing through the seat rail bracket used in the conventional vehicle. As a result, floor body rigidity is increased to achieve superior driving stability.
