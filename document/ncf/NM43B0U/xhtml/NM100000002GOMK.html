FUEL TANK

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A multiple layer plastic fuel tank assembly is used for weight reduction. This fuel tank assembly consists of 6 layers of 4 types of materials.

(b) A fuel drain mark has been provided at the lowest position of the fuel tank assembly. When dismantling (scrapping) the vehicle, drain fuel by drilling a hole at the drain mark.
