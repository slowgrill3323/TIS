HEADUP DISPLAY

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The headup display unit (meter mirror sub-assembly) consists of the ECU, Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), reflector, concave mirror and light source LEDs.

(b) By changing the angle of the concave mirror, the position of the image displayed can be adjusted.
