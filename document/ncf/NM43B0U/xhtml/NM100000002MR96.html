Title: HYBRID / BATTERY CONTROL: HYBRID BATTERY SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A small and highly efficient Nickel Metal Hydride (Ni-MH) battery is used.

(b) The HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) has high power, density and long life characteristics. The HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) compensates the vehicle drive energy and stores regenerative energy.

(c) The HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) is positioned under the rear seat, ensuring occupant comfort.

(d) Due to a battery voltage sensor and hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly that monitors the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) condition (battery voltage, battery current value and temperature) and controls the charging and discharging amount to maintain a certain charging condition, charging from external sources is generally not necessary.

(e) A reduced-noise battery cooling blower assembly and No. 2 battery cooling blower assembly are used in the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) cooling system, in order to achieve both cabin quietness and battery cooling performance.

PRECAUTION

(a) HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) electrolyte is a highly alkaline potassium hydroxide solution (odorless, transparent and colorless). Careless handling of the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) is very dangerous. Handle the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) properly in accordance with the procedures below:

Procedure

Precaution

When there is liquid leakage present in the area of the HV battery

Neutralize it with a saturated mixture of boric acid and water.

After litmus paper has been used to determine that the mixture is neutral, wipe it up with cloth.

When battery electrolyte gets on skin, into eyes, etc.*

Flush it with a saturated solution of boric acid and water, or with a large amount of water.

Remove contaminated clothes at once.

When a vehicle is scrapped

Remove the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) from the vehicle for collection via the specified route.

When stored

The battery should not be left in a damp or wet location.

When a vehicle is stored for a long time.

Disconnect the auxiliary battery negative terminal.

When in storage, the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) should be charged every 2 months to prevent HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) discharge or damage. Using the following procedure, charge the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly) with the vehicle.

  1. Connect the negative terminal of the auxiliary battery.
  2. Turn the ignition switch ON for 3 minutes and do not apply any electrical loads. (This operation is needed to allow the hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly to detect the correct State of Charge (SOC).)
  3. Enter the ON (READY) state. After the engine starts, leave it running for 30 minutes to charge the HV battery.

If the engine does not start or intermittently stops within 30 minutes, stop the operation at that time. (The HV battery does not need to be charged.)
  • *: If electrolyte gets into your eyes, call for help and do not rub your eyes. Rinse your eyes with a large amount of water and seek medical attention immediately.
