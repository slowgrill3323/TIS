Title: STEERING COLUMN: HEATED STEERING WHEEL SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A heated steering wheel system is provided depending on models.

(b) This system consists of heater element, thermostat and thermistor that are placed inside the steering wheel, and a steering heater switch (air conditioning control assembly) that is incorporated an indicator light.

(c) According to the operation of the steering heater switch (air conditioning control assembly), this system keeps the steering wheel assembly surface at a designated temperature, enhancing driver comfort.
