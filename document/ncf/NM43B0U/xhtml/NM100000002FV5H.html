SOLAR SENSOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The solar sensor (automatic light control sensor) consists of a photo diode, 2 amplifier circuits for the solar sensor (automatic light control sensor) and a frequency converter circuit for the light control sensor.

(b) The solar sensor (automatic light control sensor) detects (in the form of changes in the current that flows through the built-in photo diode) the changes in the amount of sunlight from its LH and RH sides (2 directions) and outputs these sunlight strength signals to the air conditioning amplifier assembly.

(c) The installation position is the top of the instrument panel.
