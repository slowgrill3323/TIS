COIL SPRING AND SHOCK ABSORBER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) High strength material is used for the coil spring to achieve weight reduction. A super single coating*1 or dual coating*2 is used to ensure anti-corrosive performance.

HINT:

*1: Except models with TRD Pro package option, TrailHunter grade package option or 2.5" TRD Lift Kit

*2: Models with TRD Pro package option, TrailHunter grade package option or 2.5" TRD Lift Kit

(b) A low-pressure gas (N2)-filled type shock absorber is used to provide stable damping force characteristics, and to achieve a higher dimension of both maneuverability/stability and riding comfort.

(1) A triple lip oil seal*1 or double lip oil seal*2 is used to ensure superior oil leak performance.

(2) A long type dust cover is used to ensure superior anti-dust performance.

(3) A high strength forged structure is used on the lower side to ensure superior robustness.

(4) The damping force can be adjusted in 3 stages using the Quick Switch 3 (QS3) built into the shock absorber.*3

HINT:

*1: Except models with TRD Pro package option or TrailHunter grade package option

*2: Models with TRD Pro package option or TrailHunter grade package option

*3: Models with TRD Pro package option

(c) A front lower coil spring insulator is used to ensure superior quietness.*

HINT:

*: Except models with TRD Pro package option or TrailHunter grade package option

(d) An upper support is used. The shape of the upper support is optimized to ensure robust connection performance.
