Title: EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: GRILLE SHUTTER SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A grille shutter system is used. This system blocks the flow of air into the engine compartment from the upper radiator grille.

(b) The grille shutter suitably controls cooling airflow for the engine by opening and closing the shutter, reducing drag.

(c) The grille shutter system is composed of a swing grille actuator assembly, radiator shutter assembly and ECM.

(d) The grille shutter system is controlled by the swing grille actuator assembly. The swing grille actuator assembly is integrated into the radiator shutter sub-assembly.

MAIN FEATURES

(a) The adoption of the grille shutter system has the following advantages.

  • Reduces the air resistance in the engine compartment when the vehicle is traveling at high speeds.
  • Facilitates warm-up of the engine by blocking the flow of cold air into the engine compartment when the engine coolant temperature is low. This supports fuel economy.
