Title: FRONT SUSPENSION: FRONT SUSPENSION SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2025 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2024 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A double-wishbone type suspension is used for the front suspension.

(b) The king pin offset and caster trail are optimized, achieving superior straight line stability and high speed stability. Roll steer, the roll center, and rolling rigidity are optimized, reducing rolling when cornering and contributing toward a feeling of stability during towing. The wheel rate balance between the front wheels and rear wheels is optimized, reducing vehicle pitching and achieving excellent ride comfort. As a result, superior operation stability and driving stability when towing are ensured while achieving luxurious ride comfort.

(c) A Bilstein monotube shock absorber with remote reservoir is used for TRD Off-Road package option to enhance riding comfort, controllability, and off-road drivability.

(d) A FOX internal bypass shock absorber is used for TRD Pro package option to improve handling stability, riding comfort, and high-speed performance when driving in off-road conditions.

(e) An Old Man Emu monotube shock absorber is used for TrailHunter grade package option to achieve both off-road driving performance and riding comfort.

(f) A 2.5" TRD Lift Kit is available, according to the specifications.

(g) A Bilstein monotube shock absorber with lift kit specific tuning is used for 2.5" TRD Lift Kit to achieve both driving stability and off-road driving performance.
