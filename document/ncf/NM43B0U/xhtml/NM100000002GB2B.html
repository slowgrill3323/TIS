Title: THEFT DETERRENT / KEYLESS ENTRY: THEFT DETERRENT: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The theft deterrent system will operate when an attempt is made to forcibly enter the vehicle, open the engine hood, or unlock any door without the electrical key transmitter sub-assembly or when the auxiliary battery terminals are removed and reconnected.

(b) The system consists of power door lock control system components, wireless door lock control system components, smart key system components, security horn assembly and a security indicator light.

(c) This system is controlled by the main body ECU.
