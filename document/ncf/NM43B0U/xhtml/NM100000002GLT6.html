Title: ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: ROAD SIGN ASSIST SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) The Road Sign Assist (RSA) recognizes various road signs using the forward recognition camera installed to the upper center part of the windshield, etc., and provides information to the driver via the multi-information display and headup display*. When the system judges that the road sign, etc. may not be complied with, it warns the driver by emphasizing the icon or sounding the buzzer.

  • *: Models with headup display
