WIRELESS CHARGING CONTROL

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component

Function

Mobile Wireless Charger Cradle Assembly

Charging Indicator Light

Displays the wireless charging system status by illuminating or blinking in different colors.

Charging Surface

When a Qi-compatible device is placed with the wireless charging system on, charging begins.

Certification ECU (Smart Key ECU Assembly)

Outputs a charging stop signal to the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly.

SYSTEM CONTROL

(a) The wireless charging system operates when all of the following conditions are met:

  • The ignition switch is ACC or ON.

(b) The wireless charging system temporarily stops when any of the following conditions are met:

  • When any of the doors is opened or closed.
  • When the engine*1 or HEV system*2 is started.
  • When the electrical key is outside the detection area.

    HINT:

    *1: Gasoline models

    *2: HEV models

(c) After the Qi-compatible device fully charges and charging stops, if the device is left in place for about 30 minutes, charging restarts.

(d) After the Qi-compatible device fully charges and charging stops, if the device is moved or repositioned within the charging area, or replaced with another Qi-compatible device within about 30 minutes, charging restarts.

(e) If the Qi-compatible device is moved during charging, charging restarts at the new position.

FUNCTION

(a) Charging Indicator Light

(1) The operation status of the wireless charging system is indicated by the condition and color of the charging indicator light as follows:

Charging Indicator Light

Operation Status

Off

The wireless charging system is not operating.

Illuminates in Green

The wireless charging system is on standby, finished charging*, or charging is temporarily stopped.

Illuminates in Amber

The wireless charging system detects a Qi-compatible device in the charging area and is charging.

Blinks in Amber (Pattern 1)

The wireless charging system cannot receive signals from the certification ECU (Smart Key ECU Assembly).

Blinks in Green (Pattern 1)

A communication stop between the radio and display receiver assembly is detected, or Initial wait.

Blinks in Amber (Pattern 2)

The wireless charging system is Stop charging by Temperature ERROR.

HINT:

*: Even when using a Qi-compatible device, the charging indicator light may fail to illuminate in green when charging is complete. If this occurs, check the charge condition on the device.
