WATER CONTROL VALVE

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The water control valve rotates the rotary valve according to the DC motor, and switches the coolant path by opening and closing the 3 coolant passages.

(b) Before the engine is warmed up, the coolant path is isolated to decrease warmup time in order to improve fuel economy.

(c) After engine warm-up, the coolant temperature is optimally controlled by changing the coolant flow in the radiator in accordance with the driving conditions, contributing to increase fuel economy.
