Title: HYBRID / BATTERY CONTROL: HYBRID CONTROL SYSTEM: INVERTER; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

INVERTER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The inverter converts high voltage DC from the HV supply battery assembly into three-phase AC, and drives the MG.

(b) The activation of the power transistors is controlled by the MG ECU. In addition, the inverter transmits information that is needed for current control, such as the output amperage or voltage, to the ECM via the MG ECU.

(c) The power transistors used in the inverter are Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors (IGBTs).

(d) The bridge circuit for the MG contains 6 IGBTs, mounted on the high voltage and high current module portion of an PM. In addition, a signal processor/protective function processor has been integrated into the control portion of the PM. The PM is used to operate the power transistors based on signals from the MG ECU.
