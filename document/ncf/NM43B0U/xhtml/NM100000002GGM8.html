DIGITAL REAR-VIEW MIRROR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A gel type Electro Chromic (EC) mirror cell (which consists of a liquid type electrochromic material situated between transparent conductive films) is used, achieving glare-prevention using an electrochemical reduction-oxidation reaction*. Also, the Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) screen and guided light plate are overlapped in the lower layer to create a mechanism that displays images from the inner mirror camera assembly in the mirror surface.

HINT:

*: A phenomenon wherein a material is colorless while voltage is not applied, but achieves coloration due to an electrochemical reduction-oxidation reaction when voltage is applied.
