CAM TIMING OIL CONTROL SOLENOID

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The cam timing oil control solenoid assemblies for Dual VVT-i are installed to the No. 2 timing gear cover assembly.

(b) The oil passage of the camshaft timing valve assembly is switched by controlling the shaft movement amount to achieve the optimal valve timing based on the duty signal from the ECM.
