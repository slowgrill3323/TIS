GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) Front Differential (4WD Models)

(1) An SD20DD type front differential is provided. (for Part-time 4WD Models)

  1. An Automatic Disconnecting Differential (A.D.D.) actuator (differential lock coil) is built into the SD20DD type front differential to achieve a size reduction.
  2. The A.D.D. actuator connects and disconnects the driveline between the transfer and the front wheels. For details, see the TRANSFER / 4WD / AWD (VF2DM) section.

(2) An SD20D type front differential is provided. (for Full-time 4WD Models)

(b) Rear Differential

(1) A BD21C, BD21CN, BD24D or BD24DN type rear differential is used.

(2) A built-in actuator type differential lock mechanism is used for the BD21CN or BD24DN type rear differential to achieve a size and weight reduction and ensure superior performance against water ingress and impacts from outside, ensuring river crossing performance and improving driving performance on rough roads.

BD21CN Rear Differential (with Electronic Differential Lock Mechanism)
