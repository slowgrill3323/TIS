AIR CONDITIONING UNIT

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A dual zone (left and right independent temperature control) automatic air conditioning system, single zone automatic air conditioning system or manual air conditioning system is provided depending on the model.

(b) The air conditioning unit consists of the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly, heater radiator unit sub-assembly, servo motors, evaporator temperature sensor (No. 1 cooler thermistor) and blower with fan motor sub-assembly.

(c) A cold storage evaporator is used depending on the model.

(d) A Straight Flow Aluminum-II (SFA-II) heater core that is compact and offers advanced performance is used.

(e) A particle type clean air filter (air refiner element) is provided as standard equipment.
