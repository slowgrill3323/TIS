TURBOCHARGER CONTROL

FUNCTION

(a) For the turbocharger control, wastegate valve control which control the turbo pressure is used.

(1) The ECM cooperatively controls the throttle valve, Dual VVT-i and wastegate valve to achieve a driving condition with optimal fuel efficiency during normal driving. Also, the ECM controls each actuator in accordance with the driver's intentions during acceleration to achieve a high torque response.

(b) Wastegate Valve Control

  • The vacuum regulating valve assembly opens and closes the wastegate valve according to signals from the ECM.

(1) During Light Loads

  • Opening the wastegate valve reduces the pressure of the exhaust manifold and improves fuel efficiency by achieving a reduction in pumping loss and improved combustion (reduction of remaining gas).

(2) During Acceleration and High Loads

  • Closing the wastegate valve increases the engine torque and improves responsiveness during acceleration.

HINT:

During racing, the wastegate valve is open and the opening and closing operation does not operate.
