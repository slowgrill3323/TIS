HV BATTERY COOLING BLOWER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A sirocco type fan, which is highly efficient and low noise, is used.

(b) Air intake positions are provided under the rear seat to improve quietness inside the cabin. Low-temperature cabin air is introduced from beneath the rear seat, in order to improve HV battery cooling blower assembly and No. 2 HV battery cooling blower assembly cooling performance.

(c) Air inside the vehicle is drawn in from the bottom of the right and left rear seat intake ducts and flows from the intake duct No. 1 to the battery cooling blower assemblies, and then from the battery cooling blower assemblies to the intake duct No. 2 and into the HV battery (HV supply battery assembly). Cooling air flows from the bottom to the top between the hybrid battery modules and is emitted beneath the rear seat after heat is exchanged.
