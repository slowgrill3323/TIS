Title: SUSPENSION CONTROL: STABILIZER WITH DISCONNECTION MECHANISM SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) A stabilizer with disconnection mechanism system is available, according to the specifications.

(b) The stabilizer with disconnection mechanism system uses the front active stabilizer control actuator assembly provided on the front stabilizer bar to switch the front stabilizer bar between locked (coupled) and free (decoupled). This allows the stabilizer effect to be changed, which achieves a balance between off-road running performance, a comfortable ride and handling stability.

(c) In addition to the manual function by which the driver operates the stabilizer control switch to switch the front stabilizer bar between locked and free while the vehicle speed is below the threshold, there is an automatic lock switching function that automatically switches the front stabilizer bar to the locked state when the vehicle speed exceeds the threshold value while driving in the free state.
