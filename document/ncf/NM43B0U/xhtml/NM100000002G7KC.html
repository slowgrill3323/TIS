LED DRIVER MODULE

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The LED driver module LH and RH illuminate or turn off the LED used as a light source for the low beam and high beam headlight. When the high beam headlights are illuminated, a constant current flows to the LED to instantly stabilize the headlights. Also, changes in the LED current due to fluctuations in the voltage are reduced to prevent the lights from flickering
