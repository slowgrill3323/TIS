OIL FILTER

CONSTRUCTION

(a) A cartridge type oil filter sub-assembly using a new type high performance filter paper is used to reduce the size and weight while ensuring low pressure loss.

(b) By mounting the oil filter sub-assembly horizontally, the oil filter can be replaced without touching hot oil, enhancing serviceability.

(c) An integrated structure is used that allows the oil filter to be mounted directly on the oil cooler.
