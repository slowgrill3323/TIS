Title: T24A-FTS (BATTERY / CHARGING): CHARGING SYSTEM: GENERAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

GENERAL

OUTLINE

(a) In order to enhance fuel economy, charging control which optimally controls the generated voltage is utilized.

(b) The ECM optimally controls the power generated by the generator with regulator assembly based on the state of the battery, electrical load and driving conditions.

(c) Operation of the clutch set to the generator pulley with clutch, cancels generator pulley with clutch inertia and helps to prevent slipping of the fan and generator V-ribbed belt. This realizes a low tension fan and generator V-ribbed belt that achieves reduced friction.

(d) A European Norm (EN) battery is used.
