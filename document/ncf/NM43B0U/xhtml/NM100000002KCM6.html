AIR FUEL RATIO SENSOR

CONSTRUCTION

(a) The basic construction of the air fuel ratio sensor and No. 2 air fuel ratio sensor is the same. However, they are divided into the cup type and the planar type, in accordance with the different types of heater construction that are used.

(b) The planar type air fuel ratio sensor uses ceramic, which excels in heat conductivity and insulation, to integrate a sensor element with the heater, thus achieving the excellent warm-up performance of the sensor.
