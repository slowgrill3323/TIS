(1) Install the purge VSV (duty vacuum switching valve) to the fuel vapor feed pipe.

(2) Connect the No. 1 fuel vapor feed hose to the purge VSV (duty vacuum switching valve) and slide the hose clip to secure it.

NOTICE:

Align the hose and the hose clip at the locations shown in the illustration and install them.

(3) Connect the No. 3 fuel vapor feed hose to the purge VSV (duty vacuum switching valve) and slide the hose clip to secure it.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure that the tabs of the clip are within the area shown in the illustration.
  • Align the hose and the hose clip at the locations shown in the illustration and install them.

(4) Connect the No. 2 fuel vapor feed hose and connector to the purge VSV (duty vacuum switching valve).
