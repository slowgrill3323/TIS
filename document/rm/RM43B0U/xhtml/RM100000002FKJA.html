2. REMOVE REAR LATERAL CONTROL ROD ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove the bolt (A).

(2) Remove the bolt (B), nut and rear lateral control rod assembly from the rear axle housing assembly and vehicle body.

NOTICE:

Because the nut has its own stopper, do not turn the nut. Loosen the bolt with the nut secured.
