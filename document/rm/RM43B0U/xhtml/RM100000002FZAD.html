INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT CLUTCH START CANCEL SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(a) Check the operation.

(1) Connect a positive (+) lead from the auxiliary battery to terminal J25-8 (IG), J25-7 (ILL+), J25-3 (B), J25-4 (D) or J25-6 (S+) and a negative (-) lead to terminal J25-2 (E) or J25-1 (ILL-).

(2) Connect one end of the relay coil to J25-5 (S -) and connect the other end of the relay coil to the negative (-) terminal of the auxiliary battery.

HINT:

If a relay is not available, use a 180 Ω resistor.

(3) Press and hold the switch on the clutch start cancel switch assembly for 1.5 seconds to activate device.

(4) Check the following.

  1. The orange indicator LED turns ON to indicate device activation.
  2. The device remains active for 60 seconds before deactivating.
  3. The relay activates when the device activates (this can be checked by either listening for relay activation or by checking continuity of the relay switch side using the electric tester).

(5) After 60 seconds, the device will deactivate. Activate the device again by pressing and holding the switch for 1.5 seconds.

(6) When the device is activated, press the switch again for a short period of time (30 milliseconds).

(7) Check if the device is activated by a short press.

If the result is not as specified, replace the clutch start cancel switch assembly.
