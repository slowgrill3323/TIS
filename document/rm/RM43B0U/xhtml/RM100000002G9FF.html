1. INSPECT HEADLIGHT LEVELING SWITCH

(a) Check the headlight leveling switch.

(1) Connect the positive (+) end of the auxiliary battery to terminal J39-5 (B) of the headlight leveling switch and the negative (-) end of the auxiliary battery to terminal J39-2 (E).
