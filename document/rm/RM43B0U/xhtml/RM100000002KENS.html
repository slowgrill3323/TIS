PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. CLEAN REAR QUARTER WINDOW ASSEMBLY LH

*a

When reusing the rear quarter window assembly LH

-

-

(a) When reusing the rear quarter window assembly LH :

(1) Using a scraper, grind off the adhesive and double-sided tape left on the rear quarter window assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the rear quarter window assembly LH.

(2) Using non-residue solvent, clean the outer edge of the rear quarter window assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Be sure to clean the rear quarter window assembly LH even if it is new.

2. INSTALL REAR QUARTER WINDOW ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Follow the procedure below to perform rear quarter window assembly LH installation.

1. Using the suction cups, align the matchmarks and install the rear quarter window assembly LH.

NOTICE:

  • After the body side primer dries (in approximately 3 minutes), install the rear quarter window assembly LH.
  • From inside the cabin, check that the clips are attached.

2. Gently push the front surface of the rear quarter window assembly LH from outside of vehicle to fully contact with the adhesive.

HINT:

Pushing force: 98 N (10 kgf) or higher

3. Remove any excess adhesive or apply as necessary.

NOTICE:

Apply adhesive up to the edge of the rear quarter window assembly LH.

4. Remove any excess adhesive before it hardens.

5. Using protective tape, hold the rear quarter window assembly LH in place until the adhesive hardens.

NOTICE:

Follow the instructions for each adhesive manufacturer or the corresponding owner's manual for the minimum amount of time necessary for drying and prior to driving the vehicle after applying adhesive.

4. CHECK FOR LEAK AND REPAIR

(1) Inspect for leaks by the below procedure.

1. After the adhesive has hardened, apply water from the outside of the vehicle. Check that no water leaks into the cabin.

2. If water leaks into the cabin, allow the water to dry and add adhesive.

3. Remove the protective tape.
