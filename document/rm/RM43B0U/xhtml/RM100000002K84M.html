DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when LIN communication between the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) and multiplex network master switch assembly, power window regulator motor assembly (driver door), power window regulator motor assembly (front passenger door), power window regulator motor assembly (rear RH door)*1, power window regulator motor assembly (rear LH door)*1, sliding roof ECU (sliding roof drive gear assembly)*2 or side auto step controller ECU assembly*3 stops for 10 seconds or more.

  • *1: for Double Cab
  • *2: w/ Sliding Roof System
  • *3: w/ Power Running Board System
