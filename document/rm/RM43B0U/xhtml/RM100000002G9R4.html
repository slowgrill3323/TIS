1. ADJUST STEERING WHEEL POSITION

Extend

(1) Adjust steering wheel position by the below procedure.

1. Release the tilt and telescopic lever and fully extend and lower the steering column assembly.

2. Lock the tilt and telescopic lever.

2. REMOVE LOWER STEERING COLUMN COVER

NOTICE:

Removing the lower steering column cover in the incorrect order will cause the parts to break.

Push Area

Push

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(1) Turn the steering wheel and remove the 3 screws.

(2) Press the part in the direction of the arrow shown in the illustration to detach the claws.

(3) Detach the claws and remove the lower steering column cover.

NOTICE:

  • for Power Tilt and Power Telescopic Steering Column:

    Do not damage the tilt and telescopic switch.

  • for Manual Tilt and Manual Telescopic Steering Column:

    Do not damage the tilt and telescopic lever.
