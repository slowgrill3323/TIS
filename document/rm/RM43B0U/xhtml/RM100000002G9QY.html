Protective Tape

(1) Remove the windshield wiper motor assembly.

1. Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disconnect the No. 1 wiper ring rod from the wiper motor pivot as shown in the illustration.

2. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 "TORX" screws.

3. Move the lever as shown in the illustration.

4. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the "TORX" screw and the windshield wiper motor assembly.

NOTICE:

Do not remove the wiper crank arm from the windshield wiper motor assembly.
