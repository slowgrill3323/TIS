DTC

B135013

Acoustic Vehicle Alerting Speaker Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

The front vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit consists of the vehicle approaching speaker controller and front vehicle approaching speaker assembly (vehicle approaching speaker assembly).

This DTC is stored when a malfunction is detected in the front vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit or vehicle approaching speaker controller.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B135013

Acoustic Vehicle Alerting Speaker Circuit Open

Detection condition:

  1. The ignition switch is ON.
  2. Power source voltage is 10 V or more.

Malfunction status:

  • When ECU output sound signal is being monitored with warning sound not being output, a front vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit open, short or short to ground is detected.

Malfunction duration:

  • 5 seconds or more
  • Vehicle approaching speaker controller
  • Front vehicle approaching speaker assembly (vehicle approaching speaker assembly)
  • Harness or connector
