PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL CANISTER PUMP MODULE (LEAK DETECTION PUMP SUB-ASSEMBLY)

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the canister pump module (leak detection pump sub-assembly) is necessary.

(1) Attach the 2 claws and install a new canister pump module (leak detection pump sub-assembly) to the filter assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow foreign matter such as grease, oil or water to adhere to the O-rings.
  • Ensure that the claws are engaged properly.

2. INSTALL NO. 3 CHARCOAL CANISTER BRACKET

(2) Attach the 2 claws and connect the fuel tank vent hose sub-assembly to the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(3) Attach the clip and connect the fuel emission hose to the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(4) Push the fuel tank vent hose to the charcoal canister sub-assembly and push in the retainer to attach the lock claws.

NOTICE:

  • Check that there are no scratches or foreign matter around the connecting parts of the tube connector and pipe (charcoal canister sub-assembly) before performing this work.
  • After connecting the fuel tank vent hose sub-assembly, check that the fuel tank vent hose is securely connected by pulling on the tube connector.
