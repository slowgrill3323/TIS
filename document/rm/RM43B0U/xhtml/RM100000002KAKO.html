PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL INVERTER WATER PUMP ASSEMBLY (WITH MOTOR)

(1) Temporarily install the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) with the nut A.

NOTICE:

If the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) is dropped or subjected to a strong impact, replace it with a new one.

(2) Tighten the nut A and nut B.

Torque:

7.0 N·m {71 kgf·cm, 62 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

The inverter water pump assembly (with motor) may be damaged by overtightening the bolt. Therefore, be sure to use a torque wrench for tightening.

(3) Connect the connector.

NOTICE:

  • If the connector of the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) is equipped with a dust prevention cap, remove the cap just before connecting the connector.
  • Make sure that the connector is connected securely.

2. CONNECT NO. 4 INVERTER COOLING HOSE

*a

Upper Side

*b

LH Side

*c

120°

*d

2 to 7 mm (0.0787 to 0.2756 in.)

(1) Connect the No. 4 inverter cooling hose to the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) and slide the hose clip to secure them.

NOTICE:

  • To prevent foreign matter from entering the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) and inverter cooling system, do not remove the pieces of cloth from the pipes and disconnected hoses until installation.
  • Do not apply excessive force to the No. 4 inverter cooling hose.

HINT:

  • Make sure to align the alignment marks of the hoses with the ribs of the inverter water pump assembly (with motor).
  • Make sure that the hose clips are positioned as shown in the illustration.
