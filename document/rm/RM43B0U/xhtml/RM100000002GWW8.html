Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: TRAILER BRAKE CONTROL SYSTEM: C115E19; Output Circuit Over Current Circuit Current Above Threshold; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C115E19

Output Circuit Over Current Circuit Current Above Threshold

DESCRIPTION

The trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) is connected via the trailer socket and direct line.

The trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) outputs a signal to the trailer socket.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

C115E19

Output Circuit Over Current Circuit Current Above Threshold

With the ignition switch ON and the IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) output circuit current value continuously exceeds 27.8 to 40 A for 1 second or more
  • Harness or connector (vehicle)
  • Harness or connector (trailer)
  • Brake assembly (trailer)
  • Trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay)
