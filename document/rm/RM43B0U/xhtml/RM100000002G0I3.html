(1) Remove the 9 bolts, 2 nuts and adjusting screw.

(2) Insert the blade of an oil pan seal cutter between the No. 2 oil pan sub-assembly and stiffening crankcase assembly, cut through the applied sealer and remove the No. 2 oil pan sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Be careful not to damage the contact surfaces of the stiffening crankcase assembly and No. 2 oil pan sub-assembly.
  • Be careful not to damage the No. 2 oil pan sub-assembly flange.

7. REMOVE ENGINE OIL LEVEL SENSOR

(1) Disconnect the engine oil level sensor connector.

(2) Remove the clip from the engine oil level sensor.

(3) Using an 8 mm socket wrench, remove the 2 bolts and engine oil level sensor.

NOTICE:

If the engine oil level sensor gauge assembly is dropped or subjected to a strong impact, replace it with a new one.
