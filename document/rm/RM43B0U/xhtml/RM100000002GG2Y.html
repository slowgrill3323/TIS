C007800

Different Diameter Tire

The vehicle speed exceeds 20 km/h (12 mph) and a difference exceeding 20% between the value output from the speed sensors of the front wheels and the rear wheels occurs the specified number of times or more.
  • Incorrect tire size
  • Tire air pressure not adjusted correctly
  • Speed sensor system
  • Skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)
