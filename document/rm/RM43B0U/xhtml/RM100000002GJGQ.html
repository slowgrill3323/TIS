3. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

(1) Using SST, remove the front drive shaft assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the differential side gear shaft oil seal, inboard joint boot and front drive shaft dust cover.
  • Do not drop the front drive shaft assembly.
  • When carrying the front drive shaft assembly, hold it horizontally.

HINT:
