DTC

B157111

Indicator (Green) Circuit Short to Ground

DTC

B157113

Indicator (Green) Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the DCM (telematics transceiver) detects an open or short in the manual (SOS) switch green indicator circuit of the manual (SOS) switch. The manual (SOS) switch green indicator illuminates after the ignition switch is turned to ON.

If the safety connect system is not active, the manual (SOS) switch green indicator will turn off.

If the safety connect system is active, the manual (SOS) switch green indicator will blink while communicating with the call center.
