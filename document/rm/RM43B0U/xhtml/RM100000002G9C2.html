(1) Remove the front door belt moulding assembly LH by the following procedure.

1. Put protective tape around the front door belt moulding assembly LH.

2. Push the front door belt moulding assembly LH and using moulding remover A, detach the claw as shown in the illustration.

3. Detach the claws and remove the front door belt moulding assembly LH.
