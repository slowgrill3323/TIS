PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE OIL PRESSURE SENDER GAGE ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

To prevent burns, do not remove the oil pressure sender gauge assembly while the engine is hot.

(1) Disconnect the oil pressure sender gauge assembly connector.

(2) Using a 27 mm deep socket wrench, remove the oil pressure sender gauge assembly.

NOTICE:

If the oil pressure sender gauge assembly is dropped or subjected to a strong impact, replace it with a new one.
