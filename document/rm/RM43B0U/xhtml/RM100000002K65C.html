Title: DRIVE SHAFT / PROPELLER SHAFT: FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

INSPECTION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.
  • When using a vise, do not overtighten it.

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

(a) Check that there is no excessive play in the radial direction of the inboard joint and outboard joint.

(b) Check that the inboard joint slides smoothly in the thrust direction.

(c) Check that there is no excessive play in the radial direction of the inboard joint.

(d) Check that the inboard joint boot and outboard joint boot are not cracked or damaged, and no grease is leaking out.

(e) Check that the inboard joint boot and outboard joint boot are not stretched or contracted when the front drive shaft assembly is at the standard length.

Dimension (A) (for Gasoline Models Other Than TRD Package):

for LH Side

588.15 mm (1.93 ft.)

for RH Side

666.65 mm (2.19 ft.)

Dimension (A) (for Gasoline Model TRD Package or 1Motor-HEV Model):

for LH Side

594.27 mm (1.95 ft.)

for RH Side

671.27 mm (2.20 ft.)

NOTICE:

Keep the front drive shaft assembly level during inspection.

If the inboard joint boot and outboard joint boot are stretched or contracted, correct them.
