Title: THEFT DETERRENT / KEYLESS ENTRY: DIGITAL KEY SYSTEM: REGISTRATION; 2024 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 - 03/2024]

REGISTRATION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. CLEAR ALL DIGITAL KEYS USING MULTI-DISPLAY

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to use the electrical key when turning the ignition switch to ON and clearing all the digital keys.
  • If all digital keys are cleared, both the owner key and share keys are cleared.

HINT:

In this document, an electrical key refers to a registered electrical key transmitter sub-assembly.

Procedure

Steps

1. Start of erasure
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON using electrical key.
  2. Enter the following multi-display menu:

    Settings / Info & Security / Delete the digital keys

2. Confirmation of registered electrical keys
  1. In accordance with the display of the multi-display, touch the electrical key to the engine switch.
  2. Confirm that the buzzer sounds once (short beep).

3. End of erasure
  1. In accordance with the display of the multi-display, delete all digital keys.
