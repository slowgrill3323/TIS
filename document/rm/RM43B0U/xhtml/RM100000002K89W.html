1. INSTALL NO. 2 VEHICLE APPROACHING SPEAKER ASSEMBLY (for Rear Side)

NOTICE:

  • When installing the No. 2 vehicle approaching speaker assembly, take care not to damage it.
  • Do not reuse a No. 2 vehicle approaching speaker assembly that has been dropped or subjected to a strong shock.
  • Do not push the No. 2 vehicle approaching speaker assembly center area.

23 N·m {235 kgf·cm, 17 ft·lbf}
