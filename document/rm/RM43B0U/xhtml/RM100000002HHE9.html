PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. RE-PAIRING THE WIRELESS CAMERA WITH TRAILER CAMERA ECU

Process

Procedure

1. Start of pairing mode
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON.
  2. Check that the shift position is in P.
  3. Press the panoramic view monitor switch to display panoramic view monitor system screen on multi-display.
  4. Select "AUX" button on the panoramic view monitor screen to enter the trailer camera menus.
  5. Select "Trailer Camera Settings" button on the "AUX view" screen.
  6. Select "Camera Device Management" on the "Trailer Camera Settings " menu screen.
  7. Select "Add Camera".
  8. Message is shown to put wireless camera into pairing mode.

2. Pairing a camera
  1. Supply electric power to the wireless camera from a USB-C port or a 12 V DC power supply.
  2. Move the wireless camera so that the lens is facing up.
  3. Rotate the wireless camera longitudinally to face up, then face down, then face up again. Repeat this 3 times.
  4. When the wireless camera is in pairing mode, the LED on the wireless camera flashes orange at approximately 1-second intervals.

    NOTICE:

    That if LED on wireless camera does not show orange flashing at approximately 1-second intervals after step 3, to repeat the wireless camera rotation procedure (step2 and 3).

  5. Selecting "OK" on the multi-display displays a QR code.
  6. Place the wireless camera 6 inches away from the QR code.

    NOTICE:

    After step 6, it can be noted the LED flashing will change to approximately 3-second intervals to signify the wireless camera has read the QR code and is attempting reboot for connection test.

  7. When pairing is complete, a message appears on the multi-display.

    NOTICE:

    The wireless camera will automatically show streaming image and status icons on the multi-display after camera reboot and connection established.

    This should be checked to ensure proper pairing was executed.

3. End of pairing

Finish pairing a camera.
