Title: MIRROR (INT): TRAILER CAMERA SYSTEM: Inner Mirror Camera Image is not Displayed; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

Inner Mirror Camera Image is not Displayed

DESCRIPTION

The display signal from inner mirror camera assembly is transmitted to the inner rear view mirror assembly via the trailer camera ECU (communication telematics box).

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Perform pairing after replacing or removing and installing the trailer camera ECU (communication telematics box).

    Click here

  • Inspect the fuse for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
