AUTO LSD Indicator Light Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The AUTO LSD is a system. When the VSC OFF switch is pushed on, the AUTO LSD function is available and the AUTO LSD indicator light illuminates.

HINT:

The AUTO LSD does not operate even if the VSC OFF switch is pressed under the following conditions:

  • The TRAC or VSC system is faulty.
  • The temperature inside the brake actuator assembly increases and the AUTO LSD operation is suspended.
