2. INSTALL NO. 3 GEAR SHIFT FORK SHAFT

(1) Install the No. 3 gear shift fork to the No. 3 gear shift fork shaft with a new bolt.

Secure the shift fork with the bolt from the side as shown in the illustration.

3. INSTALL NO. 1 GEAR SHIFT FORK SHAFT

(1) Pass the No. 1 gear shift fork shaft through the No. 1 gear shift fork head, set the shaft on the cloth that was placed on the 2 V-blocks, and then hold the shaft in place as shown in the illustration.

CAUTION:

  • Make sure to perform this procedure with 2 people and firmly hold the gear shift fork shaft in place.
  • Make sure to protect the gear shift fork shaft with a cloth before performing this procedure. If the gear shift fork shaft is damaged, shifting operations will worsen.

(2) Using a 5 mm pin punch and hammer, tap a new slotted pin into the No. 1 gear shift head and No. 1 gear shift fork shaft.

Standard depth:

0 to 0.5 mm (0 to 0.0196 in.)

NOTICE:

4. INSTALL NO. 4 GEAR SHIFT FORK SHAFT

(1) Pass the No. 4 gear shift fork shaft through the No. 4 gear shift fork head, set the shaft on the cloth that was placed on the 2 V-blocks, and then hold the shaft in place as shown in the illustration.

CAUTION:

(3) Install the No. 4 gear shift fork shaft to the No. 1 gear shift fork.
