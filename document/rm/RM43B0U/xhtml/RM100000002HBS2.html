ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT LOWER NO. 1 SUSPENSION ARM SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Check for looseness.

(1) Lift up the vehicle.

(2) Move the front lower No. 1 suspension arm sub-assembly up and down by hand and check for rattle.

HINT:

If the front lower No. 1 suspension arm sub-assembly is loose, replace it with a new one.
