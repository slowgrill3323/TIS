FREEZE FRAME DATA

HINT:

  • Freeze frame data is recorded at the moment when a DTC is stored.
  • Once freeze frame data is stored when a DTC is stored, it is not updated or cleared until the DTC is cleared.

CHECK FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) According to the display on the GTS, display the freeze frame data for the selected DTC.

Body Electrical > Blind Spot Monitor "B" > Trouble Codes

CLEAR FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) Turn the blind spot monitor system on.

(b) Clear the DTCs and freeze frame data.

Body Electrical > Blind Spot Monitor "B" > Clear DTCs

Body Electrical > Blind Spot Monitor "A" > Clear DTCs

(c) Clear the DTCs and freeze frame data.

NOTICE:

Clearing the DTCs will also clear the freeze frame data.
