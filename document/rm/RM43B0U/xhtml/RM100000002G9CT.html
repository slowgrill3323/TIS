PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL LIGHT WIRE (w/ Outer Bed Light)

2. INSTALL REAR BODY SIDE PANEL PROTECTOR LH

NOTICE:

When installing the rear body side panel protector LH, heat the rear body side panel protector LH and quarter panel sub-assembly LH using a heat light.

Standard Value:

Part

Temperature

Quarter Panel Sub-assembly LH

40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)

Rear Body Side Panel Protector LH

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

NOTICE:

Do not heat the quarter panel sub-assembly LH andrear body side panel protector LH excessively.

(a) for Short Deck without Outer Bed Light:

*a

Double-sided Tape

-

-

(1) Clean the surface of the quarter panel sub-assembly LH.

1. Using a heat light, heat the quarter panel sub-assembly LH surface.

2. Remove any remaining double-sided tape from the quarter panel sub-assembly LH.

3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

NOTICE:

  • Installing the rear body side panel protector LH with some double-sidedtape remaining may cause poor adhesion. Perform this procedure until the tapeis sufficiently removed.
  • Make sure to use a cloth when removing. Using a screwdriver, etc., may causedamage and poor adhesion.

(2) Using a heat light, heat the quarter panel sub-assembly LH and rear body side panel protector LH.

(3) Attach the clips and double-sided tape and install the rear body side panel protector LH.

NOTICE:

Apply pressure so that it does not lift up from the quarter panel sub-assembly LH.

HINT:

The application strength of the rear body side panel protector LH will weaken if reapplied. If reapplication is necessary, be sure to replace it with a new one.
