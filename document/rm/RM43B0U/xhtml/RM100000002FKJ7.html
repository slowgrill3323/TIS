PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. TEMPORARILY INSTALL LOWER CONTROL ARM ASSEMBLY

(1) Temporarily install the lower control arm assembly to the rear axle housing assembly and vehicle body with the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.

NOTICE:

  • Because the nut has its own stopper, do not turn the nut. Tighten the bolt with the nut secured.
  • Insert the bolt from the outside of the vehicle.
