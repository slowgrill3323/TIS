PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE GENERATOR PULLEY CAP

NOTICE:

  • Do not reuse the generator pulley cap.
  • If the generator pulley cap is removed, replace the generator pulley cap and generator pulley with clutch with new ones as the correct grease amount cannot be confirmed.

HINT:

Although grease will be observed when the generator pulley cap is removed, this does not indicate a malfunction.

(1) Using a screwdriver, remove the generator pulley cap from the generator pulley with clutch.

  • Check that the generator assembly is secured in the vise tightly.
  • Hold SST tightly during the operation.
