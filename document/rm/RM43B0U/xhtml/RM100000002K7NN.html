Title: WINDOW / GLASS: WINDSHIELD DEICER SYSTEM: Windshield Deicer does not Operate; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

Windshield Deicer does not Operate

DESCRIPTION

When the windshield deicer switch on the radio and display receiver assembly is operated, windshield deicer operation request signal is sent to the air conditioning amplifier assembly via CAN communication line.

When the air conditioning amplifier assembly receives windshield deicer operation request signal, it turns on the windshield deicer relay assembly (DEICER) to operate the windshield deicer system.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
  • If the windshield deicer system is operated when the auxiliary battery voltage is low, due to the electrical load limit control that gives voltage supply priority to the power steering system, the windshield deicer system may not operate. In this case, check the vehicle control history (RoB) of the power steering system and check that "Battery Voltage Drop: X208F" is not stored before proceeding with troubleshooting.
