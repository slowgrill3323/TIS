ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT TRANSMISSION OIL

(a) Park the vehicle on a level surface.

(b) Remove the No. 2 engine under cover assembly (w/ Transfer Skid Plate).

HINT:

Click here

(c) Remove the transmission filler plug and gasket.

(d) Check that the oil level is between 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug opening.

If the result is not as specified, add transmission oil.

Manual transmission oil:

Oil grade

Oil viscosity

Standard capacity

Gear oil API GL-3 (GL-4)

SAE 75W-90

2.5 liters (2.6 US qts, 2.2 Imp. qts)

NOTICE:

  • Too much or too little oil will lead to transmission problems.
  • After adjusting the oil level, drive the vehicle and check the oil level again.

*a

0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.)

(e) Inspect for oil leaks when the oil level is low.

(f) Install a new gasket and the transmission filler plug.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

(g) Install the No. 2 engine under cover assembly (w/ Transfer Skid Plate).
