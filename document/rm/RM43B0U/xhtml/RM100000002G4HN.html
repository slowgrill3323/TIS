Rear Vertical Acceleration Sensor Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

DESCRIPTION

The rear acceleration sensor is built into the absorber control ECU and detects the vertical acceleration of the vehicle.

Note

C171712

Rear Vertical Acceleration Sensor Circuit Circuit Short to Battery

Either condition is met:

  • The power source voltage of the rear acceleration sensor is 5.5 V or higher continuously for 0.5 seconds
  • The output voltage of the rear acceleration sensor is 4.7 V or higher continuously for 1 second

C171714

Rear Vertical Acceleration Sensor Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

Either condition is met:

  • The power source voltage of the rear acceleration sensor is 4.3 V or less continuously for 0.5 seconds
  • The output voltage of the rear acceleration sensor is 0.3 V or less continuously for 1 second
