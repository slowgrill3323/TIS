Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (except Gasoline Model): C13B951,C13B954; SM Valve Learning Not Programmed; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

C13B951

SM Valve Learning Not Programmed

DTC

C13B954

SM Valve Learning Missing Calibration

DESCRIPTION

The No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) stores the individual characteristics of each linear solenoid built into the brake actuator assembly. Based on the stored information, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) calibrates the individual differences and controls systems.

The No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) receives the shift position P signal from the TCM through the CAN communication system.
