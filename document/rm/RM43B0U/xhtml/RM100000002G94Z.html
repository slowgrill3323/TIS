3. INSPECT OUTER MIRROR LH (w/ Blind Spot Monitor System)

(a) Check the outer rear view mirror indicator operation.

(1) Connect 4 new 1.5 V dry-cell batteries in series.

(2) Apply 6 V dry-cell batteries to the terminals of the connector, and check the blind spot monitor indicator condition.

NOTICE:

  • Do not use rechargeable batteries as they may not output a voltage of 1.5 V.
  • Do not apply a voltage of higher than 6 V.

OK:

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

Positive (+) end of the 6 V dry-cell battery → 1

Negative (-) end of the 6 V dry-cell battery → 2

Outer rear view mirror indicator comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the outer mirror LH.
