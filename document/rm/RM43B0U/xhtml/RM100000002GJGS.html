Title: DRIVE SHAFT / PROPELLER SHAFT: FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

INSPECTION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.
  • When using a vise, do not overtighten it.

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

(a) Using a dial indicator, check the front propeller shaft assembly runout.

Maximum runout:

0.61 mm (0.0240 in.)

If the front propeller shaft assembly runout is more than the maximum, replace the front propeller shaft assembly.

2. INSPECT SPIDER BEARING

(a) Check the spider bearings for wear or damage.

If necessary, replace the spider bearings.

(b) Check the spider bearing axial play by turning the yoke while holding the front propeller shaft assembly tightly.

Maximum bearing axial play:

0.05 mm (0.00197 in.)

If the axial play is more than the maximum, replace the spider bearings.
