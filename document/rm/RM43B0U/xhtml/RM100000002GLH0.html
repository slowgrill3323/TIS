A/C Switch Indicator does not Turn On

DESCRIPTION

If none of the switch indicators on the air conditioning control assembly illuminate, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor

A/C switch indicator does not illuminate
  • Air conditioning amplifier assembly malfunction
  • Air conditioning control assembly malfunction
  • A/C switch malfunction
  • A/C switch indicator malfunction (including when indicator turned off without switch operation)
  • One of servo motors malfunction (initialization incomplete)
  • Damper and link operation (jamming)
  • Refrigerant pressure (low pressure)
  • Low ambient temperature (including ambient temperature sensor)
