1. INSPECT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY

*a

Component without harness connected

(Power Window Regulator Motor Assembly)

*b

Motor Gear

Clockwise

Counterclockwise

(a) Check the operation of the power window regulator motor assembly.

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the power window regulator motor assembly.

(2) Check that the motor gear rotates smoothly as follows.

OK:

Measurement Condition

Specified Condition

Auxiliary battery positive (+) → Q24-2 (DOWN)

Auxiliary battery negative (-) → Q24-1 (UP)

Motor gear rotates clockwise

Auxiliary battery positive (+) → Q24-1 (UP)

Auxiliary battery negative (-) → Q24-2 (DOWN)

Motor gear rotates counterclockwise

If the result is not as specified, replace the power window regulator motor assembly.
