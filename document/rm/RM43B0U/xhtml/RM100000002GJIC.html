PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL

Click here

2. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB GREASE CAP

(1) Using a screwdriver and hammer, remove the front axle hub grease cap.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the axle hub.

3. REMOVE FRONT AXLE SHAFT NUT

(1) Using SST and a hammer, release the staked part of the front axle shaft nut.

09930-00010 09931-00010 09931-00020

NOTICE:

Loosen the staked part of the front axle shaft nut completely, otherwise the threads of the drive shaft may be damaged.

(2) Using SST, remove the front axle shaft nut.

HINT:

Keep depressing the brake pedal to prevent the drive shaft from rotating.

09229-55010

4. DISCONNECT FRONT SPEED SENSOR

NOTICE:

  • Keep the tip of the front speed sensor and installation hole free of foreign matter.
  • Do not rotate or apply excessive force to the front speed sensor when removing it from the steering knuckle. Rotating or applying excessive force may result in damage to the front speed sensor.

(1) Remove the bolt and disconnect the speed sensor harness from the flexible hose bracket.

NOTICE:

If the upper arm is removed from the steering knuckle without disconnecting the speed sensor harness, the speed sensor is pulled along with the upper arm, resulting in damage to the speed sensor.

(2) Remove the bolt, skid control sensor clamp and sensor wire.

(3) Remove the bolt and disconnect the front speed sensor from the steering knuckle.

NOTICE:

Pull out the sensor while trying as much as possible not to rotate it.

5. DISCONNECT FRONT DISC BRAKE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove the bolt and disconnect the flexible hose bracket from the steering knuckle.

(2) Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the front disc brake cylinder assembly from the steering knuckle.

NOTICE:

  • Do not disconnect the from the front disc brake cylinder assembly.
  • Do not twist or bend the front flexible hose.
  • Do not kink or damage the front brake tube.

(1) Support the front suspension lower arm with a jack. Insert a wooden block between the jack and the front suspension lower arm to prevent damage.

NOTICE:

  • Do not lift the front suspension lower arm more than necessary with a jack.
  • Keep the front suspension lower arm supported until installation of the steering knuckle assembly is complete.

(2) Remove the clip and nut.

(3) Using SST, disconnect the upper ball joint from the steering knuckle assembly.

09628-62011

NOTICE:

Do not damage the ball joint dust cover.

(4) Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the lower ball joint attachment from the steering knuckle assembly.

10. REMOVE STEERING KNUCKLE OIL SEAL

(1) Using a screwdriver and hammer, tap out the steering knuckle oil seal from the steering knuckle.
