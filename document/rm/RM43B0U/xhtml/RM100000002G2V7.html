Headlight Dimmer Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The steering sensor receives the following switch information:

  • Light control switch in tail, TAIL, HEAD, DRL OFF*1 or AUTO position
  • Dimmer switch in high, low or high flash (pass) position
  • Fog light switch in front or off position*2
    • *1: w/ DRL OFF Switch
    • *2: w/ Fog Light
