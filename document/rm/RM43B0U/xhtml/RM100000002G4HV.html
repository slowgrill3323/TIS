Title: SUSPENSION CONTROL: ADAPTIVE VARIABLE SUSPENSION SYSTEM: C173414; Rear Left Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C173414

Rear Left Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

DESCRIPTION

The rear absorber control actuator LH (rear shock absorber assembly LH) switches the damping force of the absorber based on an output signal from the absorber control ECU.

DTC Detection Condition

Note

C173414

Rear Left Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

Either condition is met:

  • An open circuit is detected between the rear absorber control actuator LH (rear shock absorber assembly LH) and absorber control ECU.
  • A short circuit is detected between the rear absorber control actuator LH (rear shock absorber assembly LH) and absorber control ECU.
  • Rear absorber control actuator LH (rear shock absorber assembly LH)
  • Wire harness or connector
  • No. 2 parking brake wire assembly
  • Absorber control ECU
