INSPECT BATTERY SAVING CONTROL OPERATION

(a) The operations and conditions of this control are described below.

Control

Condition

Lights that Operate
*1: for Gasoline Model

*2: except Gasoline Model

*3: w/ Footwell Light

*4: w/ Vanity Light

*5: w/ Courtesy Light

Basic operation

When 20 minutes elapse after the ignition switch is turned from ACC or IG to off and the lights that operate remain on, the lights are turned off.

HINT:

Battery saving control is canceled when any of the following operations is performed before the lights are turned off by battery saving control. After the operation is performed, the lights remain on for another 20 minutes and then the lights are turned off.

  • The ignition switch is turned to ACC or IG.
  • Any door is opened and closed.
  • The tail gate is opened and closed.
  • Any door is unlocked.
  • The key enters any exterior actuation area.
  • Engine switch illumination*1
  • Power switch illumination*2
  • Map light assembly
  • No. 1 room light assembly
  • No. 1 interior illumination light assembly (driver side footwell light)*3
  • No. 1 interior illumination light assembly (front passenger side footwell light)*3
  • Vanity light assembly*4
  • Courtesy light assembly*5

Interior light auto cut operation

When 20 minutes elapse after the ignition switch is turned from on (ACC or IG) to off and the lights that operate remain on, the lights are turned off.

HINT:

Battery saving control is canceled when any of the following operations is performed before the lights are turned off by battery saving control. After the operation is performed, the lights remain on for another 20 minutes and then the lights are turned off.

  • The ignition switch is turned to ACC or IG.
  • Map light assembly
  • No. 1 room light assembly
  • Vanity light assembly*4
