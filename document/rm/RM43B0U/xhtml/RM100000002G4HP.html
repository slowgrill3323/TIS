Title: SUSPENSION CONTROL: ADAPTIVE VARIABLE SUSPENSION SYSTEM: C173114; Front Right Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C173114

Front Right Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

DESCRIPTION

The front absorber control actuator RH (front shock absorber assembly RH) switches the damping force of the absorber based on an output signal from the absorber control ECU.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Note

C173114

Front Right Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

Either condition is met:

  • An open circuit is detected between the front absorber control actuator RH (front shock absorber assembly RH) and absorber control ECU.
  • A short circuit is detected between the front absorber control actuator RH (front shock absorber assembly RH) and absorber control ECU.
  • Front absorber control actuator RH (front shock absorber assembly RH)
  • Wire harness or connector
  • Front speed sensor RH
  • Absorber control ECU
