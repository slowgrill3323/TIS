PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT NO. 1 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY (for LH Side)

(a) With the speaker in the installed condition, check there are no abnormalities such looseness or rattling.

(b) Check that there is no foreign matter inside the speaker.

(c) Check that the speaker cone is not damaged.

(d) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

(1) for 10 Speakers:
