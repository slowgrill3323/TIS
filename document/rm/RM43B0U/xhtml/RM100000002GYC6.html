Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: RED INDICATOR REMAINS ON; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

RED INDICATOR REMAINS ON

DESCRIPTION

This means that the DCM (telematics transceiver) has detected a malfunction in the safety connect system and stored a DTC or specific vehicle control history (RoB) Code.

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK DTC

(a) Turn the ignition switch to ON and wait for 10 seconds or more.

(b) Clear the DTCs.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Clear DTCs

(c) Check for DTCs and check that no DTCs are output.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Trouble Codes

OK:

No DTCs are output.
