DISPOSAL

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. DISPOSE OF SEAT SUSPENSION ABSORBER ASSEMBLY

(a) Turn the knob to the "Offroad" position.

*a

Offroad

(b) Slowly unscrew the valve to release the remaining pressure.

Unscrew in this Direction

HINT:

Release the pressure until there is no pressure shown on the display.

(c) Open the clamps and pull them off.

(d) Disconnect the connectors.

(e) Drain oil from the seat suspension absorber assembly and connectors.

HINT:

Moving the cylinder piston rods can help drain the oil.
