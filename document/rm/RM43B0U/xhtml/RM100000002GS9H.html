B00731A

Second Row Left Seat Belt Pretensioner Deployment Control Circuit Resistance Below Threshold
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a line short in the rear pretensioner squib LH circuit for 2 seconds during the primary check.
  • Rear pretensioner squib LH (rear seat outer belt assembly LH) malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
