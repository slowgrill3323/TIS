Steering Wheel does not Heat Up When Heated Steering Wheel Switch is Pressed

DESCRIPTION

The heated steering wheel system heats the steering wheel assembly when the system is turned on using the steering heater switch.

The heated steering wheel system uses a thermistor inside of the steering wheel assembly to detect the heater temperature and maintain the set temperature.

A thermostat is installed in the steering wheel heater unit as a safety mechanism.
