Seat Heater for Front Left Seat does not Operate

DESCRIPTION

When the seat heater switch (front left side) is operated, the air conditioning amplifier assembly receives the signal. The air conditioning amplifier assembly receives the signal and operates the front left side seat heater.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • If the auxiliary battery voltage is low, the seat heater system may not operate. When "High Power Consumption Partial Limit on AC/Heater Operation" is displayed on the multi-information display in the combination meter assembly, inspect the auxiliary battery, referring to On-vehicle Inspection for the charging system.

    except Gasoline Model:

    Click here

    for Gasoline Model:

    Click here

  • If the seat heater is operated when the battery voltage is low, due to the electrical load limit control that gives voltage supply priority to the power steering system, the mirror heater may not operate. In this case, check the vehicle control history of the power steering system and check that "Battery Voltage Drop: X208F" is not stored before proceeding with troubleshooting.

    Click here

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
