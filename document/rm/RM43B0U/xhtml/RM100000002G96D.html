Title: WINDOW / GLASS: POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR (for Rear Door): INSPECTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY LH

NOTICE:

  • Do not apply positive (+) auxiliary battery voltage to any terminals, except terminal P1-2 (B), to avoid damaging the pulse sensor inside the motor.
  • Perform initialization of the power window system after removing, inspecting or replacing the power window regulator motor assembly LH.

(a) Check that the motor gear rotates smoothly as follows.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Power Window Regulator Motor Assembly LH)

*b

Motor Gear

Counterclockwise

Click Location & Routing(P1)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition
  1. Connect the positive (+) lead of the auxiliary battery to terminal P1-2 (B) and the negative (-) lead to terminal P1-1 (GND), and hold for 3 seconds or more.
  2. While terminals P1-2 (B) and P1-1 (GND) are connected, connect the negative (-) lead of the auxiliary battery to terminal P1-10 (UP).
  3. After disconnecting the negative (-) lead of the auxiliary battery from terminal P1-10 (UP), connect terminal P1-10 (UP) to the negative (-) lead of the auxiliary battery again within 1 second.

Motor gear rotates counterclockwise (UP)
  1. Connect the positive (+) lead of the auxiliary battery to terminal P1-2 (B) and the negative (-) lead to terminal P1-1 (GND), and hold for 3 seconds or more.
  2. While terminals P1-2 (B) and P1-1 (GND) are connected, connect the negative (-) lead of the auxiliary battery to terminal P1-7 (DOWN).
  3. After disconnecting the negative (-) lead of the auxiliary battery from terminal P1-7 (DOWN), connect terminal P1-7 (DOWN) to the negative (-) lead of the auxiliary battery again within 1 second.

Motor gear rotates clockwise (DOWN)

If the result is not as specified, replace the power window regulator motor assembly LH.
