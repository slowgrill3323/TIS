DTC

B15CC49

Backup Battery Internal Electronic Failure

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is set when the DCM (telematics transceiver) detects one of the following:

  • The mobilephone battery voltage drops or the mobilephone battery malfunctions.
  • The mobilephone battery temperature is (temporarily) high.
