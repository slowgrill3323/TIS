Title: AUDIO / VIDEO: ACTIVE NOISE CONTROL SYSTEM (w/ Stereo Component Amplifier): B1AA296,B1AA31C; Front Left Microphone Circuit Component Internal Failure; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

B1AA296

Front Left Microphone Circuit Component Internal Failure

DTC

B1AA31C

Front Left Microphone Circuit Circuit Voltage Out of Range

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction occurs in the active noise control microphone (FR LH) system.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B1AA296

Front Left Microphone Circuit Component Internal Failure

Stereo component equalizer assembly detects malfunction in active noise control microphone (FR LH) for 4 seconds or more continuously when engine speed is 1200 rpm or more*
  • Harness or connector
  • Active noise control microphone (FR LH)
  • Stereo component equalizer assembly

Active Noise Control

A

B1AA31C

Front Left Microphone Circuit Circuit Voltage Out of Range

Stereo component equalizer assembly detects active noise control microphone (FR LH) connection malfunction for 4 seconds or more continuously*
  • Harness or connector
  • Active noise control microphone (FR LH)
  • Stereo component equalizer assembly

Active Noise Control

A

HINT:

*: Malfunction monitoring is not performed during the following conditions to prevent erroneous detection.

  • Before 3 seconds have elapsed after the ignition switch is turned to ON.
  • The auxiliary battery voltage is less than 9.5 V.
  • Before 3 seconds have elapsed after the auxiliary battery voltage has returned to normal.
