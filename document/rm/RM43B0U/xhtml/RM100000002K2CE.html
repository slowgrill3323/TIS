PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL TORQUE CONVERTER ASSEMBLY

*1

Flex Plate

*2

Hybrid Module Assembly Housing

*a

Dimension (A)

-

-

(1) Using a vernier caliper and straightedge, measure the dimension A between the transmission assembly contact surface of the hybrid module assembly and the torque converter assembly contact surface of the flex plate[*1].

*1

Front Oil Pump Oil Seal

-

-

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Align the matchmark on the automatic transmission case sub-assembly with the one on the torque converter assembly and engage the splines of the input shaft with the turbine runner splines.

NOTICE:

Install the torque converter assembly to the input shaft while keeping it horizontal.

(2) Rotate the torque converter assembly approximately 180° and engage the splines of the stator shaft with the stator assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the front oil pump oil seal.
  • Install the torque converter assembly to the input shaft while keeping it horizontal.

(3) Rotate the torque converter assembly approximately 180° again, align the matchmark on the torque converter assembly with the one on the automatic transmission case sub-assembly and insert the key of the torque converter assembly into the groove of the oil pump drive gear.

NOTICE:

  • Do not push the torque converter assembly excessively when rotating it.
  • Do not damage the front oil pump oil seal.
  • Install the torque converter assembly to the input shaft while keeping it horizontal.

*a

Dimension (B)

-

-

Rotates Smoothly

-

-

(1) Using a vernier caliper and a straightedge, measure dimension (B) shown in the illustration and check that (B) is smaller than (A) measured in step [*1].

Standard:

(B) = (A) - 1.0 mm (0.0394 in.) or less

NOTICE:

  • If the transmission assembly is installed to the hybrid module assembly housing with the torque converter assembly not sufficiently inserted, the torque converter assembly may be damaged.
  • Make sure to deduct the thickness of the straightedge.

(2) Check that the torque converter assembly rotates smoothly.

NOTICE:

Do not push the torque converter assembly excessively when rotating it.

13. INSTALL HYBRID VEHICLE TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY WITH TRANSFER ASSEMBLY

*1

Flex Plate

*2

Crankshaft

*a

Hybrid module input shaft sub-assembly centerpiece

*b

Apply clutch spline grease

Knock Pin

Bolt A

Bolt B

-

-

(1) Confirm that the 2 knock pins are installed to the engine assembly and are not damaged.

(2) Apply clutch spline grease to the surface of the ball bearing that contacts the hybrid module input shaft sub-assembly centerpiece.

Clutch Spline Grease:

Toyota Genuine Clutch Spline Grease or equivalent

Maximum Grease Amount:

Approximately 1 g (0.0353 oz.)

(3) While keeping the engine assembly and hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly horizontal, align the 2 knock pins with the holes in the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly and install the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly with the 7 bolts.

Torque:

for bolt A :

71 N·m {724 kgf·cm, 52 ft·lbf}

for bolt B :

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Do not use excessive force when installing the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly.
  • When mounting the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly to the engine assembly, make sure to securely fit the 2 knock pins into the knock holes.
  • When tightening the bolts, make sure that the contact surfaces of the engine assembly and the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly are in close contact with one another.
  • When installing the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly, make sure that the oil cooler tube does not become damaged.

13.5 N·m {138 kgf·cm, 10 ft·lbf}

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

HINT:

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

HINT:

It is not necessary to remove the oil cooler tube clamps unless they are being replaced.

(8) Connect the 2 hoses of the oil cooler tube sub-assembly to the transmission oil thermostat, and slide the 2 hose clips to secure the hose.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow any foreign matter into the hoses.
  • Make sure that the claws of the hose clip are within the location shown in the illustration.
  • The paint mark must be within the range shown in the illustration.
  • Make sure that the clip does not overlap the pipe spool.
