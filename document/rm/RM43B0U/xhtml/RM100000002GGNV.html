DTC

B154913

Voice Recognition Microphone2 Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

The telephone microphone assembly RH is connected to the radio and display receiver assembly via voice recognition microphone 2 signal detection line.

This DTC is stored when the radio and display receiver assembly detects disconnection of telephone microphone assembly RH.
