2. INSTALL REAR DOOR CHECK ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Clean the bolt hole in the vehicle body.

(2) When reusing a bolt:

1. Clean the threads of the bolt with non-residue solvent.

2. Apply adhesive to the threads of the bolt.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent.

(3) Install the rear door check assembly with the bolt and 2 nuts.

Torque:

Bolt :

29 N·m {296 kgf·cm, 21 ft·lbf}

Nut :

8.5 N·m {87 kgf·cm, 75 in·lbf}

20. INSTALL REAR DOOR GLASS SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) Connect the rear power window regulator switch assembly connector.

(2) Connect the cable to the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

    Click here

  • except Gasoline Model

    Click here

(3) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(4) Move the rear door window regulator assembly until the installation bolts can be seen.

(5) Insert the rear door glass sub-assembly into the rear door panel as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the rear door glass sub-assembly.

(6) Install the rear door glass sub-assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(7) Move the rear door glass sub-assembly until the open position.

(8) Turn the ignition switch off.

(9) Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.
