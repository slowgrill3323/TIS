Title: POWER OUTLETS (INT): WIRELESS CHARGING SYSTEM: OPERATION CHECK; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

OPERATION CHECK

WIRELESS CHARGING SYSTEM OPERATION CHECK

*A

for Automatic Transmission

*B

for Manual Transmission

*a

Indicator Light (Green/Amber)

*b

Charging Area

(a) The engine is started or turn the ignition switch to ON or ACC.

HINT:

If the ignition switch is turned off when the wireless charging system is on, the wireless charging system will automatically turn on the next time the engine is started or the ignition switch is turned to ON or ACC.

(b) Turn the wireless charging system on and check that the indicator light (green) illuminates.

(c) Place a rechargeable device on the charging area and check that the indicator light (amber) illuminates, indicating charging is in progress.

HINT:

Refer to System description for details about Qi-compatible rechargeable devices.

Click here

(d) Check that the indicator alternately illuminates green and amber when a device that supports fast charging is placed on the charging area and then the radio and display receiver assembly is operated to switch to fast charging.

(e) Check that the indicator light (green) illuminates when charging is complete.

HINT:

Some mobile devices, cases or accessories may not cause the indicator (green) to illuminate when charging is complete even though they are Qi-compatible. Check the mobile device to confirm the charge status.

(f) Turn the wireless charging system off and check that the indicator light turns off.
