DTC

C139100

Abnormal Leak in Accumulator

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored if internal or external brake fluid leaks are detected due to improper sealing in the brake booster with master cylinder assembly or brake booster with accumulator pump assembly. Internal leaks are suspected if the pump motor operates frequently without braking.

C139100

Abnormal Leak in Accumulator

Either of the following is detected:

  • When the brake pedal is not depressed and the pump motor is operating, the accumulator pressure sensor value does not increase a certain number of times.
  • When the brake pedal is not depressed and the pump motor is not operating, the accumulator pressure sensor decrease rate exceeds a certain threshold a certain number of times.
  • Brake fluid leaks
  • Brake booster with master cylinder assembly

    (Malfunctioning internal seal etc.)

  • Brake booster with accumulator pump assembly

    (Accumulator internal gas pressure decrease, etc.)
