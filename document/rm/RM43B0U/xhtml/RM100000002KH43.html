6. REMOVE FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 "TORX" screws.

(2) Move the front seat assembly LH to the rearmost position.

(3) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 "TORX" screws.

(4) Move the front seat assembly LH to the center position.

(5) Move the seatback of the front seat assembly LH to the perpendicular position.

(6) Move the seat cushion of the front seat assembly LH (lifter and vertical) to the highest position.

(7) Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

  • Wait at least 60 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.
