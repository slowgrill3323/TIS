Code

Tester Display

X20D8

Zero Point Calibration of Steering Angle Sensor Not Acquired

History of being unable to obtain the steering angle zero point
  • Sensor installation
  • Yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly)

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

This Vehicle Control History (RoB) may be output during situations when the steering sensor zero point calibration temporarily cannot be acquired, such as when the stop light is stuck ON.
