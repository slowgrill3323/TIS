Title: AL80E (AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION SYSTEM: P271371; Pressure Control Solenoid "D" Actuator Stuck; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

P271371

Pressure Control Solenoid "D" Actuator Stuck

DESCRIPTION

The solenoid (SLT) valve is controlled via current control performed by the TCM based on signals, etc. from the accelerator position sensor and transmission revolution sensor.

As a result, the line pressure is adjusted as appropriate for throttle position and engine output.

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

If the difference in speed between the input shaft revolution (speed) and the output shaft revolution (speed) is abnormal at each gear position (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th or reverse gear position), the TCM illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

When the solenoid (SLT) valve remains ON, oil pressure goes down and clutch engagement force decreases.

NOTICE:

If driving continues under these conditions, the clutches will burn out and the vehicle will no longer be drivable.
