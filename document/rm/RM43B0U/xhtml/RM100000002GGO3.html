DTC

B1ACA71

Front Right Speaker Cable Actuator Stuck

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the stereo component amplifier assembly detects an open in the front speaker RH circuit by energizing the speaker line.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B1ACA71

Front Right Speaker Cable Actuator Stuck

Open in the front speaker RH circuit is detected.

(1 trip detection logic)
  • Harness or connector
  • Stereo component amplifier assembly
  • Front No. 1 speaker assembly RH
  • Front No. 2 speaker assembly RH
  • Front No. 3 speaker assembly RH
  • DCM (telematics transceiver)*
