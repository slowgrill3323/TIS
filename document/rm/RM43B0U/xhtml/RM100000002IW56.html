MONITOR DESCRIPTION

These DTCs indicate a problem with the park/neutral position switch assembly or the wire harness in the park/neutral position switch circuit.

The park/neutral position switch assembly detects the shift lever position and sends a signal to the TCM. For safety, the park/neutral position switch assembly detects the shift lever position so that the engine can be started only when the shift lever is in P or N.

The park/neutral position switch assembly sends a signal to the TCM according to the shift lever position (P, R, N or D).

The TCM determines that there is a problem with the switch or related parts if it receives more than 1 position signal simultaneously. The TCM will then illuminate the MIL and store a DTC.
