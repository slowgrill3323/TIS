NOTICE:

  • Keep the motor cable terminals and their installation area free from foreign matter, water, etc.
  • Do not touch the waterproof seal or terminals of the motor cable.

(1) Using an insulated tool, remove the 3 bolts and motor cable from the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly.

(2) Cover the hole where the motor cable was installed with tape or equivalent (non-residue type) to prevent entry of foreign matter.
