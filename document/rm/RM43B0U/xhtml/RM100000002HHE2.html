PRECAUTION

PRECAUTION FOR TRAILER CAMERA SYSTEM

(a) Precautions for the wireless camera

(1) Do not bend antenna masts other than the direction of the antenna hinges.

(2) Do not cause mechanical impacts to the wireless camera. If the wireless camera is dropped or impacted, replace it.

(3) After replacing the wireless camera, make sure to perform pairing.

(b) Precautions for the trailer camera ECU (communication telematics box)

(1) Do not touch any terminal pins of any ECU connector directly.

(2) Do not cause mechanical impacts to the communication telematics box. If the communication telematics box is dropped or impacted, replace it.

(3) Do not remove USB rubber cover from the communication telematics box unless performing firmware update via supplemental instruction provided at time of re-flash activity.

(4) After replacing the trailer camera ECU (communication telematics box), make sure to perform pairing.
