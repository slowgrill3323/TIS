Title: MIRROR (INT): DIGITAL REAR-VIEW MIRROR SYSTEM: FAIL-SAFE CHART; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

FAIL-SAFE CHART

PROTECTION CONTROL

(a) Inner rear view mirror assembly high internal temperature

(1) When the temperature inside the inner rear view mirror assembly is higher than the operating range, the inner rear view mirror assembly changes to protection mode and displays the high temperature indicator.

HINT:

When the temperature exceeds the operating range, the display gradually becomes darker, and if the temperature continues to rise, the display turns off.
