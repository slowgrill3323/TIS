(1) Remove the tire valve cap.

NOTICE:

Keep the removed tire valve cap.

(2) Remove the valve core to release the air from the tire.

NOTICE:

Make sure that a sufficient amount of air has been released.

(3) Using a tire remover, remove the tire from the disc wheel.

NOTICE:

  • Be careful not to damage the tire pressure warning valve and transmitter due to interference between the valve and the tire bead.
  • Set the tire remover shoe as shown in the illustration.

(4) Using a T10 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the bolt and tire pressure monitor sensor from the tubeless tire valve.

(5) Cut the tubeless tire valve and remove it by pulling from the outer side of the disc wheel as shown in the illustration.
