Push Up the Lever

(1) Coat 2 new O-rings with ATF, and install them to the 2 temperature sensors.

NOTICE:

Insert the O-rings vertically to prevent them from cracking or cutting.

(2) Coat a new O-ring with ATF, and install it to the transmission wire.

(3) Install the transmission wire to the automatic transmission case sub-assembly with the bolt.

Torque:

5.4 N·m {55 kgf·cm, 48 in·lbf}

(4) Connect the transmission wire connector.

HINT:

Push up the lever until the claw of the transmission wire connector makes a connection sound.

(5) Connect the reference connector shown in the illustration first, then connect the remaining 10 connectors.

NOTICE:

Make sure that the transmission wire is securely inserted into the clamp.

(6) Install the 2 temperature sensors and the 2 temperature sensor clamps to the transmission valve body assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}
