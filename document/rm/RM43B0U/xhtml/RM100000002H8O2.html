Operation not Accepted Even If Air Conditioning Switch is Operated

DESCRIPTION

If the air conditioning system cannot be operated using the air conditioning control assembly, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor

Air conditioning system cannot be operated using air conditioning control assembly

  • (With the exception of the switch indicators, no inputs are reflected when other switches operated)
  • LIN communication malfunction between air conditioning amplifier assembly and air conditioning control assembly
  • Air conditioning amplifier assembly malfunction (battery and ground circuit)
  • Air conditioning control assembly malfunction
