PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE EMBLEM

NOTICE:

When removing the radiator grille emblem, if the double-sided tape is difficult to remove, heat the adhesive of the radiator grille emblem using a heat light.

Standard Value:

Part

Temperature

Radiator Grille Emblem

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

NOTICE:

Do not heat the radiator grille emblem excessively.

Protective Tape

-

-

(1) Put protective tape around the radiator grille emblem.

(2) Heat the adhesive of the radiator grille emblem using a heat light.

(3) Remove the radiator grille emblem by the following procedure.

1. Insert piano wire between the vehicle body and radiator grille emblem.

2. Tie objects that can serve as handles (for example, wooden blocks) to the piano wire ends.

3. Pull the piano wire and scrape off the double-sided tape that holds the rear body radiator grille emblem to the vehicle body.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.
