2. REMOVE AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION EXTENSION HOUSING OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST, remove the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal from the extension housing sub-assembly.

09308-00010

NOTICE:

When removing the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal, make sure not to damage the output shaft or extension housing sub-assembly.

3. INSTALL AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION EXTENSION HOUSING OIL SEAL

(a) Coat the lip of a new automatic transmission extension housing oil seal with a small amount of MP grease.

(b) Using SST and hammer, install the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal to the extension housing sub-assembly.

09950-60011 09951-00460 09951-00550 09952-06010 09950-70010 09951-07150 09710-14013 09710-00021

Automatic Transmission Extension Housing Oil Seal Installation Depth:

3.3 to 3.9 mm (0.130 to 0.154 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Keep the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal lip free of foreign matter.
  • Do not install the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal at an angle.
  • When installing the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal to the extension housing sub-assembly, do not damage the automatic transmission extension housing oil seal.
