1. INSTALL REAR PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER KIT

HINT:

Use the same installation procedure for both the sleeve yoke side and flange yoke side spider bearings.

(1) Measure dimension A between the snap ring grooves.

(2) Install the 4 spider bearings to the spider journal portions, and then measure dimension B of the universal joint.

NOTICE:

When measuring dimension B, fix the spider and spider bearings in a vise and hold them firmly together.

(1) Coat the splines of the intermediate shaft with MP grease.

(2) Install the center support bearing to the intermediate shaft.

(3) Align the matchmarks on the joint yoke and intermediate shaft.

(4) Install the washer.

(5) Clamp the yoke in a vise, and press the bearing into position by installing a new lock nut.

(6) Loosen the lock nut.

(8) Using a chisel and hammer, stake the lock nut.
