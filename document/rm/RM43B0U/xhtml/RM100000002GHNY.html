ADJUSTMENT

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. ADJUST PARK/NEUTRAL POSITION SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(a) While pushing the shift lock release button, move the shift lever to N.

(b) Using SST, remove the bolt of the park/neutral position switch assembly.

09729-00120

(c) Apply adhesive to threads of the bolt.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1344, Three Bond 1344 or equivalent

(d) Align the neutral basic line with the groove as shown in the illustration.

*a

Neutral Basic Line

*b

Groove

(e) Hold the park/neutral position switch assembly in position and using SST install the bolt.

09729-00120

Torque:

Specified tightening torque

12.8 N*m (131 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)

HINT:

  • When using SST, make sure that SST is parallel to the torque wrench.
  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using a SST (fulcrum length of 200 mm (7.874 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 162 mm (6.3779 in.)):5.7 N*m (58 kgf*cm, 50 in.*lbf)

*a

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

(f) After the adjustment, perform the park/neutral position switch assembly operation check.
