DTC

C1AE287

Ultrasonic Sensor (Front Left Center) Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when an open circuit or short occurs in the communication line between the front center ultrasonic sensor LH and the front corner ultrasonic sensor LH, or when a malfunction occurs in the front center ultrasonic sensor LH.
