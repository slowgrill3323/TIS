PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL BRAKE ACTUATOR BRACKET CUSHION

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the brake actuator bracket cushions is necessary.

2. INSTALL NO. 1 BRAKE ACTUATOR CASE COLLAR

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the No. 1 brake actuator case collars is necessary.

NOTICE:

Make sure that the No. 1 brake actuator case collar is in full contact with the brake actuator bracket cushion.

Torque:

19 N·m {194 kgf·cm, 14 ft·lbf}

3. INSTALL BRAKE ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

  • Do not remove the hole plugs of a new brake actuator assembly before connecting the brake tubes because the brake actuator assembly is filled with brake fluid.
  • Do not hold the brake actuator assembly by the connector.
  • Do not drop the brake actuator assembly when carrying it.

(1) Temporarily install the brake actuator assembly to the brake actuator bracket assembly with the 3 bolts.

(2) Tighten the 3 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

6.5 N·m {66 kgf·cm, 58 in·lbf}

4. INSTALL NO. 3 BRAKE ACTUATOR BRACKET

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the No. 3 brake actuator bracket is necessary.

(1) Temporarily install the No. 3 brake actuator bracket to the body with the 2 bolts.

(2) Tighten the 2 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

19 N·m {194 kgf·cm, 14 ft·lbf}

5. INSTALL BRAKE ACTUATOR WITH BRACKET

NOTICE:

  • Do not kink or damage the brake tubes.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake tubes from the connecting parts.
  • Be careful not to allow any brake fluid to enter the connector.
  • Do not hold the brake actuator with bracket by the connector.

(1) Temporarily install the brake actuator with bracket to the body with the 3 nuts.

(2) Tighten the 3 nuts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

19 N·m {194 kgf·cm, 14 ft·lbf}

6. CONNECT BRAKE TUBE

NOTICE:

  • Do not kink or damage the brake tubes.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake tube from the connecting parts.

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

*h

Union Nut Wrench

(1) Temporarily tighten the 6 brake tubes to the correct position on the brake actuator assembly as shown in the illustration.

(2) Using a union nut wrench, fully tighten the 6 brake tubes.

Torque:

Specified tightening torque (A) :

15.2 N·m {155 kgf·cm, 11 ft·lbf}

Specified tightening torque (B) :

19.5 N·m {199 kgf·cm, 14 ft·lbf}

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using a union nut wrench (fulcrum length of 22 mm (0.866 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 162 mm (6.38 in.)) (A):

    13.4 N*m (137 kgf*cm, 10 ft.*lbf)

  • When using a union nut wrench (fulcrum length of 20 mm (0.787 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 162 mm (6.38 in.)) (B):

    17.4 N*m (177 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)

7. CONNECT ENGINE ROOM MAIN WIRE

NOTICE:

  • Make sure that the connector is locked securely.
  • Make sure that the actuator connector can be connected smoothly.
  • Do not allow water, oil or dirt to enter the connector.

9. CONNECT CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR SUB-ASSEMBLY (for Manual Transmission)

Torque:
