3. REMOVE PARK/NEUTRAL POSITION SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(1) Disconnect the park/neutral position switch assembly connector.

(2) Using a screwdriver, bend the tabs of the lock washer.

(3) Remove the nut and lock washer.

(4) Using SST, remove the bolt and park/neutral position switch assembly from the automatic transmission case sub-assembly.

09729-00120

HINT:

Make sure that the manual valve lever shaft has not been rotated prior to installing the park/neutral position switch assembly as the detent spring may become detached from the manual valve lever shaft.
