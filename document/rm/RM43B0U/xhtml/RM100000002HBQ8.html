2. DRAIN MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL

(a) Remove the manual transmission filler plug and gasket.

(b) Remove the drain plug sub-assembly and gasket and then drain the manual transmission oil.

(c) Install a new gasket and the drain plug sub-assembly.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

3. ADD MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL

(a) Add manual transmission oil until the oil level is within 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom of the manual transmission case plug opening.
