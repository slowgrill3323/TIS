PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FRONT SEAT CUSHION HEATER ASSEMBLY LH

*a

Hole

-

-

(1) Remove the release paper from the front seat cushion heater assembly LH and attach it to the separate type front seat cushion pad aligning with the 2 holes as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Do not reuse the front seat cushion heater assembly LH and separate type front seat cushion pad.
  • After removing the peeling paper, do not touch the surface of the tape with your fingers or cotton work gloves.
  • Apply pressure to paste the front seat cushion heater assembly LH to the separate type front seat cushion pad securely.
