PRECAUTION

PRECAUTION

(a) Do not replace with a normal absorber control ECU from another vehicle, as this may adversely affect other systems. When replacing the part, be sure to install a new one.

PRECAUTIONS FOR REPLACING OR REMOVING AND INSTALLING PART

(a) When the absorber control ECU is replaced with a new one, perform "Write ECU Software".

(b) When the absorber control ECU is replaced with a new one, perform "Load Specification Information".

(c) When the absorber control ECU is replaced with a new one, perform "Vertical Acceleration Sensor Signal Judgment".

(d) When the acceleration sensor assembly is replaced or removed and installed, perform "Vertical Acceleration Sensor Signal Judgment".
