PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL BLE DOOR DIGITAL KEY RECEIVER ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

If the BLE door digital key receiver assembly is dropped, replace it with a new one.

Cleaning Area

Double-sided Tape

(1) Clean the rear bumper surface.

1. Using a heat light, heat the double-sided tape remaining on the rear bumper surface.

Heating Temperature

Area

Temperature

Area

Temperature

Rear Bumper

40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)

-

-

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts, touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

NOTICE:

Do not heat the rear bumper excessively.

2. Remove any remaining double-sided tape from the rear bumper surface.

3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

(2) When reusing BLE door digital key receiver assembly, remove remaining double-sided tape from the BLE door digital key receiver assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Installing the ble indoor digital key receiver assembly with some double-sided tape remaining may cause poor adhesion. Perform this procedure until the tape is sufficiently removed.
  • Make sure to use a cloth when removing. Using a screwdriver, etc., may cause damage and poor adhesion.

(3) Adhere new double-sided tape to BLE door digital key receiver assembly.

(4) Engage the 2 guides to install the BLE door digital key receiver assembly.

HINT:

Press the BLE door digital key receiver assembly firmly to install it.

(5) Connect the connector.
