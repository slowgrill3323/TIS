Title: SUSPENSION CONTROL: ADAPTIVE VARIABLE SUSPENSION SYSTEM: C171512; Front Right Vertical Acceleration Sensor Circuit Circuit Short to Battery; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C171512

Front Right Vertical Acceleration Sensor Circuit Circuit Short to Battery

DESCRIPTION

The acceleration sensor assembly outputs an electric voltage corresponding to the vertical acceleration of the vehicle to the absorber control ECU.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

Note

C171512

Front Right Vertical Acceleration Sensor Circuit Circuit Short to Battery

Either condition is met:

  • The power source voltage of the acceleration sensor assembly RH is 5.5 V or higher continuously for 0.5 seconds
  • The output voltage of the acceleration sensor assembly RH is 4.7 V or higher continuously for 1 second
  • Acceleration sensor assembly RH
  • Wire harness or connector
  • Absorber control ECU
