PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT UPPER SUSPENSION ARM BUSHING

*1

Front Upper Suspension Arm Bushing

-

-

(1) Using a hammer and chisel, strike and bend the entire flange of the front upper suspension arm bushing as shown in the illustration.

(2) Using SST and a press, press out the front upper suspension arm bushing.

09613-26010 09710-20011 09710-06071 09710-28012

HINT:

Remove the rear side bushing using the same procedure as for the front side.

2. REMOVE FRONT UPPER BALL JOINT DUST COVER

(1) Using snap ring expander, remove the dust cover set ring and front upper ball joint dust cover from the front upper suspension arm assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the front upper ball joint dust cover.
  • If the front upper ball joint dust cover is damaged, replace it.
