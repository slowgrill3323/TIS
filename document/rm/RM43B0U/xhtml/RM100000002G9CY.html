3. REMOVE NO. 1 BLACK OUT TAPE LH

NOTICE:

When removing the No. 1 black out tape LH, if the double-sided tape is difficult to remove, heat the adhesive of the No. 1 black out tape LH using a heat light.

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.
