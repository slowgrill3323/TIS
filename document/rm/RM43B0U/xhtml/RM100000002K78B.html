ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. VISUALLY INSPECT HOSES, CONNECTIONS AND GASKETS

(a) Check that there is no damage and cracks on the hoses and gaskets, and that there are no air or oil leaks from connections.

HINT:

Cracked hoses and gaskets or loose connections may cause the engine to run improperly or stall.

2. INSPECT TURBOCHARGER SUB-ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Perform this procedure in a safe location with another person.

(a) Put the engine in Inspection Mode (Maintenance Mode).

Powertrain > Hybrid Control > Utility

Inspection Mode

(b) Start the engine and warm it up.

(c) Fully accelerate the vehicle with the shift lever in 2nd and measure the boost pressure when the engine speed is 4500 rpm (approximately 50 km/h (31 mph).

3. INSPECT WASTE GATE VALVE ACTUATOR

(a) Remove the No. 1 turbo insulator.

(b) Using a vacuum pump, apply a vacuum of 35 +/- 4.0 kPa (263 +/- 35 mmHg, 10 +/- 1.2 in.Hg) to the diaphragm chamber and check that the waste gate valve actuator link operates as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:
