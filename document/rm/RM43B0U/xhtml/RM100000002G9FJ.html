Install in this Direction (2)

(1) Insert the stop light switch assembly to the stop light switch mounting adjuster until the switch body slightly touches the brake pedal.

NOTICE:

  • When inserting the stop light switch assembly, make sure not to engage the brake pedal.
  • Operate it while supporting it with one hand so that the brake pedal does not move.

(2) Turn the stop light switch assembly 1/4 turn clockwise as shown in the illustration to install it.

Torque:

1.5 N·m {15 kgf·cm, 13 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

When inserting the stop light switch assembly, support the pedal from behind so that the pedal is not pushed in.

(3) Check the protrusion of the plunger.
