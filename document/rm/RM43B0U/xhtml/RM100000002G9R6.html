INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT WASHER MOTOR AND PUMP ASSEMBLY

HINT:

This check should be performed with the washer motor and pump assembly installed to the washer jar.

(a) Fill the washer jar with washer fluid.

(b) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the washer motor and check the operation of the washer motor.

OK:

Measurement Condition

Specified Condition

Auxiliary battery positive (+) → A36-1 (B)

Auxiliary battery negative (-) → A36-2 (SW)

Washer motor operation is normal

If the result is not as specified, replace the washer motor and pump assembly.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Washer Motor and Pump Assembly)
