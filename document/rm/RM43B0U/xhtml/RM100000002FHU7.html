C13807F

Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck Off

Either of the following is detected:

  • When the IGR terminal voltage exceeds 10 V and the +BS terminal voltage is from 9.5 to 17.4 V, stop light drive output (STPO) is ON, STP is OFF and STP2 is OFF continuously for 2 seconds or more.
  • When the IGR terminal voltage exceeds 10 V and the +BS terminal voltage is from 9.5 to 17.4 V, STP is OFF and STP2 is ON continuously for 5 seconds or more.
  • Wire harness and connector
  • Stop light switch assembly
  • STP circuit
  • STP2 circuit
  • Skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)
