B210096

Left PRB Motor Component Internal Failure

DTC

B210196

Right PRB Motor Component Internal Failure

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are output when a malfunction occurs in the side auto step motor assembly (power lift step assembly LH) or side auto step motor assembly (power lift step assembly RH) circuit.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • The power running board system uses the CAN communication system and LIN communication system. Inspect the communication function by following How to Proceed with Troubleshooting.

    Click here

  • When a side auto step controller ECU assembly is replaced, the side auto step controller ECU assembly must be initialized. Functions such as deploying/stowing and jam protection do not operate if the initialization is not performed.
