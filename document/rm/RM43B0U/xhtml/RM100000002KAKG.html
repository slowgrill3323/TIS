9. REMOVE UPPER INVERTER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to pull the upper inverter cover sub-assembly straight up, as a connector is connected to the bottom of the cover.
  • Do not touch the upper inverter cover sub-assembly waterproofing rubber.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter or water to enter the hybrid motor control inverter assembly.

13. DISCONNECT MOTOR CABLE

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

Protective Tape

-

-

(1) Disengage the clamp.

(2) Using an insulated tool, remove the 3 bolts (A).

(3) Using an insulated tool, remove the 2 bolts (B), and disconnect the motor cable from the hybrid motor control inverter assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow any foreign matter or water to enter the hybrid motor control inverter assembly.
  • Do not touch the connector waterproofing rubber or terminals.
  • Do not damage the terminals, connector housings or hybrid motor control inverter assembly during disconnection.
  • Insulate the disconnected terminals with insulating tape.
  • Cover the hole where the cable was connected with tape (non-residue type) or equivalent to prevent entry of foreign matter.

(4) Protect the openings of the hybrid motor control inverter assembly with protective tape (non-residue type) to prevent the entry of foreign matter or water.

21. REMOVE HYBRID MOTOR CONTROL INVERTER ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

Even after the coolant is drained, coolant remains in the hybrid motor control inverter assembly due to its internal structure. Therefore, plug or cover the pipes with a plastic bag when removing the hybrid motor control inverter assembly from the vehicle so that coolant does not spill out.

(1) Remove the 2 nuts and hybrid motor control inverter assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Since the hybrid motor control inverter assembly is very heavy, 2 people are needed to remove the hybrid motor control inverter assembly. When removing the hybrid motor control inverter assembly, do not damage the parts around it.
  • To prevent damage, do not hold the hybrid motor control inverter assembly by the connectors.
  • To prevent damage due to static electricity, do not touch the terminals of the disconnected connectors.
  • Cover the connectors with insulating tape to prevent foreign matter or water from entering.
  • Do not hold the pipe when removing the inverter.

HINT:

Even after the coolant is drained, coolant remains in the inverter due to its internal structure. Therefore, seal or cover the pipes when removing the inverter so that coolant does not spill out.

NOTICE:

  • Set the inverter in a position where the AMD connector will not be subjected to loads during work.
