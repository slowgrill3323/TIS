Remove in this Direction (2)

(1) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the claws as shown in the illustration to separate the washer nozzle sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the windshield glass.

(2) Disconnect the washer hose to remove the washer nozzle sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Washer nozzle sub-assemblies cannot be reused.
