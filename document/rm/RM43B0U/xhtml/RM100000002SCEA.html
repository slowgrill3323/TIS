PROCEDURE

1. DISPOSE OF REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Always use proper safety equipment and be careful when drilling because shards of metal may fly about.

HINT:

The gas is colorless, odorless and non-poisonous.

(a) Extend the piston rod and secure the rear shock absorber assembly at an angle in a vise.

(b) for Type A:

(1) Using a hacksaw, slowly make a hole at the position indicated by the arrow in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

(c) for Type B:

(1) Using a drill or similar tool, slowly make a hole at the position indicated by the arrow in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

(d) for Type C:

(1) Slowly remove the screw to discharge the gas inside. If the screw cannot be removed, using a drill or similar tool, slowly make a hole at the position indicated by the arrow in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

Screw

(e) for Type D:

(1) Using a drill or similar tool, slowly make a hole at the position indicated by the arrow in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.
