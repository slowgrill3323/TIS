INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Check the illumination.

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the rear combination light assembly LH and check that the light illuminates.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(X4)
 Click Connector(X4)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

X4-3 (TL) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

X4-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Taillight/marker illuminates

X4-2 (TRN) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

X4-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Stop/turn light illuminates

X4-4 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

X4-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Back up light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the rear combination light assembly LH.

Component without harness connected
