PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FRONT SUSPENSION LOWER ARM HOLE PLUG (w/ Hole Plug)

2. CONNECT FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT ATTACHMENT

*a

MP grease

-

-

(1) Apply MP grease to the locations shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not apply MP grease to the tapered or threaded parts of the ball joint.

(2) Connect the front lower ball joint attachment to the front lower No. 1 suspension arm sub-assembly.

(3) Install the nut and a new cotter pin.

Torque:

159 N·m {1621 kgf·cm, 117 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

If the holes for the cotter pin are not aligned, tighten the nut further up to 60°.

3. TEMPORARILY INSTALL FRONT LOWER NO. 1 SUSPENSION ARM SUB-ASSEMBLY

*1

Front No. 2 Suspension Camber Adjust Cam

*2

Front No. 1 Suspension Camber Adjust Cam

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Temporarily install the front lower No. 1 suspension arm sub-assembly, 2 front No. 2 suspension camber adjust cams and 2 front No. 1 suspension camber adjust cams with the 2 nuts.

(2) Align the matchmarks on the front No. 2 suspension camber adjust cam and front No. 1 suspension camber adjust cam with the matchmarks on the vehicle body.

4. INSTALL FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT ATTACHMENT

Torque:

300 N·m {3059 kgf·cm, 221 ft·lbf}
