Title: DRIVE SHAFT / PROPELLER SHAFT: PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY (for 4WD): INSPECTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

(a) w/o Center Support Bearing:

Using a dial indicator, check the rear propeller shaft assembly runout.

Maximum runout:

1.02 mm (0.0402 in.)

If the rear propeller shaft assembly runout is more than the maximum, replace the rear propeller shaft assembly.

(b) w/ Center Support Bearing (Intermediate Shaft Side):

Using a dial indicator, check the rear propeller shaft assembly runout.

Maximum runout:

0.61 mm (0.0240 in.)

If the rear propeller shaft assembly runout is more than the maximum, replace the rear propeller shaft assembly.

(c) w/ Center Support Bearing (Propeller Shaft Side):

Using a dial indicator, check the rear propeller shaft assembly runout.

Maximum runout:

0.61 mm (0.0240 in.)

If the rear propeller shaft assembly runout is more than the maximum, replace the rear propeller shaft assembly.

2. INSPECT SPIDER BEARING

(a) Check the spider bearings for wear or damage.

(b) w/o Center Support Bearing:

Check the axial play of each spider bearing by turning the yoke while holding the shaft tightly.

Maximum runout:

0 mm (0 in.)

If the bearing axial play is more than the maximum, replace the spider bearing.

(c) w/ Center Support Bearing:

Check the axial play of each spider bearing by turning the yoke while holding the shaft tightly.

Maximum runout:

0.05 mm (0.00197 in.)

If the bearing axial play is more than the maximum, replace the spider bearing.

3. INSPECT NO. 1 CENTER SUPPORT BEARING ASSEMBLY (w/ Center Support Bearing)

(a) Check that the bearing turns freely.

If the bearing is damaged, worn, or does not turn freely, replace it.
