Recirculated/Fresh Air Modes do not Switch

DESCRIPTION

If the air inlet mode cannot be changed between recirculation/fresh or exhaust gas odors can be smelled even though recirculation mode is selected, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor
  • Fresh/recirculation mode does not change
  • Fresh/recirculation mode does not change automatically
  • Exhaust gas odor when set to recirculation
  • Blower servo motor malfunction
  • Damper and link operation (jamming)
  • Blower assembly malfunction
  • High engine coolant temperature

PROCEDURE

HINT:
