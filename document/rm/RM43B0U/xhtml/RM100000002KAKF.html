PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL WIRE HARNESS CLAMP BRACKET

Torque:

7.0 N·m {71 kgf·cm, 62 in·lbf}

2. INSTALL NO. 6 ENGINE ROOM WIRE

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

3. INSTALL HYBRID MOTOR CONTROL INVERTER ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

(1) Temporarily install the hybrid motor control inverter assembly.

(2) Connect the engine room main wire with the 2 bolts and clamp.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(3) Install the hybrid inverter protector assembly with the bolt.

Torque:

9.0 N·m {92 kgf·cm, 80 in·lbf}

(4) Install the hybrid motor control inverter assembly with the 3 nuts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

21 N·m {214 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Since the hybrid motor control inverter assembly is very heavy, 2 people are needed to install the hybrid motor control inverter assembly. When installing the hybrid motor control inverter assembly, do not damage the parts around it.
  • To prevent damage, do not hold the hybrid motor control inverter assembly by the connectors.
  • To prevent damage due to static electricity, do not touch the terminals of the disconnected connectors.
  • Do not hold the pipe when installing the inverter.

4. CONNECT NO. 6 ENGINE ROOM WIRE

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

Torque:

7.6 N·m {77 kgf·cm, 67 in·lbf}

5. CONNECT NO. 2 INVERTER COOLING HOSE

*a

Retainer

*b

2.5 mm (0.098 in.) or less

Push

Push in

(1) Connect the No. 2 inverter cooling hose to the hybrid motor control inverter assembly and lock the hose with the retainer as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Push in the retainer until a click sound is heard.
  • Pull on the pipe to confirm that the pipe is securely connected.
  • To prevent foreign matter from entering the cooling system, do not remove the pieces of cloth or plastic bags from the pipe and disconnected hose until installation.

6. CONNECT NO. 1 INVERTER COOLING HOSE

*a

Retainer

*b

2.5 mm (0.098 in.) or less

Push

Push in

(1) Connect the No. 1 inverter cooling hose to the hybrid motor control inverter assembly and lock the hose with the retainer as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Push in the retainer until a click sound is heard.
  • Pull on the pipe to confirm that the pipe is securely connected.
  • To prevent foreign matter from entering the cooling system, do not remove the pieces of cloth or plastic bags from the pipe and disconnected hose until installation.

7. CONNECT ENGINE ROOM MAIN WIRE

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

Install in this Direction (1)

Install in this Direction (2)

(1) Connect the engine room main wire to the hybrid motor control inverter assembly and lock the connector with the lock lever as shown in the illustration.

(2) Connect the connector.

(3) Install the bolt.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

8. INSTALL MOTOR CABLE BRACKET

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

9. CONNECT FLOOR UNDER WIRE

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

(1) Temporarily install the floor under wire to the hybrid motor control inverter assembly with the 4 bolts.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow any foreign matter or water to enter the hybrid motor control inverter assembly.
  • Do not touch the connector waterproofing rubber or terminals.
  • Do not damage the terminals, connector housings or hybrid motor control inverter assembly when connecting them.

(2) Using an insulated tool, tighten the 2 bolts (A).

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(3) Using an insulated tool, tighten the 2 bolts (B).

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

10. CONNECT MOTOR CABLE

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

(1) Temporarily install the motor cable to the hybrid motor control inverter assembly with the 5 bolts.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow any foreign matter or water to enter the hybrid motor control inverter assembly.
  • Do not touch the connector waterproofing rubber or terminals.
  • Do not damage the terminals, connector housings or hybrid motor control inverter assembly when connecting them.

(2) Using an insulated tool, tighten the 2 bolts (A).

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(3) Using an insulated tool, tighten the 3 bolts (B).

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(4) Engage the clamp.

12. INSTALL INVERTER TERMINAL COVER

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

  • Visually confirm that the inverter terminal cover waterproofing rubber is securely installed before installing the inverter terminal cover.
  • Do not touch the inverter terminal cover waterproofing rubber.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

13. INSTALL UPPER INVERTER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

  • Visually confirm that the upper inverter cover sub-assembly waterproofing rubber is securely installed before installing the upper inverter cover sub-assembly.
  • Do not touch the upper inverter cover sub-assembly waterproofing rubber.
  • Make sure that the interlock is fully engaged.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}
