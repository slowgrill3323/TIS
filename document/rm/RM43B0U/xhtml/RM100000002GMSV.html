PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 TURBO PRESSURE SENSOR

(1) Disconnect the No. 1 turbo pressure sensor connector.

(2) Using an 8 mm socket wrench, remove the bolt and No. 1 turbo pressure sensor from the intake manifold.

NOTICE:

If the No. 1 turbo pressure sensor has been struck or dropped, replace it.

2. REMOVE NO. 2 TURBO PRESSURE SENSOR

(1) Disconnect the No. 2 turbo pressure sensor connector.

(2) Using an 8 mm socket wrench, remove the bolt and No. 2 turbo pressure sensor from the intercooler assembly.

NOTICE:

If the No. 2 turbo pressure sensor has been struck or dropped, replace it.
