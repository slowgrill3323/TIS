Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (for Gasoline Model): C004013; Brake Pedal Switch "A" Circuit Open; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C004013

Brake Pedal Switch "A" Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

The brake pedal load sensing switch signal turns on when the brake pedal is depressed with a force exceeding a predetermined level.

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) uses this circuit to detect if the brake pedal is depressed or not.

C004013

Brake Pedal Switch "A" Circuit Open

When the +BS terminal voltage is from 9.5 to 17.4 V, an open or short in the brake pedal load sensing switch continues for 0.3 seconds or more.

  • Wire harness and connector
