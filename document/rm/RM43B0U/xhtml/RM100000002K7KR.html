PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL NO. 1 COOLER THERMISTOR

Click here

2. INSTALL NO. 1 COOLER EVAPORATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove the vinyl tape from the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly and then apply a sufficient amount of compressor oil to the fitting surface with the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

(2) Install the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly to the heater case.

NOTICE:

When the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly is removed, make sure to install a new one. The No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly cannot be reused.

(3) Attach the clamp.

*a

No. 1 Cooler Thermistor Lead Wire

-

-

(1) While inserting the No. 1 cooler thermistor into the guide, insert the guides and attach the claws as shown in the illustration.

(2) Install the heater case with the 2 screws.

3. INSTALL SPRING NUT

4. INSTALL NO. 1 AIR CONDITIONING RADIATOR DAMPER SERVO SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Align the direction of the fitting on the heater case to the fitting on the No. 1 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.

(2) Align the guide and install the No. 1 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly with the 2 screws.

5. INSTALL NO. 3 AIR CONDITIONING RADIATOR DAMPER SERVO SUB-ASSEMBLY

*a

Reference Points

-

-

(1) Connect the 3 links of the No. 3 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly to the 3 links of the air conditioner unit assembly as shown in the illustration.

(2) Align with the reference points on the gear as shown in the illustration.

(3) Install the No. 3 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly with the 5 screws.

6. INSTALL NO. 1 AIR CONDITIONING RADIATOR DAMPER SERVO SUB-ASSEMBLY (for Dual Type)

(1) Align the direction of the fitting on the heater case to the fitting on the No. 1 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.

(2) Align the guide and install the No. 1 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly with the 2 screws.

7. INSTALL AIR CONDITIONING HARNESS ASSEMBLY

8. INSTALL COOLER EXPANSION VALVE

Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) Install the cooler expansion valve using the following procedure.

1. Remove the vinyl tape from the cooler expansion valve.

2. Install the cooler expansion valve.

NOTICE:

Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)

9. INSTALL AIR CONDITIONING TUBE AND ACCESSORY ASSEMBLY

(1) Install the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly using the following procedure.

1. Remove the vinyl tape from the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly and then apply a sufficient amount of compressor oil to 2 new O-rings and the fitting surface of the cooler expansion valve and air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

2. Install the 2 O-rings to the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-rings and O-ring fitting surfaces free of foreign matter.

3. Using a 4 mm hexagon socket wrench, install the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly with the 2 hexagon bolts.

Torque:

3.5 N·m {36 kgf·cm, 31 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)

10. INSTALL PACKING

(1) Install a new packing.

NOTICE:

Completely remove the remaining packing from the attachment surface during removal.
