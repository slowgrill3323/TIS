1. INSTALL REAR HOOD MOULDING LH

NOTICE:

When installing the rear hood moulding LH, heat the rear hood moulding LH and hood panel using a heat light.

Standard Value:

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

(1) Clean the surface of the hood panel.

1. Remove any remaining double-sided tape from the hood panel.

2. Using a heat light, heat the hood panel surface.

3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

NOTICE:

  • Installing the rear hood moulding LH with some double-sided tape remaining may cause poor adhesion. Perform this procedure until the tape is sufficiently removed.
  • Make sure to use a cloth when removing. Using a screwdriver, etc., may cause damage and poor adhesion.

(2) Using a heat light, heat the hood panel and a new rear hood moulding LH.

(3) Attach the double-sided tape and install the rear hood moulding LH as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Apply pressure so that it does not lift up from the hood panel.
