PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL LOWER NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY

*a

Turns off

-

-

(1) Check that the ignition switch is off.

(2) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

  • Wait at least 60 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

Remove in this Direction

-

-

(1) Attach the claw and install the lower No. 2 instrument panel airbag assembly to the lower No. 2 instrument panel assembly.

(2) Attach the claw and guide as shown in the illustration.

(3) Install the lower No. 2 instrument panel airbag assembly with the 3 bolts.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

(4) Install the lower No. 2 instrument panel assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

7.5 N·m {76 kgf·cm, 66 in·lbf}

(5) Open the lower No. 2 instrument panel assembly to install the 3 screws.

(6) Connect the airbag connector and clamp.

NOTICE:

  • When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.
  • Make sure the lock button is securely locked.

HINT:

Refer to How to Connect or Disconnect Airbag Connector: Click here
