4. REMOVE ACCELERATOR PEDAL (WITH SENSOR) ROD ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

  • Avoid physical shock to the accelerator pedal (with sensor) rod assembly.
  • If the accelerator pedal (with sensor) rod assembly has been struck or dropped, replace it.
  • Do not disassemble the accelerator pedal (with sensor) rod assembly.
  • The accelerator pedal (with sensor) rod assembly does not require lubrication.
  • Do not apply oil or other lubricants to the accelerator pedal (with sensor) rod assembly. If applied, the accelerator pedal (with sensor) rod assembly must be replaced.
