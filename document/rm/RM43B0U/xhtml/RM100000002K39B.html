Front Window Fogging

DESCRIPTION

If the windshield frequently fogs up even though the air conditioning system is operating, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor

Front glass fogs up

  • (Does not cool)
  • Compressor with motor assembly malfunction
  • Insufficient refrigerant gas volume
  • Cooler expansion valve (opened excessively)
  • DEF servo motor malfunction
  • Damper and link operation (jamming)
