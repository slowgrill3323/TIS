Title: SUSPENSION CONTROL: ADAPTIVE VARIABLE SUSPENSION SYSTEM: C173214; Front Left Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C173214

Front Left Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

DESCRIPTION

The front absorber control actuator LH (front shock absorber assembly LH) switches the damping force of the absorber based on an output signal from the absorber control ECU.

DTC No.

DTC Detection Condition

Note

C173214

Front Left Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Ground or Open

Either condition is met:

  • An open circuit is detected between the front absorber control actuator LH (front shock absorber assembly LH) and absorber control ECU.
  • A short circuit is detected between the front absorber control actuator LH (front shock absorber assembly LH) and absorber control ECU.
  • Front absorber control actuator LH (front shock absorber assembly LH)
  • Wire harness or connector
  • Front speed sensor LH
  • Absorber control ECU
