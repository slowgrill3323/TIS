ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT STEERING WHEEL FREE PLAY

Pre-procedure1

(a) for Gasoline Model:

Start the engine and make sure that the vehicle is in a state in which the power steering can operate.

(b) except Gasoline Model:

Turn the ignition switch to ON (READY) so that the power steering is ready to operate.

(c) Stop the vehicle and align the front wheels facing straight ahead.

Procedure1

(d) Gently turn the steering wheel right and left, and check for steering wheel free play.

Post-procedure1

(e) If the free play exceeds the maximum, check the steering system.
