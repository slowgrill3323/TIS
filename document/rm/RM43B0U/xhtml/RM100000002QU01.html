(a) for LH side:

Remove in this Direction

-

-

(1) Disconnect the 2 connectors.

(2) Disconnect the 5 connectors.

(3) Disconnect the 3 connectors and remove the engine wire in the direction shown in the illustration while pressing the 2 claws.

(4) Remove the nut and disconnect the clamp.

(5) Disconnect the connector.

CAUTION:

Make sure to wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

Insulate the disconnected terminals and connector with insulating tape.

(1) Disconnect the 2 connectors, remove the 3 nuts, and disconnect the 4 claws.

(2) Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the 4 claws.

(1) Remove the nut, disconnect the 2 claws and disconnect the engine wire.

(2) Disconnect the connector, remove the 2 nuts, and disconnect the 4 claws.

(3) Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the 4 claws.

86. REMOVE ENGINE ASSEMBLY

(a) Attach an engine sling device and hanger the engine with a chain block.

(b) Remove the 4 bolts.

(c) Remove the engine assembly by operating the engine sling device and chain block.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure that the engine is clear of all wiring and hoses.
  • While lowering the engine from the vehicle, do not allow it to contact the vehicle.
