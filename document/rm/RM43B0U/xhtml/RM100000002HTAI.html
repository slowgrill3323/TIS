DTC CHECK / CLEAR

CHECK DTC

(a) DTCs which are stored in the ECM can be output to the GTS.

(1) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine / Trouble Codes.

Powertrain > Engine > Trouble Codes

(2) Confirm the DTCs and freeze frame data, and then write them down.

(3) Confirm the details of the DTCs.
