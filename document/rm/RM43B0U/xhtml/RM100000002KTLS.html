CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

w/ Front Camera:

NOTICE:

When replacing the windshield glass of a vehicle equipped with a forward recognition camera, make sure to use a Toyota genuine part. If a non-Toyota genuine part is used, the forward recognition camera may not be able to be installed due to a missing bracket. Also the dynamic radar cruise control system, front camera system, lane tracing assist system, road sign assist system, pre-collision system or automatic high beam system may not operate properly due to a difference in the transmissivity or black ceramic border.

PROCEDURE

1. CLEAN WINDSHIELD GLASS

*a

When reusing the windshield glass

-

-

(1) When reusing the windshield glass:

1. Using a scraper, grind off the adhesive and double-sided tape left on the windshield glass.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the windshield glass.

(2) Using non-residue solvent, clean the outer edge of the windshield glass.

NOTICE:

Be sure to clean the windshield glass even if it is new.

(a) When reusing the windshield glass:

NOTICE:

6. INSTALL WINDSHIELD GLASS

Adhesive

(1) Follow the procedure below to clean the installation surface of the vehicle body.

1. If the adhesive left on the installation surface of the vehicle body is significantly uneven, smooth it out using a utility knife, etc.

Standard Dimension:

NOTICE:

2. Using non-residue solvent, clean the installation surface of the vehicle body.

*a

Matchmarks

*b

Suction Cups

(1) Follow the procedure below to perform work before installing the windshield glass.

1. Install the suction cups to the windshield glass.

2. Temporarily place the windshield glass on the body and position it so that the gap between the body and the edge of the windshield glass is the same width at the top and sides.

3. Attach adhesive tape on the windshield glass and body panel, and place matchmarks for installation using a pen (when using a new windshield glass).

NOTICE:

  • From inside the cabin, check that the No. 1 windshield glass stopper and the No. 2 windshield glass stopper can be attached.
  • When reusing the windshield glass, check and correct the matchmark positions.
  • Do not pinch the windshield deicer wire harness between the body and the windshield glass. (w/ Windshield Deicer)

4. Remove the windshield glass.

5. Apply body primer to the exposed sections of the body surface on the vehicle side (where no adhesive remains).

NOTICE:

  • When applying the primer, take care of the panel joints and spot welds.
  • Do not apply to the adhesive.
  • Do not apply too much body primer.

*a

(1) Follow the procedure below to perform windshield glass installation.

1. Using the suction cups, align the matchmarks and install the windshield glass.

NOTICE:

  • After the body side primer dries (in approximately 3 minutes), install the windshield glass.
  • From inside the cabin, check that the No. 1 windshield glass stopper and the No. 2 windshield glass stopper are attached.
  • Do not pinch the windshield deicer wire harness between the body and the windshield glass. (w/ Windshield Deicer)

2. Gently push the front surface of the windshield glass from outside of vehicle to fully contact with the adhesive.

HINT:

Pushing force: 98 N (10 kgf) or higher

3. Remove any excess adhesive or apply as necessary.

NOTICE:

Apply adhesive up to the edge of the windshield glass.

4. Remove any excess adhesive before it hardens.

5. Using protective tape, hold the outside moulding and glass in place until the adhesive hardens.

NOTICE:

Follow the instructions for each adhesive manufacturer or the corresponding owner's manual for the minimum amount of time necessary for drying and prior to driving the vehicle after applying adhesive.

(g) w/ Windshield Deicer:

(1) Connect the connector.

NOTICE:

Do not twist the wire harness during installation when connecting the connector.

7. CHECK FOR LEAK AND REPAIR

(1) Follow the procedure below to check for water leak and repair.

1. Remove the protective tape.

2. After the adhesive has hardened, apply water from outside the vehicle and check for leaks from in the cabin.

3. If water leaks into the cabin, seal any leaks with adhesive after the water has dried.
