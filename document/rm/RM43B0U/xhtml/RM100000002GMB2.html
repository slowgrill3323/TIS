Title: SEAT: SEAT HEATER SYSTEM: OPERATION CHECK; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

OPERATION CHECK

CHECK FRONT SEAT HEATER

(a) for Front Left Side:

(1) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(2) Operate the seat heater switch (multi-display*1 or air conditioning controller assembly) on the left side to switch the seat heater operation in order from "AUTO" → "HI" → "MID" → "LO" → "OFF" and check that the heater level indicator illuminates or turns off.

  • *1: for Multi-display 14 Inch Type

(3) Check that the seat surface heat changes according to the level indicator light illumination condition when the seat heater switch is operated.

(4) Check that the level indicator light switches from HI → MID → LO and that the seat surface heat changes according to the air conditioning temperature setting when the refreshing seat switch is operated with the seat heater set to AUTO.

HINT:

  • The seat heater operates when set to heating, and the seat heater operates when set to cooling.
  • The surface warmth of the seat changes even according to the outside temperature. Therefore, check with the outside temperature at a constant temperature.

(b) for Front Right Side:

(1) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(2) Operate the seat heater switch (multi-display*1 or air conditioning controller assembly) on the right side to switch the seat heater operation in order from "AUTO" → "HI" → "MID" → "LO" → "OFF" and check that the heater level indicator illuminates or turns off.

(3) Check that the seat surface heat changes according to the level indicator light illumination condition when the seat heater switch is operated.

(4) Check that the level indicator light switches from HI → MID → LO and that the seat surface heat changes according to the air conditioning temperature setting when the refreshing seat switch is operated with the seat heater set to AUTO.

HINT:

  • The seat heater operates when set to heating, and the seat heater operates when set to cooling.
  • The surface warmth of the seat changes even according to the outside temperature. Therefore, check with the outside temperature at a constant temperature.
