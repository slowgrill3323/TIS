Horizontally Insert a Thin-bladed Screwdriver

(1) Press the claw of the power distribution box assembly as shown in the illustration and release the lock of the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU).

(2) With the lock released, insert a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape horizontally in between the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) and the power distribution box assembly.

NOTICE:

Use a screwdriver with a diameter between 5.0 mm (0.197 in.) and 6.3 mm (0.248 in.) and a length of approximately 90 mm (3.54 in.).

(3) Using the screwdriver, carefully raise the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) to the position where the connector becomes disconnected.

NOTICE:

  • Do not pry and lift with the screwdriver.
  • Replace the power distribution box assembly or the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) when the connector terminal, locking section or case is damaged or deformed.

(4) Lift up the rib of the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) and slide the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) to remove it as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not touch the ECU connector terminal with your fingers.
