PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER KIT

HINT:

Use the same procedure for both spiders.

*a

Matchmark

*b

Turn

*c

Hold

*d

Hammering Point

(1) Place matchmarks on the flange yoke and propeller shaft.

(2) Using a brass bar and hammer, slightly tap in the spider bearing outer races.

(3) Using needle-nose pliers, remove the 4 snap rings from the grooves.

(4) Using SST, remove the spider bearing from the front propeller shaft assembly.

09332-25010

HINT:

Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.

(5) Tap off the spider bearing with a hammer.

NOTICE:

Do not tap the shaft.

HINT:

Remove the spider bearing on the opposite side using the same procedure.

(6) Remove the flange yoke from the front propeller shaft assembly.

(7) Install the 2 removed spider bearing outer races to the spider.

(8) Using SST, remove the spider bearing from the flange yoke.

09332-25010

HINT:

Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.

(9) Tap off the spider bearing with a hammer.

(10) Remove the spider.
