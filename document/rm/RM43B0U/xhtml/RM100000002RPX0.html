Title: HYBRID / BATTERY CONTROL: HYBRID CONTROL SYSTEM (w/o 130A Generator): P0C7396; Motor Electronics Coolant Pump "A" Component Internal Failure; 2025 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [12/2024 -        ]

DTC

P0C7396

Motor Electronics Coolant Pump "A" Component Internal Failure

DTC SUMMARY

MALFUNCTION DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) is malfunctioning. The cause of this malfunction may be one of the following:

Inverter water pump assembly (with motor) malfunction

  • Inverter water pump assembly (with motor) internal malfunction
  • Open or short in wire harness
  • Improperly connected connector

Hybrid cooling system malfunction

  • Foreign matter in inverter water pump assembly (with motor)
  • Hybrid cooling system coolant is frozen

HINT:

If this DTC is stored, the driving torque may be restricted.

DESCRIPTION

The inverter water pump assembly (with motor) transmits rotation speed information to the hybrid vehicle control ECU. The hybrid vehicle control ECU monitors the speed and detects malfunctions.

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

The hybrid vehicle control ECU monitors speed of the inverter water pump assembly (with motor). If there is an abnormality in speed, the hybrid vehicle control ECU will illuminate the MIL and store a DTC.

  1. Connect the GTS to the DLC3.
  2. Turn the ignition switch to ON and turn the GTS on.
  3. Clear the DTCs (even if no DTCs are stored, perform the clear DTC procedure).
  4. Turn the ignition switch off and wait for 2 minutes or more.
  5. Turn the ignition switch to ON and turn the GTS on.
  6. Turn the ignition switch to ON (READY). [*1]
  7. Wait for 2 minutes or more. [*2]

    [*1] to [*2] : Normal judgment procedure.

  8. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Hybrid Control / Utility / All Readiness.
  9. Check the DTC judgment result.

    HINT:

    • If the judgment result shows NORMAL, the system is normal.
    • If the judgment result shows ABNORMAL, the system has a malfunction.
    • If the judgment result shows INCOMPLETE, perform the normal judgment procedure again.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

If this vehicle is jump started, etc. and excessive voltage is applied to the auxiliary battery, the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) may suspend control as a self-protection function and store DTCs.

(When the auxiliary battery voltage returns to normal, the inverter water pump assembly (with motor) will resume normal operation. In this case it is not necessary to replace the inverter water pump assembly (with motor)).
