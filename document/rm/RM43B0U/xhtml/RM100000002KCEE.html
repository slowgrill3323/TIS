PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FLOOR SIDE AIRBAG SENSOR LH

HINT:

The illustrations are representative examples, and details may differ.

*a

Turns off

-

-

(1) Check that the ignition switch is off.

(2) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

  • Wait at least 60 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

(1) Attach the claw to hold the floor side airbag sensor LH in place.

NOTICE:

  • If the floor side airbag sensor LH has been dropped, or there are any cracks, dents or other defects in the case or connector, replace it with a new one.
  • When installing the floor side airbag sensor LH, make sure that the wire harness does not interfere with or is not pinched between other parts.
  • Make sure that the floor side airbag sensor LH claw is securely inserted into the body hole.

(2) Install the floor side airbag sensor LH with the bolt.

Torque:

9.0 N·m {92 kgf·cm, 80 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Tighten the bolt while holding the floor side airbag sensor LH because the floor side airbag sensor LH claw is easily damaged.

(3) Check that there is no looseness in the installation parts of the floor side airbag sensor LH.

(4) Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.
