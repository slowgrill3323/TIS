NOTICE:

When replacing the windshield glass of a vehicle equipped with a forward recognition camera, make sure to use a Toyota genuine part. If a non-Toyota genuine part is used, the forward recognition camera may not be able to be installed due to a missing bracket. Also the dynamic radar cruise control system, front camera system, lane tracing assist system, road sign assist system, pre-collision system or automatic high beam system may not operate properly due to a difference in the transmissivity or black ceramic border.

14. REMOVE WINDSHIELD GLASS

NOTICE:

  • The windshield glass may fall during work. Therefore, use suction cups to hold the windshield glass from outside the vehicle.
  • Be careful not to damage the windshield glass when cutting as the No. 1 windshield glass stopper or No. 2 windshield glass stopper are installed to the windshield glass.
  • When removing the windshield glass from the body, do not damage the windshield deicer wire harness. (When reusing the windshield glass with windshield deicer)

HINT:

Depending on the vehicle, 1-piece type or 2-piece type windshield glass stoppers may be installed.

Piano Wire

-

-

(1) When using a piano wire, perform the following procedure.

1. Pass the piano wire (0.6 mm is appropriate) and install both ends on the mighty puller as shown in the illustration.

2. Tie objects that can serve as handles (for example, wooden blocks) to both wire ends.

3. Pull the piano wire back and forth and cut through the adhesive of the windshield glass leaving the windshield glass stopper.

NOTICE:

  • When working near the instrument panel, place a plastic sheet so that the piano wire does not damage the instrument panel.
  • When working near the curtain shield airbag, be careful not to damage it with the piano wire. (w/ Curtain Shield Airbag)
  • Do not forcefully allow the piano wire to come to contact with the edges of the windshield glass.
  • Be careful as the piano wire will break if it crosses itself.
