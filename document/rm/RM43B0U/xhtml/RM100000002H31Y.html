2. REMOVE TRANSFER ADAPTER OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST, remove the transfer adapter oil seal from the transfer adapter rear.

09308-00010

NOTICE:

When removing the transfer adapter oil seal, make sure not to damage the output shaft or transfer adapter rear.

3. INSTALL TRANSFER ADAPTER OIL SEAL

(a) Coat the lip of a new transfer adapter oil seal with a small amount of MP grease.

(b) Using SST and hammer, install the transfer adapter oil seal to the transfer adapter rear.

09950-60011 09951-00650 09950-70010 09951-07150

Transfer Adapter Oil Seal Installation Depth:

0 to 0.6 mm (0 to 0.0236 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Keep the transfer adapter oil seal lip free of foreign matter.
  • Do not install the transfer adapter oil seal at an angle.
  • When installing the transfer adapter oil seal to the transfer adapter rear, do not damage the transfer adapter oil seal.

*a

0 to 0.6 mm (0 to 0.0236 in.)
