B00D512

Restraint System Passenger Disable Indicator Circuit Short to Battery
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a short circuit +B signal in the passenger airbag ON/OFF indicator "OFF" circuit when the airbag ECU assembly sends an "ON" request signal to the "OFF" indicator in the passenger airbag ON/OFF indicator.
  • Passenger airbag OFF indicator malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
  • Harness or connector
  • Passenger airbag OFF indicator (Map light assembly)
  • Airbag ECU assembly
