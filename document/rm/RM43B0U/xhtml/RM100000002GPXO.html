PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT STEERING COLUMN ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. ALIGN FRONT WHEELS FACING STRAIGHT AHEAD

3. INSTALL STEERING COLUMN ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

Make sure that the wire harness is not interfering with the steering column assembly.

HINT:

As the illustration shown is an example, the actual details may differ.

(1) Install the steering column assembly with the bolt and 2 nuts.

Torque:

36 N·m {367 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • After temporarily tightening the bolt, fully tighten the 2 nuts then fully tighten the bolt.
  • After the column is installed, check the tilt and telescopic operation.
  • If a large amount of force is required to operate the tilt and telescopic mechanism or abnormal noise occurs, loosen the nuts and move the bracket forward or backward until the tilt and telescopic operation works smoothly.

    Tighten the nuts and check the tilt and telescopic operation again.

(2) Engage each wire harness clamp to the steering column assembly.

(3) for Manual Transmission:

Connect the connector from the steering lock actuator with ECU assembly.

4. TEMPORARILY INSTALL STEERING INTERMEDIATE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

(2) Install the steering intermediate shaft assembly to the steering column assembly.

(3) Temporarily install the bolt.

13. INSTALL TURN SIGNAL SWITCH ASSEMBLY WITH SPIRAL CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

  • Do not remove/install the spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly with the auxiliary battery connected and the ignition switch ON.
  • Do not rotate the spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly without the steering wheel assembly installed, with the auxiliary battery connected and the ignition switch ON.
  • Ensure that the steering wheel assembly is installed and aligned straight when inspecting the steering sensor.
