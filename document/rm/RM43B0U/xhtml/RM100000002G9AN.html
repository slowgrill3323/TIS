HINT:

  • If the rear body tailgate assembly is damaged or there is a history of body repairs, the installation area of the rear body tailgate assembly may be deformed and the fitting position of the rear body tailgate assembly may deviate from the reference value ranges. Therefore, visually check the rear body tailgate assembly installation area (such as the hinge and lock) for dents and warping.
  • If an abnormality is found in the visual check, check the fitting position and adjust the installation area according to the check results.
