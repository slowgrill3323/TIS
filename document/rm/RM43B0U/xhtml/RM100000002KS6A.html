3. REMOVE CRANK POSITION SENSOR

(1) Disconnect the crank position sensorconnector.

(2) Using an 8 mm socket wrench, remove the bolt and crank position sensorfrom the cylinder block sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

If the crank position sensorhas been struck or dropped, replace it.

(3) Perform this procedure only when reusing the crankshaft position sensor.

  1. Remove the O-ring from the crankshaft position sensor.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to only remove the O-ring with bare hands.
  • When removing the O-ring, be careful not to damage the O-ring groove of the crankshaft position sensor.
  • If the O-ring groove of the crank position sensoris damaged, replace the crank position sensorwith a new one.
