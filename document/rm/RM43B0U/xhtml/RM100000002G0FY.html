PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL NO. 1 VACUUM PUMP O-RING

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when reusing the vacuum pump assembly.

2. INSTALL VACUUM PUMP ASSEMBLY

*a

Coupling Teeth

*b

Groove

*c

8 mm Deep Socket Wrench

*d

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

Push in this Direction

Install in this Direction

(1) Connect the vacuum pump connector to the vacuum pump assembly, and then push the vacuum pump connector until the retainer passes the protrusion of the vacuum pump assembly tube.

NOTICE:

Check that there is no foreign matter on the connecting parts.

HINT:

This is done to prevent the vacuum pump connector from interfering with the fuel pump spacer when installing the vacuum pump assembly.

(2) Install the vacuum pump assembly so that the coupling teeth of the vacuum pump assembly and groove of the camshaft are engaged.

NOTICE:

  • Ensure that the vacuum pump assembly is installed securely.
  • Be careful not to pinch the No. 1 vacuum pump O-ring.

(3) Using an 8 mm deep socket wrench and SST, install the vacuum pump assembly with the 3 bolts.

09961-00950

Torque:

Specified tightening torque :

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Perform the procedure by fully tightening the bolt (A), and then fully tightening the bolts (B).
  • After installation, check that there are no gaps between the matching surfaces and that the vacuum pump assembly is not installed at an angle.

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using SST (fulcrum length of 150 mm (5.91 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 162 mm (6.38 in.)):

    5.2 N*m (53 kgf*cm, 46 in.*lbf)

Install in this Direction

(1) Push the vacuum pump connector until the retainer passes the protrusion of the vacuum pump assembly tube, and then push in the retainer.

NOTICE:

After connecting the vacuum pump connector, check that the vacuum pump assembly and vacuum pump connector are securely connected by pulling on them.

(2) Engage the clamp to install the No. 2 vacuum hose assembly.
