DTC

B27C487

Front Passenger Side BLE Antenna Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when a LIN communication malfunction between the digital key ECU assembly and front door outside handle assembly RH (BLE door digital key receiver assembly) is detected.
