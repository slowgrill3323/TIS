2. REMOVE TRANSMISSION CASE OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST, remove the transmission case oil seal from the automatic transmission case sub-assembly.

09308-00010

NOTICE:

When removing the transmission case oil seal, make sure not to damage the output shaft or automatic transmission case sub-assembly.

3. INSTALL TRANSMISSION CASE OIL SEAL

(a) Coat the lip of a new transmission case oil seal with a small amount of MP grease.

(b) Using SST and hammer, install the transmission case oil seal to the automatic transmission case sub-assembly.

09950-60011 09951-00650 09950-70010 09951-07150

Transmission Case Oil Seal Installation Depth:

0 to 0.6 mm (0 to 0.0236 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Keep the transmission case oil seal lip free of foreign matter.
  • Do not install the transmission case oil seal at an angle.
  • When installing the transmission case oil seal to the automatic transmission case sub-assembly, do not damage the transmission case oil seal.

*a

0 to 0.6 mm (0 to 0.0236 in.)
