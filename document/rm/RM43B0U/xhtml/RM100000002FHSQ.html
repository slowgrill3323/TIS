Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (for Gasoline Model): C14D71C; Multi-axis Acceleration Sensor Module "A" Supply Voltage Circuit Voltage Out of Range; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C14D71C

Multi-axis Acceleration Sensor Module "A" Supply Voltage Circuit Voltage Out of Range

DESCRIPTION

The airbag ECU assembly has a built-in yaw rate and acceleration sensor and detects the vehicle condition.

This DTC is stored when the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) receives a sensor supply voltage malfunction signal from the acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly).

This DTC may be stored due to an intermittent low power source voltage.
