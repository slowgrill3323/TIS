Title: MAINTENANCE: DIFFERENTIAL OIL: REPLACEMENT; 2024 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 - 03/2024]

REPLACEMENT

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. DRAIN DIFFERENTIAL OIL

(a) Stop the vehicle on a level place.

(b) for Front Differential:

(1) Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, remove the front differential filler plug and gasket.

(2) Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, remove the front differential drain plug and gasket, and drain the oil.

(3) Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, install a new gasket and the front differential drain plug.

Torque:

64.7 N·m {660 kgf·cm, 48 ft·lbf}

(c) for Rear Differential:

(1) Remove the rear axle housing filler plug and gasket.

(2) Remove the rear axle housing drain plug and gasket, and drain the oil.

(3) Install a new gasket and the rear axle housing drain plug.

Torque:

49 N·m {500 kgf·cm, 36 ft·lbf}

2. ADD DIFFERENTIAL OIL

(a) Add differential oil so that the oil level is between 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.197 in.) from the bottom lip of the differential filler plug hole.

NOTICE:

  • Too much or too little oil will lead to differential problems.
  • After changing the oil, drive the vehicle and then check the oil level again.

Differential oil type and viscosity:

Toyota Genuine Differential gear oil LT 75W-85 GL-5 or equivalent

Front differential capacity:

Rear Differential Capacity:

Differential Model

Standard Differential Oil Capacity

*a

0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.197 in.)

(b) for Front Differential:

(1) Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, install a new gasket and the front differential filler plug.

Torque:

39.2 N·m {400 kgf·cm, 29 ft·lbf}

(c) for Rear Differential:

(1) Install a new gasket and the rear axle housing filler plug.

Torque:

49 N·m {500 kgf·cm, 36 ft·lbf}
