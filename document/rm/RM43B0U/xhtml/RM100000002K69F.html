*1: Check vehicle control history (wrong assembly confirmation)

  1. Using the GTS, check the vehicle control history.

    Click here

    HINT:

    If "Hybrid Battery Pack Sensor Module Mismatch" is displayed, the wrong type of battery voltage sensor is installed.

  2. If the battery voltage sensor had been replaced just before the malfunction occurred, check the part number of the battery voltage sensor.

    HINT:

    • The type of battery voltage sensor to be used varies depending on the vehicle model.
    • If the wrong type of battery voltage sensor is installed, the ignition switch cannot be turned to ON (READY).
  3. After installing the battery voltage sensor, perform the following to check that the ignition switch can be turned to ON (READY).
    1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (READY).
    2. Turn the ignition switch off and wait for 2 minutes or more.
    3. Turn the ignition switch to ON (READY) again.

*2: If the battery cooling blower malfunctions, DTC P0A8100, P0A8111, P0A8115, P0A814B, P0A8196, P0A8198, P0A9600, P0A9611, P0A9615, P0A964B, P0A9696 or P0A9698 will be stored.

If only "Maintenance Required for Traction Battery Cooling Parts See Owner's Manual" is displayed on the multi-information display, only perform the inspection of the HV battery cooling system intake port, filter and duct.
