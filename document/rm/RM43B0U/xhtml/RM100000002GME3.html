2. REMOVE POWER TAIL GATE UNIT ASSEMBLY RH

Connector

-

-

(1) Disconnect the connector.

(2) Using a thin-bladed screwdriver with its tip wrapped in protective tape, slightly raise the 2 stop rings as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Do not remove the stop rings from the power tail gate unit assembly. Raise the stop rings just enough to allow the ball joints to be detached.
  • Do not reuse the power tail gate unit assembly if a stop ring has been removed.

(3) Disengage the 2 ball joints to remove the power tail gate unit assembly.
