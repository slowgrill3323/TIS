DTC

B143A87

Lost Communication with Air Inlet Damper Control Servo Motor LIN Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning harness assembly and No. 5 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly connect the air conditioning amplifier assembly and the No. 5 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly. The air conditioning amplifier assembly supplies power and sends operation instructions to the No. 5 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly through the air conditioning harness assembly and No. 5 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly. The No. 5 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly sends pulse signals to inform the air conditioning amplifier assembly of the damper position.
