The necessary procedures (adjustment, calibration, initialization or registration) that must be performed after parts are removed, installed or replaced during the front seat airbag assembly removal/installation are shown below.

Front passenger seat

Zero point calibration (Occupant classification system)
  • Occupant classification system
  • Passenger airbag ON/OFF indicator
  • Airbag system (front passenger side)
  • Seat belt warning system (front passenger)

HINT:

When the cable is disconnected/reconnected to the auxiliary battery terminal, systems temporarily stop operating. However, each system has a function that completes learning the first time the system is used.

    Front camera system

    Drive the vehicle straight ahead at 35 km/h (22 mph) or more for 5 seconds or more.

    After turning the ignition switch to ON, opening the door, and then fully deployed the power running board system, close the door and stowed the power running board system.

    Power tail gate system

    Reset tail gate close position.

19. REMOVE FRONT SEAT AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

CAUTION:

  • When storing the front seat airbag assembly LH, keep the airbag deployment side (*a) facing upward.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

(1) Detach the clamp.

(2) Remove the 2 nuts and front seat airbag assembly LH.

CAUTION:

  • Do not reuse the nut.
  • If the installation surface or surrounding area of the front seat airbag assembly LH is deformed or damaged, replace the front seatback frame sub-assembly LH, the separate type front seatback cover and separate type front seatback pad.
