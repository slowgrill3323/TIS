DTC

C1AE487

Ultrasonic Sensor (Front Right Corner) Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when an open circuit or short occurs in the communication line between the front corner ultrasonic sensor RH and the front center ultrasonic sensor RH, or when a malfunction occurs in the front corner ultrasonic sensor RH.
