DTC

C1AE687

Ultrasonic Sensor (Rear Left Corner) Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is output when an open circuit or short occurs in the communication line between the rear corner ultrasonic sensor LH and the clearance warning ECU assembly, or when a malfunction occurs in the rear corner ultrasonic sensor LH.
