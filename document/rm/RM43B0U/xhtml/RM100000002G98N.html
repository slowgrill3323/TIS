PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL SLIDING ROOF HOUSING ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

  • Carrying the sliding roof housing sub-assembly to the vehicle must be performed by multiple people.
  • Be careful not to damage the sliding roof housing sub-assembly, body exterior or interior parts.

Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) As shown in the illustration, pull the sliding roof housing sub-assembly in from the opening in the back window glass.

Nut

Bolt

(1) Install the sliding roof housing sub-assembly.

1. Install the sliding roof housing sub-assembly with the 6 nuts and 8 bolts order shown in the illustration.

2. Tighten the 6 nuts and 18 bolts order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

8.3 N·m {85 kgf·cm, 73 in·lbf}

*b

1. Connect the sliding roof drain hose assembly as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Slide the hose to the base of the drain pipe.

2. Attach the claws to secure the sliding roof drain hose assembly.

HINT:

  • Make sure that the clamp is on the marking or between the marking and hose end.
  • Make sure that the lock of the clamp is facing up

3. Use the same procedure for the other side.

(1) Install the sliding roof weatherstrip as follows.

1. Apply adhesive to the rim of sliding roof glass.

2. Align joint line to the rear center.

3. Align weatherstrip marking dot to the corner portion of the sliding roof glass as shown in the illustration.

4. Install the lip of the weatherstrip firmly.

3. INSTALL SLIDING ROOF GLASS SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Measure the width "a" of the sliding roof weatherstrip.

HINT:

This measurement will be used in a later step.

(2) Temporarily install the sliding roof glass sub-assembly.

1. Using a T25 "TORX" socket wrench, temporarily install the sliding roof glass sub-assembly with the 4 "TORX" screws.

Torque:

4.0 N·m {41 kgf·cm, 35 in·lbf}

2. After tightening the screws, loosen them 90 to 180 degrees.

(1) Perform a level check:

  1. Check the difference in level "a" between the roof panel and the upper surface of the sliding roof glass sub-assembly when the sliding roof glass sub-assembly is fully closed.

HINT:

"+" represents the condition that the glass is above the panel level. "-" represents the condition that the glass is the condition that the glass is below the panel level.

4. INSPECT FOR LEAK

(1) Inspect for leaks by the following procedure.

  1. After adjusting the sliding roof glass sub-assembly, check for water leakage into the vehicle interior.
  2. If there are any leaks, readjust the sliding roof glass sub-assembly.
