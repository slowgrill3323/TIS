2. INSTALL FLYWHEEL HOUSING SIDE COVER

(1) Align the protrusion with the cylinder block sub-assembly and engage the claw as shown in the illustration to install the flywheel housing side cover.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure the claw makes a click sound, indicating that it fits tightly.
  • Replace the flywheel housing side cover with a new one if the claw does not fit tightly or is deformed.
