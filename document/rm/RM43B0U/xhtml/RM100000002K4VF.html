C13B516

Electric Parking Brake Actuator Supply Voltage Circuit Voltage Below Threshold
  • Diagnosis Condition:

    Ignition switch ON or electric parking brake switch (integration control and panel assembly) pulled to lock side with ignition switch off.

  • Malfunction Status:

    Voltage at terminal +BS is less than 6 V*

  • Detection Time:

    Approximately 0.5 seconds

HINT:

*: When the auxiliary battery voltage is 12 V.
