DTC

C1AE887

Ultrasonic Sensor (Rear Right Center) Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when an open circuit or short occurs in the communication line between the rear center ultrasonic sensor LH and the rear center ultrasonic sensor RH, or when a malfunction occurs in the rear center ultrasonic sensor RH.
