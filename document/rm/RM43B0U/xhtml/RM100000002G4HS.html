DTC

C173312

Rear Right Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit Circuit Short to Battery

DESCRIPTION

The rear absorber control actuator RH (rear shock absorber assembly RH) switches the damping force of the absorber based on an output signal from the absorber control ECU.
