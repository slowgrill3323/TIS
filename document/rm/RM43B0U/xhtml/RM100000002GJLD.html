PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL DIFFERENTIAL MOTOR ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove any old FIPG material.

(2) Using gasoline or alcohol, wipe off any residual FIPG material on the contact surfaces.

(3) Apply seal packing to the differential tube as shown in the illustration.

Seal packing:

Toyota Genuine Seal Packing 1281, Three Bond 1281 or equivalent

HINT:

Install the actuator within 3 minutes of applying seal packing.

(4) Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads at the tip of the bolts.

(5) Install the actuator to the differential tube with the 5 bolts.

Torque:

20.6 N·m {210 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

2. INSTALL WIRE HARNESS AND CONNECTOR

3. ADD FRONT DIFFERENTIAL OIL
