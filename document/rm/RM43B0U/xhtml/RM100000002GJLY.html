10. REMOVE SEATBACK COVER WITH PAD

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(1) Pull out the fasteners as shown in the illustration.

(2) Open the 2 fasteners.

(3) Detach the hook.

(4) Pass the wire harness of the front seat airbag assembly through the hole of the separate type front seatback cover.

(5) Detach the claw and guide and remove the 2 front seat headrest supports.

(6) Remove the front seatback cover with pad from the front seatback frame sub-assembly LH as shown in the illustration.

11. REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE FRONT SEATBACK COVER

NOTICE:

Be careful not to dirty or damage the separate type front seatback cover and separate type front seatback pad.

(1) w/ Seat Heater System:

Pass the wire harness of the front seatback heater assembly LH through the slit of the separate type front seatback cover.

(2) Remove the 13 hog rings and separate type front seatback cover from the separate type front seatback pad.
