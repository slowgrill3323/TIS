DISPOSAL

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. DISPOSE OF BRAKE BOOSTER PUMP WITH ACCUMULATOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Secure the brake booster pump with accumulator assembly in a vise as shown in the illustration.

(b) Using a drill, slowly drill a hole approximately 12 mm (0.472 in.) deep indicated by the area (A) in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

*a

30 mm (1.18 in.)

Area (A)

CAUTION:

Make sure to wear safety glasses as brake fluid and shards of metal may be ejected due to the release of pressurized gas.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to sufficiently secure the pump or accumulator of the brake booster pump with accumulator assembly before performing this procedure.
  • Make sure to drill the hole slowly so that pressurized gas will not be released rapidly.

HINT:

  • Nitrogen gas, which is colorless, odorless and harmless, will be discharged.
  • The recommended drill bit diameter is 6 mm (0.236 in.) or less.
