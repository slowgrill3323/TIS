11. INSTALL NO. 3 HOOD BULGE PROTECTOR (w/ Hood Bulge)

NOTICE:

When installing the rear No. 3 hood bulge protector using a heat light.

Standard Value:

Part

Temperature

NO. 3 HOOD BULGE PROTECTOR

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

(1) Clean the hood bulge assembly.

1. Using a heat light, heat the No. 3 hood bulge protector surface.

2. Remove any remaining double-sided tape from the No. 3 hood bulge protector

3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

4. Apply primer to the area shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Installing the rear bumper protector with some double-sided tape remaining may cause poor adhesion. Perform this procedure until the tape is sufficiently removed.
  • Make sure to use a cloth when removing. Using a screwdriver, etc., may cause damage and poor adhesion.
