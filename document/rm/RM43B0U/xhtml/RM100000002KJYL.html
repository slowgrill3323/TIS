DIAGNOSIS SYSTEM

FUNCTION OF INDICATOR LIGHT

(a) When a malfunction occurs in differential system, rear differential lock indicator light rapidly blinks to inform the user of the malfunction.

  • Rapidly blinking: Blinks at 0.25 second intervals (0.25 seconds on and 0.25 seconds off)

(b) The illumination state of the indicator lights when a DTC is output.
