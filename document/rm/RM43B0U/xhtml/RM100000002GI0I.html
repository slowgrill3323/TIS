PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 2 COOLER COMPRESSOR BRACKET

2. REMOVE MAGNET CLUTCH HUB

(1) Remove the magnet clutch hub using the following procedure.

1. Clamp the compressor with magnet clutch in a vise between aluminum plates.

2. Using SST, hold the magnet clutch hub.

09985-00270

3. Remove the bolt, magnet clutch hub and magnet clutch washer(s).

HINT:

There is no set number of magnet clutch washers that are used for adjusting clutch clearance.

3. REMOVE MAGNET CLUTCH ROTOR

(1) Using SST, remove the snap ring and magnet clutch rotor.

09904-00011

NOTICE:

Do not damage the seal cover of the bearing when removing the snap ring.

4. REMOVE MAGNET CLUTCH STATOR

(1) Using SST, remove the snap ring and magnet clutch stator.

09904-00011
