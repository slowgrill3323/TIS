5. INSPECT NO. 2 TRANSMISSION HUB SLEEVE

(a) Check the sliding condition between the No. 2 transmission hub and No. 2 transmission hub sleeve.

(b) Check that the splines of the No. 2 transmission hub sleeve are not worn.

(c) Using a vernier caliper, measure the width of the No. 2 transmission hub sleeve groove (A) and the thickness of the claw part of the No. 2 or No. 3 gear shift forks (B), and calculate the clearance.
