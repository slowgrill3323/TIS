Ambient Illumination Light Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) controls the operation of the following lights:

  • No. 1 interior illumination light assembly (driver side footwell light)*
  • No. 1 interior illumination light assembly (front passenger side footwell light)*
    • *: w/ footwell light

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
  • Before replacing the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU), refer to Registration.

HINT:

The DOME CUT relay supplies power to the inside handle illumination lights. If all the lights that use power from the DOME CUT relay do not turn on, check the interior light auto cut circuit first.
