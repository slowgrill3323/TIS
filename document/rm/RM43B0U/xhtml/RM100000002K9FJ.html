PROCEDURE

1. DRAIN COOLANT (for Inverter)

CAUTION:

  • To avoid the danger of being burned, do not remove the reserve tank cap or drain cock plug while the coolant (for inverter) is still hot.
  • Pressurized, hot coolant (for inverter) and steam may be released and cause serious burns.

NOTICE:

  • Do not reuse the drained coolant because it may contain foreign objects.
  • Collect the drained coolant and measure its volume to establish a benchmark. When adding coolant, make sure to add more coolant than the measured amount.

(a) Connect a hose with an inside diameter of 9 mm (0.354 in.) to the drain cock as shown in the illustration.

*1

Drain Cock Plug

*a

Hose

(b) Loosen the drain cock plug and drain the coolant.

HINT:

Collect the coolant in a container and dispose of it according to the regulations in your area.

(c) Remove the inverter reserve tank cap.

(d) Tighten the drain cock plug.

2. ADD COOLANT (for Active Test)

NOTICE:

  • Do not reuse the drained coolant because it may contain foreign objects.
  • If the vehicle is driven with air in the inverter cooling system, damage may occur and the following DTCs may be set.

(c) While adding coolant to the inverter reserve tank assembly to keep the coolant at the FULL line and compensate for the drop in the coolant level as the air bleeds, operate and stop the inverter water pump assembly at 1 minute intervals.

Standard:

Air bleeding from the inverter cooling system is completed when the noise made by the inverter water pump assembly becomes smaller and the circulation of coolant in the inverter reserve tank assembly improves.

HINT:

Loud noises made by the inverter water pump assembly and poor circulation of coolant in the inverter reserve tank assembly indicate that there is air in the cooling system.

(d) After the air is completely bled from the inverter cooling system, add coolant to the Full line of inverter reserve tank assembly and install the reserve tank cap.

NOTICE:

Make sure that more coolant than the volume collected coolant volume is added.

(b) Operation for entering Inspection Mode:

(1) If using the GTS, turn the ignition switch to ON and perform the following operations.

Powertrain > Hybrid Control > Utility

Tester Display

Inspection Mode
  1. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Hybrid Control / Utility / Inspection Mode
  2. Select "4WD/AWD MAINTENANCE MODE (4WD for measuring Exhaust Gas)" according to the screen display and press "Next". (*1)

    NOTICE:

    • Proceeding to step 2 (*1) will enter Inspection Mode and cause the inverter water pump with motor assembly to operate.
    • Because turning the ignition switch to ON (READY) will decrease the W/P rotation speed and prevent adequate air bleeding, perform replacement without turning the ignition switch to ON (READY).
    • After entering Inspection Mode, the inverter water pump with motor assembly will continue operating until Inspection Mode is canceled.

(2) If not using the GTS, turn the ignition switch to ON and perform the following operations within 60 seconds.

  1. With the shift position in P, fully depress the accelerator pedal 4 times.
  2. With the brake pedal depressed with your left foot, change the shift position to N and fully depress the accelerator pedal 4 times.
  3. Change the shift position to P and fully depress the accelerator pedal 4 times. (*2)

    NOTICE:

    • Depressing the accelerator pedal from the fully released position to the fully depressed position counts as 1 time.
    • Proceeding to step 3 (*2) will enter Inspection Mode and cause the inverter water pump with motor assembly to operate.
    • Because turning the ignition switch to ON (READY) will decrease the W/P rotation speed and prevent adequate air bleeding, perform replacement without turning the ignition switch to ON (READY).
    • After entering Inspection Mode, the inverter water pump with motor assembly will continue operating until Inspection Mode is canceled.

(c) Operation for canceling Inspection Mode:

(1) Turn the ignition switch off.

NOTICE:

  • Inspection Mode is not canceled simply by selecting "End" in the GTS. Be sure to turn the ignition switch off.
  • After canceling Inspection Mode, turn the ignition switch to ON again, and check that the display on the multi-information display when entering Inspection Mode has gone out.

(d) While adding coolant to the inverter reserve tank assembly to keep the coolant at the FULL line and compensate for the drop in the coolant level as the air bleeds, operate and stop the inverter water pump assembly at 1 minute intervals.

Standard:

Air bleeding from the inverter cooling system is completed when the noise made by the inverter water pump assembly becomes smaller and the circulation of coolant in the inverter reserve tank assembly improves.

HINT:

Loud noises made by the inverter water pump assembly and poor circulation of coolant in the inverter reserve tank assembly indicate that there is air in the cooling system.

(e) After the air is completely bled from the inverter cooling system, add coolant to the Full line of inverter reserve tank assembly and install the reserve tank cap.

NOTICE:

Make sure that more coolant than the volume collected coolant volume is added.
