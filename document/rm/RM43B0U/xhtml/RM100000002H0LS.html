Oil Pressure Gauge Malfunction

DESCRIPTION

The oil pressure sender detects the oil pressure generated when the engine is started. The combination meter determines that the engine is running when the engine speed is 400 rpm or more, and determines that the engine is cranking when the engine speed is 200 rpm or less. The oil pressure receiver gauge detects and indicates the oil pressure applied to the oil pressure sender.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When replacing the combination meter assembly, always replace it with a new one. If a combination meter assembly which was installed to another vehicle is used, the information stored in it will not match the information from the vehicle and a DTC may be stored.
  • When replacing any of the following ECUs, update the ECU security key.
