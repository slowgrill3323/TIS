Tachometer Malfunction

DESCRIPTION

The combination meter assembly receives engine speed signals from the ECM via CAN communication. The combination meter assembly displays the engine speed calculated based on the data received from the ECM.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When replacing the combination meter assembly, always replace it with a new one. If a combination meter assembly which was installed to another vehicle is used, the information stored in it will not match the information from the vehicle and a DTC may be stored.
  • When replacing the combination meter assembly, update the ECU security key.

    Click here

  • When replacing the ECM, update the ECU security key.

    Click here

  • Before replacing the ECM, refer to Registration.
