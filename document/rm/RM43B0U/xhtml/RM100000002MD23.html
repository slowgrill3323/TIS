(1) Apply protective tape around the headlight assembly LH.

(3) Slide the headlight assembly LH in the direction indicated by the arrow and detach the guide.

NOTICE:

  • Do not break the bracket.
  • Check that the retainer bracket is positioned correctly and there is no breakage.
