Title: TIRE PRESSURE MONITORING: TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM: OPERATION CHECK; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

OPERATION CHECK

CHECK TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM FUNCTION

(a) Using the Data List, check that the current tire pressure is normal.

Click here

(1) Slowly reduce the tire pressure of the front or rear tires and check that the tire pressure on the Data List changes.

(2) Further reduce the tire pressure and check that the warning light illuminates.

(b) After reducing the tire pressure, set the tire pressure to the specified value.
