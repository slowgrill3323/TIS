(1) Check that the clip is securely installed to the shift lever knob sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

If the clip is not installed on the inside of the shift lever knob sub-assembly claw, the shift lever knob sub-assembly may fall off.

(2) Install the shift lever knob sub-assembly to the transmission floor shift assembly.

NOTICE:

Check that the clip is positioned as shown in the illustration.

(3) Pull the shift lever knob sub-assembly upward by hand and check that it is not coming off.

(4) Engage the claw to connect the shifting hole cover sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Make sure that the shifting hole cover sub-assembly does not twist after installing the shift lever knob sub-assembly.
