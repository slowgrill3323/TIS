Title: POWER OUTLETS (EXT): TOWING CONVERTER SYSTEM: SYSTEM DESCRIPTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

INPUT SIGNAL

Item

Detail

Stop lights

Receives a stop light signal from the stop light switch via direct line.

Turn indicator lights

Receives turn indicator light signals from the turn signal flasher via direct line.

Taillights

Receives a taillight signal from the tail fuse via direct line.

NOTICE:

  • Use only the specified bulbs if replacement is necessary.
  • Do not connect other lights, such as fog lights, etc. to the wire harness or trailer.
  • Do not splice wires into the wire harness.
  • Do not exceed the allowable rated current of the towing converter relay.
  • Never disassemble or modify the parts related to the towing converter system or the vehicle.
  • Make sure there are no shorts in the lighting circuit of the trailer.

HINT:

  • The towing converter system has an Auto Current Cut (ACC) function to prevent the towing converter system from being affected by malfunctions in the trailer wire harness. If the current in the towing converter system circuit exceeds its rated current due to additional lights or a short, the towing converter relay will interrupt the power supply to the towing converter system.

    If the automatic current cut function operates, reset the towing converter system.
