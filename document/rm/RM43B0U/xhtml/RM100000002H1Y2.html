PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT STARTER ASSEMBLY (NO-LOAD PERFORMANCE TEST)

  • Do not use a thin cable to perform the inspection.
  • If a thin cable is used, the large electric current passing through the cable could cause it to overheat and catch fire, possibly resulting in injuries.

NOTICE:

Perform each of the following tests within 3 to 5 seconds to prevent the coil from burning out.

(a) Connect the positive (+) lead to terminal Terminal 50.

(c) With the clutch pinion gear in the outward position, disconnect the negative (-) lead and check that the clutch pinion gear returns inward.

If the result is not as specified, replace the starter assembly.

(d) Hold the starter assembly in a vise between aluminum plates.

NOTICE:

Ensure that the starter assembly is secured in the vise to prevent it from falling out.
