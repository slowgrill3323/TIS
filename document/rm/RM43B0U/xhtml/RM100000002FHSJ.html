C054017

Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Voltage Above Threshold

When vehicle speed exceeds 3 km/h (2 mph) and stop light switch assembly is OFF, master cylinder pressure continuously exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm 2 , 255 psi) for 5 seconds or more.

  • Wire harness and connector
  • Stop light switch assembly
  • Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
  • Master cylinder pressure sensor circuit malfunction
  • Skid control ECU (Brake actuator assembly)
