B00B513

Driver Seat Track Position Restraints Sensor Circuit Open
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a line open circuit signal in the seat position sensor circuit for 2 seconds.
  • Seat position airbag sensor malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
  • Harness or connector
  • Seat position airbag sensor
  • Front seat inner belt assembly LH
  • Airbag ECU assembly
