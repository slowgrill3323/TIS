1. REMOVE REAR SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) for Gasoline Model:

*a

*b

Location Pin

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(1) Using a screwdriver, push the buckle button to release the lock and disconnect the rear center outer seat belt assembly.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

(2) Fold up the 2 rear seat cushion assemblies.

(3) Remove the 2 bolts.

(4) Pull the belt of the rear seatback lock assembly and unlock the latch.

(5) Pull out the location pins as shown in illustration and remove the rear seat assembly LH.

(b) except Gasoline Model:

*a

Latch

*b

Location Pin

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(1) Remove the 3 clips.

(2) Using a screwdriver, push the buckle button to release the lock and disconnect the rear center outer seat belt assembly.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

(3) Fold up the 2 rear seat cushion assemblies.

(4) Remove the 2 bolts.

(5) Pull the belt of the rear seatback lock assembly and unlock the latch.

(6) Pull out the location pins as shown in illustration and remove the rear seat assembly LH.

(1) Remove and carry the rear seat assembly LH out of the vehicle.

NOTICE:

  • Carrying the rear seat assembly LH from the vehicle must be performed by multiple people.
  • Do not damage the rear seat assembly LH, body exterior or interior parts.
