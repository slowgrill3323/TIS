ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT VACUUM SENSOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Start the engine.

(b) Turn the ignition switch off.

(c) According to the display on the GTS, read the Data List.

Powertrain > Stop and Start > Data List

Tester Display

Brake Boost Pressure

(d) Compare the "Brake Boost Pressure" values before and after the brake pedal was depressed 5 times.

Standard:

Internal pressure changes by 20 kPa (150 mmHg, 5.91 in.Hg) or more.

HINT:

  • Standard atmospheric pressure is 101 kPa (758 mmHg, 29.8 in.Hg).
  • For every 100 m (328 ft.) increase in altitude, atmospheric pressure drops by 1 kPa (8 mmHg, 0.295 in.Hg).
  • Atmospheric pressure varies with weather.
