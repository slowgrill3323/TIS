Title: T24A-FTS (EMISSION CONTROL): CANISTER: INSPECTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT CHARCOAL CANISTER SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Visually check the charcoal canister sub-assembly for cracks or damage.

If cracks or damage is found, replace the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(b) Check the charcoal canister sub-assembly operation.

*a

Vent Line Port

*b

Purge Line Port

*c

Air Line Port

Air

(1) With the purge line port closed, blow air at 5 kPa (0.1 kgf/cm2, 0.7 psi) into the vent line port and check that air flows from the air line port.

(2) With the vent line port closed, blow air at 5 kPa (0.1 kgf/cm2, 0.7 psi) into the air line port and check that air flows from the purge line port.

*a

Vent Line Port

*b

Purge Line Port

*c

Air Line Port

Air

If the result is not as specified, replace the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(c) Check for air leaks.

(1) Connect a pressure gauge to the vent line port of the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

*a

Air Line Port

*b

Purge Line Port

*c

Vent Line Port

*d

Pressure Gauge

(2) With the purge line port and air line port closed, apply pressurized air at 19.6 kPa (0.2 kgf/cm2, 2.8 psi) into the vent line port, and then confirm that pressure is retained for 1 minute.

If the result is not as specified, replace the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

2. INSPECT NO. 2 CHARCOAL CANISTER SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Visually check the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly for cracks or damage.

If cracks or damage is found, replace the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(b) Check the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly operation.

(1) Connect a positive (+) lead from the battery to terminal 5 (VLVB) and a negative (-) lead to terminal 1 (VGND).

(2) Check that a clicking sound is heard from the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly.

If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(3) With the connector closed, blow air at 5 kPa (0.1 kgf/cm2, 0.7 psi) into the air line port and check that air flows from the fuel tank vent hose.

*a

Fuel Tank Vent Hose

*b

Air Line Port

Air

If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(c) Check for air leaks.

(1) Connect the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly to the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(2) Connect a pressure gauge to the vent line port of the charcoal canister sub-assembly.

*a

Purge Line Port

*b

Air Line Port

*c

Vent Line Port

*d

Pressure Gauge

(3) With the purge line port, air line port and connector closed, apply pressurized air at 19.6 kPa (0.2 kgf/cm2, 2.8 psi) into the vent line port, and then confirm that pressure is retained for 1 minute.

If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly.

(4) Disconnect the No. 2 charcoal canister sub-assembly to the charcoal canister sub-assembly.
