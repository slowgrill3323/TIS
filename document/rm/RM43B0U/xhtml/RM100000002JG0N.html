7. INSPECT NO. 2 BRAKE DISC

(a) Check if the contact surfaces of the No. 2 brake discs, No. 2 brake plates and No. 2 brake flanges are worn or burnt.

If necessary, replace them.

NOTICE:

  • If the lining of any No. 2 brake disc is peeled or discolored, or even if part of a groove is damaged, replace all the No. 2 brake discs.
  • Before installing new No. 2 brake discs, soak them in ATF for at least 15 minutes.
