Title: L580F (HYBRID TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): HYBRID TRANSMISSION SYSTEM: P079571; Pressure Control Solenoid "C" Actuator Stuck; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

P079571

Pressure Control Solenoid "C" Actuator Stuck

DESCRIPTION

The TCM detects the gear position based on signals from the transmission revolution sensor (NT) and transmission revolution sensor (SP2).

If the detected gear position differs from the commanded gear position, the TCM detects a mechanical problem in the related solenoid valve, transmission valve body assembly or automatic transmission assembly.

P079571

Pressure Control Solenoid "C" Actuator Stuck

One of the following conditions is met (2-trip detection logic):

  • When the TCM commands 3rd gear, 7th gear or R position, the transmission revolution sensor is detected 5 times in neutral for 0.5 seconds or more
  • When the TCM commands 1st gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 3rd gear for 0.5 seconds or more
  • When the TCM commands 2nd gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 3rd gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • When the TCM commands 4th gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 3rd gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • When the TCM commands 5th gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 3rd gear or 7th gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • When the TCM commands 6th gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 7th gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • When the TCM commands 8th gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 7th gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • Solenoid (SL3) valve
  • Solenoid (SL6) valve
  • Solenoid (SL) valve
  • Solenoid (SLT) valve
  • Transmission valve body assembly (for transmission side)
  • Automatic transmission assembly (clutch, brake, gear, etc.)

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

The TCM commands gear shifts by turning the solenoid valves on and off. According to the input shaft revolution (speed) and output shaft revolution (speed), the TCM detects the actual gear (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th or reverse gear position). When the gear commanded by the TCM and the actual gear are not the same, the TCM illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.
