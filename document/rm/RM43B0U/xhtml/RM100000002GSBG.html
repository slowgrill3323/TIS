B009387

Left Side Restraints Sensor 3 Missing Message
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a line short circuit signal, open circuit signal, short circuit to ground signal or short circuit to B+ signal in the side collision sensor LH circuit (bus 1) for 2 seconds.
  • No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly LH malfunction
  • Side airbag pressure sensor RH malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
  • Harness or connector
  • No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly LH
  • Side airbag pressure sensor RH
  • Airbag ECU assembly

Comes on

SRS Airbag

Not applicable
