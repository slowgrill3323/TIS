OPERATION CHECK

STABILIZER WITH DISCONNECTION MECHANISM SYSTEM OPERATION CHECK

(a) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(b) Operate the stabilizer control switch to drive the actuator of the stabilizer with disconnection mechanism.

(c) Check that the Data List Item "Status of Stabilizer" is switched between locked and free.

Chassis > Stabilizer with Disconnection Mechanism > Data List

Tester Display

Status of Stabilizer

(d) Check that no DTCs are detected.
