7. REMOVE POWER TAIL GATE SENSOR ASSEMBLY LH (w/ Power Tail Gate)

(1) Remove the 2 screws.

(2) Disconnect the connector and remove the tail gate sensor assembly LH.

(1) Using a T40 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the screw.

(2) Remove the screw.
