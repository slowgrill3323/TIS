CAUTION:

The front differential carrier assembly is a heavy component. Make sure that it is supported securely.

NOTICE:

  • Securely support the front differential carrier assembly while performing this step to avoid excessively tilting or dropping the front differential carrier assembly.
  • The remaining oil may leak out when removing the front differential carrier assembly.
  • Do not damage the installation surface when removing the front differential carrier assembly.
  • Remove the bolts with the front differential carrier assembly secured.
