Title: POWER OUTLETS (INT): ACCESSORY POWER OUTLET SYSTEM (except Gasoline Model): Accessory Power Outlet System does not Operate; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

Accessory Power Outlet System does not Operate

DESCRIPTION

The voltage inverter converts DC from the HV battery to AC voltage using the hybrid control system.

When the power outlet switch is ON with the ignition switch ON (READY), household electrical devices up to 2400 W can be used.
