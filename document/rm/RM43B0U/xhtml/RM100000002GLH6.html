Weak Blower Output

DESCRIPTION

If the airflow from the front registers is weak regardless of the blower speed setting displayed on the air conditioning control assembly, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor
  • Blower airflow volume is continuously weak
  • Blower airflow volume is sometimes weak
  • Customize setting
  • Control signal malfunction between air conditioning assembly and blower motor with fan sub-assembly
  • Blower motor with fan sub-assembly malfunction
  • Air conditioning amplifier assembly malfunction
  • Evaporator or heater core fin clogging
  • Clean air filter clogging
  • Evaporator temperature sensor (No. 1 cooler thermistor) malfunction
  • Aspirator loser

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM

(a) Operate the blower switch with the following conditions met and check that the airflow volume changes.

Blower Speed

Condition

LO → HI
  • Temperature setting: MAX COLD
  • Air outlet mode: FACE
  • A/C switch: Off

Airflow volume increases.

2.

CHECK ECO MODE CONTROL OPERATION

(a) Confirm setup of the ECO drive mode and turn it off if it becomes ON.

HINT:

  • When ECO drive mode is on, the air conditioning amplifier assembly ensures a minimum level of air conditioning control based on certain conditions, contributing to improved fuel efficiency. Accordingly, explain to customers that the air volume will be weaker in these situations.

    Control

    Outline

    Air Inlet Control

    Sets the blower level in AUTO mode lower than normal, and suppresses the power consumption.
  • In some situations, ECO mode control may not operate even though it is enabled.
