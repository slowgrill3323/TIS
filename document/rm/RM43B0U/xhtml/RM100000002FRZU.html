C061011

Right Electric Parking Brake Actuator Control Circuit Short to Ground
  • Diagnosis Condition:

    Electric parking brake not operating

  • Malfunction Status:

    The ECU power supply is normal but there is a malfunction in the electric parking brake actuator RH internal circuit (short to GND).

  • Detection Time:

    Approximately 1 second
  • Parking brake actuator assembly RH
  • No. 1 parking brake wire assembly
  • Wire harness and connector
  • Skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)

An electric parking brake system malfunction is displayed on the multi-information display.
