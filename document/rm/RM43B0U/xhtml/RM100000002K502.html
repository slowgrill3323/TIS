Zero Point Calibration of Steering Angle Sensor Malfunction (X20D7)

DESCRIPTION

Code

Tester Display

Measurement Item

Trouble Area

Output ECU

X20D7

Zero Point Calibration of Steering Angle Sensor Malfunction

History of the steering sensor zero point calibration position differing from the stored value
  • Poor adjustment of the center position of the steering wheel
  • Poor adjustment of wheel alignment

No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

After performing the inspection, check and clear the vehicle control history (RoB).
