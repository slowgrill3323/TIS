ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. CHECK TRANSFER OIL

Pre-procedure1

(a) Remove the transfer case plug (filler).

Procedure1

(b) Check that the oil level is between 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug hole.

*a

0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.)

(c) If the transfer oil level is low, check for transfer oil leaks, and then add transfer oil.

Oil grade:

Toyota Genuine Transfer Gear Oil LF or Equivalent

Viscosity:

SAE 75W

(d) Wait approximately 5 minutes and check that the oil level has not changed.

Post-procedure1

(e) Install the new transfer case plug (drain).

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}
