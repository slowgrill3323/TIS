PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL PROPELLER SHAFT GUARD BRACKET

Torque:

27 N·m {275 kgf·cm, 20 ft·lbf}

2. INSTALL REAR PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

*A

w/ Center Support Bearing

-

-

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) w/ Center Support Bearing:

Temporarily install the center support bearing to the crossmember with the 2 mounting bolts.

(2) Completely remove any oil or the like and clean the contact surfaces of the propeller shaft flange and differential flange.

(3) Connect the propeller shaft to the differential side, and install the 4 bolts, 4 nuts and 4 washers.

Torque:

74.4 N·m {759 kgf·cm, 55 ft·lbf}

(4) Align the matchmarks on the propeller shaft flange and transfer flange.

(5) Connect the propeller shaft to the transfer side, and install the 4 nuts and 4 washers.

Torque:

74.4 N·m {759 kgf·cm, 55 ft·lbf}

3. INSTALL NO. 1 CENTER SUPPORT BEARING ASSEMBLY (w/ Center Support Bearing)

*a

90°

*b

Bracket Center Line

*c

Shaft Axial Direction

-

-

(1) Check that the center line of the bracket is at right angles to the shaft axial direction.

(2) Tighten the 2 mounting bolts.

Torque:

36.8 N·m {375 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

4. INSTALL PROPELLER SHAFT GUARD (w/ Transmission Side Propeller Shaft Guard)

Torque:

27 N·m {275 kgf·cm, 20 ft·lbf}

5. INSTALL PROPELLER SHAFT GUARD (for Center Side)

Torque:

60 N·m {612 kgf·cm, 44 ft·lbf}
