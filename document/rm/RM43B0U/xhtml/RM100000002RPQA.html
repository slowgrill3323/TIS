DESCRIPTION

The power steering ECU (rack and pinion power steering gear assembly) detects the steering force based on the signal from the torque sensor (rack and pinion power steering gear assembly). Power assist is restricted or stopped as a fail-safe while an abnormality is detected in the torque sensor system.
