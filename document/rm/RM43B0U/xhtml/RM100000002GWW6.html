Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: TRAILER BRAKE CONTROL SYSTEM: C115A11; Output Circuit Short to GND Circuit Short to Ground; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C115A11

Output Circuit Short to GND Circuit Short to Ground

DESCRIPTION

The trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) is connected via the trailer socket and direct line.

The trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) outputs a signal to the trailer socket.
