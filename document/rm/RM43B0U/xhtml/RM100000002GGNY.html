Title: AUDIO / VIDEO: AUDIO AND VISUAL SYSTEM: B15A387; Stereo Component Amplifier Missing Message; 2024 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 - 03/2024]

DTC

B15A387

Stereo Component Amplifier Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the radio and display receiver assembly detects that the stereo component amplifier assembly has disconnected.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B15A387

Stereo Component Amplifier Missing Message

When 60 seconds has elapsed after turning the ignition switch to ON and the stereo component amplifier assembly cannot be detected, the stereo component amplifier assembly is judged to be disconnected after comparison with the past registered devices history.

(2 trip detection logic)
  • Radio and display receiver assembly
  • Stereo component amplifier assembly
  • Harness or connector
