NOTICE:

  • If the problem occurs in certain locations or times of day, the possibility of wave interference is high.
  • If a malfunction occurred after optional components were installed by the dealer, check if there is wave interference from the components.
  • Before performing troubleshooting for the vehicle, refer to Precautions for Inspection and Registration to check that the cause of the malfunction is not related to vehicle settings or vehicle components.

  • If the digital key system has been disabled, enable the system before performing troubleshooting.
