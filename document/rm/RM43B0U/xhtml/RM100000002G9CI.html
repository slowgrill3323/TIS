PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL WINDSHIELD OUTSIDE MOULDING CLIP

(1) Install the No. 4 nose piece to an air riveter and insert the mandrel section of a new windshield outside moulding clip into the nose piece.

(2) Press the windshield outside moulding clips perpendicularly to the installation holes. Using the air riveter, install the 5 windshield outside moulding clips.

NOTICE:

  • When installing the windshield outside moulding clips to the panel surface, do not tilt the riveter.
  • Do not leave a gap between the windshield outside moulding clip head portion and the panel.

HINT:

Pull the trigger again to cut off the windshield outside moulding clip if it is not cut off.
