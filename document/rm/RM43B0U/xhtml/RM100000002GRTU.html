SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

GENERAL

The windshield deicer thin heater wires are attached to the inside of the windshield glass to help deice the window surface more quickly. The operating status is displayed in radio and display receiver assembly while the windshield deicer system is operating. The system automatically turns off after approximately 15 minutes.

NOTICE:

If the windshield deicer system is operated when the auxiliary battery voltage is low, due to the electrical load limit control that gives voltage supply priority to the power steering system, the windshield deicer system may not operate. In this case, check the vehicle control history (RoB) of the power steering system and check that "Battery Voltage Drop: X208F" is not stored before proceeding with troubleshooting.

FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENT

Component

Outline

Air conditioning amplifier assembly

Sends windshield deicer activation request signals to the windshield deicer relay assembly (DEICER).

Windshield deicer relay assembly (DEICER)

Receives front deicer activation request signals from the air conditioning amplifier assembly and supplies power to the front window deicer.

Radio and display receiver assembly (windshield deicer switch)

Sends signals to the air conditioning amplifier assembly to operate the deicer.

Windshield glass (windshield deicer wire)

Receives power from the windshield deicer relay assembly (DEICER) and heats the windshield glass (windshield deicer wire).
