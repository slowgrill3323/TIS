INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR SUSPENSION HOLLOW SPRING

(a) Perform a visual inspection of the rear suspension hollow spring.

Standard:

Visually check the rear suspension hollow spring and confirm there are no abnormalities, leaks, etc., and that it is not completely compressed.

(b) If there are any abnormalities, replace the rear suspension hollow spring with a new one.
