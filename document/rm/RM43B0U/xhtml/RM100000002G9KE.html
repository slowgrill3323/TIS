PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

CAUTION:

  • Do not install the curtain shield airbag assembly LH partially twisted 360° from the normal condition.
  • Do not twist the line (*a) to prevent incorrect installation upward from below or downward from above. In addition, visually check that the line (*a) is not twisted after installation is complete.

*a

Turns off

-

-

(1) Check that the ignition switch is off.

(2) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

  • Wait at least 60 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

(1) Attach the hooks.

(2) Insert the hooks and tighten the 10 new bolts and 5 claws in the order shown in the illustration to install the curtain shield airbag assembly LH.

Torque:

11 N·m {112 kgf·cm, 8 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

After tightening the bolts, check that the hook has not become detached.

(1) Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.
