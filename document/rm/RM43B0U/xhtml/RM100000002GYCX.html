DCM Data Signal Circuit between Radio Receiver and DCM

DESCRIPTION

This circuit is used to send and receive signals between the DCM (telematics transceiver) and the radio and display receiver assembly.

(b) Disconnect the J34 and J116 radio and display receiver assembly connectors.
