Remove in this Direction

-

-

(1) Remove the 3 clips.

(2) Fold up the 2 rear seat cushion assemblies.

(3) Remove the 2 bolts.

(4) Pull the belt of the rear seatback lock assembly and unlock the latch.

(5) Pull out the location pins as shown in illustration and remove the rear seat assembly RH.

(1) Remove and carry the rear seat assembly RH out of the vehicle.

NOTICE:

  • Carrying the rear seat assembly RH from the vehicle must be performed by multiple people.
  • Do not damage the rear seat assembly RH, body exterior or interior parts.
