Title: ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEM: FRONT CAMERA SYSTEM: C138071; Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

C138071

Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck

DESCRIPTION

for Gasoline Model:

  • Stop light switch and skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) outputs a stop light signal. When the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) detects a circuit malfunction related to the stop light, it communicates to the forward recognition camera via CAN and outputs DTC C138071.

except Gasoline Model:

  • Stop light switch and No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) outputs a stop light signal. When the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) detects a circuit malfunction related to the stop light, it communicates to the forward recognition camera via CAN and outputs DTC C138071.
