Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM (except Gasoline Model): Cooling is Poor; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

Cooling is Poor

DESCRIPTION

If the cooling effect of the air conditioning system is weak, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor
  • Cooling is poor
  • Takes time to cool down
  • Customize setting
  • Compressor with motor assembly malfunction
  • Excessive refrigerant
  • Insufficient refrigerant gas volume
  • Dirty cooler condenser
  • Cooling system malfunction
  • A/M servo motor malfunction
  • Damper and link operation (jamming)
  • Automatic light control sensor malfunction
  • Warm air flow

WIRING DIAGRAM

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK ECO MODE CONTROL OPERATION

(a) Confirm setup of the ECO drive mode and turn it off if it becomes ON.

HINT:

  • When ECO drive mode is on, the air conditioning amplifier assembly ensures a minimum level of air conditioning control based on certain conditions, contributing to improved fuel efficiency. Accordingly, explain to customers that the air volume will be weaker in these situations.

    Control

    Outline

    Blower Control

    Automatically switches the air inlet port to the internal air circulation mode when the outside air temperature is equal to or higher than a predetermined temperature and reduces the power consumption.

    Air Inlet Control

    Sets the blower level in AUTO mode lower than normal, and suppresses the power consumption.
  • In some situations, ECO mode control may not operate even though it is enabled.
