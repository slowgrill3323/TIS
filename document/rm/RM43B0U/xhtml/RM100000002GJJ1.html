4. INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL RING GEAR BACKLASH

(1) Using a dial indicator, measure the backlash of the differential ring gear.

Standard backlash:

0.10 to 0.20 mm (0.00394 to 0.00787 in.)

If the backlash is not within the specification, adjust the side bearing preload or perform repairs as necessary.

HINT:

Perform the measurement at 3 or more positions around the circumference of the differential ring gear.

HINT:

The illustration shown is an example only. The illustration may differ from the actual parts according to the model.

If the result is not as specified, adjust the preload.

11. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL BEARING CAP

*a

Matchmark

*b

LH Side (Ring Gear Side)

*c

RH Side (Differential Lock Coil Side)

-

-

(1) Place matchmarks on the differential bearing cap and differential carrier.

(2) Remove the 4 bolts and 2 differential bearing caps.

(3) Remove the 2 plate washers.

HINT:

  • Put identification marks on the plate washers to show the location for installation (LH side (ring gear side) or RH side (differential lock coil side)), or keep them separate so that they can be distinguished.
  • Measure the thickness of each plate washers and note it.

12. REMOVE ELECTRONIC DIFFERENTIAL LOCK CASE SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove the electronic differential lock case sub-assembly from the differential carrier.

NOTICE:

After removing electronic differential lock case sub-assembly, remove differential lock indicator switch. Otherwise electronic differential lock case sub-assembly and differential lock indicator switch will be damaged.

*1

Differential lock indicator switch

*2

Electronic differential lock case sub-assembly

HINT:

Tag the 2 rear differential case bearings (outer) so that they can be reinstalled in the correct locations.

19. REMOVE REAR DRIVE PINION REAR BEARING (OUTER)

(1) Using a brass bar and hammer, tap out the rear drive pinion rear bearing (outer) and adjusting shim from the differential carrier.

HINT:

If the bearing is damaged during removal, replace it.

20. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL RING GEAR

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Place matchmarks on the differential ring gear and differential case.

(2) Remove the 12 differential ring gear set bolts.

(3) Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap on the differential ring gear to remove it from the differential case.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the position plate when removing the ring gear.
  • When removing the ring gear, do not damage the connector and wire harness of the differential case.
