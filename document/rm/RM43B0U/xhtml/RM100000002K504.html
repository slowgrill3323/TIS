ACC Low Pressure (X21A9)

DESCRIPTION

Code

Tester Display

Measurement Item

Trouble Area

Output ECU

X21A9

ACC Low Pressure

History of a continuous and significant decrease in accumulator pressure
  • Accumulator pressure dropped due to frequent brake pedal operation
  • Pump motor malfunction
  • Accumulator deterioration
  • Accumulator pressure sensor (brake booster with master cylinder assembly)
  • Supply voltage reduced

No. 1 skid control ECU (brake booster with master cylinder assembly)
