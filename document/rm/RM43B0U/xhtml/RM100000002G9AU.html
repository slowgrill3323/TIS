Title: DOOR / HATCH: TAIL GATE SUPPORT: DISPOSAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DISPOSAL

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. DISPOSE OF TAIL GATE SUPPORT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Horizontally hold the tail gate support assembly LH in a vise with the piston-rod pulled out.

(b) Wearing safety glasses, gradually cut a part within the area (a) or (b) shown in the illustration using a metal saw to release the gas.

Specifications:

Area

Measurement

a

25 mm (0.984 in.)

b

Radial Groove

NOTICE:

Although the gas inside the tail gate support assembly LH is colorless, odorless and harmless, there is a possibility that metal debris could scatter. Therefore, cover it with a piece of cloth or other material.
