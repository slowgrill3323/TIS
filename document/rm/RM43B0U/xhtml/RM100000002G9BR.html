2. REMOVE REAR SPOILER COVER

NOTICE:

When removing the rear spoiler cover, if the double-sided tape is difficult to remove, heat the adhesive of the rear spoiler cover using a heat light.

Standard Value:

Part

Temperature

Rear Spoiler Cover

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

NOTICE:

Do not heat the rear spoiler cover excessively.

(1) Heat the adhesive of the rear spoiler cover using a heat light.

(2) Remove the rear spoiler cover.
