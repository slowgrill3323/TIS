DTC

C1AE987

Ultrasonic Sensor (Rear Right Corner) Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when an open circuit or short occurs in the communication line between the rear corner ultrasonic sensor RH and the rear center ultrasonic sensor RH, or when a malfunction occurs in the rear corner ultrasonic sensor RH.
