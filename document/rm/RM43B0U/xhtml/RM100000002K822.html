1. INSTALL REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

*a

*b

*c

45°

-

-

Bolt A

Bolt B

Bolt C

Pretensioner Connector

(1) When the inclination of the retractor is 15° or less, check that the belt can be pulled from the retractor. When the inclination of the retractor is over 45°, check that the belt locks.

NOTICE:

Do not disassemble the retractor.

(2) Attach the guide and temporarily install the retractor with the bolt B and bolt C.

(3) Tighten the bolt B and bolt C in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

Bolt B :

12.5 N·m {127 kgf·cm, 9 ft·lbf}

Bolt C :

42 N·m {428 kgf·cm, 31 ft·lbf}

(4) Connect the pretensioner connector.

HINT:

Refer to How to Connect or Disconnect Airbag Connector:

Click here

(5) Tighten the bolt A and install the shoulder anchor of the rear seat outer belt assembly LH.

Torque:

42 N·m {428 kgf·cm, 31 ft·lbf}
