Click here

3. INSTALL REAR CONSOLE BOX MOUNTING BRACKET (w/o Voltage Inverter)

(1) Temporarily tighten the bolt.

(2) Install the rear console box mounting bracket with the 4 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

17.5 N·m {178 kgf·cm, 13 ft·lbf}
