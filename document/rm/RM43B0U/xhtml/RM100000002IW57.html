DTC

P071011

Transmission Fluid Temperature Sensor "A" Circuit Short to Ground

DESCRIPTION

The No. 1 ATF temperature sensor installed in the transmission valve body assembly detects the ATF temperature inside the transmission oil pressure control circuit and inputs signals to the TCM according to the ATF temperature.

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

This DTC indicates a short in the No. 1 ATF temperature sensor circuit. The No. 1 ATF temperature sensor converts the ATF temperature into an electrical resistance value. Based on the resistance, the TCM determines the ATF temperature, and can detect a short in the No. 1 ATF temperature sensor circuit.

If the No. 1 ATF temperature sensor output value is 200°C (392°F) or higher, the TCM interprets this as a malfunction of the No. 1 ATF temperature sensor or in the wire harness, illuminates the MIL and stores this DTC.
