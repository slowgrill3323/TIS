4. REMOVE LOWER STEERING COLUMN COVER

NOTICE:

Removing the lower steering column cover in the incorrect order will cause the parts to break.

Push Area

Operate in this direction

Push in this direction

-

-

(1) Release the tilt and telescopic lever and fully extend and lower the steering column assembly.

(2) Lock the tilt and telescopic lever.

(3) Remove the 3 screws.

(4) While pressing the push area shown in the illustration to disengage the 2 claws, slightly lower the lower steering column cover.

5. REMOVE UPPER STEERING COLUMN COVER

6. REMOVE TURN SIGNAL SWITCH ASSEMBLY WITH SPIRAL CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

  • Do not remove/install the spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly with the auxiliary battery connected and the ignition switch ON.
  • Do not rotate the spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly without the steering wheel assembly installed, with the auxiliary battery connected and the ignition switch ON.
  • Ensure that the steering wheel assembly is installed and aligned straight when inspecting the steering sensor.

As the illustration shown is an example, the actual details may differ.

16. REMOVE STEERING COLUMN ASSEMBLY

HINT:

As the illustration shown is an example, the actual details may differ.

(1) for Manual Transmission:

Disconnect the connector from the steering lock actuator with ECU assembly.

(2) Disengage each wire harness clamp from the steering column assembly.

(3) Remove the bolt, 2 nuts and steering column assembly.
