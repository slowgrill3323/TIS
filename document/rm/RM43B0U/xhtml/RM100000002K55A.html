X2003

Brake Control System Malfunction
  • An acceleration sensor or ABS malfunction signal is received for 1 second or more from the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)*1
  • An acceleration sensor or ABS malfunction signal is received for 1 second or more from the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)*2

Electronically controlled brake system

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

The trailer brake control system uses the CAN communication system. First, confirm that there are no malfunctions in the CAN communication system. Refer to How to Proceed with Troubleshooting.
