PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FRONT SHOULDER BELT ANCHOR ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY

(1) Attach the hook and temporarily install the rear shoulder belt anchor adjuster assembly with the 2 bolts.

(2) Tighten the 2 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

42 N·m {428 kgf·cm, 31 ft·lbf}

2. INSTALL FRONT SHOULDER BELT ANCHOR PLATE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

3. INSTALL FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

(1) When the inclination of the retractor is 15° or less, check that the belt can be pulled from the retractor. When the inclination of the retractor is over 45°, check that the belt locks.

NOTICE:

Do not disassemble the retractor.

(2) Attach the guide.

(3) Install the front seat outer belt assembly LH with the bolt.

Torque:

12.5 N·m {127 kgf·cm, 9 ft·lbf}

(4) Connect the pretensioner connector.
