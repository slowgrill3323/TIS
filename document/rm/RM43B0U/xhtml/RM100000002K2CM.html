PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL MOTOR CABLE

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

  • Keep the motor cable terminals and their installation area free from foreign matter, water, etc.
  • Do not touch the waterproof seal or terminals of the motor cable.

(1) Using an insulated tool, install the motor cable to the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly with the 3 bolts.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

2. INSTALL MOTOR CABLE TERMINAL CAP

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

  • Keep the terminal cap and its installation area free from foreign matter, water, etc.
  • Do not pinch the O-ring.
  • Check that the terminal cap is securely installed.

3. INSTALL CONNECTOR COVER

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}
