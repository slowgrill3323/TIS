B009B87

Left Side Restraints Sensor 5 Missing Message
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a line short circuit signal, open circuit signal, short circuit to ground signal or short circuit to +B signal in the side collision sensor LH circuit (bus 1) for 2 seconds.
  • No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly RH*1 malfunction
  • Side airbag pressure sensor LH malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
  • Harness or connector
  • No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly RH*1
  • Side airbag pressure sensor LH
  • Airbag ECU assembly

Comes on

SRS Airbag

A

Does not apply to test/check mode
  • *1: w/ No. 2 Side Airbag Sensor
