PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL COOLER CONDENSER ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

Do not damage the radiator assembly when installing the cooler condenser assembly.

Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) Install the cooler condenser assembly with the 2 nuts and 2 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

9.8 N·m {100 kgf·cm, 87 in·lbf}

HINT:

If a new cooler condenser assembly is installed, add compressor oil to the condenser as follows.

Capacity:

Add 40 cc (1.35 fl. oz)

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

2. INSTALL NO. 3 AIR CONDITIONING TUBE AND ACCESSORY ASSEMBLY

(a) w/ Bracket:

(1) Install the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly using the following procedure.

1. Remove the vinyl tape from the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly and connecting part of the cooler condenser assembly and then sufficiently apply compressor oil to a new O-ring and the fitting surface of the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

2. Install the O-ring to the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-ring and O-ring fitting surface free of foreign matter.

3. Connect the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly to the cooler condenser assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

9.8 N·m {100 kgf·cm, 87 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)

(b) w/o Bracket:

(1) Install the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly using the following procedure.

1. Remove the vinyl tape from the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly and connecting part of the cooler condenser assembly and then sufficiently apply compressor oil to a new O-ring and the fitting surface of the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

2. Install the O-ring to the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-ring and O-ring fitting surface free of foreign matter.

3. Connect the No. 3 air conditioning tube and accessory assembly to the cooler condenser assembly with the bolt.

Torque:

9.8 N·m {100 kgf·cm, 87 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)

3. INSTALL NO. 1 COOLER REFRIGERANT DISCHARGE HOSE

(1) Install the No. 1 cooler refrigerant discharge hose using the following procedure.

1. Remove the vinyl tape from the No. 1 cooler refrigerant discharge hose and connecting part of the cooler condenser assembly and then sufficiently apply compressor oil to a new O-ring and the fitting surface of the No. 1 cooler refrigerant discharge hose.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

2. Install the O-ring to the No. 1 cooler refrigerant discharge hose.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-ring and O-ring fitting surface free of foreign matter.

3. Connect the No. 1 cooler refrigerant discharge hose to the cooler condenser assembly with the bolt.

Torque:

5.4 N·m {55 kgf·cm, 48 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)

4. INSTALL AIR CONDITIONING TUBE AND ACCESSORY ASSEMBLY

(1) Install the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly using the following procedure.

1. Remove the vinyl tape from the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly and connecting part of the cooler condenser assembly and then sufficiently apply compressor oil to a new O-ring and the fitting surface of the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

2. Install the O-ring to the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-ring and O-ring fitting surface free of foreign matter.

3. Connect the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly to the cooler condenser assembly with the bolt.

Torque:

9.8 N·m {100 kgf·cm, 87 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)
