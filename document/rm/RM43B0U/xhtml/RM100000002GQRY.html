Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: INTUITIVE PARKING ASSIST SYSTEM: C1AE187; Ultrasonic Sensor (Front Left Corner) Missing Message; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C1AE187

Ultrasonic Sensor (Front Left Corner) Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when an open circuit or short occurs in the communication line between the front corner ultrasonic sensor LH and the clearance warning ECU assembly, or when a malfunction occurs in the front corner ultrasonic sensor LH.
