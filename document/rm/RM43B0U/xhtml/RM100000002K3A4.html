DTC

B14711C

A/C Inverter High Voltage Power Resource Circuit Voltage Out of Range

DESCRIPTION

The hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly monitors the voltage of the HV battery. The hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly stops compressor control and stores this DTC when the monitored voltage is outside the specified range.

This DTC will be stored as a history DTC. Compressor control may not resume unless the ignition switch is turned off.
