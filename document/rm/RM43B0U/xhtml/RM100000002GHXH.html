PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL TURBINE OUTLET ELBOW GASKET

(1) Install a new turbine outlet elbow gasket to the converter with catalyst assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Turbine outlet elbow gaskets are located in the grooves all around.
  • After assembly, remove any peeling residue on the flange surface.

2. INSTALL MANIFOLD STAY

*1

Turbo Charger Sub-assembly

*2

Converter With Catalyst Assembly

*a

Knock Pin

*b

Hole

(1) Temporarily install the manifold stay to the converter with catalyst assembly with the nut.

NOTICE:

Tighten the nut by hand until it contacts the surface.

(2) Temporarily install the converter with catalyst assembly with the 2 bolts.

(3) Align the converter with catalyst assembly with the knock hole on the turbocharger sub-assembly side to connect the converter with catalyst assembly.

NOTICE:

Make sure that there is a knock pin on the converter with catalyst assembly.

(4) Temporarily tighten the manifold stay nut.

(5) Remove the converter with catalyst assembly.

(6) Fully tighten the manifold stay nut.

Torque:

43 N·m {438 kgf·cm, 32 ft·lbf}

(1) Set a new exhaust pipe clamp to the converter with catalyst assembly.

NOTICE:

Make sure the end of the exhaust pipe clamp does not open 140 mm (5.51 in.) or more (standard amount approximately 60 mm (2.36 in.)).

(2) Temporarily install the converter with catalyst assembly with the 2 bolts.

NOTICE:

Tighten the bolt by hand until it contacts the surface.

(3) Align the converter with catalyst assembly with the knock hole on the turbocharger sub-assembly side to connect the converter with catalyst assembly.

NOTICE:

Make sure that there is a knock pin on the converter with catalyst assembly.

(4) Fully tighten the exhaust pipe clamp nut.

Torque:

21 N·m {214 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

8. INSTALL FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

*1

*2

*3

(1) Using a vernier caliper, measure the free length of the compression springs.

Minimum length:

43 mm (1.69 in.)

(2) If the free length is less than the minimum, replace the compression spring.

(3) Temporarily install 2 new gaskets to the converter with catalyst assembly and front exhaust pipe assembly.

(4) Using a plastic-faced hammer and wooden block, tap in 2 gaskets until its surface is flush with the converter with catalyst assembly and front exhaust pipe assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Be sure to install the gasket so that it faces the correct direction.
  • Do not reuse the gaskets.
  • Do not damage the gaskets.
  • Do not push in the gaskets by using the exhaust pipes when connecting them.
