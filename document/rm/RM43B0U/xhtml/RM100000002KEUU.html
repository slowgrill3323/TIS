(a) Conditions necessary for the power tail gate to open:

  • The tail gate is unlocked (except wireless one motion function).*
  • The vehicle speed is 3 km/h (2 mph) or less (only when the ignition switch is turned to ON).
  • The shift lever is in P, the brake pedal is depressed or the parking brake lever is pulled up (this can be determined only when the ignition switch is turned to ON).
  • *: Depending on the customize settings, the tail gate may not unlock and only the driver door unlocks when the first unlock operation is performed. Check that the customize settings in the table below are set to "Disable", and check that the tail gate unlocks.

CHECK BASIC OPERATION

(a) Check the opening operation.

(1) With the tail gate fully closed, push the tail gate opener switch for 0.8 seconds or more and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts opening.

(2) With the tail gate fully closed, push the electrical key transmitter sub-assembly (electrical key) for 0.8 seconds or more and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts opening.

(3) With the tail gate fully closed, push the power tail gate elbow switch LH (luggage electrical key switch) and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts opening.

(4) With the tail gate fully closed, push the power tail gate elbow switch RH (luggage electrical key switch) and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts opening.

(5) With the tail gate fully closed, push the tail gate handle switch (tail gate opener switch assembly) and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts opening.

(b) Check the close operation.

(1) With the tail gate fully open, push the tail gate opener switch for 0.8 seconds or more and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts closing.

(2) With the tail gate fully open, push the electrical key transmitter sub-assembly (electrical key) for 0.8 seconds or more and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts closing.

(3) With the tail gate fully open, push the power tail gate elbow switch LH (luggage electrical key switch) and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts closing.

(4) With the tail gate fully open, push the power tail gate elbow switch RH (luggage electrical key switch) and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts closing.

(5) With the tail gate fully open, push the tail gate handle switch (tail gate opener switch assembly) and check that the power tail gate warning buzzer sounds for 0.8 seconds, the hazard warning lights flash twice and the tail gate starts closing.

(c) Check the tail gate closer operation.

(1) Operate the multi-information display in the combination meter assembly and disable the power tail gate system.

(2) When the tail gate is closed manually, check that the tail gate closer operates.

CHECK JAM PROTECTION FUNCTION

HINT:

  • The multiplex network door ECU detects changes in the operating speed of the tail gate while the power tail gate is operating.
  • When the power tail gate sensor assemblies built into both sides of the tail gate are compressed, it is judged that foreign objects are in the path of the power tail gate and the operation will stop.
  • The power tail gate sensor assemblies operate only while the tail gate is closing. They do not operate while the tail gate is opening.
  • To resume power operation after jam, manually close the tailgate.

    Click here

(a) Check the jam protection function (the power tail gate sensor assembly) while the power tail gate is closing.

NOTICE:

Do not check this function using a part of your body such as your hand. Also, pay thorough attention that nothing gets caught by accident in this process.

(1) Fully open the tail gate.

(2) Press a switch to close the power tail gate.

(3) While the tail gate is closing, press a power tail gate sensor assembly using the handle of a hammer or equivalent and check that the buzzer sounds twice for 0.1 seconds and the power tail gate stops.

(b) Check the jam protection function (the pulse sensor in the multiplex network door ECU) while the power tail gate is closing.

NOTICE:

Do not check this function using a part of your body such as your hand. Also, pay thorough attention that nothing gets caught by accident in this process.

(1) Fully open the tail gate.

(2) Press a switch to close the power tail gate.

(3) While the tail gate is closing, insert a tire or equivalent between the tail gate and vehicle body, in order to interrupt the closing operation and check that the buzzer sounds twice for 0.1 seconds and the power tail gate stops.

(c) Check the jam protection function while the power tail gate is opening.

(1) Fully close the tail gate.

(2) Press a switch to open the power tail gate.

(3) While the tail gate is opening, use your hands to press on the outside surface of the tail gate so that the operating speed decreases and check that the buzzer sounds twice for 0.1 seconds and the power tail gate stops.
