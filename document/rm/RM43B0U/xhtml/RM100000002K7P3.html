PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL AIR CONDITIONING PRESSURE SENSOR

*a

Turn

*b

Hold

(1) Remove the attached vinyl tape from the air conditioning pressure sensor installation area of the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly and then apply a sufficient amount of compressor oil to the fitting surface of a new air conditioning pressure sensor O-ring and the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

Compressor Oil:

for Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 12 or equivalent

except Gasoline Model

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

NOTICE:

Keep the O-ring and O-ring fitting surface free of foreign matter.

(2) Using a 27 mm deep socket wrench, install the air conditioning pressure sensor.

Torque:

10.8 N·m {110 kgf·cm, 8 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Do not apply excessive force to the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.
  • Make sure not to cut the O-ring while installing it. (Cut O-rings cannot be installed.)

(3) Connect the connector.
