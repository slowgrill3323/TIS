DIAGNOSIS SYSTEM

FUNCTION OF INDICATOR LIGHT

(a) When a malfunction occurs in touch select 2-4 and high-low system, the 4HI indicator light, 4LO indicator light or rear differential lock indicator light* rapidly blinks to inform the user of the malfunction.

  • Rapidly blinking: Blinks at 0.25 second intervals (0.25 seconds on and 0.25 seconds off)
  • *: w/ Rear Differential Lock
