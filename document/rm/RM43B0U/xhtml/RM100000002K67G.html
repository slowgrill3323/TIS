Title: HYBRID / BATTERY CONTROL: HYBRID CONTROL SYSTEM (w/o 130A Generator): Shut Down Signal Circuit; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

Shut Down Signal Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The cause of the malfunction may be a shutdown signal.

Check whether there is a shutdown signal +B short circuit.

Related Parts Check

Area

Inspection

HSDN terminal

Check that the HSDN terminal voltage decreases to check for an open or short circuit while READY off (IG ON).

ECM, inverter, wire harness

Check for open or short circuit in ECM, inverter and wire harness.

SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

Power supply to the motor is cut off due to a shutdown signal sent from the ECM to the motor generator control ECU (MG ECU).

WIRING DIAGRAM

Refer to the wiring diagram for the ECM Power Source Circuit.
