PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Place the front seat assembly LH in the vehicle.

NOTICE:

  • 2 or more people are required when reinstalling the front seat assembly LH into the vehicle.
  • Protect the front seat leg.
  • Be careful not to damage the front seat assembly LH, body exterior or interior parts.

*A

for Driver Side

*B

for Front Passenger Side

(1) Connect the front seat airbag assembly LH connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.

HINT:

Refer to How to Connect or Disconnect Airbag Connector.

Click here

(2) Connect the connector and attach the clamp under the front seat assembly LH to install the floor wire harness.

(1) Slide the front seat assembly LH to the rearmost position.

(2) Insert the 2 location pins of the front seat assembly through the 2 body holes and place the front seat assembly.

(3) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, tighten the 2 "TORX" screws in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

(4) Slide the front seat assembly LH to the frontmost position.

(5) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, tighten the 2 "TORX" screws in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}
