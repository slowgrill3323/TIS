Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: INTUITIVE PARKING ASSIST SYSTEM: C1AE496; Ultrasonic Sensor (Front Right Corner) Component Internal Failure; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C1AE496

Ultrasonic Sensor (Front Right Corner) Component Internal Failure

DESCRIPTION

The front corner ultrasonic sensor RH is installed on the front bumper. The clearance warning ECU assembly detects obstacles based on signals it receives from the front corner ultrasonic sensor RH. If an open circuit or other malfunction occurs in the front corner ultrasonic sensor RH, the front corner ultrasonic sensor RH does not operate correctly.
