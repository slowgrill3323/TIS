PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL NO. 2 SIDE AIRBAG SENSOR ASSEMBLY

*a

Turns off

-

-

(1) Check that the ignition switch is off.

(2) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

  • Wait at least 60 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

(1) Attach the claw to hold the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly in place.

NOTICE:

  • If the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly has been dropped, or there are any cracks, dents or other defects in the case or connector, replace it with a new one.
  • When installing the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly, make sure that the wire harness does not interfere with or is not pinched between other parts.
  • Make sure that the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly guide is securely inserted into the body hole.

(2) Install the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly with the bolt.

Torque:

9.0 N·m {92 kgf·cm, 80 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

Tighten the bolt while holding the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly because the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly guide is easily damaged.

(3) Check that there is no looseness in the installed parts of the No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly.

(4) Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.
