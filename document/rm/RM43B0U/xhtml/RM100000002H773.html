2. INSTALL FRONT NO. 1 STABILIZER BAR BUSHING

Click here

3. TEMPORARILY TIGHTEN FRONT NO. 2 STABILIZER BRACKET LH

4. TEMPORARILY TIGHTEN FRONT NO. 2 STABILIZER BRACKET RH

5. INSTALL FRONT ACTIVE STABILIZER CONTROL ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

Temporarily tighten the 4 bolts (A) and then fully tighten the 6 bolts in the order of (A) and (B).

*1

Front Stabilizer Bracket

*2

Front Active Stabilizer Control Actuator Assembly with Front Stabilizer Bracket

Connector

195 N·m {1988 kgf·cm, 144 ft·lbf}

HINT:
