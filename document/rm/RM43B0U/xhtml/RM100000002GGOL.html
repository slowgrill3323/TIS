AVC-LAN Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The audio and visual system communicates with each device via AVC-LAN communication. If a malfunction such as a short in a communication line, short to +B or short to ground occurs in the AVC-LAN circuit, communication will stop and the audio and visual system will not operate correctly. The malfunctioning device can be identified if the system returns to normal when the malfunctioning device is disconnected from the AVC-LAN circuit.
