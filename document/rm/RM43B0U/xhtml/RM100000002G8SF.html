PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT INNER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY (w/o Garage Door Opener System)

(a) Inspect the operation of the electrochromic inner rear view mirror assembly.

*a

Black Colored Tape

*b

Forward Sensor

*c

Bright

*d

Dark

*e

Auxiliary Battery

*f

Component without harness connected

(Inner Rear View Mirror Assembly)

(1) Connect a positive (+) lead from the auxiliary battery to terminal 1 (IG) and a negative (-) lead to terminal 2 (E).

(2) Attach black colored tape to the forward sensor to prevent it from sensing.

(3) Light up the mirror with an electric light, and check that the mirror surface changes from bright to dark.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W14)
 Click Connector(W14)

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

W14-1 (IG) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W14-2 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Light up the mirror with an electric light

Inner rear view mirror surface Bright → Dark

If the result is not as specified, replace the inner rear view mirror assembly.
