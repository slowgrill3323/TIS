INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT BRAKE CYLINDER AND PISTON

(a) Check the rear disc brake cylinder bore and rear disc brake piston for rust and scoring. If necessary, replace the rear disc brake cylinder and rear disc brake piston.

2. INSPECT PAD LINING THICKNESS

(a) Using a ruler, measure the rear disc brake pad lining thickness.

Standard Thickness:

10.0 mm (0.394 in.)

Minimum Thickness:

1.0 mm (0.0394 in.)

(b) If a rear disc brake pad lining thickness is less than the minimum thickness, replace the rear disc brake pads.

HINT:

Be sure to check the rear disc thickness when replacing the rear disc brake pads with new ones.

3. INSPECT REAR DISC BRAKE PAD SUPPORT PLATE

(a) Make sure that the rear disc brake pad support plates have sufficient rebound, no deformation, cracks or wear, and that all rust and dirt is cleaned off. If necessary, replace the rear disc brake pad support plates.

4. INSPECT DISC THICKNESS (for Gasoline Model)
