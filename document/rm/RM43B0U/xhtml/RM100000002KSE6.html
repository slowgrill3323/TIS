4. ADJUST FRONT DOOR PANEL SUB-ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

Make sure to turn the ignition switch off before adjusting the door lock strikers.

(a) Using SST, loosen the hinge bolts on the body and adjust the door position.

09812-00010

(b) Tighten the hinge bolts on the body after the adjustment.

Torque:

26 N·m {265 kgf·cm, 19 ft·lbf}

(c) Loosen the hinge bolts on the door and adjust the door position.

(d) Tighten the hinge bolts on the door after the adjustment.

Torque:

21 N·m {214 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

(e) Using a T40 "TORX" socket wrench, adjust the striker position by slightly loosening the striker mounting screws and hitting the striker with a brass bar and a hammer.

(f) Using a T40 "TORX" socket wrench, tighten the striker mounting screws after the adjustment.

Torque:

23 N·m {235 kgf·cm, 17 ft·lbf}
