PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL RING GEAR AND DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION BACKLASH

(1) Using SST and a dial indicator, measure the ring gear backlash.

09564-32011

Standard backlash:

0.09 to 0.21 mm (0.00354 to 0.00827 in.)

If the backlash is not as specified, adjust the side bearing preload or perform repairs as necessary.

HINT:

Perform the measurement at 3 or more positions around the circumference of the ring gear.

2. INSPECT FRONT DRIVE PINION COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY

*a

30 mm (1.18 in.)

-

-

(1) Using a dial indicator, measure the runout of the front drive pinion companion flange vertically and laterally.

Maximum Runout:

Item

Specified Condition

Vertical runout

0.10 mm (0.00394 in.)

Lateral runout

0.10 mm (0.00394 in.)

If the runout is more than the maximum, replace the front drive pinion companion flange.

3. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL MOTOR ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

Click here

4. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL TUBE ASSEMBLY

(1) Using an E14 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 4 bolts.

(2) Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap out the differential tube.

5. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT OIL SEAL RH

Click here

6. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL CLUTCH HUB

(1) Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring.

(2) Remove the differential clutch hub.

7. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

(1) Using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring.

(2) Remove the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly RH from the front differential tube assembly.

8. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT BEARING RH

(1) Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring.

(2) Using SST, a brass bar and a press, press out the front differential side gear shaft bearing RH.

09950-00020

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the front differential side gear shaft bearing RH.
  • Do not drop the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly RH.

9. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR INTER SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Using SST, remove the side gear inter shaft.
