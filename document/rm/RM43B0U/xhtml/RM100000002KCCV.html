1. PRECAUTION

CAUTION:

  • Be sure to read precaution thoroughly before servicing.

    Click here

  • Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.
  • There is risk of injury.

NOTICE:

  • If the front seat airbag assembly has been deployed, replace the front seat airbag assembly, front seatback spring sub-assembly, front seatback pad and front seatback cover sub-assembly with the necessary parts in accordance with the extent of the collision damage.
  • Replace any other damaged parts as necessary.
  • After the ignition switch is turned off, there may be a waiting time before disconnecting the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

13. REMOVE FRONT SEAT AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

CAUTION:

  • When storing the front seat airbag assembly LH, keep the airbag deployment side (*a) facing upward.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

(1) Disengage the clamp.

(2) Remove the 2 nuts and front seat airbag assembly LH.

CAUTION:

  • Do not reuse the nut.
  • If the installation surface or surrounding area of the front seat airbag assembly LH is deformed or damaged, replace the front seatback frame sub-assembly LH, separate type front seatback pad and separate type front seatback cover.
