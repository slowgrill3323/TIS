C14C616

Electronic Brake Booster Control Module "A" Supply Voltage Circuit Voltage Below Threshold

Either of the following is detected:

  • Any of the following is detected when the ECU internal voltage is 6.92 V or less:
    • When operation of the main relay is requested, the relay output voltage is less than a certain value for 10.05 seconds or more.
    • The stroke sensor value invalid for 10.2 seconds or more.
    • The reaction force pressure sensor value invalid for 10.2 seconds or more.
    • The voltage supplied to the servo pressure sensor is a certain value or less or a certain value or more for 10.06 seconds or more.
    • The servo pressure sensor value invalid for 10.2 seconds or more.
    • The voltage supplied to the reaction force pressure sensor is a certain value or less or a certain value or more for 10.06 seconds or more.
  • When the internal voltage of the ECU is 8.3 V or less, the accumulator pressure sensor value is invalid for 10.8 seconds or more.
  • Improperly connected connector, deformation or corrosion of terminals
  • Wire harness and connector
  • No. 1 skid control ECU (brake booster with master cylinder assembly)

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

C14C7 (Case 1):

  • When the voltage at IG1 or IG2 drops due to a malfunction in the auxiliary battery and hybrid control system (charging circuit), the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

C14C7 (Case 2):

  • When the ECU internal voltage is a certain value or less and the brake pedal stroke sensor assembly is invalid for a certain amount of time, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply voltage and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

C14C7 (Case 3):

  • When the ECU internal voltage is a certain value or less and the reaction force pressure sensor is invalid for a certain amount of time, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply voltage and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

C14C7 (Case 4 and 6):

  • When the ECU internal voltage and pressure sensor power supply voltage is a certain value or less for a certain amount of time, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply voltage and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

C14C7 (Case 5):

  • When the ECU internal voltage is a certain value or less and the servo pressure sensor is invalid for a certain amount of time, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply voltage and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

C14C7 (Case 7):

  • When any of the following conditions continue for a certain amount of time, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply voltage and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.
    • When the ignition switch is off, the ECU internal voltage and BS monitor voltage is certain value or less.
    • When the ignition switch is ON (READY), the ECU internal voltage, BS monitor voltage and IG1 voltage is certain value or less.

C14C7 (Case 8):

  • When the ECU internal voltage is a certain value or less and the accumulator pressure sensor is invalid for a certain amount of time, the No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) judges that there is a drop in the power supply voltage and illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.

CONFIRMATION DRIVING PATTERN

NOTICE:

When performing the normal judgment procedure, make sure that the driver door is closed and is not opened at any time during the procedure.

HINT:

  • After repair has been completed, clear the DTC and then check that the vehicle has returned to normal by performing the following All Readiness check procedure.
  • When clearing the permanent DTCs, refer to the "CLEAR PERMANENT DTC" procedure.
  1. Connect the GTS to the DLC3.
  2. Turn the ignition switch to ON and turn the GTS on.
  3. Clear the DTCs (even if no DTCs are stored, perform the clear DTC procedure).
  4. Turn the ignition switch off.
  5. Turn the ignition switch to ON (READY) and turn the GTS on.
  6. Wait for 11 seconds or more. [*]

    HINT:

    [*]: Normal judgment procedure.

    The normal judgment procedure is used to complete DTC judgment and also used when clearing permanent DTCs.

  7. Enter the following menus: Chassis / Brake/EPB* / Utility / All Readiness.

    *: Electric Parking Brake System

  8. Check the DTC judgment result.

    HINT:

    • If the judgment result shows NORMAL, the system is normal.
    • If the judgment result shows ABNORMAL, the system has a malfunction.
    • If the judgment result shows INCOMPLETE, perform driving pattern again.
