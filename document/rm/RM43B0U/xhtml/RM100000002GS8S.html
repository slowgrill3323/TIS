Right Side Airbag Deployment Control Circuit Short to Battery
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a short to +B in the front side squib RH circuit for 0.5 seconds.
  • Front side squib RH (front seat airbag assembly RH) malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
  • Harness or connector
