INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY

(a) With the speaker in the installed condition, check there are no abnormalities such looseness or rattling.

(b) Check that there is no foreign matter inside the speaker.

(c) Check that the speaker cone is not damaged.

(d) When there is a malfunction such as noise from a speaker or no sound at all, replace the speaker with a new one and check that the malfunction disappears.

OK:

Malfunction disappears.

HINT:

  • When there is a possibility that either the right or left front No. 3 speaker assembly is defective, inspect by interchanging the right one with the left one.
  • Perform the inspection above on both the LH and RH sides.
