3. REMOVE OUTPUT SHAFT COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY (for Front Side)

*a

*b

(1) Using a chisel and hammer, loosen the staked part of the transfer output shaft companion flange nut.

NOTICE:

  • Completely loosen the staked part of the lock nut when removing it.
  • Do not damage the threads of the driven sprocket.

(2) Using SST to hold the output shaft companion flange sub-assembly, remove the transfer output shaft companion flange nut.

09330-00021

(3) Using SST, remove the output shaft companion flange sub-assembly.

Apply a lubricant to the threads and end of SST.

22. REMOVE TRANSFER FRONT DRIVE SHIFT FORK SHAFT

NOTICE:

  • When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.
  • When using a vise, do not overtighten it.

*1

Shift Detent Ball Spring Plug

*2

Shift Detent Ball Compression Spring

*3

Shift Detent Ball

*4

Transfer Front Drive Shift Fork Shaft

*5

No. 1 Transfer Gear Shift Fork

*6

No. 2 Transfer Gear Shift Fork

(1) Remove the 2 shift detent ball spring plugs.

(2) Using a magnet hand, remove the 2 shift detent ball compression springs and 2 shift detent balls.

(3) Using 2 screwdrivers and a hammer, remove the 2 transfer gear shift fork shaft snap rings.

HINT:

Use a piece of cloth to prevent the transfer gear shift fork shaft snap rings from flying off.

(4) Remove the bolt and transfer shift shaft stopper.

(5) To remove the transfer front drive shift fork shaft, shift the No. 1 transfer gear shift fork and No. 2 transfer gear shift fork to the positions shown in the illustration.

(6) Remove the transfer front drive shift fork shaft.

(7) Using a magnet hand, remove the 2 shift interlock pin or rollers.

23. REMOVE TRANSFER FRONT DRIVE CHAIN

NOTICE:

  • When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.
  • When using a vise, do not overtighten it.

(1) Using 2 screwdrivers and a hammer, remove the transfer gear shift fork shaft snap ring.

HINT:

  • Use a piece of cloth to prevent the transfer gear shift fork shaft snap ring from flying off.
  • Remove only the transfer gear shift fork shaft snap ring on the front side of the transfer shift actuator shaft.

(2) Using a snap ring expander, remove the transfer output shaft snap ring.

(3) Using a plastic-faced hammer, carefully tap the rear transfer case, and remove the transfer output shaft rear, transfer driven sprocket and transfer front drive chain with the No. 1 transfer gear shift fork and No. 2 transfer gear shift fork.

24. REMOVE TRANSFER SHIFT ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

(1) Using 2 screwdrivers and a hammer, remove the transfer gear shift fork shaft snap ring.

HINT:

Use a piece of cloth to prevent the transfer gear shift fork shaft snap ring from flying off.

(2) Remove the 3 bolts and transfer shift actuator assembly.

(2) for Rear Side:

09555-55010 09950-60011 09951-00270 09950-70010 09951-07100

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop or damage the driven sprocket.
