  • When replacing any of the following ECUs, always replace it with a new one. If any of the following ECUs is replaced with one which was installed to another vehicle, the information stored in it will not match the information from the vehicle and a DTC may be stored.
    • Forward recognition camera
    • Millimeter wave radar sensor assembly
    • Combination meter assembly
  • When the forward recognition camera has been replaced with a new one, make sure to clear all stored vehicle control history of each system and the forward recognition camera beam axis alignment data.
