DTC

B15D987

Operation Panel Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the radio receiver assembly detects that the operation panel device is disconnected.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B15D987

Operation Panel Missing Message

When 60 seconds has elapsed after turning the ignition switch to ON and the operation panel device cannot be detected, the operation panel device is judged to be disconnected after comparison with the registered USB devices history.

(2 trip detection logic)
  • Switch and volume assembly
  • Radio and display receiver assembly
  • Harness or connector
