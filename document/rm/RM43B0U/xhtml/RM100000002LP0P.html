PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FRONT SEATBACK HEATER ASSEMBLY LH

*a

Hole

-

-

(1) Remove the release paper from the front seatback heater assembly LH and attach it to the separate type front seatback pad aligning with the 2 holes as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Do not reuse the front seatback heater assembly LH and separate type front seatback pad.
  • After removing the peeling paper, do not touch the surface of the tape with your fingers or cotton work gloves.
  • Apply pressure to paste the front seatback heater assembly LH to the separate type front seatback pad securely.
