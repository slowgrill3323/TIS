Taillight Relay Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) controls the operation of the TAIL relay.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
  • Before replacing the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU), refer to Registration.

    except Gasoline Model: Click here

    for Gasoline Model: Click here

  • First perform the communication function inspections in How to Proceed with Troubleshooting to confirm that there are no CXPI communication malfunctions before troubleshooting this symptom.

    Click here
