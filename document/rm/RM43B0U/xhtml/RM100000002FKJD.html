2. REMOVE REAR STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Remove the nut of the rear stabilizer link assembly LH from the vehicle body.

HINT:

If the ball joint turns together with the nut, use a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench to hold the stud bolt.

(2) Remove the nut and rear stabilizer link assembly LH from the rear stabilizer bar.

HINT:

If the ball joint turns together with the nut, use a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench to hold the stud bolt.

3. REMOVE REAR STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY RH

(a) Perform the same procedure as for the LH side.
