Title: VF2DM (TRANSFER / 4WD / AWD): TRANSFER ASSEMBLY: REMOVAL; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

  • To prevent burns, do not touch the engine, exhaust pipe or other high temperature components while the engine is hot.
  • The transfer assembly is very heavy. Be sure to follow the procedure described in the repair manual, or the transmission jack may suddenly drop or a part may fall.

    *a

    Object Exceeding Weight Limit of Transmission Jack

NOTICE:

Change the vehicle to H2 before removing the transfer shift actuator assembly.

If the vehicle cannot be changed to H2, since the new transfer shift actuator assembly is set in the H2 position, connect all the connectors to the vehicle side wire harnesses to set the new transfer shift actuator assembly to the drive state of the vehicle before installing the new transfer shift actuator assembly.

14. REMOVE TRANSFER ASSEMBLY

(1) Using a transmission jack, plate lift attachments and a belt sling, etc., support the transfer assembly.

NOTICE:

Using a belt sling, etc., secure the transfer assembly to the transmission jack to prevent it from falling.

(2) Remove the 8 bolts and transfer assembly from the transmission assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Check the center of gravity while removing the transfer assembly.
  • Do not drop the transfer assembly.
