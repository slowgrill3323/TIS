1. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

Remove in this Direction

-

-

(1) Apply protective tape around the rear combination light assembly LH.

(2) Remove the 2 bolts.

(3) Pull the rear combination light assembly LH toward the rear of the vehicle to detach the pin to remove it.
