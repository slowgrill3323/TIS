4WD/AWD Rear Differential Lock Switch "A" Stuck On

Diagnosis Condition:

  • With ignition switch turned to ON

Malfunction Status:

  • With vehicle speed higher than 0 km/h (0 mph), differential of rear wheel speed 1 km/h (0.6 mph) or higher, rear differential lock position switch (differential lock indicator switch) is stuck ON

Malfunction Time:

  • 2 seconds or more

Number of Trips:

  • 1 trip
  • Wire harness and connector
