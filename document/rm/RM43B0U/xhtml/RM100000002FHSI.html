C05401C

Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Voltage Out of Range

When vehicle speed is 7 km/h (4.3 mph) or more, braking is performed (stop light switch assembly is ON) and master cylinder pressure differential value is below 20 MPa/s, difference between master cylinder pressure estimated from vehicle deceleration and master cylinder pressure sensor input value is large for 1 second or more 5 times or more for 9 trips.

C05402A

Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Signal Stuck In Range

Any of the following is detected:

  • When vehicle speed is 7 km/h (4.3 mph) or more and master cylinder pressure exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm2, 255 psi), master cylinder pressure does not increase 0.048 MPa (0.5 kgf/cm2, 6.9 psi) or more continuously for 60 seconds.
  • When vehicle speed is 7 km/h (4.3 mph) and master cylinder pressure is 2.45 MPa (25.0 kgf/ cm2, 355 psi) or more, master cylinder pressure does not increase 0.048 MPa (0.5 kgf/cm2, 6.9 psi) or more continuously for 30 seconds.
  • Master cylinder pressure does not increase 0.048 MPa (0.5 kgf/cm2, 6.9 psi) or more 5 times or more while vehicle speed decreases from 40 km/h (25 mph) to 0 km/h (0 mph).

C054049

Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Internal Electronic Failure

Either of the following is detected:

  • When voltage at terminal +BS is 9.5 V or higher, power supply voltage of master cylinder pressure sensor is continuously low for 1 second or more.
  • When master cylinder pressure sensor power supply voltage has risen, master cylinder pressure sensor has overheated or overcurrent of master cylinder pressure sensor continues for 1.2 seconds or more.

Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Component Internal Failure

  • Master cylinder temperature exceeds 150°C (302°F) continuously for 0.1 seconds.
