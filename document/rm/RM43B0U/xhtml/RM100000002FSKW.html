8. CONNECT CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER TO 2 WAY TUBE

*a

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

-

-

(1) Using a union nut wrench, connect the clutch master cylinder to 2 way tube to the clutch master cylinder assembly.

Torque:

Specified tightening torque :

15.2 N·m {155 kgf·cm, 11 ft·lbf}

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using a union nut wrench (fulcrum length of 22 mm (0.866 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 162 mm (6.38 in.)): 13.4 N*m (137 kgf*cm, 10 ft.*lbf)

9. INSTALL CLUTCH START SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(1) Using a 14 mm deep socket wrench, install the clutch start switch assembly.

NOTICE:

To prevent damage to the clutch start switch assembly, when removing the clutch start switch assembly, hold it so it does not rotate as you remove it.

Torque:

15.7 N·m {160 kgf·cm, 12 ft·lbf}
