Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (for Gasoline Model): P057113; Brake Switch "A" Circuit Open; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

P057113

Brake Switch "A" Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) detects the brake operating conditions through a signal transmitted by the stop light switch assembly.

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) incorporates a circuit to detect an open circuit.

This DTC is output when an open circuit is detected in the stop light signal input line.
