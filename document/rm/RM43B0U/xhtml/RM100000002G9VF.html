Title: SUSPENSION CONTROL: STABILIZER WITH DISCONNECTION MECHANISM SYSTEM: PRECAUTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

PRECAUTION

TROUBLESHOOTING PRECAUTIONS

(a) When there is a malfunction with terminal contact points or part installation problems, removal and installation of the suspected problem parts may return the system to the normal condition either completely or temporarily.

(b) Before disconnecting connectors or removing/installing parts, determine the malfunctioning area by checking and writing down the vehicle conditions (output DTCs, etc.) at the time the malfunction occurred.

(c) Since the system may be influenced by malfunctions in systems other than the stabilizer with disconnection mechanism system, be sure to check for DTCs in other systems.

(d) To check the stabilizer with disconnection mechanism, a DTC may be stored by another system, so after checking the stabilizer disconnect mechanism, check and clear DTCs for other systems.

HANDLING PRECAUTIONS

(a) Unless specified by the inspection procedure, make sure that the ignition switch is off when removing or installing the stabilizer control ECU or sensors.

(b) After removing and installing the stabilizer control ECU or sensors, check DTCs and ensure that the system is normal.

(c) If the ignition switch is turned to ON with the connector of the stabilizer control ECU disconnected, DTCs may be stored in other systems. Be sure to check and clear the DTCs after the inspection.

(d) Before troubleshooting the stabilizer with disconnection mechanism, stop the warning and diagnostic notifications to stop communication functions.
