(1) Disconnect the 2 bolts and 2 nuts to separate the cooler condenser assembly from the radiator assembly.

Do not damage the cooler condenser assembly when separating the cooler condenser assembly.

39. REMOVE RADIATOR ASSEMBLY

(1) Disconnect the 2 clamps from the radiator assembly.

(2) Disconnect the intercooler assembly connecter.

(3) Disconnect the 6 bolts to separate the radiator assembly from the vehicle body.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the cooler condenser assembly and radiator assembly when separating the radiator assembly.
