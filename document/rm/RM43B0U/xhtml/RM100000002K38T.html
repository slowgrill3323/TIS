DTC

B14B287

Lost Communication with Front Panel LIN Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning control assembly communicates with the air conditioning amplifier assembly via LIN communication.

If a malfunction occurs in the LIN communication system, the air conditioning amplifier assembly will not operate, even if the air conditioning control assembly is operated.
