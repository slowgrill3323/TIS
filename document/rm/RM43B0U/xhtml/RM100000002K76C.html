IGR Circuit Circuit Voltage Above Threshold

DESCRIPTION

If a malfunction is detected in the power supply circuit, the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) stores this DTC.

IGR Circuit Circuit Voltage Below Threshold

At a vehicle speed of 8 km/h (5 mph) or higher, the IGR terminal voltage is continuously below 7.9 V for 5 seconds or more
