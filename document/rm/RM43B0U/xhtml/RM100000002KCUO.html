FREEZE FRAME DATA

FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) When a DTC is stored, the 4 wheel drive control ECU stores the current vehicle state as Freeze Frame Data.

HINT:

Freeze Frame Data at the time a DTC is stored:

When the 4 wheel drive control ECU stores data at the time a DTC is stored, no updates will be performed until the data is cleared.

CHECK FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) Display the DTCs on the DTC check screen.

Powertrain > Four Wheel Drive > Trouble Codes

(b) Check the freeze frame data on the GTS.

Powertrain > Four Wheel Drive
