PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL ENGINE OIL LEVEL SENSOR

(1) Remove any dirt or foreign matter from the engine oil level sensor connector installation hole.

(2) Apply a light coat of engine oil to the O-ring of the engine oil level sensor.

(3) Using an 8 mm socket wrench, install the engine oil level sensor with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

(4) Install a new clip to the engine oil level sensor.

(5) Connect the engine oil level sensor connector.

NOTICE:

If there is any engine oil on the connector, clean the connector using a cloth or similar.

2. INSTALL NO. 2 OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY

*b

Seal Packing

(1) Apply seal packing in a continuous line as shown in the illustration.

Toyota Genuine Seal Packing Black, Three Bond 1207B or equivalent

Standard Seal Packing Diameter:

2.5 to 3.5 mm (0.0984 to 0.138 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Install the No. 2 oil pan sub-assembly within 3 minutes and tighten the bolts and nuts within 10 minutes of applying seal packing.
  • Do not start the engine for at least 2 hours after installation.
  • Do not add engine oil for at least 2 hours after the installation.
  • Make sure that the diameter at the start and end of each line of seal packing is 3 to 7 mm (0.118 to 0.276 in.).

(2) Install the No. 2 oil pan sub-assembly with the 9 bolts, 2 nuts and adjusting screw in several steps in the order shown in the illustration.
