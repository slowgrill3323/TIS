*a

*b

Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) Insert the quick heater assembly into the heater case and attach the claw as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Do not hold the quick heater assembly by the element or fin. Also, do not damage the quick heater assembly.

    *a

    Element

    *b

    Fin
  • Foul odors may occur due to oil adhering to the quick heater assembly.
  • Check that the heights of the claw fitting surface on the heater case and the claw of the quick heater assembly are aligned.

HINT:

If the claw of the quick heater assembly are damaged, install the quick heater assembly with a screw (90159-60431).

*a

Screw Insertion Hole

(2) Install the quick heater assembly with the screw.
