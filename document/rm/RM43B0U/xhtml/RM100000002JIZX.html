DTC

P096849

Pressure Control Solenoid "C" Control Internal Electronic Failure

DESCRIPTION

The TCM performs shifting from 1st to 8th gear with ON-OFF combinations of solenoid (SL1, SL2, SL3, SL4, SL5, SL6, SL and SC3) valves.

As a fail-safe function, the TCM turns the other solenoids ON or OFF if an open circuit or short occurs in any of the solenoid circuits. (If an open circuit or short circuit occurs, the TCM cuts power to the malfunctioning solenoid valve.)

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

This DTC indicates current control command value and current feedback value are inconsistent in the solenoid (SL3) valve circuit.

The TCM commands gear shifts by turning the solenoid valves on or off.

When there is current control command value and current feedback value are inconsistent in any solenoid valve circuit, the TCM detects the problem, illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.
