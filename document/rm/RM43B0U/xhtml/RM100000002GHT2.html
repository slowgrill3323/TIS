Air Vent cannot be Switched

DESCRIPTION

If the air outlet mode does not change even though the air outlet display changes, the following factors may be the cause.

Symptom

Factor

Air vent cannot be changed

  • (Display changes)
  • MODE servo motor malfunction
  • Damper and link operation (jamming)
  • Air conditioner unit assembly malfunction

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK NO. 3 AIR CONDITIONING RADIATOR DAMPER SERVO SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Check that the No. 3 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly is installed correctly.
