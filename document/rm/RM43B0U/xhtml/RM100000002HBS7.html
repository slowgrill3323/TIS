A vehicle stops while brake control by parking support brake system not operate and after brake control by parking support brake system operates and then vehicle runs more than 10 km/h (6.2 mph).

    • When the engine is started and the battery voltage permission conditions are met after the ignition switch is turned off and 8 hours have elapsed
    • When the battery voltage permission conditions are met after "Starter (Hood Closed)" is performed using the GTS
  • *3: When the battery fluid temperature is -10°C (14°F) or less, or 60°C (140°F) or higher (for Mexico Models: -10°C (14°F) or less, or 55°C (131°F) or higher), stop and start control is prohibited. When the battery fluid temperature returns to -10°C (14°F) or higher, and 60°C (140°F) or less (for Mexico Models: -10°C (14°F) or higher, and 55°C (131°F) or less), stop and start control is permitted.

WARNING CONTROL

If any of the following operations are performed while the engine is stopped due to stop and start control, the system will not restart the engine. The engine will change the mode from "stopped due to system control" to "stalled" or the driver will be warned using the buzzer.

Operation

Warning Control

The engine hood is opened with the shift lever in D

The engine stops (mode changes to "stalled", and the engine cannot be restarted until the ignition switch is turned off and then to ON)

The driver door is opened with the shift lever in D

The engine starts, warning buzzer sounds and stop and start indicator blinks

The driver seat belt is unfastened

The engine starts

WARNING INDICATOR AND BUZZER OPERATION

The driver is informed of the system control status and warnings by the stop and start indicator, stop and start cancel indicator and the buzzer.

HINT:

The stop and start indicator and the stop and start cancel indicator are displayed at the same location in the combination meter.

One or the other will be displayed according to the order of priority, but they will not be illuminated simultaneously.

  • The battery charge amount may be low.
  • The battery voltage is low
  • The battery fluid temperature is high
  • A refresh charge may be occurring.

"Stop & Start system Unavailable"
  • The engine may have been started with the hood opened.
  • The stop and start system is temporarily disabled.
