8. REMOVE FRONT TRANSMISSION BEARING RETAINER OIL SEAL

(a) Using a screwdriver, pry out the front transmission bearing retainer oil seal.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the lip of the front transmission bearing retainer oil seal and front bearing contact surfaces.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

*a

Protective Tape

9. INSTALL FRONT TRANSMISSION BEARING RETAINER OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST and a press, press in a new front transmission bearing retainer oil seal.
