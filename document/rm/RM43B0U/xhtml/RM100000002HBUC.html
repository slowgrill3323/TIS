PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT BRAKE CYLINDER AND PISTON

(a) Check the disc brake cylinder assembly bore and front disc brake piston for rust and scoring.

If necessary, replace the disc brake cylinder assembly and front disc brake piston.

2. INSPECT PAD LINING THICKNESS

(a) for type A:

(1) Using a ruler, measure the front disc brake pad lining thickness.

Standard Thickness:

11.5 mm (0.452 in.)

Minimum Thickness:

1.0 mm (0.0394 in.)

3. INSPECT FRONT DISC BRAKE ANTI-RATTLE SPRING

(a) Clean the front disc brake anti-rattle spring and check it for deformation and cracks.

If necessary, replace the front disc brake anti-rattle spring.
