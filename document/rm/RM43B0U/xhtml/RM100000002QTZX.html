PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL TIMING CHAIN CASE OIL SEAL

*a

0 to 2 mm (0 to 0.0787 in.)

-

-

(1) Apply MP grease to the lip of a new timing chain case oil seal.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow foreign matter to contact the lip of the timing chain case oil seal.
  • Do not allow MP grease to contact the dust seal.

(2) Install the timing chain case oil seal to SST.

09223-00010

(3) Set SST and the crankshaft pulley set bolt as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Make sure to leave the crankshaft pulley set bolt screwed into the crankshaft by 2 or 3 threads.

(4) Using SST and the crankshaft pulley set bolt, evenly tap in the timing chain case oil seal until its surface is flush with the end face of the timing chain or belt cover sub-assembly.

Oil Seal Protrusion Height:

0 to 2 mm (0 to 0.0787 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Do not tap the timing chain case oil seal at an angle.
  • Keep the lip free of foreign matter.
