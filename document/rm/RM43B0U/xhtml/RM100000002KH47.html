PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Place the front seat assembly LH in the vehicle.

NOTICE:

  • 2 or more people are required when reinstalling the front seat assembly LH into the vehicle.
  • Be careful not to damage the front seat assembly LH, body exterior or interior parts.
  • Protect the front seat leg.

(1) Connect the connector of the front seat airbag assembly LH.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.

HINT:

Refer to How to Connect or Disconnect Airbag Connector.

Click here

(2) Connect each connector and attach the clamp under the front seat assembly LH to install the floor wire harness.

(1) Connect the cable to the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

Click here

(2) Insert the 2 location pins of the front seat assembly LH to the body holes.

(3) Move the front seat assembly LH to the rearmost position.

(4) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, install the 2 "TORX" screws in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

(5) Move the front seat assembly LH to the frontmost position.

(6) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, install the 2 "TORX" screws in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}
