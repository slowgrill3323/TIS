Title: MAINTENANCE: VF4CM TRANSFER OIL: REPLACEMENT; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

REPLACEMENT

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE LOWER TRANSFER CASE PROTECTOR (w/ Protector)

(a) Remove the 4 bolts and lower transfer case protector.

*a

Rear Side of Vehicle

*b

Front Side of Vehicle

2. DRAIN TRANSFER OIL

(a) Remove the transfer case filler plug and gasket.

(b) Remove the transfer case drain plug and gasket, and drain the transfer oil.

(c) Install a new gasket and the transfer case drain plug.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

3. ADD TRANSFER OIL

(a) Add transfer oil.

Oil grade:

Toyota Genuine Transfer Gear Oil LF or Equivalent

Viscosity:

SAE 75W

Standard Capacity:

1.40 liters (1.48 US qts, 1.23 Imp. qts)

NOTICE:

Add transfer oil slowly.

(b) Wait approximately 5 minutes and check that the oil level has not changed.

(c) Check that the transfer oil level is between 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug hole.

NOTICE:

  • After driving, check the transfer oil level again.

*a

0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.)

(d) Install a new gasket and the transfer case filler plug.

Torque:

37 N·m {377 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

(e) Inspect for transfer oil leak.

4. INSTALL LOWER TRANSFER CASE PROTECTOR (w/ Protector)

(a) Install the lower transfer case protector with the 4 bolts.

Torque:

18 N·m {184 kgf·cm, 13 ft·lbf}
