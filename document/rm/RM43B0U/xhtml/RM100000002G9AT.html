2. REMOVE TAIL GATE SUPPORT ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, slightly raise the 2 stop rings as shown in the illustration.

(2) Disengage the 2 ball joints to remove the tail gate support assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not remove the stop rings from the tail gate support assembly. Raise the stop rings just enough to allow the ball joints to be disengaged.
  • Do not reuse the tail gate support assembly if a stop ring has been removed.
