PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. DISCONNECT FUEL PUMP HARNESS

(1) Disconnect the 2 fuel pump harness connectors.

(2) Detach the 2 clamps to remove the fuel pump harness.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the wire harness.
  • When disengaging each wire harness from the clamp, detach one wire at a time.

3. REMOVE NO. 1 FUEL SUB-TANK

(1) Detach the claw and disconnect the jet pump.

(2) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped in protective tape, disconnect the jet pump from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the jet pump and No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

(3) Detach the 3 claws and disconnect the No. 1 fuel sub-tank from the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.

(4) Detach the 3 claws and remove the No. 1 fuel sub-tank from the fuel suction support.

4. REMOVE NO. 1 FUEL SUCTION SUPPORT
