CHECK DTC, FREEZE FRAME DATA AND VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (RoB)

(a) Check DTCs and freeze frame data.

Chassis > Adaptive Variable Suspension System > Trouble Codes

(1) Check and record DTCs.

(2) Check and record freeze frame data.

(b) Clear the DTCs and freeze frame data.

Chassis > Adaptive Variable Suspension System > Clear DTCs

(c) Recheck for DTCs.

Chassis > Adaptive Variable Suspension System > Trouble Codes

(1) Reproduce the problem symptoms based on the recorded DTCs and freeze frame data, and then check if the DTCs are output again.

HINT:

  • When DTCs indicating a CAN communication system malfunction are output, first troubleshoot and repair the CAN communication system.
  • If the GTS cannot communicate with the absorber control ECU, check CAN communication between the DLC3 and the absorber control ECU, and also check the IG circuit of the absorber control ECU.
