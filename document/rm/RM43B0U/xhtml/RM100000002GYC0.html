DTC

B153713

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction occurs in the telephone and GPS antenna assembly (for Front Side).

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B153711

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

Telephone antenna (sub) impedance (Ω) is lower than the malfunction threshold for 10 seconds or more when the ignition switch is ON

(Short circuit)
  • DCM (telematics transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna assembly (for Front Side)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (antenna cord sub-assembly)

A

B153713

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Open

Telephone antenna (sub) impedance (Ω) is higher than the malfunction threshold for 10 seconds or more when the ignition switch is ON

(Open circuit)
  • DCM (telematics transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna assembly (for Front Side)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (antenna cord sub-assembly)
