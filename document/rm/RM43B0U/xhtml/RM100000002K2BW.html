VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (RoB)

CHECK VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (HYBRID TRANSMISSION SYSTEM)

HINT:

  • The vehicle control history data (hybrid transmission system) stores the history of the reject function and system protection operations.
  • The number of occurrences, date and distance are stored in batches for each item.

(a) Connect the GTS to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(c) Turn the GTS on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Transmission / Utility / Vehicle Control History (RoB).

Powertrain > Transmission > Utility

CLEAR VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (HYBRID TRANSMISSION SYSTEM)

(a) Connect the GTS to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(c) Turn the GTS on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Transmission / Utility / Vehicle Control History (RoB) (Clear).

NOTICE:

By performing this procedure, all stored Vehicle Control History items will be cleared.

VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY FREEZE FRAME DATA (HYBRID TRANSMISSION SYSTEM)

(a) Connect the GTS to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(c) Turn the GTS on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Transmission / Utility / Vehicle Control History (RoB).

(e) Select a vehicle control history item to access the applicable freeze frame data.

(f) Check the freeze frame Data recorded with the Vehicle Control History.
