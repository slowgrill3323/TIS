9. REMOVE FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 "TORX" screws.

(2) Slide the front seat assembly LH to the rearmost position.

(3) Using a T50 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 "TORX" screws.

(4) Pull out the 2 location pins from the body holes.

(5) Slide the front seat assembly LH to the center position.

(6) Set the seatback of the front seat assembly LH to the perpendicular position.
