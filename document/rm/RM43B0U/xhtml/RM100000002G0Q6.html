(1) Secure the generator assembly in a vise between aluminum plates.

NOTICE:

Do not overtighten the vise.

(2) Temporarily install a new generator pulley with clutch to the rotor shaft.

(3) Fit SST to the generator pulley with clutch.

09820-30010

(4) Fit a T50 "TORX" socket wrench into the rotor shaft end.

HINT:

Make sure that SST and the T50 "TORX" socket wrench are secured to the rotor shaft and generator pulley with clutch.

(5) Using a 17 mm union nut wrench, tighten the generator pulley with clutch by turning SST as shown in the illustration.

Torque:

Specified tightening torque :

80 N·m {816 kgf·cm, 59 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Check that the generator assembly is secured in the vise tightly.
  • Hold the T50 "TORX" socket wrench tightly during the operation.
  • Check that the generator pulley with clutch rotates smoothly.

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using a 17 mm union nut wrench (fulcrum length of 30 mm (1.18 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 400 mm (1.312 ft.)): 74.4 N*m (759 kgf*cm, 55 ft.*lbf)

Remove in this Direction

-

(1) Remove SST and the T50 "TORX" socket wrench from the generator pulley with clutch.

(2) Remove the generator assembly from the vise.
