1. INSTALL REAR DOOR WINDOW FRAME MOULDING SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

NOTICE:

When installing the rear door window frame moulding sub-assembly LH, heat the rear door panel LH and rear door front window frame moulding LH using a heat light.

Standard Value:

Part

Temperature

Rear Door Panel LH

40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)

Rear Door Window Frame Moulding Sub-assembly LH

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts.
  • Touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

(1) Clean the surface of the rear door panel LH.

1. Using a heat light, heat the rear door panel LH surface.

2. Remove any remaining double-sided tape from the rear door panel LH.

3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

NOTICE:

  • Installing the rear door window frame moulding sub-assembly LH with some double-sided tape remaining may cause poor adhesion. Perform this procedure until the tape is sufficiently removed.
  • Make sure to use a cloth when removing. Using a screwdriver, etc., may cause damage and poor adhesion.
  • Clean a new rear door panel LH surface.

(2) Using a heat light, heat the rear door panel LH and a new rear door window frame moulding sub-assembly LH.

(3) Attach the clips and double-sided tape and install the rear door window frame moulding sub-assembly LH.

NOTICE:

The application strength of the rear door window frame moulding sub-assembly LH will weaken if reapplied. If reapplication is necessary, be sure to replace it with a new one.

HINT:

Apply pressure so that it does not lift up from the rear door panel LH.
