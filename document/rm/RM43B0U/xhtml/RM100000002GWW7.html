C115E00

Functional Safety Failure (Predetermined Maximum Output Exceeded)

DTC

C115E94

Functional Safety Failure (Unintended Braking) Unexpected Operation

DESCRIPTION

The trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) is connected via the trailer socket and direct line.

The trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) outputs a signal to the trailer socket.

C115A15

Output Circuit Short to Battery Circuit Short to Battery or Open

With the ignition switch ON and an IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, a voltage of 4.0 V or more is input to the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) output circuit for 1 second or more
  • Harness or connector (vehicle)
  • Harness or connector (trailer)
  • Trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay)
  • Breakaway switch operation (trailer)

C115E00

Functional Safety Failure (Predetermined Maximum Output Exceeded)

With the ignition switch ON, an IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V and the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) performing control, a voltage of 4.0 V or more is input to the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) output circuit for less than 1 second
  • Harness or connector (vehicle)
  • Harness or connector (trailer)
  • Trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay)
  • Breakaway switch operation (trailer)

Trailer Brake Controller

A

C115E94

Functional Safety Failure (Unintended Braking) Unexpected Operation

With the ignition switch ON, an IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V and the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) not performing control, a voltage of 4.0 V or more is input to the trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay) output circuit for less than 1 second
  • Harness or connector (vehicle)
  • Harness or connector (trailer)
  • Trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay)
  • Breakaway switch operation (trailer)
