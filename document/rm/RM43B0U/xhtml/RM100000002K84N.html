Title: NETWORKING: LIN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM: B232588; Door LIN Bus off; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

B232588

Door LIN Bus off

DESCRIPTION

If the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) detects a communication error with an ECU connected to the door bus lines for 8 seconds or more, DTC B232588 will be stored.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Priority

B232588

Door LIN Bus off

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) detects a communication error with an ECU connected to the door bus lines for 8 seconds or more.

  • Power window regulator motor assembly (driver door)
  • Multiplex network master switch assembly

  • Power window regulator motor assembly (rear RH door)*1

  • Side auto step controller ECU assembly*2
