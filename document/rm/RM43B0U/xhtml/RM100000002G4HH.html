1. INSPECT ACCELERATION SENSOR ASSEMBLY RH

*a

Component without harness connected

(Acceleration Sensor Assembly RH)

-

-

Upside

Downside

(a) Connect 3 dry cell batteries of 1.5 V in series.

(b) Connect the positive (+) end of the batteries to terminal 3 (SGB) of the acceleration sensor assembly RH and the negative (-) end of the batteries to terminal 2 (SGG). Then measure the voltage between terminal 1 (SGFR) and terminal 2 (SGG).

(c) Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Voltage:

NOTICE:

  • Do not apply a voltage of more than 6 V.
  • If the acceleration sensor assembly RH is dropped, replace with a new part.

HINT:

When the acceleration sensor assembly RH is tilted, it may output a different value.
