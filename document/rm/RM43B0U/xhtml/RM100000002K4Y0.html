Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (except Gasoline Model): C139A00,C13D900,C14EC7E; Brake Pressure Control solenoid Circuit Malfunction; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

Brake Pressure Control solenoid "C" Actuator Stuck On

DESCRIPTION

The No. 1 skid control ECU (brake booster with master cylinder assembly) controls the brake fluid pressure of the master cylinder based on the output of the brake fluid pressure sensor.

DTCs may be stored if one of the following occurs:

  • Brake fluid leaks.
  • Foreign matter enters a solenoid valve.
  • Line pressure drops during air bleeding.
  • Brake pads are replaced.
  • Rotors are replaced.

HINT:

Brake pressure control solenoid "C": Solenoid SLA
