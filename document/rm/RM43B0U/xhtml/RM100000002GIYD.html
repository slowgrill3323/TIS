OPERATION CHECK

CHECK POWER RUNNING BOARD OPERATION

(a) When the side auto step switch assembly is "OUT" position, the operation and condition of this control are as described below.

Operation

Condition

Left and right power running boards are deployed immediately

The vehicle speed is less than 5 km/h (3 mph).

(b) When the side auto step switch assembly is "AUTO" position, the operation and condition of this control are as described below.

Check that the left side power running board operates as follows

Operation

Condition

Power running board is deployed immediately

When any of the following conditions is met:

  • Vehicle speed must be below 5 km/h (3 mph) for board deploy.
  • Open either the front left door or the rear left door.

Power running board is stowed

When any of the following conditions is met:

  • Close the front and rear left side doors.
  • Turn the side auto step switch assembly to "AUTO" position.
  • Drive the vehicle at a speed of 5 km/h (3 mph) or more.

Check that the right side power running board operates as follows

Operation

Condition

Power running board is deployed immediately

When any of the following conditions is met:

  • Vehicle speed must be below 5 km/h (3 mph) for board deploy.
  • Open either the front right door or the rear right door.

Power running board is stowed

When any of the following conditions is met:

  • Close the front and rear right side doors.
  • Turn the side auto step switch assembly to "AUTO" position.
  • Drive the vehicle at a speed of 5 km/h (3 mph) or more.

(c) When the side auto step switch assembly is "OFF" position, the operation and condition of this control are as described below.

Operation

Left and right power running boards are stowed immediately

(d) Check the JAM protection function.

CAUTION:

Do not put your hands between the vehicle body and power running board to check functions. Also, prevent any part of your body from being caught during inspection.

(1) Check the jam protection function while the power running board is being stowed.

  1. Open the door and fully deploy the power running board.
  2. Close the door and stow the power running board.
  3. While the power running board is being stowed, insert a tire or equivalent between the power running board and vehicle body, in order to interrupt the stowing operation.
  4. Check that the jam protection function is immediately activated and that the power running board reverses operation and moves to the deployed position.

    HINT:

    • When there has been a jam 3 times in succession, the side auto step controller ECU assembly judges that there is an obstacle (a foreign obstacle) and stops the power running board in the position where the jam protection function has operated.
    • The jam protection operating condition is displayed on the combination meter assembly.

(2) Check the jam protection function while the power running board is deploying.

  1. Close the door and fully stow the power running board.
  2. Open the door and deploy the power running board.
  3. While the power running board is being deployed, interrupt the deploying operation using a tire or equivalent.
  4. Check that the jam protection function is immediately activated and that the power running board reverses operation and moves to the stowed position.

    HINT:

    • When there has been a jam 3 times in succession, the side auto step controller ECU assembly judges that there is an obstacle (a foreign obstacle) and stops the power running board in the position where the jam protection function has operated.
    • The jam protection operating condition is not displayed on the combination meter assembly.
