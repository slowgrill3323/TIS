VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (RoB)

NOTICE:

  • Replacing or repairing related components may cause vehicle control history codes to be stored.
  • When checking the vehicle control history, make sure to record the output histories. Then, clear the histories and recheck the output histories again.

CHECK VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (OCCUPANT CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM)

(a) Following the instructions on the GTS, display the vehicle control history (RoB) check screen and read the codes.
