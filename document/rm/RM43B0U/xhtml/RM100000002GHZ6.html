(1) Turn the crankshaft pulley until the flat of the camshaft faces the fuel pump lifter assembly.

HINT:

This prevents the camshaft nose from pushing up the fuel pump lifter assembly when installing the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly.

(2) Apply 30 cc (1.8 cu. in.) of engine oil to the pump drive cam.

(3) Apply engine oil to the fuel pump lifter assembly.

(4) Apply engine oil to the inside of the fuel pump lifter guide and the outside of the fuel pump lifter assembly.

(5) Set the fuel pump lifter assembly on the fuel pump lifter guide as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Align the stopper key of the fuel pump lifter assembly with the key groove of the fuel pump lifter guide.

2. INSTALL FUEL PUMP SPACER GASKET

3. INSTALL FUEL PUMP SPACER

4. TEMPORARILY INSTALL FUEL (ENGINE ROOM SIDE) PUMP ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

When replacing the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly, it is necessary to replace the No. 1 fuel pipe sub-assembly with a new one.

(1) Apply engine oil to a new O-ring and install it to the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the O-ring.

(2) Set the fuel pump spacer and fuel (engine room side) pump assembly on the No. 5 camshaft bearing cap.

(3) Temporarily install the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly with the 2 bolts, leaving some allowance for left and right movement.

5. TEMPORARILY INSTALL NO. 1 FUEL PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

Do not damage the seals of the union nuts of the No. 1 fuel pipe sub-assembly.

(1) Temporarily install the No. 1 fuel pipe sub-assembly and tighten the 2 union nuts by hand in the order shown in the illustration.

6. INSTALL FUEL (ENGINE ROOM SIDE) PUMP ASSEMBLY

(1) Tighten the 2 bolts.

Torque:

28.5 N·m {291 kgf·cm, 21 ft·lbf}

(2) Connect the fuel (engine room side) pump assembly connector.
