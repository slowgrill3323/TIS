Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) Install the instrument panel assembly as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the instrument panel assembly or vehicle interior.
  • Make sure that the wire harness does not get caught on anything as it may become damaged.

(2) Connect the connector.

Install in this Direction

-

-

(1) Attach each guide as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the instrument panel assembly or vehicle interior.
  • Make sure that the wire harness does not get caught on anything as it may become damaged.

(1) Install the 2 clips.

(2) Install the nut <C>.

Torque:

5.0 N·m {51 kgf·cm, 44 in·lbf}

(3) Install the 6 bolts <B>.

Torque:

5.0 N·m {51 kgf·cm, 44 in·lbf}

(4) Install the 2 bolts <A>.

Torque:

20 N·m {204 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

(1) Attach the 2 claws.

(2) Attach each clamp.

(3) Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.

HINT:

Refer to How to Connect or Disconnect Airbag Connector:

Click here

(4) Connect each connector.

50. INSTALL LOWER NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY WITH LOWER NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL ASSEMBLY

*a

Turns off

-

-

(1) Check that the ignition switch is off.

(2) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

  • Wait at least 60 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.
  • If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.

(1) Attach each claw and clip.

(2) Install the 5 bolts.

Torque:

Bolt <E> :

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

Bolt <F> :

7.5 N·m {76 kgf·cm, 66 in·lbf}

(3) Install the 3 screws <D>.

(4) Connect the airbag connector and clamp.

NOTICE:

  • When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.
  • Make sure the lock button is securely locked.

HINT:

Refer to How to Connect or Disconnect Airbag Connector:

Click here

(5) Check that there is no looseness in the lower No. 2 instrument panel airbag assembly.
