Title: T24A-FTS (BATTERY / CHARGING): CHARGING SYSTEM (for 1Motor-HEV Model without 130A Generator): P058A01; Auxiliary Battery Monitor Module Range/Performance; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

P058A01

Auxiliary Battery Monitor Module Range/Performance

DESCRIPTION

The battery state sensor assembly detects the auxiliary battery voltage, current and temperature of the auxiliary battery. The battery state sensor assembly calculates State of Charge (SOC) based on the voltage and current of the auxiliary battery and sends it to the hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly. Based on the signals received, the hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly adjusts the charging voltage of the DC/DC converter.

The battery state sensor assembly calculates the auxiliary battery temperature based on changes in resistance of a built-in thermistor and sends it to the hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly. The hybrid vehicle control ECU assembly reduces the amount of charging current based on this signal in order to protect the auxiliary battery.

P058A01

Auxiliary Battery Monitor Module Range/Performance

Any of the following conditions is met with the ignition switch ON (1 trip detection logic):

  • The current is 999.939 A or more, or -1000 A or less for 3.2 seconds or more.
  • A current detection circuit malfunction signal is received.
  • The auxiliary battery temperature is 105°C (221°F) or more or -40°C (-40°F) or less for 12.288 seconds or more.
  • A temperature detection circuit malfunction signal is received.
  • The voltage is 15.98 V or more, or 6.0 V or less for 3.2 seconds or more.
  • A voltage detection circuit malfunction signal is received.
  • A writing error occurs in the auxiliary battery state sensor internal memory (battery identification data).
  • A pulse discharge circuit malfunction condition continues for 4.608 seconds or more.
