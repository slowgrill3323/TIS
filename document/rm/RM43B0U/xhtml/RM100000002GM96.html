Title: SEAT: CLIMATE CONTROL SEAT SYSTEM: OPERATION CHECK; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

OPERATION CHECK

CHECK CLIMATE CONTROL SEAT SYSTEM

(a) for Driver Side:

(1) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(2) Set the climate control switch (seat blower switch of multi-display or air conditioning control assembly) on the driver side to "HI" and check that the seat blower operates.

OK:

Seat cushion side and seatback side operate together

(3) Operate the climate control switch (seat blower switch of multi-display or air conditioning control assembly) on the driver side to switch the seat blower operation in order from "HI" → "MID" → "LO" → "OFF" and check that the blower level indicator illuminates or turns off.

OK:

Blower level indicator changes according to operation of refreshing seat switch (seat blower switch) on driver side

(4) Check that the level indicator light switches from HI → MID → LO and that the seat surface heat changes according to the air conditioning temperature setting when the refreshing seat switch is operated with the seat blower set to AUTO.

HINT:

  • The seat heater operates when set to heating, and the seat blower operates when set to cooling.
  • The surface warmth of the seat changes even according to the outside temperature. Therefore, check with the outside temperature at a constant temperature.

(b) for Front Passenger Side:

(1) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

(2) Set the climate control switch (seat blower switch of multi-display or air conditioning control assembly) on the front passenger side to "HI" and check that the seat blower operates.

OK:

Seat cushion side and seatback side operate together

(3) Operate the climate control switch (seat blower switch of multi-display or air conditioning control assembly) on the front passenger side to switch the seat blower operation in order from "HI" → "MID" → "LO" → "OFF" and check that the blower level indicator illuminates or turns off.

OK:

Blower level indicator changes according to operation of refreshing seat switch (seat blower switch) on front passenger side

(4) Check that the level indicator light switches from HI → MID → LO and that the seat surface heat changes according to the air conditioning temperature setting when the refreshing seat switch is operated with the seat blower set to AUTO.

HINT:

  • The seat heater operates when set to heating, and the seat blower operates when set to cooling.
  • The surface warmth of the seat changes even according to the outside temperature. Therefore, check with the outside temperature at a constant temperature.
