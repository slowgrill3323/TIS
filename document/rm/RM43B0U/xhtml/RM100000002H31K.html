*b

*d

Front Side of Vehicle

*e

RH Side of Vehicle

*f

120° (Claw of Hose Clip Location)

(1) Connect the No. 1 oil cooler outlet hose to the oil cooler assembly and slide the hose clip to secure the hose.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to align the paint mark of the No. 1 oil cooler outlet hose with the paint mark of the oil cooler assembly.
  • Make sure that the claws of the hose clip are within the location shown in the illustration.
  • Make sure that the clip does not overlap the pipe spool.
