1. INSPECT FUEL LID LOCK WITH MOTOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Check the operation of the fuel lid lock with motor assembly.

(1) Push in the moving part and check that the part is fixed in the closed position.

(2) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the fuel lid lock with motor assembly connector, and check that the fuel lid lock with motor assembly operates smoothly as follows.
