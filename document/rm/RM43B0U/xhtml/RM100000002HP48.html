PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT LUMBAR SUPPORT ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Check that the lumbar support adjuster assembly LH moves forward and backward smoothly when the auxiliary battery is connected to the lumbar support adjuster assembly LH connector terminals.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(r9)
 Click Connector(r9)

Tester Connection

Operational Direction

r9-2 (PLRI) - auxiliary battery positive (+)

r9-1 (PLRO) - auxiliary battery negative (-)

Forward

r9-1 (PLRO) - auxiliary battery positive (+)

r9-2 (PLRI) - auxiliary battery negative (-)

Backward

If the result is not as specified, replace the lumbar support adjuster assembly LH.
