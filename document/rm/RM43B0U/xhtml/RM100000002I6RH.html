MANUAL OPEN OPERATION

HINT:

Perform this procedure when the tailgate cannot be opened due to a system malfunction. (w/ Power Tail Gate)

1. REMOVE TAIL GATE PROTECTOR

Click here

NOTICE:

If the tail gate protector is damaged, replace it with a new one.

2. UNLOCK TAIL GATE LOCK

(a) Insert a screwdriver through the hole in the top of the tailgate and push the lever to unlock it.

HINT:

Do the both side at the same time.
