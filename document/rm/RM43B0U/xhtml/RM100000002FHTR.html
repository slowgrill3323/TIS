DTC

C116514

ABS Pump Motor Drive Circuit Supply Voltage Circuit Short to Ground or Open

DESCRIPTION

If a malfunction is detected in the power supply circuit, the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) power source voltage drops, the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) will store this DTC.

If the battery voltage is temporarily low, this DTC may be stored.
