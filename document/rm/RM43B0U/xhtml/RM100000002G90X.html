38. REMOVE CAMSHAFT HOUSING SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Uniformly loosen and remove the 18 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

(2) Remove the camshaft housing sub-assembly by prying between the cylinder head sub-assembly and camshaft housing sub-assembly with a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the contact surfaces of the cylinder head sub-assembly and camshaft housing sub-assembly.

51. REMOVE STIFFENING CRANKCASE ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove the 10 bolts.

(2) Remove the sfiffening crankcase assembly by prying between the stiffening crankcase assembly and crankshaft bearing cap with a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape.

NOTICE:

56. REMOVE STUD BOLT

(1) Using an E6 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 stud bolts from the stiffening crankcase assembly.

(2) Using an E8 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the stud bolt from the cylinder block sub-assembly.

(3) Using an E6 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 stud bolts from the no. 2 timing chain cover sub-assembly.

(4) Using an E8 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the stud bolt from the timing chain cover assembly.
