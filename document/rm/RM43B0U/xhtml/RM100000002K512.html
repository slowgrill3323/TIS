Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (except Gasoline Model): P057113; Brake Switch "A" Circuit Open; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

P057113

Brake Switch "A" Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

The No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) detects the brake operating conditions via the stop light switch signal.

The No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) incorporates circuitry to detect an open circuit.

This DTC is output when an open circuit is detected in the stop light signal input line.

DTC C13807E, P057100, or P057113 may be stored individually or simultaneously if there is a short between terminals in the stop light circuit (including the stop lamp connector, junction connector, etc.) due to water exposure.
