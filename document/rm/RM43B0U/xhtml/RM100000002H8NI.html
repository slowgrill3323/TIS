DTC

B140287

Lost Communication with Front Air Outlet Damper Control Servo Motor LIN Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning harness assembly connects the air conditioning amplifier assembly and the No. 3 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly.

The No. 3 air conditioning radiator damper servo sub-assembly sends damper position information to the air conditioning amplifier assembly.
