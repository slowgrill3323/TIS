Deck Light Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) receives the deck light switch assembly information and controls the following lights.

  • Center stop light assembly (cargo light)
  • Bed light LH
  • Bed light RH
  • Trailer reverse assist light assembly
