Title: NETWORKING: CXPI COMMUNICATION SYSTEM: B235287,B235587,B235887,B235987; Power Integration No.1 System Missing Message; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

Smart LDM Right Missing Message

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when CXPI communication between the semiconductor power integration ECU, power distribution box assembly, headlight assembly LH or headlight assembly RH and main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) stops for 10 seconds or more.
