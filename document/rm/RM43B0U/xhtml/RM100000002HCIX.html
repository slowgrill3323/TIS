INITIALIZATION

VERTICAL ACCELERATION SENSOR SIGNAL JUDGMENT

HINT:

  • When the absorber control ECU and acceleration sensor assembly are replaced or removed and installed, perform Judge Vertical Acceleration Sensor Signals.
  • Perform the Health Check using the GTS and check that no DTC is output.
  • To check the acceleration sensor, keep the vehicle stationary for 1 second or more. If the vehicle is not kept stationary for 1 second or more, it may cause the inspection item to display NG.

(a) Select "Vertical Acceleration Sensor Signal Judgment".

Chassis > Adaptive Variable Suspension System > Utility

Tester Display

Vertical Acceleration Sensor Signal Judgment

(b) If the inspection item display does not change to OK, check the relevant location.
