NOTICE:

(3) Lightly press the drill against the windshield outside moulding clips to drill off the windshield outside moulding clip flanges, and remove the 5 windshield outside moulding clips.

CAUTION:

Be careful of the drilled windshield outside moulding clips, as they may be hot.

NOTICE:

  • Pressing the drill too firmly will cause the windshield outside moulding clip to turn and result in the windshield outside moulding clip not being drilled through.
  • Prying the windshield outside moulding clips with the drill may damage the windshield outside moulding clip installation holes or drill bit.
