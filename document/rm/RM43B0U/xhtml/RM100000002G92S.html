77. REMOVE ENGINE ASSEMBLY

(a) Attach an engine sling device and hanger the engine with a chain block.

(b) Remove the 4 bolts.

(c) Remove the engine assembly by operating the engine sling device and chain block.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure that the engine is clear of all wiring and hoses.
  • While lowering the engine from the vehicle, do not allow it to contact the vehicle.
