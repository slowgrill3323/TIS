PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. DRAIN CLUTCH FLUID

NOTICE:

Immediately wash off any clutch fluid that comes into contact with any painted surfaces.

2. REMOVE SMART KEY ECU ASSEMBLY

Click here

3. REMOVE NO. 3 INSTRUMENT PANEL TO COWL BRACE SUB-ASSEMBLY

Click here

4. DISCONNECT CLUTCH RESERVOIR TUBE

(1) Disconnect the clutch reservoir tube.

HINT:

Use a container to collect the fluid.

5. DISCONNECT CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER TO FLEXIBLE HOSE TUBE

(1) Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the clutch master cylinder to flexible hose tube from the clutch pedal support way.

HINT:

Use a container to collect the fluid.

6. REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL SUPPORT ASSEMBLY

7. REMOVE CLUTCH START SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(1) Using a 14 mm deep socket wrench, loosen the lock nut and remove the clutch start switch assembly.

NOTICE:

To prevent damage to the clutch start switch assembly, when removing the clutch start switch assembly, hold it so it does not rotate as you remove it.

8. DISCONNECT CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER TO 2 WAY TUBE

(1) Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the clutch master cylinder to 2 way tube from the clutch master cylinder assembly.

9. DISCONNECT NO. 1 CLUTCH RESERVOIR HOSE

10. REMOVE CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER ASSEMBLY

11. REMOVE CLUTCH RESERVOIR TUBE

12. REMOVE CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR SUB-ASSEMBLY

Remove in this Direction
