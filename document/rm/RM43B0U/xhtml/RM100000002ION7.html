TCM Power Source Circuit

DESCRIPTION

When the ignition switch is turned to ON, auxiliary battery voltage is applied to terminal MREL of the ECM through the coil of the EFI-MAIN NO. 1 relay. This causes the EFI-MAIN NO. 1 relay to close, which supplies power to terminal +B of the TCM.
