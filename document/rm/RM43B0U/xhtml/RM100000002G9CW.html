PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE TOP SIDE PANEL RAIL B RH (for LH Side with Side Rail)

(a) for Short Deck:

(1) Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 4 "TORX" screws.

(2) Detach the guide and remove the top side panel rail B RH.

(b) for Long Deck:

(1) Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 5 "TORX" screws.

(2) Detach the guide and remove the top side panel rail B RH.

2. REMOVE TOP SIDE PANEL RAIL B RH (for RH Side with Side Rail)

(a) Use the same procedure described for the LH side.

3. REMOVE SIDE GATE SUPPORT CAP

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the other parts.

4. REMOVE REAR DECK TRIM COVER (for LH Side without Side Rail)

(a) for Short Deck:

(1) Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 4 "TORX" screws.

(2) Detach the guide and remove the rear deck trim cover.

(b) for Long Deck:

(1) Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 5 "TORX" screws.

(2) Detach the guide and remove the rear deck trim cover.

5. REMOVE REAR DECK TRIM COVER (for RH Side without Side Rail)

(a) Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
