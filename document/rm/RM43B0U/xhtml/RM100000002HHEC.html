Trailer Camera Icon is not Displayed or Blinking Intermittently

DESCRIPTION

The trailer camera ECU sends the user interface via the HMI menu to parking ECU for trailer camera operations.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Perform pairing after replacing or removing and installing the trailer camera ECU (communication telematics box).

    Click here

  • Inspect the fuse for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
