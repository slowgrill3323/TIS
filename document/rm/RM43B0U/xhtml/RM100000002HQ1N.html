PRECAUTION

PRECAUTION FOR POWER DISTRIBUTION BOX ASSEMBLY AND SEMICONDUCTOR POWER INTEGRATION ECU

(a) Do not remove or install the power distribution box assembly or semiconductor power integration ECU with the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal connected.

NOTICE:

  • After the ignition switch is turned off, there may be a waiting time before disconnecting the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

    Click here

  • When disconnecting and reconnecting the auxiliary battery.

    HINT:

    When disconnecting and reconnecting the auxiliary battery, there is an automatic learning function that completes learning when the respective system is used.

    Click here

(b) Make sure that the power distribution box assembly or semiconductor power integration ECU is not exposed to water, as it is not waterproof.

(c) Some load is connected to a dedicated circuit to prevent overcurrent. Therefore, parts other than those specified for the vehicle may not be able to be used.

(d) When replacing the power distribution box assembly or semiconductor power integration ECU, the system may not operate normally if the same part number is not used.

(e) Check that systems are operating normally before proceeding with troubleshooting.
