(2) Install the 2 side gears, 2 pinion gears, 2 side gear thrust washers, 2 pinion thrust washers and pinion shaft to the front differential case.

HINT:

Align the holes of the front differential case and pinion shaft.

(3) Measure the side gear backlash.

Using a dial indicator, measure the side gear backlash while holding one pinion gear toward the front differential case.

Standard Backlash:

w/ A.D.D.: 0.10 to 0.25 mm (0.00394 to 0.00984 in.) or less

w/o A.D.D.: 0.25 mm (0.00984 in.) or less

If the backlash is not as specified, install 2 side gear thrust washers with different thicknesses.

(4) Using a 5 mm pin punch and hammer, tap in the straight pin through the differential case and hole of the pinion shaft.

3. INSTALL DIFFERENTIAL RING GEAR

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Clean the contact surface of the front differential case and differential ring gear.

(2) Heat the differential ring gear in boiling water that is approximately 100°C (212°F).

(3) Carefully remove the differential ring gear from the boiling water.

(4) After the moisture on the differential ring gear has completely evaporated, quickly install the differential ring gear to the front differential case.

(5) Align the matchmarks on the differential ring gear and differential case.

(6) After the ring gear cools down, apply thread lock adhesive to the 12 set bolts and install them.

Thread lock:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1360K, Three Bond 1360K or equivalent

Torque:

115 N·m {1173 kgf·cm, 85 ft·lbf}
