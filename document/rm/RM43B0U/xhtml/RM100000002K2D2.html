(1) Using a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench, remove the drain (ATM) plug sub-assembly and gasket from the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly and drain the transmission fluid.

(2) Using a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench, remove the No. 1 transmission oil filler tube from the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly and drain the transmission fluid.

(3) Using a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench, install the No. 1 transmission oil filler tube to the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly.

Torque:

1.7 N·m {17 kgf·cm, 15 in·lbf}

(4) Temporarily install the gasket and drain (ATM) plug sub-assembly to the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly.

HINT:

Gasket replacement and tightening of the drain (ATM) plug sub-assembly are performed after the transmission fluid level is adjusted.

(5) Remove the drain plug and gasket from the hybrid module assembly and drain the transmission fluid.

(6) Install a new gasket and drain plug to the hybrid module assembly.

Torque:

20 N·m {204 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

4. REMOVE TRANSMISSION OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY

(1) Remove the 4 bolts, transmission oil pan sub-assembly and transmission oil pan gasket from the hybrid module assembly.

(2) Remove the transmission oil cleaner magnet from the transmission oil pan sub-assembly.

(3) Examine the particles in the transmission oil pan sub-assembly.

  1. Use the removed transmission oil cleaner magnets to collect any steel chips.

    Examine the chips and particles in the transmission oil pan sub-assembly and on the transmission oil cleaner magnets to determine what type of wear might be found in the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly.

    Steel (magnetic)

    Bearing, gear and clutch plate wear

    Brass (non-magnetic)

    Bushing wear

5. REMOVE TRANSMISSION VALVE BODY ASSEMBLY

(1) Using a screwdriver, disconnect the 2 solenoid valve connectors of each of the solenoid valves as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the solenoid valves or solenoid valve connectors.

(2) Remove the 4 bolts and transmission valve body assembly from the hybrid module assembly.

(3) Remove the bolt and wire harness clamp.

(4) Remove the 2 O-rings from the hybrid module assembly.
