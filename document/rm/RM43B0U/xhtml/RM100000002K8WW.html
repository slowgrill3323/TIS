PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL INTERCOOLER ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. LOOSEN COOLER CONDENSER ASSEMBLY

3. INSTALL RADIATOR ASSEMBLY

(1) Install the radiator assembly with the 6 bolts to the vehicle body.

Torque:

18 Nm {184 kgfcm, 13 ftlbf}

NOTICE:

Do not damage the cooler condenser assembly and radiator assembly when installing the radiator assembly.

(2) Connect the intercooler assembly connecter.

(3) Connect the 2 clamps to the radiator assembly.

4. INSTALL COOLER CONDENSER ASSEMBLY

(1) Install the cooler condenser assembly with the 2 nuts and 2 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

9.8 Nm {100 kgfcm, 87 inlbf}

NOTICE:

Do not damage the cooler condenser assembly when installing the cooler condenser assembly.

5. INSTALL EXTENSION WIRE ASSEMBLY

Torque:

8.0 Nm {82 kgfcm, 71 inlbf}

6. INSTALL FAN ASSEMBLY WITH MOTOR

(1) Temporarily install the fan assembly with motor to the radiator assembly with guide.

(2) Install the fan assembly with motor the 4 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

12 Nm {122 kgfcm, 9 ftlbf}

(1) Connect the 2 cooling fan motor connectors.

(2) Connect the clamp to the fan assembly with motor.
