21. INSTALL TRANSFER BEARING RETAINER SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

(1) Clean the 5 bolt holes.

(2) Apply seal packing to the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH as shown in the illustration.

Seal Packing:

Toyota Genuine Seal Packing 1281, Three Bond 1281 or equivalent

Seal width (A):

1.2 mm (0.0472 in.)

NOTICE:

If the removed transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH will be reused: After removing the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH, be sure to perform the following before reinstalling it: 1) using a knife, cut off any old seal packing on the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH contact surface, 2) clean off any remaining old seal packing from the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH contact surface, and 3) reapply seal packing to the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH.

(3) Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads on the end of the 5 new bolts.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1344, Three Bond 1344 or equivalent

(4) Install the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH with the 5 bolts.

Torque:

11.5 N·m {117 kgf·cm, 8 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Tighten the bolts of the transfer bearing retainer sub-assembly RH within 10 minutes of applying the seal packing.

    The seal packing will dry very quickly.

  • Be careful not to damage the transfer bearing retainer oil seal RH.
