49. REMOVE SEAT TRACK UPPER RAIL COVER LH (for Front Side)

Remove in this Direction

-

-

(1) Connect the No. 4 terminal of the slide motor to the positive (+) auxiliary battery terminal and the No. 3 terminal to the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to move the front seat adjuster assembly LH forward so that the claw of the seat track upper rail cover LH can be detached.

(2) Using a thin-bladed screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the seat track upper rail cover LH.

HINT:

Tape the thin-bladed screwdriver tip before use.

50. REMOVE SEAT TRACK UPPER RAIL COVER RH (for Front Side)

(a) Use the same procedure described for the LH side.

51. REMOVE SEAT TRACK UPPER RAIL COVER LH (for Rear Side)

Remove in this Direction

-

-

(1) Connect the No. 3 terminal of the slide motor to the positive (+) auxiliary battery terminal and the No. 4 terminal to the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to move the front seat adjuster assembly LH backward so that the claw of the seat track upper rail cover can be detached.

(2) Using a thin-bladed screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the seat track upper rail cover.

HINT:

Tape the thin-bladed screwdriver tip before use.

52. REMOVE SEAT TRACK UPPER RAIL COVER RH (for Rear Side)

(a) Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
