Electric Parking Brake does not Operate

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.

HINT:

Even if the electric parking brake is operating normally, the parking brake indicator light on the combination meter may be malfunctioning.
