PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE REAR PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER KIT (w/ Center Support Bearing)

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Place matchmarks on the propeller shaft yoke and universal joint yoke.

(2) Using a brass bar and hammer, slightly tap in the 4 spider bearings.

(3) Using needle-nose pliers, remove the 4 snap rings from the grooves.

*a

Hold

*b

Turn

*c

Hammering Point

-

-

(1) Using SST, push out the spider bearing from the propeller shaft assembly.

09332-25010

HINT:

Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.

(2) Clamp the protruding spider bearing in a vise.

(3) Using a hammer, tap the universal joint yoke to remove the spider bearing.

NOTICE:

Do not tap the intermediate shaft tube.

(4) Disconnect the intermediate shaft from the propeller shaft.

(5) Using SST, push out the spider bearing from the propeller shaft yoke.

09332-25010

HINT:

Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.

(6) Clamp the protruding spider bearing in a vise.

(7) Using a hammer, tap the propeller shaft yoke to remove the spider bearing.

NOTICE:

Do not tap the propeller shaft tube.

HINT:

Use the same removal procedure for the spider bearing on the opposite side.

(8) Remove the spider from the propeller shaft.

2. REMOVE NO. 1 CENTER SUPPORT BEARING ASSEMBLY (w/ Center Support Bearing)

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Fix the center support bearing in a vise.

(2) Using a chisel and hammer, loosen the staked part of the lock nut. Then remove the lock nut.

(3) Using a brass bar and hammer, tap the end of the intermediate shaft to remove the joint yoke, 2 washers and center support bearing from the intermediate shaft.

3. REMOVE REAR PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER KIT

HINT:

Use the same removal procedure for both the sleeve yoke side and flange yoke side spider bearings.

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Place matchmarks on the flange yoke and shaft.

(2) Using a brass bar and hammer, slightly tap in the spider bearing.

(3) Using needle-nose pliers, remove the snap ring from the grooves.

HINT:

Remove the snap ring on the opposite side using the same procedure.

*a

Hold

*b

Turn

*c

Hammering Point

-

-

(1) Using SST, push out the spider bearing from the shaft.

09332-25010

NOTICE:

Push out the spider bearing until the SST claws contact the shaft's universal joint yoke.

HINT:

Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.

(2) Clamp the protruding spider bearing in a vise.

(3) Using a hammer, tap the shaft yoke to remove the spider bearing.

NOTICE:

Do not tap the shaft tube.

HINT:

Use the same removal procedure for the spider bearing on the opposite side.

(4) Using SST, push out the spider bearing from the flange yoke.

09332-25010

NOTICE:

Push out the spider bearing until the SST claws contact the sleeve yoke.

HINT:

Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.

(5) Clamp the protruding spider bearing in a vise.

(6) Using a hammer, tap the flange yoke to remove the spider bearing.

HINT:

Use the same removal procedure for the spider bearing on the opposite side.

(7) Remove the spider from the flange yoke.
