Bolt A

Remove in this Direction

-

(1) Loosen the 11 bolts A and bolt B.

(2) Remove the 11 bolts A.

(3) Remove bolt B while supporting the hybrid module assembly, and then remove the hybrid module assembly.

CAUTION:

Be sure to perform the procedure with 2 or more people, as the hybrid module assembly is very heavy.

NOTICE:

  • To avoid damage to the knock pins, do not pry between the hybrid module assembly and the transmission assembly more than necessary.
  • When removing the hybrid module assembly, do not remove the torque converter assembly.
  • Remove the torque converter straight from the transmission while supporting the front module so that it will not come off.
