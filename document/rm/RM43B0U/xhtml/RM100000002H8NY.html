Air Conditioning Compressor Magnetic Clutch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning amplifier assembly outputs the magnet clutch on request signal to the ECM. The ECM sends a magnet clutch on permission signal to the air conditioning amplifier assembly. The air conditioning amplifier assembly turns the magnet clutch on based on this signal.
