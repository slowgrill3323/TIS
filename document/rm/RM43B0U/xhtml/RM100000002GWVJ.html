C115062

Trailer Brake Manual Slider Position Sensor Circuit Signal Compare Failure
  1. With the ignition switch ON and the IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, the manual trailer brake output slider standard value is continuously outside the specified range for 1 second or more
  2. With the ignition switch ON and the IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, the manual trailer brake output slider input 1 is continuously outside the specified range for 1 second or more
  3. With the ignition switch ON and the IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, the manual trailer brake output slider input 2 is continuously outside the specified range for 1 second or more
  4. With the ignition switch ON and the IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, the manual trailer brake output slider difference is continuously outside the specified range for 1 second or more
  5. With the ignition switch ON and the IGR terminal voltage from 11 to 14 V, the manual trailer brake output slider comparison is continuously outside the specified range for 1 second or more
  • Trailer brake control switch
  • Trailer brake control ECU (brake control with bracket relay)
  • Harness or connector
