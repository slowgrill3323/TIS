(1) Slide the 2 hose clips and disconnect the 2 transmission oil hoses from the transmission oil thermostat.

HINT:

Use a container to catch any ATF which flows out of the 2 oil cooler tubes and automatic transmission assembly.

(2) Remove the bolt (A) and No. 1 flexible hose clamp from the No. 1 oil cooler tube clamp.

(3) Remove the bolt (B) and No. 2 flexible hose clamp from the No. 2 oil cooler tube clamp.

(4) Remove the bolt (C) and No. 2 flexible hose clamp from the No. 3 oil cooler tube clamp.

(5) Remove the bolt (D) and disconnect the oil cooler tube from the engine assembly.

HINT:

It is not necessary to remove the oil cooler tube clamps unless they are being replaced.

(6) Remove the bolt (E) and No. 3 oil cooler tube clamp from the engine assembly.

HINT:

It is not necessary to remove the oil cooler tube clamps unless they are being replaced.

28. REMOVE HYBRID VEHICLE TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY WITH TRANSFER ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

  • The hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly can be removed without being tilted.
  • When titling the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly, do not tilt it excessively to allow the cooling fan and fan shroud to contact each other.

(1) Remove the 7 bolts and hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not use excessive force to pry out the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly when separating it from the engine assembly to prevent the knock pins from being damaged.
  • When removing the hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with transfer assembly, make sure that the oil cooler tube does not become damaged.
