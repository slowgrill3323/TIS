Title: AL80E (AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION SYSTEM: P070312; Brake Switch "B" Circuit Short to Battery; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

P070312

Brake Switch "B" Circuit Short to Battery

DESCRIPTION

The TCM uses the ON signal input from the stop light switch assembly for shift-down control when going downhill and for lock-up ON/OFF control.

A DTC is output when the stop light switch assembly stays on even when stop and go are performed repeatedly.

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

This DTC indicates that the stop light switch is remaining on. When the stop light switch remains on during GO and STOP driving, the TCM interprets this as a malfunction of the stop light switch, then illuminates the MIL and stores this DTC. The vehicle must GO (30 km/h (18.65 mph) or more) and STOP (less than 3 km/h (1.86 mph)) 5 times for 2 driving cycles in order to store this DTC.

  1. Clear the DTCs (even if no DTCs are stored, perform the clear DTC procedure).
  2. Turn the ignition switch off and wait for at least 30 seconds.
  3. Repeat the procedure below 5 times [*1]:
    • Drive the vehicle (vehicle speed is 30 km/h (18.65 mph) or more)
    • Stop the vehicle (vehicle speed is 3 km/h (1.86 mph) or less)

      HINT:

      [*1]: Normal judgment procedure.

      The normal judgment procedure is used to complete DTC judgment and also used when clearing permanent DTCs.

  4. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Transmission / Utility / All Readiness.
