Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (for Gasoline Model): C00409E,C00409F; Brake Pedal Switch "A" Stuck On; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C00409E

Brake Pedal Switch "A" Stuck On

DTC

C00409F

Brake Pedal Switch "A" Stuck Off

DESCRIPTION

The brake pedal load sensing switch signal turns on when the brake pedal is depressed with a force exceeding a predetermined level.

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) uses this circuit to detect if the brake pedal is depressed or not.

C00409E

Brake Pedal Switch "A" Stuck On

Either of the following is detected:

  • When the +BS terminal voltage is 9.5 V or higher with the stop light switch signal off, the brake pedal load sensing switch signal on, the master cylinder pressure below 0.7 MPa (71.1 kgf/cm 2 , 101 psi), and the engine speed 500 rpm or higher, the deceleration is below 0.08 G for 10 seconds or more.
  • When the +BS terminal voltage is 9.5 V or higher, the condition that the brake pedal load sensing switch signal remains on while the vehicle speed increases from 0 km/h (0 mph) to 30 km/h (19 mph) occurs 5 times in succession.
  • Brake pedal load sensing switch (brake pedal support assembly)
  • Wire harness and connector
  • Skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)

Brake/EPB

B

C00409F

Brake Pedal Switch "A" Stuck Off

Either of the following is detected:

  • When the +BS terminal voltage is from 9.5 to 17.4 V with the stop light switch signal on, the brake pedal load sensing switch signal off, and the master cylinder pressure 6 MPa (61.2 kgf/cm 2 , 870 psi) or more, the deceleration is 0.4 G or more for 1 second or more.
  • When the +BS terminal voltage is from 9.5 to 17.4 V with the stop light switch signal on, the brake pedal load sensing switch signal off, and the master cylinder pressure exceeding 6 MPa (61.2 kgf/cm 2 , 870 psi), the vehicle speed is 0 km/h (0 mph) for 5 seconds or more.
  • Brake pedal load sensing switch (brake pedal support assembly)
  • Wire harness and connector
  • Skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)
