Inverter Low-voltage Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The cause of the malfunction may be the low-voltage circuit.

Check whether there is an open circuit in the inverter +B low-voltage power source system.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

This diagnostic procedure is referenced to in the diagnostic procedure of several DTCs.

If the result of this diagnostic procedure is normal, proceed as directed in the procedure for the DTC.

CAUTION:

Refer to the precautions before inspecting high voltage circuit.
