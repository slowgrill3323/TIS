PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE OIL SEAL

(1) Using SST and a press, press in a new steering knuckle oil seal to the steering knuckle.

09950-60021 09951-01030 09950-70010 09951-07100

NOTICE:

  • The oil seal is a non-reusable part.
  • Do not damage the steering knuckle oil seal.

2. INSTALL FRONT AXLE HUB AND BEARING ASSEMBLY WITH ABS SENSOR

Click here

3. INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE ASSEMBLY

*a

Wooden Block

*b

Jack

(1) Connect the front lower ball joint attachment to the steering knuckle assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

300 N·m {3059 kgf·cm, 221 ft·lbf}

(2) Support the front suspension lower arm with a jack. Insert a wooden block between the jack and the front suspension lower arm to prevent damage.

(3) Install the steering knuckle assembly to the front suspension upper arm with a new nut.

Torque:

125 N·m {1275 kgf·cm, 92 ft·lbf}

(4) Install a new clip.

HINT:

If the holes for the clip are not aligned, tighten the nut up to another 60°.

9. CONNECT FRONT SPEED SENSOR

NOTICE:

  • Keep the tip of the front speed sensor and installation hole free of foreign matter.
  • Do not rotate or apply excessive force to the front speed sensor when removing it from the steering knuckle. Rotating or applying excessive force may result in damage to the front speed sensor.

Torque:

Bolt A :

8.5 N·m {87 kgf·cm, 75 in·lbf}

Bolt B :

12.5 N·m {127 kgf·cm, 9 ft·lbf}

10. INSTALL FRONT AXLE SHAFT NUT

(1) Clean the threaded parts on the front drive shaft assembly and a new front axle shaft nut using non-residue solvent.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to perform this work even when using a new front drive shaft assembly.
  • Keep the threaded parts free of oil and foreign matter.

(2) Using SST, install the new front axle shaft nut.

09229-55010

Torque:

340 N·m {3467 kgf·cm, 251 ft·lbf}

HINT:

Keep depressing the brake pedal to prevent the drive shaft from rotating.

(3) Using a chisel and hammer, stake the front axle shaft nut.

NOTICE:

Check that the staked part of the front axle shaft nut and the threaded part of the front drive shaft assembly are free of cracks. (Plating separation on the front axle shaft nut is acceptable.)

HINT:

Stake the front axle shaft nut toward the center of the front drive shaft assembly.

11. INSTALL FRONT AXLE HUB GREASE CAP

(1) Install a new front axle hub grease cap.

NOTICE:

Make sure to securely fit the grease cap to the axle hub.
