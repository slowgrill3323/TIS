(1) Install the rear No. 2 shock absorber cushion to the rear shock absorber assembly LH.

(2) Temporarily install the rear shock absorber assembly LH and rear No. 1 shock absorber cushion with a new nut as shown in the illustration.

(3) Temporarily install the lower side of the rear shock absorber assembly LH with the bolt.

(4) w/ AVS:

Connect the connector.

2. TEMPORARILY INSTALL REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY RH

3. TEMPORARILY INSTALL REAR LATERAL CONTROL ROD ASSEMBLY (for Coil Spring Type)

(1) Temporarily install the rear lateral control rod assembly to the rear axle housing assembly and vehicle body with the nut and bolt.

NOTICE:

Because the nut has its own stopper, do not turn the nut. Tighten the bolt with the nut secured.

5. STABILIZE SUSPENSION

(1) Lower the vehicle.

(2) Bounce the vehicle up and down several times to stabilize the suspension.

Torque:

Specified tightening torque :
