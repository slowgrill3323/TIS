C124A05

Braking System information System Programming Failure

When the vehicle speed is 40 km/h (25 mph) or more, one of the following conditions is met:

  • The engine type, powertrain variation and destination information sent from the ECM do not match the information stored in the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly).
  • No system information stored in the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly).
  • Incorrect ECM installed
  • Skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly)
