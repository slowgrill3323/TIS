DTC

B15FE11

XM Tuner Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

DTC

B15FE13

XM Tuner Antenna Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction occurs in the roof antenna assembly which is connected to the radio and display receiver assembly.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Priority

B15FE11

XM Tuner Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

The XM antenna is not connected.

(2 trip detection logic)
  • Antenna cord sub-assembly
  • No. 2 Antenna cord sub-assembly
  • No. 3 Antenna cord sub-assembly
  • Roof antenna assembly
  • Radio and display receiver assembly

B15FE13

XM Tuner Antenna Circuit Open

A short occurs in the XM antenna.

(2 trip detection logic)
  • Antenna cord sub-assembly
  • No. 2 Antenna cord sub-assembly
  • No. 3 Antenna cord sub-assembly
