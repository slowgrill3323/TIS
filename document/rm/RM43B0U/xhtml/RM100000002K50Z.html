Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (except Gasoline Model): C13807E; Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck On; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

DTC

C13807E

Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck On

DESCRIPTION

When a stop light illumination request is received from another system during pre-collision brake operation, etc., the stop light drive output (STPO) is turned on and the stop lights are illuminated.

DTC C13807E, P057100, or P057113 may be stored individually or simultaneously if there is a short between terminals in the stop light circuit (including the stop lamp connector, junction connector, etc.) due to water exposure.
