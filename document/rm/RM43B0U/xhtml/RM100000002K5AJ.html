Title: AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL: FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL CLUTCH SLEEVE AND DIFFERENTIAL CLUTCH HUB (w/ A.D.D.)

(a) Check that there is no wear or damage to the clutch hub and clutch sleeve.

Replace parts as necessary.

(b) Check that the clutch sleeve slides smoothly on the clutch hub.

Replace parts as necessary.

2. INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL CLUTCH SLEEVE AND DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR INTER SHAFT (w/ A.D.D.)

(a) Check for wear and damage to the clutch hub and side gear inter shaft.

Replace parts as necessary.

(b) Check that the clutch sleeve slides smoothly on the side gear inter shaft.

Replace parts as necessary.

3. INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL CLUTCH SLEEVE (w/ A.D.D.)

(a) Using a feeler gauge, measure the clearance between the sleeve fork and clutch sleeve.

Maximum clearance:

0.15 to 0.35 mm (0.00591 to 0.0138 in.)

If the clearance is more than the maximum, replace the fork or clutch sleeve.
