14. INSTALL FUEL DELIVERY GUARD

(1) Temporarily install the fuel delivery guard to the cylinder head sub-assembly and intake manifold with the bolt and nut A.

(2) Tighten the bolt and nut A in the order shown in the illustration.

Nut A :

(3) Connect the 2 clamps.
