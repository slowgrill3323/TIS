PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL NO. 1 BREATHER PLUG

2. INSTALL FLEX PLATE

(1) Apply a few drops of adhesive to 2 or 3 threads at the tip of each of the 12 bolts.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

(2) Install the flex plate and flex plate spacer to the hybrid module assembly with the 12 bolts.

Torque:

91 N·m {928 kgf·cm, 67 ft·lbf}

3. TEMPORARILY INSTALL HYBRID MODULE ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

When docking is performed with the torque converter assembly disconnected, internal gear parts may be damaged and cause a breakdown.

During the docking procedure, do not reuse an automatic transmission assembly with dimensions of 9.704 to 11.704 mm (0.382 to 0.460 in.) or less as shown in the illustration.

Do not push the torque converter assembly excessively when rotating it.

(1) Make sure that the knock pins are installed in 2 locations on the hybrid module assembly side and check that they are not damaged.

(2) Align the claw and knock pin hole of the torque converter assembly and the claw and knock pin of the flex plate with the positions shown in the illustration.

(3) Hold the hybrid module assembly and hybrid vehicle transmission assembly parallel. Align the knock pins with the knock pin holes, temporarily install the bolt in the positions shown in the illustration, and connect.

CAUTION:

Be sure to perform the procedure with 2 or more people, as the hybrid module assembly is very heavy.

NOTICE:

  • Accurately align the knock pins with the knock pin holes when installing to the hybrid module assembly and hybrid vehicle transmission assembly.
  • Check that the torque converter assembly moves smoothly.

4. INSTALL FLEX PLATE AND TORQUE CONVERTER SETTING BOLT

*a

for RH Side

*b

for LH Side

(1) Using SST, install the flex plate to the torque converter assembly with the 6 flex plate and torque converter setting bolts.

09961-00950

Torque:

48 N·m {489 kgf·cm, 35 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Check the bolt hole positions of the flex plate and torque converter assembly.
  • Turn the torque converter to a position where the flex plate and torque converter setting bolts can be installed, and install the flex plate and torque converter setting bolts while securing the hole of the flex plate with a wrench.
  • Install the flex plate and torque converter setting bolts in the order shown in the illustration.
  • If a flex plate and torque converter setting bolt falls into the hybrid module assembly, be sure to remove it with a magnet or other tool.

5. TIGHTEN HYBRID MODULE ASSEMBLY

(1) Install the hybrid module assembly and hybrid vehicle transmission assembly with the 12 bolts.

Torque:

29.4 N·m {300 kgf·cm, 22 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

Check that the contact surfaces are in close contact to each other and tighten the bolt.
