Stop&Start Operating Permission (A/C)

Item to Check or Action to be Taken

Conditions Required for Permission
  • Set the air conditioning system temperature to be approximately equivalent to the ambient temperature.
  • Front defroster is turned off
  • Turn off the blower switch and A/C switch, and then confirm that Data List item "Stop&Start Operating Permission (A/C)" indicates a value which permits engine stop by the stop and start system.
  • Confirm the air conditioning system conditions required for the stop and start system to operate are set.

Any of the following conditions are met:

  • Defogger Switch: OFF, and Blower Switch: OFF
  • Other than when stop and start control is prohibited

Heater:

Any of the following conditions are met:

  • Blower Switch: OFF
  • Blower Switch: ON, and A/C Switch: OFF

    (Ambient temperature higher than 10°C (50°F), engine coolant temperature higher than 55°C (131°F), or vehicle cabin temperature more than -5°C (23°F) lower than the set temperature)

  • Blower Switch: ON, and A/C Switch: OFF

    (after certain period of time since engine restarted by stop and start system)

Cooler:

Any of the following conditions are met:

  • Blower Switch: OFF
  • A/C switch: OFF
  • Ambient temperature 23°C (73°F) or less
  • Evaporator internal temperature less than 9°C (48°F), air mix damper opening value more than 10%, and vehicle cabin temperature less than 3°C (37°F) above set temperature or blower level less than 16 (when ignition switch turned to ON for first time in one trip)
