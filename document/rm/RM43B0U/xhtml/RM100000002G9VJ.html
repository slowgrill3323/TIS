Diagnostic Note

Abnormal History Clear after Actuator Replacement

Function to clear the actuator failure information history stored in the stabilizer control ECU.

HINT:

With the "Abnormal History Clear after Actuator Replacement", the failure history detected by the stabilizer control ECU for the first time is cleared.

Conditions for clearing: After replacing the actuator, and after operating the stabilizer with disconnection mechanism at least 2 times by the stabilizer control switch operation, check that no DTCs were detected, and clear any that have been.

NOTICE:

After repairs, clear the abnormality history before returning the vehicle.
