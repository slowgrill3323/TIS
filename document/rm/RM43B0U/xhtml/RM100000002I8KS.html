Starter Signal Circuit

DESCRIPTION

While the engine is being cranked, current flows from terminal STAR of the certification ECU (smart key ECU assembly) to the park/neutral position switch assembly*1 or clutch start switch*2 and to terminal STA of the ECM (STA signal).

  • *1: for Automatic Transmission
  • *2: for Manual Transmission
