PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL TRANSMISSION REVOLUTION SENSOR (NT)

(1) Coat a new O-ring with ATF and install it to the transmission revolution sensor (NT).

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage or twist the O-ring.

(2) Install the transmission revolution sensor (NT) with the bolt.

Torque:

5.4 N·m {55 kgf·cm, 48 in·lbf}

(3) Connect the transmission revolution sensor (NT) connector.
