Title: AL80E (AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION SYSTEM: P077571; Pressure Control Solenoid "B" Actuator Stuck; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

P077571

Pressure Control Solenoid "B" Actuator Stuck

DESCRIPTION

The TCM detects the gear position based on signals from the transmission revolution sensor (NT) and transmission revolution sensor (SP2).

If the detected gear position differs from the commanded gear position, the TCM detects a mechanical problem in the related solenoid valve, transmission valve body assembly or automatic transmission assembly.

One of the following conditions is met (2-trip detection logic):

  • When the TCM commands 5th, 6th, 7th or 8th gear, the transmission revolution sensor is detected 5 times in neutral for 0.5 seconds or more
  • When the TCM commands 1st gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 5th gear for 0.5 seconds or more
  • When the TCM commands 2nd gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 5th gear or 8th gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • When the TCM commands 3rd gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 5th gear or 7th gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • When the TCM commands 4th gear, the actual gear is detected 5 times as 5th gear or 6th gear for 0.5 seconds or more*
  • Solenoid (SL2) valve
  • Solenoid (SLT) valve
  • Transmission valve body assembly
  • Automatic transmission assembly (clutch, brake, gear, etc.)

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

The TCM commands gear shifts by turning the solenoid valves on and off. According to the input shaft revolution (speed) and output shaft revolution (speed), the TCM detects the actual gear (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th or reverse gear position). When the gear commanded by the TCM and the actual gear are not the same, the TCM illuminates the MIL and stores a DTC.
