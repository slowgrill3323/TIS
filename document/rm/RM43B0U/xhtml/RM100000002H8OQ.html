Title: LIGHTING (INT): LIGHTING SYSTEM: Instrument Panel Illumination does not Turn On; 2024 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 - 03/2024]

Instrument Panel Illumination does not Turn On

DESCRIPTION

The power distribution box assembly controls the instrument panel illuminations.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
  • First perform the communication function inspections in How to Proceed with Troubleshooting to confirm that there are no CXPI communication malfunctions before troubleshooting this symptom.

    Click here
