Sensor Frozen Indication (Dirty or Frozen)

DESCRIPTION

When the ultrasonic sensor is dirty or frozen, "Parking Assist Unavailable Clean Parking Assist Sensor" is displayed on the multi-information display in the combination meter assembly.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Perform registration after replacing or removing and installing the ultrasonic sensor or clearance warning ECU assembly.
