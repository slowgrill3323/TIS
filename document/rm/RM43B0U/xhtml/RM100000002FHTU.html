C116496

ABS Pump Motor Drive Circuit Component Internal Failure

DESCRIPTION

The pump motor is built into the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly). The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) monitors the motor power supply circuit of the pump motor.
