VSC OFF Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) is connected to the combination meter assembly via CAN communication.

  • except 2WD

    Pressing the VSC OFF switch turns off TRAC operation, and pressing and holding this switch turns off TRAC and VSC operation. If TRAC and VSC operations are turned off, "Traction Control Turned Off" will be displayed on the multi-information display and the VSC OFF indicator light will come on.

  • for 2WD

    If the VSC OFF switch is pressed when the vehicle speed is 50 km/h or less, the system enters AUTO LSD mode and VSC operation stops. Also, the AUTO LSD indicator light and VSC OFF indicator light illuminate.

    When the VSC OFF switch is pressed and held for 3 seconds or more with the vehicle stopped, the system enters VSC OFF mode and TRAC and VSC operation stop. Also, "Traction Control Turned Off" will be displayed on the multi-information display and the VSC OFF indicator light will come on.
