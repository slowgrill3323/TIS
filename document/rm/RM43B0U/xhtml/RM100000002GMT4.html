PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE AIR FUEL RATIO SENSOR

(1) Disengage the wire harness clamp.

(2) Disconnect the air fuel ratio sensor connector.

(3) Using SST, remove the air fuel ratio sensor from the converter with catalyst assembly.

NOTICE:

If the air fuel ratio sensor has been struck or dropped, replace it.
