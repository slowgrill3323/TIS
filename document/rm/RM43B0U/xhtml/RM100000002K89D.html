7. REMOVE FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE (for LH Side)

(1) Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 2 brake tubes while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench.

NOTICE:

  • Do not kink or damage the brake tube.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake tube from the connecting parts.

(2) Remove the 2 clips.

(3) Remove the bolt and front flexible hose from the vehicle body.

8. REMOVE FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE (for RH Side)

(a) Use the same procedure as for the LH side.

9. REMOVE NO. 2 BRAKE TUBE HEAT INSULATOR (for RH Side)

10. REMOVE BRAKE TUBE FLEXIBLE HOSE (for RH Side)

(1) Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 2 brake tubes while holding the brake tube flexible hose with a wrench.

NOTICE:

  • Do not kink or damage the brake tube.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake tube from the connecting parts.

(2) Remove the 2 clips and brake tube flexible hose from the vehicle body.

11. REMOVE BRAKE TUBE FLEXIBLE HOSE (for RH Side)

(1) Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 2 brake tubes while holding the brake tube flexible hose with a wrench.

NOTICE:

  • Do not kink or damage the brake tube.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake tube from the connecting parts.

(2) Remove the 2 clips and brake tube flexible hose from the vehicle body.
