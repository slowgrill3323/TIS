Low Beam RH Circuit Voltage Out of Range

DESCRIPTION

The light control LED ECU internally boost the power supply voltage to ensure a constant supplied current for the low beam LED of their respective headlight.

By monitoring the LED power supply voltage, abnormal current and malfunctions can be detected.
