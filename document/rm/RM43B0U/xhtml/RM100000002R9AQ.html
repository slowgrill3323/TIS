Protective Tape

NOTICE:

Insulate each disconnected high-voltage connector with insulating tape. Wrap the connector from the wire harness side to the end of the connector.

(2) Remove the nut and disconnect the guide and floor under wire.

(3) Disconnect the 2 No. 1 traction battery device box assembly connectors.

NOTICE:

Insulate each disconnected high-voltage connector with insulating tape. Wrap the connector from the wire harness side to the end of the connector.

(4) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the claw to disconnect the floor under wire.

(5) Remove the nut and disconnect the floor under wire.
