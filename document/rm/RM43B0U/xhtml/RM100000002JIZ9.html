16.

CHECK OPERATION AND CONDITION

(a) Operation conditions for downhill assist control.

  • Transfer position in H4
  • Shift lever not in P
  • Front differential lock and rear differential lock not selected
  • Accelerator pedal and brake pedal not depressed
  • Driving uphill at a vehicle speed of 30 km/h (19 mph) or lower

(b) Operation conditions for crawl control.

  • Transfer position in L4
  • Shift lever not in P or N
  • Driver side door closed
  • VSC system normal
  • Parking brake released
  • Engine started
