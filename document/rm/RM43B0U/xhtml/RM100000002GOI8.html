B1AAA96

Rear Center Microphone Circuit Component Internal Failure

DTC

B1AAB1C

Rear Center Microphone Circuit Circuit Voltage Out of Range

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction occurs in the active noise control microphone (RR) system.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

DTC Output from

Priority

B1AAA96

Rear Center Microphone Circuit Component Internal Failure

Engine running, engine speed is 1200 rpm or higher 3 seconds after the ignition switch is turned to ACC, the stereo component equalizer assembly detects a malfunction in the active noise control microphone (RR) for 4 seconds or more continuously*
  • Harness or connector
  • Active noise control microphone (RR)
  • Stereo component equalizer assembly

Active Noise Control

A

B1AAB1C

Rear Center Microphone Circuit Circuit Voltage Out of Range

When the stereo component equalizer assembly detects a active noise control microphone (RR) connection malfunction for 4 seconds or more continuously 3 seconds after the ignition switch is turned to ACC*
  • Harness or connector
  • Active noise control microphone (RR)
  • Stereo component equalizer assembly

Active Noise Control

A

HINT:

*: Malfunction monitoring is not performed during the following conditions to prevent erroneous detection.

  • The auxiliary battery voltage is less than 9.5 V.
  • Before 3 seconds have elapsed after the auxiliary battery voltage has returned to normal.
