DTC

P050031

Vehicle Speed Sensor "A" No Signal

DESCRIPTION

The No. 2 skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) converts the vehicle speed pulse signals output from the speed sensors installed to each wheel into vehicle speed signals and inputs these signals to the hybrid vehicle control ECU.

Hybrid Control

A

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

If there is no speed signal from the No. 2 skid control ECU even though the hybrid vehicle control ECU determines that the vehicle is being driven, the hybrid vehicle control ECU interprets this as a malfunction in the speed signal circuit. The hybrid vehicle control ECU then illuminates the MIL and stores this DTC.

  1. Connect the GTS to the DLC3.
  2. Turn the ignition switch to ON and turn the GTS on.
  3. Clear the DTCs (even if no DTCs are stored, perform the clear DTC procedure).
  4. Turn the ignition switch off and wait for 2 minutes or more.
  5. Turn the ignition switch to ON and turn the GTS on. [*1]
  6. Drive the vehicle at 20 km/h (12 mph) or more for 5 seconds or more.

    CAUTION:

    When performing the confirmation driving pattern, obey all speed limits and traffic laws.

  7. Stop the vehicle. [*2]

    HINT:

    [*1] to [*2]: Normal judgment procedure.

    The normal judgment procedure is used to complete DTC judgment and also used when clearing permanent DTCs.

  8. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Hybrid Control / Utility / All Readiness.
  9. Check the DTC judgment result.

    GTS Display

    Description

    NORMAL

    • System normal

    ABNORMAL
    • DTC judgment completed
    • System abnormal

    INCOMPLETE
    • DTC judgment not completed
    • Perform driving pattern after confirming DTC enabling conditions

    HINT:

    • If the judgment result is NORMAL, the system is normal.
    • If the judgment result is ABNORMAL, the system has a malfunction.
    • If the judgment result shows INCOMPLETE, perform the normal judgment procedure again.
