Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM (for Gasoline Model): C13807E; Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck On; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma [12/2023 -        ]

DTC

C13807E

Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck On

DESCRIPTION

When a stop light illumination request is received from another system during pre-collision brake operation, etc., the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) turns the stop light drive output (STPO) on and the stop light control relay (stop light switch assembly) operates.

C13807E

Stop Lamp Relay Actuator Stuck On

When the IGR terminal voltage exceeds 10 V and the +BS terminal voltage is 9.5 V or higher, stop light drive output (STPO) is OFF, STP is OFF and STP2 is ON continuously for 5 seconds or more.

  • Stop light switch assembly
  • STP circuit
  • STP2 circuit
