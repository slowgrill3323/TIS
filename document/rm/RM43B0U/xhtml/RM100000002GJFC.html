5. REMOVE TRANSFER EXTENSION HOUSING TYPE T OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST, remove the transfer extension housing type T oil seal.

NOTICE:

  • Apply grease to the tip of SST center bolt before use.
  • Be careful not to damage the transfer extension housing type T oil seal and rear transfer case contact surface.

6. INSTALL TRANSFER EXTENSION HOUSING TYPE T OIL SEAL

(a) Coat the lip of a new transfer extension housing type T oil seal with MP grease.

(b) Using SST and a hammer, install the transfer extension housing type T oil seal as shown in the illustration.

09631-12090 09950-60011 09951-00600 09950-70010 09951-07100

Drive in depth (A):

48.5 to 49.5 mm (1.910 to 1.948 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Tap the transfer extension housing type T oil seal evenly so that the transfer extension housing type T oil seal is straight.
  • Do not excessively tap the transfer extension housing type T oil seal.
