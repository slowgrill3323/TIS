PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT ENGINE MOUNTING INSULATOR LH

Pre-procedure1

(a) Remove the No. 1 engine under cover assembly.

HINT:

Click here

(b) Disconnect the No. 2 ventilation hose from the front engine mounting insulator LH.

(c) Connect a vacuum pump to the front engine mounting insulator LH.

(d) Apply negative pressure of 80 kPa (600 mmHg, 23.6 in.Hg) absolute pressure and wait for 1 minute.

Standard:

Vacuum pressure holds.

Procedure1

(e) Check if fluid leak from the front engine mounting insulator LH.

Standard:

No fluid leak found.

(f) If the result is not as specified, replace the front engine mounting insulator LH.

Post-procedure1

(g) Disconnect the vacuum pump.

(h) Connect the No. 2 ventilation hose to the front engine mounting insulator LH.

(i) Install the No. 1 engine under cover assembly.
