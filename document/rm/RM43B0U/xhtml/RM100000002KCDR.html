PROCEDURE

1. PRECAUTION

CAUTION:

  • Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.
  • There is risk of injury.

2. INSTALL FRONT SEAT AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

(1) Install the front seat airbag assembly LH with 2 new nuts.

Torque:

5.5 N·m {56 kgf·cm, 49 in·lbf}

CAUTION:

  • Do not reuse the nut.
  • If the installation surface or surrounding area of the front seat airbag assembly LH is deformed or damaged, replace the front seatback frame sub-assembly LH, the separate type front seatback cover and separate type front seatback pad.

(2) Attach the clamp.
