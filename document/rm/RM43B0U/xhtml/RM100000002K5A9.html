3. REMOVE OUTPUT SHAFT COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY (for Front Side)

*a

Turn

*b

Hold

(1) Using a chisel and hammer, loosen the staked part of the transfer output shaft companion flange nut.

NOTICE:

  • Completely loosen the staked part of the lock nut when removing it.
  • Do not damage the threads of the driven sprocket.

(2) Using SST to hold the output shaft companion flange sub-assembly, remove the transfer output shaft companion flange nut.

09330-00021

(3) Using SST, remove the output shaft companion flange sub-assembly.

09950-40011 09951-04020 09953-04030 09954-04010 09955-04031 09957-04010 09958-04011

NOTICE:

Apply a lubricant to the threads and end of SST.

(4) Using SST and hammer, remove the transfer output shaft companion flange oil seal.

09950-60011 09951-00220 09951-00290 09952-06010 09950-70010 09951-07100

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the transfer output shaft companion flange oil seal and output shaft companion flange sub-assembly contact surfaces.

24. REMOVE TRANSFER SHIFT ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

*1

Center Differential Lock Fork Sub-assembly

*2

Transfer Shift Actuator Assembly

(1) Using 2 screwdrivers and a hammer, tap out the 3 transfer gear shift fork snap rings.

NOTICE:

Using a cloth, prevent the transfer gear shift fork snap ring from flying off.

(2) Remove the center differential lock fork sub-assembly installation bolt.

(3) Remove the 4 transfer shift actuator assembly installation bolts.

(4) Remove the center differential lock fork sub-assembly and transfer shift actuator assembly.

NOTICE:

Take care not to drop the center differential lock fork sub-assembly.

(5) Remove the O-ring from the transfer shift actuator assembly.

25. REMOVE REAR TRANSFER OUTPUT SHAFT, TRANSFER FRONT DRIVE CHAIN, TRANSFER DRIVEN SPROCKET

NOTICE:

  • When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.
  • When using a vise, do not over tighten it.

(1) Using a snap ring expander, remove the transfer output shaft shaft snap ring.

(2) Using a plastic-faced hammer, carefully tap the rear transfer case, and remove the rear transfer output shaft, transfer driven sprocket and transfer front drive chain.
