2. SUPPORT REAR AXLE HOUSING ASSEMBLY

*a

Wooden Block

(1) Using a jack and wooden block, support the rear axle housing assembly.

NOTICE:

  • When jacking up the rear axle housing assembly, be sure to jack it up slowly.
  • Make sure to perform this operation with the vehicle kept as low as possible.

3. SEPARATE REAR LATERAL CONTROL ROD ASSEMBLY (for Coil Spring Type)

(1) Remove the bolt and nut, and separate the rear lateral control rod assembly from the vehicle body.

NOTICE:

Because the nut has its own stopper, do not turn the nut. Loosen the bolt with the nut secured.
