PROCEDURE

PROCEDURE

1. DISCONNECT FRONT NO. 2 AXLE INBOARD JOINT BOOT CLAMP

(a) for Gasoline Model TRD Package or 1Motor-HEV Model

(1) Using pliers, grasp and move the front No. 2 axle inboard joint boot clamp as shown in the illustration to disconnect it from the inboard joint boot.

(b) for Gasoline Models Other Than TRD Package

*a

Claw

-

-

(1) Using needle-nose pliers, detach the claw engagement and disconnect the front No. 2 axle inboard joint boot clamp as shown in the illustration.

2. DISCONNECT FRONT AXLE INBOARD JOINT BOOT CLAMP

(1) Using pliers, grasp and move the front axle inboard joint boot clamp as shown in the illustration to disconnect it from the inboard joint boot.

3. DISCONNECT INBOARD JOINT BOOT

4. REMOVE INBOARD JOINT

(a) for Gasoline Model TRD Package or 1Motor-HEV Model

*a

Matchmark

*b

Outboard Joint Side

(1) Remove any old grease from the inboard joint.

(2) Place matchmarks on the inboard joint and outboard joint shaft.

NOTICE:

Do not use a punch to make the matchmark.

(3) Remove the snap ring and inboard joint from the outboard joint shaft.

(4) Place matchmarks on the outboard joint shaft, inner race and cage.

NOTICE:

Do not use a punch to make the matchmark.

(5) Remove the 6 balls.

(6) Slide the cage to the outboard joint side.

(7) Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring.

(8) Using a brass bar and hammer, remove the inner race.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the inner race.

(9) Remove the cage.

(10) Remove the front No. 2 axle inboard joint boot setting clamp and front No. 1 axle inboard joint boot setting clamp, inboard joint boot from the outboard joint shaft.

(b) for Gasoline Models Other Than TRD Package

*a

Matchmark

-

-

(1) Remove the old grease from the inboard joint.

(2) Put matchmarks on the inboard joint and outboard joint shaft.

NOTICE:

Do not use a punch for the marks.

(3) Remove the inboard joint from the outboard joint shaft.

(4) Using a snap ring expander, remove the shaft snap ring from the outboard joint shaft.

(5) Put matchmarks on the outboard joint shaft and tripod joint.

NOTICE:

Do not use a punch for the marks.

(6) Using a brass bar and hammer, tap out the tripod joint from the outboard joint shaft.

NOTICE:

  • Do not tap the rollers.
  • Do not drop the tripod joint.

5. DISCONNECT FRONT NO. 2 AXLE OUTBOARD JOINT BOOT CLAMP

(a) Use the same procedure described for the front axle inboard joint boot clamp

6. DISCONNECT FRONT AXLE OUTBOARD JOINT BOOT CLAMP

(a) Use the same procedure described for the front axle inboard joint boot clamp

7. REMOVE OUTBOARD JOINT BOOT

(1) Remove the front axle outboard joint boot clamp, outboard joint boot and front No. 2 axle outboard joint boot clamp from the front drive outboard joint assembly.

(2) Remove any old grease from the front drive outboard joint assembly.

8. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT DUST COVER

(1) Using SST and a press, remove the front drive shaft dust cover from the front drive inboard joint assembly.

09950-00020

9. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB DUST SEAL

(1) Using a screwdriver and hammer, remove the front axle hub dust seal from the front drive outboard joint assembly.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the front drive outboard joint assembly.
