B009887

Right Side Restraints Sensor 3 Missing Message
  • The airbag ECU assembly detects a line short circuit signal, open circuit signal, short circuit to ground signal or short circuit to B+ signal in the side collision sensor RH circuit (bus 1) for 2 seconds.
  • No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly RH malfunction
  • Side airbag pressure sensor LH malfunction
  • Airbag ECU assembly malfunction
  • No. 2 side airbag sensor assembly RH
  • Side airbag pressure sensor LH
  • Airbag ECU assembly

Comes on

SRS Airbag

Not applicable
