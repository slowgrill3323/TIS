Title: DOOR LOCK: POWER DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM: Only Tailgate Lock/Unlock Functions do not Operate; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

Only Tailgate Lock/Unlock Functions do not Operate

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) activates the tail gate lock actuator assembly.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When using the GTS with the vehicle ignition switch off, connect the GTS to the DLC3 and turn a courtesy light switch on and off at intervals of 1.5 seconds or less until communication between the GTS and the vehicle begins.
  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
  • If the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) is replaced, refer to Registration.
