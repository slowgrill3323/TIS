Title: PAINTING / COATING: BODY PANEL UNDERCOATING: APPLICATION AREAS; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

APPLICATION AREAS

CAUTION:

Work must be performed while wearing the appropriate protective gear and in a well-ventilated area.

  1. Apply undercoating to the chassis, floor underside, sheet metal fitting weld points of the body, and inside of the wheel house to prevent rust and noise, as well as protect the body from gravel.

    NOTICE:

    Do not coat high temperature areas, such as the tailpipe, or moving parts, such as the drive shaft.

    HINT:

    • Apply degreasing agent to a clean cloth and clean any dirt and oil from the application areas.
    • Cover the surrounding areas of the application areas with masking paper to avoid coating unnecessary areas.
    • Apply body sealer to the panel joints in advance.
    • Do not leave any gaps between the panel joints.
    • Referring to the notes above, undercoating should be applied according to the specifications for your country.
