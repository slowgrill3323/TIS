Title: PAINTING / COATING: BODY PANEL SEALING: APPLICATION AREAS; 2025 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2024 - 11/2025]

APPLICATION AREAS

  1. Be sure to apply body sealer to the body panel joints and door edges (tip of outer panel folded part), etc., to waterproof and rustproof them.

    HINT:

    • Apply degreasing agent to a clean cloth and clean the sealer application areas.
    • After removing the applied spot sealer from the sealer application areas using thinner or equivalent, rustproof the areas by applying primer or equivalent. Then apply body sealer.
    • If sealer is unnecessarily applied to an area, apply degreasing agent to a clean cloth and clean off the body sealer immediately.
