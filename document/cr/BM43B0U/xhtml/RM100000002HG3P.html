GENERAL INFORMATION

SCOPE OF THE REPAIR WORK EXPLANATION

(a) This text explains the welding panel replacement instructions from the vehicle's body in white condition. Please refer to the electronic technical manual (repair manual) for explanations of the removal and reinstallation of the equipment parts up to the body in white condition and of the installation, inspection, adjustment and final inspection of equipment parts after replacing the welding panel.

HINT:

  • Illustrations of similar vehicle models are sometimes used. In these cases, minor details may be different from the actual vehicle.
  • About a symmetrical part, only one side is described.
  • The bold lines in the illustration indicate the standard unit for supplied parts.

(b) When cutting and joining parts (except outer panels), do not cut and join areas or use repair procedures that are not shown in this manual.

NOTICE:

Only work on the specified contents to maintain body strength.

(c) When cutting and joining outer panels, cut off a necessary range depending on the damaged area, but make sure to adhere to the following precautions.

(1) When cutting outer panels, do not cut the reinforcement or other inner panels.

(2) When butt welding outer panels, do not allow heat to affect the reinforcement or other inner panels.

(d) The following procedures are omitted from this manual. However, these procedures must be performed.

(1) Use a jack or lift to perform operations.

(2) Clean all removed parts.

(3) Perform a visual check before and after performing any work.
