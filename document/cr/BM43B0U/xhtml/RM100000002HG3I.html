ANTI-RUST TREATMENT AFTER INSTALLATION OF BODY STRUCTURE PARTS OR OUTER PANELS

BODY SEALER APPLICATION

*a

Sealer Gun

(a) To prevent water from entering the body panel or rust from forming, apply body sealer to the body panel seams and hems of the doors, hood, etc.

HINT:

Apply body sealer neatly to parts that require a high quality appearance.

UNDERCOAT APPLICATION

(a) To prevent corrosion and protect the body from gravel, always apply a sufficient undercoating to the areas indicated.

VEHICLE BODY ANTI-RUST AGENT APPLICATION

(a) Apply anti-rust agent through the service holes and/or installation holes of the parts for areas which are difficult to paint, such as the closed section structural areas.
