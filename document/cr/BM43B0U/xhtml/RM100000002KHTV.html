Follow the welding conditions below when welding ultra high strength steel to assure sufficient weld strength. (When repairing this model)

  • When welding 2 panels together including 1180 MPa or 780 MPa ultra high strength steel.

    *a: Plug weld

    Plug diameter

    10 mm (0.39 in.)

    Wire type

    AWS A5.18 ER70S-6/ER70S-3

    Shield gas

    Metal active gas

    NOTICE:

    Be sure to use Metal active gas (Ar + CO2 20 to 30% or Ar + O2 2%) as the shield gas when plug welding. Sufficient weld strength cannot be assured when using 100% CO2 shield gas.
