Title: BODY PANEL: FRONT FENDER APRON: ASSEMBLY REPLACEMENT; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [12/2023 -        ]

ASSEMBLY REPLACEMENT

  • With the radiator support assembly and cowl top side panel assembly removed.

REMOVAL

Symbol Meaning

Remove Weld Points

INSTALLATION

Symbol Meaning

Plug Weld

(a) Inspect the fitting of the related parts around the new parts before welding. This affects the appearance of the finish.

(b) Temporarily install the new parts and measure each part of the new parts in accordance with the body dimension diagram. (See the body dimensions)

(c) Before temporarily installing the new parts, weld the front fender apron assembly, front fender apron sub-assembly no.2 and front fender apron set plate lower with the standard number of welding points.

(d) After welding, apply body sealer to the corresponding parts. (See the painting/coating)
