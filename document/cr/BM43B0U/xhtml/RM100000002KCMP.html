Title: PAINTING / COATING: BODY PANEL ANTI-RUST AGENT MATERIAL: APPLICATION AREAS; 2024 - 2026 MY Tacoma Tacoma HV [03/2024 -        ]

APPLICATION AREAS

  1. Apply anti-rust agent (wax) to the doors and hood edges (tips of outer panel folded parts) and undersides, areas around hinges, etc., to prevent rust. Coat the undersides of the edges using a nozzle and air gun, and coat the areas around the hinges using a brush.

    HINT:

    If anti-rust agent (wax) is unnecessarily applied to an area, apply degreasing agent to a clean cloth and clean off the anti-rust agent (wax) immediately.
